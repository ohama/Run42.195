# Thursday, December 19, 2024

Overcast. Snow in the forecast.

---

The year's ending soon.

2024 is almost over.

---

Morning.

7km run to school.

It was cold.

Minus 5 degrees.

---

While I was running, I thought about the year.

March.

It started with bus sickness.

I started walking.

April.

Walking became a habit.

May.

I started running.

June.

I bought running shoes.

July.

A slump hit.

August.

Signed up for a 10km race.

September-October.

Completed my first race.

November.

Finished a half marathon.

December.

Decided on a full marathon.

---

*A lot has changed.*

In nine months.

The kid who used to ride the bus.

Became the kid who runs 21km.

---

School.

Everything was busy with year-end closing ceremony prep.

---

Lunch period.

I talked with Minseo.

"Hey, what's your best memory from this year?"

"Running."

Minseo laughed.

"Figure."

"What about you?"

"Me? Running with you."

"Thanks."

---

After school.

I ran into my teacher.

"Sir, thank you for everything this year."

"What are you talking about? You did great."

"Because you helped me."

My teacher smiled.

"Let's prepare together next year too."

"Yes, sir!"

---

I'm in my room writing this diary.

2024 is coming to an end.

So much has happened.

It started with running.

It will end with running.

Next March.

42.195km.

My goal for the new year.
