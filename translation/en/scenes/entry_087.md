# Monday, January 6, 2025

Cloudy. Cold.

---

Break is almost over.

I'll be in tenth grade soon.

---

Morning.

Did an easy 10km run.

I ran 20km last Thursday, so today I kept it short.

A recovery run.

---

I thought while running.

This time last year.

'It was winter break after the second semester.'

I hated going to school because of motion sickness on the bus.

Things are different now.

---

I'm actually looking forward to school.

Because I can run there.

---

Afternoon.

KakaoTalk from Minseo.

"When does school start?"

"Next Monday."

"Already?"

"Yeah. Tenth grade."

"Hope we're in the same class."

---

Evening.

Tenth grade.

The middle year of middle school.

Will anything change?

---

I'm in my room writing this diary.

I'll be in tenth grade soon.

A year ago, becoming a middle schooler was scary.

Because of motion sickness.

I'm not scared now.

Actually, I'm looking forward to it.

A tenth grader who runs to school.

55 days until the race.
