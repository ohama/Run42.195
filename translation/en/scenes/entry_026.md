# Monday, June 3, 2024

Clear skies. First day of June.

---

It's been a week since I got my running shoes.

Four runs in the past week. Monday, Thursday, Saturday, Sunday. I even ran on the weekend. Because I love my new shoes that much.

I ran again today.

---

Running shoes really are different.

When I used to run in my regular sneakers, my feet hurt. Every impact traveled straight through them. My knees ached too.

But running shoes are different. They're cushioned. With every step, the padding absorbs the shock. It feels like running on clouds.

And they're light. So much lighter than regular sneakers. My feet feel like they could take off.

---

Today's stats.

7 km in 42 minutes.

The same distance that took me 50 minutes last week. Eight minutes faster.

Of course, I can't run this fast every time. It depends on how I'm feeling. But I'm definitely getting faster.

Is it the running shoes? Better stamina? Probably both.

---

I passed Grandpa Yeongcheol at the tangerine orchard.

Today I didn't stop. Just waved as I ran by. He waved back.

I glanced at him while running. He was doing something in the field. Moving between the tangerine trees.

*Grandpa works every day too.*

I run every day. Grandpa comes out to his field every day. Somehow, it feels similar.

---

I felt great when I got to school.

7:35 a.m. Fifteen minutes earlier than usual.

I was drenched in sweat. It's June now, so even the mornings are getting hot. But I felt refreshed.

I sat on the bench and drank some water. My heart was pounding. I was breathing hard.

But I felt amazing.

---

Minseo asked me.

"Hey, how long did it take you today?"

"42 minutes."

"42 minutes? You did it in 50 last time."

"Yeah. Eight minutes faster."

Minseo's eyes went wide.

"Those running shoes are no joke."

"I know. They really are different."

"Maybe I should get some..."

Minseo muttered to himself. But it sounded like a joke.

---

I looked it up during class.

Running shoes benefits.

A bunch of articles came up. About why running shoes matter.

- Shock absorption prevents injuries
- Lighter weight means better efficiency
- Proper fit means comfort

All of it was true. I'd felt it for myself.

---

When I got home, I cleaned my running shoes.

After a week of running, they'd picked up some dust. I wiped them down with a wet cloth.

My blue running shoes. My first pair of running shoes.

*Good purchase.*

Mom helped pay for half, so I could afford them. I'm grateful.

---

I'm writing in my diary.

One week with running shoes.

They definitely make a difference. I'm faster, more comfortable, and less sore.

I learned that gear matters. I had no idea good shoes could make such a difference.

It's June now. Summer's coming.

Running in the heat will be tough. But I'm going to keep at it.

If I run early in the morning, I should be fine.

Because I have my running shoes.

My running shoes that protect my feet.

I'm grateful for these shoes.
