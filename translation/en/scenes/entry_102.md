---

---

Peak condition.

"Grandpa! Hi!"

"The race is on Sunday, right?"

"Yes!"

"Thank you!"

"Come tell me when you finish the race."

"I will!"

"Great. You're all set."

"Okay."

"Understood."

A full marathon.
