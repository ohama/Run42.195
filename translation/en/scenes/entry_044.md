# Monday, August 5, 2024

Clear skies. The heat wave continues.

---

I realized something today.

7km got easy.

---

Since summer break started, I've been running every day.

I've been aiming for 8km, but today I was short on time, so I only did 7km.

But something was different.

It wasn't hard.

---

7km.

Back in May, when I first ran the whole distance, it took 45 minutes and I felt like I was dying.

I remember hitting the second wind at 4km. Couldn't breathe, legs all stiff.

But today was different.

I just ran. No particularly hard stretch. Got a little winded, but nothing I couldn't handle.

38 minutes.

Seven minutes faster.

---

*My stamina really has improved.*

I could feel it for sure.

In May, I was gasping for air after 7km. Now it's just "okay, done with today's run."

For three months, I ran two or three times a week. Every day during break.

All that time turned into stamina.

---

When I got home, I told Mom.

"Mom, 7km got easy."

"Easy?"

"Yeah. It used to be hard, but now I can just run it."

Mom smiled.

"That's amazing. Our Doyun."

"I'm going to work up to 10km."

"Okay, take it slow."

---

In the afternoon, I looked it up.

'Running stamina improvement'

A bunch of articles came up.

'Three months of consistent running and you'll definitely improve.'
'The moment comes when a distance that used to be hard becomes easy.'
'That's when you increase your distance.'

That's exactly right. That's where I am now.

7km got easy, so it's time to try for 10km.

---

On Saturday, I ran with Minseo.

Minseo showed up in running shoes. White ones. They looked nice.

"Hey, nice shoes."

"Yeah. Way more comfortable."

We ran 3km together. Minseo had an easier time than before.

"Hey, am I getting better too?"

"Yeah. You definitely look better."

Minseo smiled.

---

Minseo's stamina is improving too.

At first, 1km felt like death. Now it's 3km.

I was the same way. Hard at first, but okay now.

I think that's how it is for everyone. It's hard in the beginning. But if you keep at it, you get better.

---

Writing in my diary now.

7km got easy.

The distance that felt like dying three months ago. Now I can just run it.

My stamina improved. For sure.

Time to try for 10km.

Maybe Thursday. 10km.

I need to plan the route. Add 2km to my 8km course.

Can I do it?

In May, I wondered if I could do 7km. I did.

Now I'm wondering if I can do 10km.

I probably can.

Let's try.
