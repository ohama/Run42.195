# Monday, August 19, 2024

Clear skies. The morning air's getting cooler.

---

I heard some crazy news today.

From my PE teacher.

---

I had to go to school during summer break.

Makeup class. Math.

After class, I ran into my PE teacher in the hallway.

"Oh, Doyun."

"Hi, teacher."

"You've been running a lot lately?"

"Yeah. I run every day during the break."

"Oh nice. How much are you running?"

"10km."

The teacher's eyes went wide.

---

"10km? That's impressive."

"Thank you."

"You know what? There's a regional marathon in October."

"A marathon?"

"Yeah. The Jeju Marathon. They have a 10km category too."

The teacher pulled out his phone and showed me something.

"The 37th Jeju Marathon. First Sunday in October."

Categories: Full marathon (42.195km), Half marathon (21.0975km), 10km, 5km

---

"You interested in the 10km race?"

The teacher asked.

"Me? Race?"

"Yeah. With your level, you could definitely finish it."

"..."

A race.

I'd never thought about it before. Racing alone is completely different, right?

Running with other people. Official times. Rankings.

My heart's beating faster just thinking about it.

---

"Think about it. Registration closes mid-September."

The teacher said.

"Okay... I'll think about it."

"Racing's a great experience. More motivating than running solo."

---

I kept thinking about it on the way home.

The marathon race.

The 10km category.

Could I actually do it?

I can run 10km. Fifty-eight minutes. Not a bad time.

But a race is different, right? I'd be nervous, there'd be so many people.

Could I pull it off?

---

I texted Minseo.

"hey there's a marathon in october"

"marathon?"

"yeah 10km category too"

"you gonna do it?"

"i dunno. you?"

"i cant do 10km lol is there a 5km?"

"yeah"

"oh cool maybe i'll do 5km?"

Minseo seemed interested too.

---

I told Mom that evening.

"Mom, there's a marathon in October."

"A marathon?"

"Yeah. The 10km race. I was thinking about doing it."

Mom thought for a second.

"Do you want to do it?"

"... I'm not sure. I think I'd be nervous."

"It's normal to be nervous. It's your first time."

Mom smiled.

"I think you should try it. It's a good experience."

---

I'm writing this diary entry now.

There's apparently a marathon.

First Sunday in October. The Jeju Marathon. 10km category.

My teacher said I should do it. Said I could definitely finish.

Could I actually do it?

I've never done a race before. I'm nervous about it.

But I kind of want to try too.

Running with other people. Crossing the finish line.

Just thinking about it makes my stomach flutter.

Registration closes mid-September. There's time to decide.

Let me think about it.

Looks like Minseo might do the 5km race.

It'd be cool to do it together.
