# Thursday, August 1, 2024

Clear skies. The sunflowers have bloomed.

---

It's August.

Third day of summer break. My morning running routine has settled in.

Wake up at 5. Leave by 5:15. Home by 6:30. Shower. Breakfast.

That's my summer break morning routine.

---

Today I ran 8km.

The course is a big loop around the neighborhood. Not the way to school—I go the opposite direction.

When I left at 5:15, it was still dim outside. I ran under the streetlights.

About twenty minutes in, the sun started to rise. The sky turned orange.

---

Sunflowers had bloomed.

Someone must have planted them by the roadside. Yellow sunflowers. About five big ones.

They were lifting their heads toward the sun. Just like their name suggests.

*They're pretty.*

I stopped running for a moment to look at them. Summer flowers. The symbol of summer.

---

The cicadas were deafening.

*Meem-meem-meem-meem.*

It was barely dawn and they were already singing. It felt like every tree had cicadas clinging to it.

The sound was so loud that instead of music, I listened to the cicadas as I ran. No earphones.

*This isn't bad either.*

---

Around the 6km mark, I passed near Grandpa Yeongcheol's field.

My route's changed, so I don't run through the tangerine orchard anymore, but I could see it from a distance.

Someone was out in the field. Probably Grandpa. He must start work at dawn.

*I should stop by and say hello sometime.*

---

8km complete. Forty-seven minutes.

I came back home. Sweat poured off me like rain.

After my shower, I felt refreshed. It was only 7 a.m., but I already felt like I'd accomplished something.

Mom made breakfast.

"You eat so well now that you're running every day."

"Exercise makes me hungry."

"That's a good thing."

---

In the afternoon, I texted Minseo.

*Hey, what are you up to today?*

*Nothing lol, it's too hot so I'm just staying home*

*I ran 8km this morning*

*You're insane lol I still can't wake up before 7*

*Let's run Saturday. Evening.*

*Ok*

---

In the evening, I watched videos of people who'd completed 10km.

On YouTube. Stuff like "First 10km completion review."

Everyone said it was tough. But they felt proud after finishing.

*I'll probably feel the same.*

I've made it to 8km, so that's only 2km more. Just a little more to add.

---

I'm writing my diary now.

My summer break morning routine has settled in.

Wake up at 5, leave by 5:15, run 8km.

Today I saw sunflowers. Heard the cicadas. A summer morning.

Things I never noticed when I took the bus.

Since I started running, I've become aware of the seasons.

In spring, rapeseed flowers and cherry blossoms.
In summer, hydrangeas and sunflowers.
What's there in fall? Autumn leaves?

I want to see them while I run.

Let's push for 10km.
