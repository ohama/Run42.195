# Monday, November 4, 2024

Clear skies, chilly.

---

Race day: T-20.

Twenty days left.

---

Morning.

7km morning run to school.

Starting today, I'm not increasing distance anymore.

Time to fine-tune my condition.

---

I kept it easy.

No pushing the pace either.

6-minute pace.

---

I passed Grandpa's field.

"Grandpa! Morning!"

"Running again today? How's the race prep going?"

"Great! Twenty days to go."

"You've got this. Rooting for you."

"Thank you!"

---

Got to school.

Wiped off the sweat and headed into class.

Minseok asked, "How many km today?"

"7km. Same as always."

"You're not bumping up the distance?"

"Nope. It's taper time now."

---

Lunchtime.

Ran into my teacher.

"Teacher, twenty days left."

"You're doing great. Right now, recovery is what matters most."

"Okay."

"Don't push yourself too hard. Get good sleep, eat well."

"I will."

---

Evening.

Did stretching in my room.

Rolled out my legs with the foam roller.

The method the teacher taught me.

Marathon prep isn't just about running.

Recovery matters just as much.

---

Writing in my diary now.

T-20.

Twenty days to go.

*No more pushing hard now.* I'll keep running, but I'm cutting back on distance and speed. Sleep enough, eat well, let my body rest.

Twenty days from now.

21km.

*I can do this.*
