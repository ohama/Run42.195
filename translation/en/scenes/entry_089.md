# Monday, January 13, 2025

Clear and cold.

---

First day of tenth grade.

School reopened.

---

Morning.

I ran to school.

7km.

The same familiar route.

---

I passed Grandpa's field.

He was there.

"Grandpa! Happy New Year!"

"Oh, Doyun! You too. How's the race training going?"

"Great! I have a full marathon in March."

"Full? 42km?"

"Yeah!"

Grandpa's eyes widened.

"That's incredible. You've got this."

"Thanks, Grandpa!"

---

Arrived at school.

New classroom.

New class.

---

Minseo was there.

"Hey! We're in the same class!"

"Oh really? That's awesome."

---

New homeroom teacher introduction time.

Self-introduction round.

"My name is Kim Doyun. My hobby is running."

The class laughed.

"I'm competing in a full marathon in March."

"Whoa, seriously?"

---

Lunchtime.

Ran into my old teacher from last year.

"Teacher! Happy New Year!"

"Doyun, how have you been?"

"Good. I've run up to 25km now."

"You're doing great. Keep up the training."

"Thanks!"

---

Came back to my room and writing this diary.

First day of tenth grade.

Same class as Minseo.

Going to school is fun now.

A year ago I hated it because of the bus ride.

Now I run there instead.

48 days until the race.
