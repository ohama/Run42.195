# Thursday, January 16, 2025

Cloudy. Rain forecast.

---

Rest day today.

---

My legs are sore after that 25km run.

Coach said something that stuck with me.

"Recovery is training too."

---

This morning.

I took the bus to school.

First time in a while.

No carsickness. Not even a hint of it.

---

I looked out the bus window.

Same landscape I see while running, but so different.

From the bus, everything flew past in seconds.

Running through it is so much better.

---

At school.

Minseo asked me, "Did you take the bus today?"

"Yeah. Rest day."

"A rest day? Why?"

"Recovery. My legs are really sore."

"Oh."

---

During lunch.

I did some stretching.

Loosened up my legs.

---

After school.

I found Coach.

"Coach, I took a rest day today."

"Good move. How are your legs?"

"Better. A little better."

"Tomorrow, just an easy 5km. Recovery run. Keep it light."

"Okay."

---

I'm back in my room now, writing this.

Today I rested.

And that's okay. Recovery is training.

Push too hard, and you get injured.

Injured, and you can't race.

You have to know when to rest.

45 days until the race.
