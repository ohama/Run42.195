# Monday, November 18, 2024

Clear skies, smells like winter.

---

Race week has started.

Six days left.

---

Morning.

Just ran 3km, nice and easy.

Felt incredibly short.

But that's the plan.

---

My body feels light.

My legs have strength.

My condition is getting better.

---

School.

I focused really well.

There's some nerves, but it's better than yesterday.

---

Lunch time.

Minseo asked me.

"You ready for the race?"

"Yeah. This week I'm just taking it easy, working on my conditioning."

"You need to eat well and sleep well."

"I know."

---

Minseo said, "The twenty-fourth is a Sunday, right? What time?"

"We leave at 8."

"We'll head out at 7. Let's meet up before you go."

"Thanks."

---

After school.

I saw my coach.

"Coach, it's race week."

"I know. How are you feeling? How's your body?"

"Good. My legs feel strong."

"Perfect. Looks like you're sticking to the plan."

---

"This week just do about 3km. Keep it slow."

"Okay."

"Thursday do about 5km, then rest on Friday and Saturday."

"Got it."

"And get to bed early the night before."

"Yes."

---

Evening.

Mom asked, "What are you going to eat during the race?"

"A banana and an energy bar. Like I did for the 10km race."

"What about breakfast?"

"Coach said to eat something light two hours before we start."

"Okay. I'll get it ready."

"Thanks, Mom."

---

In my room, writing this diary.

Race week.

Six days left.

My body feels ready.

I'm nervous, but I'm not afraid.

I've been preparing since March.

Started with bus sickness.

Then walking, running, the 10km race.

And now.

21km.

*I'm ready.*
