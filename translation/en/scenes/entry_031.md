# Thursday, June 20, 2024

Clear skies. Seriously hot.

---

Summer is coming.

Late June. The rainy season's taking a break. And it's hot. Really hot.

6:30 this morning. Already 26 degrees.

The moment I started running, I was sweating.

---

Not even 1km in and my back was soaked.

At 2km, sweat dripped down my forehead. Almost got in my eyes.

3km.

I was breathing harder than usual. Must be the heat. The air felt heavy.

*This is rough.*

Running on a hot day is definitely tough.

---

I stopped at 5km.

I was planning to go 7km, but the heat was unbearable.

I walked. The remaining 2km.

Drank water as I walked. My bottle emptied fast.

*What am I going to do in summer?*

July and August will be even hotter. Can I still run then?

---

I ran into Grandpa Yeongcheol near the tangerine fields.

He was sweating too. Wearing a hat, wiping his face with a towel.

"Doyun, hot out there, isn't it?"

"Yeah. Really hot."

"Well, it's summer. Of course it's hot."

He laughed.

"Run early in the morning. Before 6. That's when it's coolest."

"Before 6?"

"Yep. Before sunrise is best. Once the sun comes up, it gets hot fast."

---

Before 6.

That means leaving at 5:30. Waking up at 5.

*That's going to be rough.*

But if I want to run, I'll have to do it. On hot days.

---

By the time I got to school, my whole body was drenched in sweat.

Changed into my gym clothes. Hung up my wet uniform to dry.

Minseo asked me.

"Dude, you really went hard this morning. You're dripping."

"It was so hot. I could only do 5km."

"You say 'only' 5km?"

Minseo laughed.

"Hey, 5km is a lot. Especially in this heat."

---

At lunch, I searched online.

'Summer running tips'

- Run early morning or evening
- Drink lots of water
- Wear light-colored clothes
- Don't push too hard

All true. I learned that firsthand today.

Running early in the morning is the way to go. Just like Grandpa said.

---

On the bus home, I thought about it.

Summer is coming.

When it gets hot, running gets harder. But I don't want to stop.

I need to find a way.

Run early. Drink lots of water. Don't overdo it.

---

Writing in my diary now.

It was really hot today.

I only managed 5km. The heat wore me out.

Grandpa Yeongcheol told me to run before 6. Said it's cooler before sunrise.

Starting tomorrow, I'll head out earlier. 5:30.

It'll be hard, but I have no choice. If I want to keep running through summer.

The hydrangeas are starting to bloom. I saw them on the way to school. Blue, purple.

Summer flowers.

The seasons are changing.

I need to change too. Adapt to summer.

Time to adjust. To the heat.
