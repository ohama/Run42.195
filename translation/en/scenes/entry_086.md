# Thursday, January 2, 2025

Clear skies, cold.

---

My first run of the new year.

2025 has started.

---

6:00 AM.

20 km long run.

Right on schedule.

---

"Rest on New Year's Day, then start running on the 2nd," Coach said.

So today's the day.

---

I head out.

Cold air.

White breath streaming out.

The air of a new year.

---

10 km.

1 hour 2 minutes.

Not bad.

15 km.

1 hour 34 minutes.

My legs are getting heavier.

20 km.

2 hours 7 minutes.

Done.

---

*20 km.*

I'm almost at a half-marathon distance.

Next week I'll push it further.

---

I get home and shower.

My legs feel stiff.

I do some stretching.

---

Evening.

Talking with my family about the new year.

"What's your New Year's goal?" Mom asks.

"Finish a full marathon," I say.

Dad laughs.

"Of course it is."

Mom says, "Don't push yourself too hard."

"I won't."

---

Back in my room, writing this entry.

My first run of the new year.

20 km.

Done.

2025.

The year of 42.195 km.

59 days until March 2nd.

I can do this.
