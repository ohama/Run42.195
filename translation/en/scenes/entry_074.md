# Thursday, November 21, 2024

Clear skies. Cold.

---

Last run before the race.

Just 5km, easy pace.

---

Morning.

I ran slowly.

A check-up run. Testing how my body feels.

---

My legs feel light.

Breathing's smooth.

Everything feels good.

---

I passed Grandpa's field.

"Grandpa! Hello!"

"The race is Sunday, right?"

"Yes!"

"Go do well. I'm rooting for you."

"Thank you!"

---

Finished 5km.

30 minutes.

No problems.

Ready.

---

School.

During lunch, Minseong asked, "How's your body feeling?"

"Perfect. Seriously, I feel light."

"That's good."

He smiled.

"I'm excited for this weekend."

---

After school.

I ran into my coach.

"Coach, I did my last run. I feel great."

"Good. Take it easy tomorrow and Saturday."

"Got it."

"Sunday morning, just go out there at your best."

---

My coach said, "Remember a few things for the race."

"Okay."

"First, the first 5km—go slow. Don't get excited and sprint."

"Okay."

"Second, from 10km on, maintain your pace. Don't push too hard."

"Okay."

"Third, after 15km it's going to hurt. Don't give up then. Even if it's slow, keep moving forward."

"Got it."

---

"And the most important thing."

"What?"

"Enjoy it. It's your first half marathon."

"Okay."

---

Evening.

I packed my gear.

- Running clothes
- Running shoes
- Race bib
- Banana
- Energy bar
- Water

Everything's ready.

---

I'm in my room writing this.

Three days left.

Tomorrow and Saturday, I rest.

Then Sunday.

21km.

I'm nervous.

But I'm not scared.

I've trained for this.

Eight months of training.

I can do it.
