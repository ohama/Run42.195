# Thursday, October 24, 2024

Clear sky, autumn light.

---

The leaves are changing color.

This morning while running, the trees along the path were turning red.

*Fall's gotten deeper already.*

---

I looked toward Hallasan.

The middle slopes were painted red and yellow.

Beautiful.

If I'd been on the bus, I would've just missed it.

But seeing it while running makes it feel more beautiful somehow.

---

I passed through a tangerine orchard.

Orange tangerines and red autumn leaves.

Everything was autumn colors.

---

Grandfather Yeongcheol was there.

"Grandfather! Hi!"

"Running again today, huh? How far?"

"7km today. Running to school."

"You're keeping at it. Good job."

"Thank you!"

---

Arrived at school.

The zelkova tree on the sports field was changing too.

*In March it was cherry blossoms.*

Seasons keep turning. Now it's fall.

---

Lunchtime.

Minseo and I walked around the courtyard.

"Hey, the leaves are really pretty."

"Yeah. I saw Hallasan this morning while running and it was amazing."

"Did you take pictures?"

"Nah. Too busy running."

"That's too bad. Next time, get some shots."

"Okay."

---

Minseo asked me something.

"Wanna run together on Saturday?"

"Sure. Where?"

"Let's go see the leaves. Not the coastal road—toward the mountains."

"Oh, cool. Hallasan loop trail?"

"Yeah. I heard the colors are incredible there."

"How far?"

"Maybe 10km?"

"Cool."

---

After school.

I reported to my teacher.

"Teacher, I ran 15km on Monday."

"Oh, nice! Was it tough?"

"Yeah. Once I got past 11km, it got hard."

"That makes sense. You haven't done that distance before."

"But I did it."

My teacher smiled.

"You'll be able to handle 17km next week too."

"Yes!"

---

On the way home.

Through the bus window, I could see the autumn leaves.

Red, orange, yellow.

Autumn light.

*Once this season passes, winter will come.*

And when winter passes, spring comes again.

A year ago in spring, I was getting carsick on the bus.

Now I'm running and seeing the autumn colors.

I've changed a lot.

---

I come into my room and write in my diary.

The leaves are changing.

Hallasan has turned red.

Fall is getting deeper.

I promised to run with Minseo on Saturday to see the leaves.

Hallasan loop trail.

Seeing the colors while running.

In March we walked through rape blossoms.

In summer, sunflowers.

In fall, the maple leaves.

Different scenery with each season.

Another joy of running.
