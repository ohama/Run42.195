# Monday, February 10, 2025

Clear skies, spring is coming.

---

Rapeseed flowers are starting to bloom.

---

Morning.

10km run to school.

I saw yellow along the road.

Rapeseed flowers.

---

*Last year in March I saw them for the first time.*

Back then I just walked past them.

Now I'm running past them.

---

I passed by Grandpa's field.

I could see green shoots in the field.

"Grandpa! The rapeseed flowers are blooming!"

"Yeah. Spring's coming."

Grandpa laughed.

---

School.

Rapeseed flowers were blooming around the track too.

Yellow.

The color of spring.

---

Minseo said something.

"Hey, the rapeseed flowers look pretty."

"Yeah. Last year around this time I walked to school for the first time."

"Oh right. When you missed the bus."

"Yeah. I walked past the rapeseed flowers that day."

"You remember that?"

"Yeah. It was hard back then. It's different now."

---

I came into my room and started writing this.

Rapeseed flowers are starting to bloom.

Spring is coming.

Last year in March I walked past the rapeseed flowers.

This year I'm running past them.

Twenty days until the race.
