# Monday, October 28, 2024

Cloudy. Chilly.

---

I signed up for the half marathon.

November 24th. 21km.

---

Last week, I did a 15km long run.

1 hour 28 minutes.

It was tough. My legs got heavy past the 11km mark.

But I finished it.

This week, I'm going for 17km.

---

Morning.

I went to the race website on my phone.

[Jeju Half Marathon]
- Date: November 24 (Sun) 08:00
- Distance: Half Marathon (21.0975km)
- Location: Jeju Sports Complex

I clicked the register button.

My hands were shaking.

I entered my information and paid.

[Registration Complete]

---

'I actually signed up.'

21km.

I was nervous for the 10km race, and this is double that.

I'm scared. Honestly.

But I want to try.

---

School.

I told Minseo.

"I signed up for the half marathon."

"Seriously?! Wow, that's amazing."

"It's on November 24th."

"Can I come cheer you on?"

"Of course. Please come."

Minseo smiled.

"Dude, you're really something. Just six months ago you were taking the bus."

"I know."

"And now you're doing a half marathon."

---

Lunchtime.

I reported to the teacher.

"I signed up for the half marathon."

"Oh, finally. Good job."

"I ran 17km yesterday. It was hard."

"Of course it was. It's a new distance for you."

"Can I really do 21km?"

The teacher said,

"If you ran 17km, you can run 21km. It's only 4km more."

"But I heard those last 4km are the hardest."

"That's true. But you can do it."

---

Evening.

I told my family.

"I signed up for the half marathon."

Mom asked.

"When is it?"

"November 24th."

Dad said.

"We'll come cheer you on again."

"Thank you."

My little sister said.

"I want to see my brother run 21km!"

---

I'm in my room writing this diary.

I signed up for the half marathon.

November 24th. 21km.

My hands were shaking.

I'm scared. 21km is really far.

Even 17km was hard.

But I want to try.

I was scared before the 10km race too.

I finished in 48 minutes 47 seconds.

I can do this one too.

No, I'm going to try.

21km.

One month away.

Let's prepare.
