# February 3, 2025 (Monday)

Clear skies, spring in the air.

---

February has begun.

27 days until the race.

---

Morning.

15km run.

Starting tapering.

---

It feels so much lighter compared to the 25km long runs.

My body feels great.

---

I passed Grandpa's field.

I could see the young rapeseed flower shoots coming in.

*Spring is coming.*

---

Grandpa was there.

"Grandpa! Hi!"

"When did you say the race was again?"

"March 2nd!"

"Less than a month left."

"Yeah. I'm preparing hard."

"Do well. I'm rooting for you."

---

School.

Minseo asked me something.

"Hey, seems like you're running less these days?"

"Yeah. It's tapering."

"Tapering?"

"You reduce your distance before the race."

"Oh."

"You have to rest your body so you can run well on race day."

---

I come into my room and write in my diary.

February has begun.

I saw young rapeseed flower shoots.

Spring is coming.

27 days until the race.

I'm reducing my distance.

My body feels good.
