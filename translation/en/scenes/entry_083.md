Clear skies, cold air.

---

---

Morning.

17km long run.

Right on schedule.

---

5:30 a.m. departure.

I started running into the darkness.

Stars were visible.

---

10km mark.

The sun rose.

The sky turned orange.

It was beautiful.

---

17km.

1 hour 47 minutes.

Complete.

---

My body melted.

---

Afternoon.

I rested in my room and thought.

Winter break.

Two weeks.

I can focus on training like I did during summer break.

---

Evening.

I talked with my family.

"What are you going to do during break?"

"Run."

Dad laughed.

"Of course."

Mom said something.

"Are you going to run on Christmas too?"

"Maybe."

"Don't push yourself too hard."

"Okay."

---

I came into my room and write this entry.

Winter break has started.

17km long run complete.

I'll train hard during this break.

In January: 20km, 25km.

In February: 30km.

One step at a time.

Toward 42.195km.
