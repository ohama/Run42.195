# Thursday, November 28, 2024

Cloudy, chilly.

---

Four days after the race.

My body's still recovering.

---

My legs are stiff.

It hurts when I go down the stairs—that sharp pain in my thighs.

My teacher mentioned something about it.

"That's normal. After running 21km, you'll have soreness for a few days."

---

I didn't run today.

Took the bus to school instead.

*The bus. After all this time.*

---

On the bus.

I was worried I might get carsick.

But I was fine.

*Man, it used to be so hard for me.*

It was weird.

---

At school.

My friends congratulated me.

"Yo, you actually finished 21km?"

"Yeah."

"That's insane. Seriously."

---

Minseo said something.

"Dude, you looked rough at the end of the race."

"I was. After the 18km mark, my legs just wouldn't cooperate."

"But you didn't give up."

"I couldn't stop. Not when I'd come this far."

---

Lunchtime.

Ran into my teacher.

"Teacher, my legs still hurt."

"Of course they do. Rest for a few more days."

"Okay."

"Starting next week, do some light walking. Running can wait until the week after."

"Understood."

---

Evening.

I did some stretching in my room.

Worked out my legs with a foam roller.

It's getting better, little by little.

---

I'm in my room now, writing this.

I'm in recovery mode.

My legs still ache.

But I'm happy.

Because I finished it.

21km. I actually did it.

That memory's still so vivid.

How I felt crossing the finish line.

The weight of the medal in my hands.

Everyone cheering.

*I won't forget this.*
