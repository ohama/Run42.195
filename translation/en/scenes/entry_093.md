# Monday, January 27, 2025

Overcast. Rain.

---

34 days until the race.

34 days left.

---

Morning.

It rained.

I ran in my raincoat.

7km commute run.

---

Running in the rain is second nature to me now.

I did plenty of it back in June.

---

Got to school.

Can't tell if that's sweat or rain water.

Washed up in the bathroom and headed to class.

---

Minseo asked me.

"You ran here in this rain?"

"Yeah."

"You're crazy."

"I'm used to it."

---

Lunch.

I ran into Coach.

"Coach, what's the plan for this week?"

"Tomorrow, 25km. Thursday off. Then we start reducing distance next week."

"Huh?"

"Tapering. You have to cut back the distance before the race."

"Oh, like the 21km week."

"Right. But we'll scale back the volume for longer—about two weeks."

---

Evening.

Looked at my training plan in my room.

Early February: 25km
Mid-February: 20km
End of February: 15km, 10km

It keeps getting shorter.

---

I'm in my room writing this.

34 days.

Run 25km this week.

Start tapering next week.

Reduce the distance.

Give my body rest.

Peak condition for race day.
