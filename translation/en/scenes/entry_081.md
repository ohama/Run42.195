# Monday, December 16, 2024

Clear. Cold.

---

Today's long run goal: 15km.

Following the plan.

---

5:30 AM.

I'm off.

The sun rises late this time of year.

I started running in the darkness.

---

The air is freezing.

My breath comes out in white clouds.

My hands went numb.

I put on gloves.

---

5km.

The sky started getting brighter.

7km.

The sun came up.

10km.

My body warmed up.

---

15km.

1 hour 32 minutes.

Done.

---

It felt easier than before the half-marathon race.

My body has adapted.

---

At school.

Minseo asked me.

"How many kilometers today?"

"15km."

"In the morning?"

"Yeah."

"Wasn't it dark?"

"It's dark at first, but by the time I'm running, the sun comes up."

---

Lunchtime.

I ran into my coach.

"Coach, I did 15km today."

"Good job. Was it hard?"

"It was fine. Easier than the half-marathon."

"Your fitness level is improving."

---

"Next week is 17km."

"Okay."

"It's Christmas week, so don't push yourself too hard."

"Understood."

---

Back in my room, I'm writing this diary entry.

Completed 15km long run.

It was easier than before.

My body is adapting.

It's still a long way to 42km.

But I'm getting better week by week.
