# Thursday, July 4, 2024

Clear. Another scorching day.

---

I'm fighting the heat.

Woke up at 5. Left the house at 5:15. Wanted to run as much as possible before sunrise.

Outside, it was still dim. Streetlights were on.

Temperature was 25 degrees. Still hot, but better than midday.

---

I started running.

The early morning air was a little cooler. Not completely cool, but bearable.

1 km. Fine.
2 km. Sweating, but fine.
3 km. Out of breath.

I remembered stopping at 3 km on Monday. Could I push further today?

*Just a little more.*

---

I passed 4 km.

It was tough. But I could handle it.

5 km.

Checked my watch. Thirty minutes. Good pace.

*Today, 5 km. Success.*

I stopped. Gasped for air.

I ran 2 km more than Monday. Thanks to leaving earlier.

---

I walked the remaining 2 km.

The sun started rising. The sky turned orange. Beautiful.

But as soon as the sun came up, it got hot. Within five minutes, the temperature shot up.

*Grandpa was right. Once the sun rises, it gets hot fast.*

---

I ran into Grandpa Yeongcheol near the tangerine orchard.

He was out early too. Looked like he was doing fieldwork at dawn.

"Good morning, Grandpa."

"Oh, Doyun. Early again today."

"Yeah. It's so hot, I have to run early."

"Smart. Dawn is the only answer in summer."

Grandpa nodded.

"Drink lots of water. Rest when it gets too hot."

"I will."

---

I arrived at school.

6:40. Way earlier than usual.

Nobody was there yet. Quiet. I sat on a bench and drank some water.

*I ran 5 km today.*

Better than Monday. Am I getting used to the heat?

---

At lunch, I told Minseo.

"Hey, I ran 5 km today."

"Oh, more than Monday?"

"Yeah. Leaving earlier helps."

"What time did you leave?"

"5:15."

"What? 5:15?"

Minseo's eyes went wide.

"That's insane. You wake up that early?"

"No choice. It's too hot otherwise."

"..."

Minseo thought for a second, then asked.

"Hey, about running together Saturday evening... you think it'll be okay?"

"Should cool down a bit once the sun sets."

"Still gonna be over 30 degrees."

"The park has shade, remember? We'll run under the trees."

"Yeah, okay."

---

I asked Mom for a bigger water bottle.

"Why?"

"I run out of water when I run. I want to carry a bigger one."

Mom smiled.

"You're really dedicated, aren't you?"

"Summer's tough. I need to drink a lot."

"Alright. Let's go buy one this weekend."

---

I'm writing my diary.

I'm fighting the heat.

Left at 5:15 today and ran 5 km. Two more than Monday.

I think I'm adapting. Little by little.

Grandpa Yeongcheol said dawn is the only answer in summer.

He's right. Once the sun rises, the heat hits hard. I have to finish before it comes up.

Saturday, Minseo and I are running together. 7 PM.

Our first run together. I'm looking forward to it.

Can Minseo keep up? Even 2 km would be good.

I should cheer him on. The beginning is hard for everyone.

I know because I went through it too.
