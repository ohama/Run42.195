# Thursday, June 27, 2024

Cloudy. Sky looks like rain.

---

Minseo said something strange today.

"Hey, what if I started running too?"

---

It was lunchtime.

I was telling him about my morning run while we ate. How I went out at 5:30 and ran 7km.

Then out of nowhere, Minseo said it.

"Hey, what if I started running too?"

"What?"

"Running. Like you."

I froze.

"Seriously?"

"Yeah. Watching you run lately, it looks pretty good."

---

Minseo running?

Honestly, I couldn't picture it. Minseo hated exercise even more than I did. In PE class, he was always like, "I'm dying here."

"But you hate working out."

"So did you."

"..."

He had a point.

"But you run every day now. If you can change, why can't I?"

---

Minseo's eyes were serious.

He wasn't joking around.

"Do you really want to?"

"Yeah. But I'm scared to do it alone. I think I could do it if we did it together."

"Together?"

"Yeah. Want to run together?"

---

Running together.

Never thought about it. I always ran alone. At my own pace, whenever I wanted.

*What would it be like running with someone?*

"... Sure."

The answer came out before I realized it.

"Really?"

Minseo's face lit up.

"Yeah. But I head out at 5:30 in the morning. You okay with that?"

"5:30?"

Minseo made a face.

"... That might be rough."

"How about we try it on the weekend then? Saturday morning."

"Oh, that works!"

---

We decided to run together on Saturday.

Meet up at the park near my house. Try about 2km first.

Minseo said excitedly,

"Hey, do you think I can actually do this?"

"You can. It's hard for everyone at first. It was for me too."

"Really?"

"Yeah. I could barely do 500m in the beginning."

"Hey, if that's true, then I can definitely do it!"

---

During class, I kept thinking about it.

Running with Minseo.

I was looking forward to it. Weirdly.

Running alone was nice. But running together would be different.

Doing something with someone else.

---

On the bus home, Minseo asked me,

"Hey, what should I wear on Saturday?"

"Just workout clothes."

"What about shoes?"

"Running shoes would be good if you have them. Regular sneakers are fine too though. Since it's your first time."

"Okay."

Minseo pulled out his phone and started searching.

"Hey, how much do running shoes cost?"

"Cheap ones are around 50,000 won."

"Hmm... I'll try with regular shoes first, then buy some if I stick with it."

---

Writing this diary now.

Minseo asked me to run with him.

I was surprised. Minseo, running.

But I was happy too. Running alone is good, but running together will be different.

We're meeting at the park on Saturday. Going to try 2km.

It feels like gaining a teammate.

Back in March, when I missed the bus and started walking, Minseo was the first one to talk to me. "Hey, did you walk here?"

Since then, Minseo has been listening to my stories. Cheering me on.

Now we're running together.

Thanks, Minseo.

Can't wait for Saturday.
