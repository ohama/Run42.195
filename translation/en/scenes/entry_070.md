# Thursday, November 7, 2024

Cloudy, windy.

---

Minseo told me about his cheering plan.

"I'm gonna come cheer for you at the race."

"Really?"

"Yeah. My family wants to come too."

---

Lunch time.

Minseo asked, "Where's the race course?"

"It starts at Jeju Sports Complex, goes out along the coastal road, then comes back to the complex."

"So I can see you somewhere in the middle?"

"Yeah. Probably around the 10km mark."

Minseo laughed.

"Got it. I'll cheer for you there."

---

"By the way... are you nervous?"

"Honestly? I'm scared. 21km is my first time."

"You were scared before the 10km race too, right?"

"Yeah."

"But you did great then. You'll do great this time too."

"Thanks."

---

After school.

Ran 5km easily.

Didn't worry about pace. Just comfortable.

Checked how my body feels.

It's fine.

---

I told Coach.

"Coach, I'm only doing 5 to 7km these days."

"Good. You shouldn't push hard right now."

"Okay."

"The week before the race, cut it down even more. Maybe 3 to 5km."

"Got it."

---

Evening.

Talked with my family about the race.

"Where will you guys cheer from?" I asked.

Dad said, "I'll wait at the finish line."

Mom asked, "Can you see me somewhere in the middle?"

"Yeah, around the 10km mark. Minseo's family is cheering from there too."

My little sister said, "I want to go to the 10km mark!"

"Sure. Let's go together."

---

I'm in my room writing this now.

Minseo's coming to cheer.

His whole family is coming.

My family too.

At the 10km mark. At the finish line.

So many people cheering me on.

*I'm grateful.*

*I'm scared, but I feel strong.*

17 days left.

Let me prepare hard.
