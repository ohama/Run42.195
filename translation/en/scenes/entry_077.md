# Monday, December 2nd, 2024

Clear skies, cold.

---

I started running again after a week off.

---

This morning.

Just 3km, slow pace.

Like the coach told me to do.

---

The cold air filled my lungs.

December's here.

Winter's started.

---

3km.

18 minutes.

Ran slowly.

Checking my legs.

They're fine.

No pain.

---

I took the bus to school instead of running there.

I haven't fully recovered yet.

Taking it slow.

---

School.

Minseo asked me.

"Did you run today?"

"Yeah. Just 3km."

"How's your body feeling?"

"Good. My legs don't hurt."

"That's great."

---

Lunchtime.

Met the coach.

"Coach, I ran 3km today."

"How was it?"

"Good. No pain."

"Perfect. This week, keep it between 3-5km."

"Got it."

---

"Have you thought about your next goal?"

"Not yet."

"Take your time thinking about it. No rush."

"Okay."

---

Evening.

I sat in my room thinking.

Next goal.

Run 10km faster?

Another half-marathon?

Or maybe...

A full marathon?

42.195km.

---

I'm in my room writing this now.

I started running again.

3km.

My body's recovering.

I thought about my next goal.

The words "full marathon" keep drifting through my head.

42.195km.

Twice the half-marathon distance.

Is it even possible?

I don't know yet.

*Let me think about it slowly.*
