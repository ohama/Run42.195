# Monday, February 17, 2025

Clear skies, spring weather.

---

Two weeks until the race.

D-13.

---

Morning.

10km run.

Slow pace.

---

The rapeseed flowers were in full bloom.

The whole road was covered in yellow.

So beautiful.

---

I was thinking while I ran.

*Two weeks from now, I'll run 42km.*

Scary.

But exciting.

---

School.

Talked with Minseo during lunch.

"Hey, I'm going to come cheer you on at the race."

"Really?"

"Yeah. Will I be able to see you somewhere in the middle?"

"You should be able to catch me at the 20km mark."

"Got it. I'll wait there."

"Thanks."

---

After school.

Ran into my teacher.

"Teacher, two weeks left."

"Nervous?"

"Yeah. But I'm excited too."

"That's a good feeling. You're going to do great."

---

Evening.

Talked with my family.

"We're coming to cheer for you at the race," Dad said.

"I'll be waiting at the finish line," Mom said.

"Go for it!" my little sister said.

---

I came back to my room and wrote this.

D-13.

Two weeks left.

My family is coming.

Minseo is coming too.

So many people cheering me on.

I'm grateful.

Let's run strong.
