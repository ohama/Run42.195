# Thursday, December 12, 2024

Clear, below freezing.

---

My coach gave me a training plan.

---

After school.

In the PE office.

My coach handed me a piece of paper.

**Full Marathon Training Plan (12 Weeks)**

| Period | Weekly Goal | Long Run |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| December | Build base fitness | 15km |
| Early January | Increase distance | 20km |
| Late January | Increase distance | 25km |
| Early February | Peak distance | 30km |
| Mid-February | Adjust conditioning | 25km |
| Late February | Taper | 15km |

---

"In December, we're building your base fitness. Gradually increase your long run distance each week."

"Okay."

"From January, we'll seriously push the distance. Up to 30 kilometers."

"30km?"

"Yeah. If you want to run a full marathon, you need to have done 30km or more."

"Understood."

---

"The key thing is not to overdo it."

"Okay."

"If your body hurts, rest. If you get injured, you'll have to skip the race."

"Understood."

---

On the bus home.

I looked at the plan.

12 weeks.

That's long.

But I trained for 4 weeks before the 21km.

42 kilometers requires 12 weeks.

---

Evening.

I taped the plan to my bedroom wall.

I'll see it every day.

---

I'm in my room writing this diary.

I got the training plan.

12 weeks.

Building up to 30km.

It's scary but also exciting.

*Because my coach made the plan.*

*I just have to follow it.*

*One week at a time.*

*One step at a time.*

*Toward 42.195km.*
