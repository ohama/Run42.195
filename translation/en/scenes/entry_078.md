# Thursday, December 5, 2024

Clear skies, below freezing.

---

First day of sub-zero temperatures.

Morning temperature: -2°C.

---

Ran to school.

It's been a while since I've done that.

7km.

---

The cold air bit at my face.

Every breath came out as white clouds of mist.

It was freezing, but running kept me warm.

---

I passed by Grandpa's field.

All the tangerines had been harvested.

The field was completely empty.

Grandpa was there.

"Grandpa! Hi!"

"You're running even in this cold? I heard you did great in the race."

"Yeah! I finished 21km!"

Grandpa laughed.

"That's impressive. Keep it up."

"Thank you!"

---

Arrived at school.

I wiped the sweat off my face.

My body was burning hot.

*I can run in winter too.*

*Cold is just an excuse.*

---

Lunch time.

I talked with Minseo.

"Hey, wasn't it crazy cold today?"

"Yeah. Minus two."

"But you still ran?"

"Yeah."

Minseo laughed.

"You're seriously amazing."

---

After school.

I ran into my teacher.

"Teacher, I ran 7km today."

"That must have been rough in this weather. Nice work."

"It was okay. Running kept me warm."

"That's one of the advantages of winter running. You don't get exhausted from the heat."

"Right."

---

"Have you thought about your next goal?"

"A little."

"What is it?"

"A full marathon."

My teacher's eyes went wide.

"A full marathon? 42.195km?"

"Yeah. I'm not totally sure yet, but..."

"That's a big goal."

"Is it impossible?"

My teacher said, "No. You can do it. You've already come this far with the half."

---

I'm back in my room writing this entry.

Winter running.

I was cold, but I could still run.

I told my teacher about it.

About thinking about a full marathon.

42.195km.

*It's scary.*

But I want to try.

*Three months ago I was walking 7km in March.*

*Now I'm running 21km.*

*Why couldn't I do 42.195km?*
