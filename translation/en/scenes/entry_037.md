# Thursday, July 11, 2024

Rain. Typhoon passing through.

---

It's been three days since I ran.

The rain started Tuesday. Wednesday the typhoon hit. It's still raining today.

I've been stuck at home for three days. School was even canceled because of the typhoon.

---

My body feels restless.

My legs are dying to run. My whole body wants to move.

But I can't go outside. The rain is coming down too hard. The wind is fierce too. Going out would be dangerous.

I looked out the window. The rain was slanting sideways—that's how strong the wind was.

*Ugh...*

---

I tried to do something at home.

Going up and down the stairs. I climbed the apartment stairwell a few times and came back down.

Got a little out of breath. But it wasn't the same as running. Something was missing.

That feeling of running outside. Wind hitting my face, scenery passing by. I missed that.

---

I messaged Minseo.

"Dude I wanna run so bad"

"lol me too"
"My sore muscles finally recovered and now I want to run again"

"lol you too?"

"Yeah isn't it weird?"
"We only ran once but I already want to do it again"

I laughed. Minseo's getting hooked on running too.

---

The rain let up a bit in the afternoon.

I looked out the window. Just a light drizzle?

*Should I go out?*

I thought about it for a second. But Mom said no.

"We're still in the typhoon's path. The wind could pick up again out of nowhere."

"But the rain got lighter..."

"No. Wait until it's completely passed tomorrow."

Nothing I could do. Mom was right.

---

I checked the weather forecast in the evening.

"Typhoon fully passes tomorrow morning. Clear skies from afternoon."

I can run tomorrow afternoon.

*Finally.*

Three days. After three days, I can run.

I'm waiting. My whole body is waiting.

---

Before bed, I took out my running shoes.

My blue running shoes. The shoes I haven't worn in three days.

I wiped them down. There wasn't any dust, but I wiped them anyway.

*See you tomorrow.*

I said it to my shoes. Weird, I know.

---

Writing in my diary now.

Three days without running.

My body feels restless. My legs are dying to run.

This is the first time I've felt like this.

Back in March when I started walking, I didn't feel this way. It was fine if I didn't walk.

But now it's different. Not running feels suffocating. I want to run.

I guess running has become part of me.

The typhoon will pass tomorrow afternoon. I'll run then.

Just a little longer. I can wait.

Minseo said he wants to run too. Even though we only ran once together.

I think that's what's special about running.

Once you get hooked, you just keep wanting more.
