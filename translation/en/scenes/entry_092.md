# Thursday, January 23rd, 2025

Clear skies, cold.

---

Lunar New Year break has started.

---

Today was just an easy 10km.

Still recovering from the 30km run.

---

Morning.

Ran nice and slow.

Checking out how my body's doing.

Legs feel good.

The soreness has mostly gone away.

---

I passed by Grandfather's field.

He was there.

"Happy New Year, Grandfather!"

"Oh, right. It is New Year's, isn't it. You too."

"Yes, sir!"

Grandfather gave me some tangerines.

"These are Jeju tangerines. Eat them."

"Thank you!"

---

10km done.

56 minutes.

Not bad.

---

Evening.

Lunar New Year dinner with the family.

Had tteokguk—rice cake soup.

"You're a year older now," Mom said.

---

I'm back in my room writing this.

It's Lunar New Year.

I'm officially a year older.

Last year on this day, I was worried about bus sickness.

This year, I'm training for a full marathon.

Things have changed a lot.

38 days until the race.
