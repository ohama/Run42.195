# Monday, December 9, 2024

Cloudy and cold.

---

I've decided.

I'm going to run a full marathon.

42.195km.

---

This morning.

While running, I was thinking.

*There's a full marathon race next March.*

The Jeju Full Marathon.

March 2nd.

---

At school.

I told Minseo.

"I'm going to run a full marathon."

"What?! 42km?!"

"Yeah."

His eyes went wide.

"But you said the half marathon was hard?"

"Yeah. It was."

"So why?"

"Because I want to try it."

---

Minseo stared at me for a while.

"...You're actually crazy."

"I know."

"But it's cool."

"Thanks."

---

After school.

I told my teacher.

"Sir, I'm going to run a full marathon."

"You've made up your mind?"

"Yes. The Jeju Full Marathon on March 2nd."

My teacher smiled.

"Good. Let's prepare together."

---

"Going from 21km to 42km takes time."

"I know."

"We need to build your base fitness over winter, then really increase the distance starting in January."

"Okay."

"I'll make you a training plan."

"Thank you, sir."

---

Evening.

I told my family.

"I'm going to run a full marathon."

Dad was surprised.

"A full one? 42km?"

"Yes."

Mom looked worried.

"You struggled so much with the half marathon..."

"I know. But I want to try it."

Dad said, "If that's what you want to do, Doyun, I'll support you."

"Thanks, Dad."

---

I'm in my room now, writing this.

I've decided.

Full marathon.

42.195km.

March 2nd.

I'm scared.

The half marathon was hard enough, and this is double.

But I want to do it.

I started the year terrified of the bus ride.

Now in December, I'm committing to a full marathon.

I've changed a lot in a year.

Three months left.

Let's do this.
