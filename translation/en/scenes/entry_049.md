# Thursday, August 22, 2024

Clear skies. You can smell autumn in the air.

---

I've decided.

I'm going to do it.

---

I've been thinking about this for days.

Should I? Shouldn't I? I'm nervous about it. Can I really do this?

But I've made my decision.

*If I don't do it, I'll regret it.*

---

I went to the race website.

"37th Jeju Marathon"

Clicked on registration. Chose my category.

- Full Marathon (42.195km)
- Half Marathon (21.0975km)
- 10km
- 5km

I picked 10km.

---

Entry fee: 20,000 won.

I told Mom.

"Mom, I want to sign up for the marathon race."

"You've decided?"

"Yeah. I want to try it."

"Good. That's a good idea. How much is it?"

"20,000 won."

"I'll pay for it."

Mom handed me her card.

---

Registration complete.

The message showed up on screen.

"Registration confirmed."
"Category: 10km"
"Participant Number: 10-0847"

10-0847.

That's my number.

My hands were shaking. I actually did it.

---

I texted Minseo.

"yo i signed up"

"fr? for real?"

"yeah 10km"

"no way!!! should i?"

"do it do it we should do this together"

"ok im signing up"

---

30 minutes later Minseo texted back.

"yo i signed up"

"what distance?"

"5km"

"sick thats awesome"

"dude we're actually doing this race together lol"

"lol im so nervous"

"me too lol"

---

I wanted to tell Grandfather Yeongcheol too.

I'll see him tomorrow morning on my run and tell him then.

*Grandfather, I'm going to run in a marathon race.*

I bet he'll say something like "you got this."

---

That evening I looked up more info about the race.

The 10km course. You run along the coastal road.

Starting point, turnaround point, finish line.

The scenery should be amazing. Running while looking at the ocean.

Time limit: 1 hour 30 minutes.

I run 10km in 58 minutes so that's plenty of time.

---

But a race will be different though.

Lots of people. Nervous energy everywhere.

I might be slower than usual. Or maybe faster.

Who knows. I'll find out when I do it.

---

Before bed I looked at my running shoes.

Blue running shoes. Bought them back in May.

I'm going to run the race in these shoes.

*You got this.*

I said it to my shoes. It's weird but whatever.

---

Writing this diary entry now.

I signed up for the race.

10km. Participant number 10-0847.

I'm nervous. Actually nervous.

Am I really doing this? Running in an actual race?

Yeah. I'm doing it.

Six months ago I started walking because I got carsick on the bus.

Now I've signed up for a marathon race.

It's crazy. Feels like a dream.

But it's real.

One and a half months until October.

Time to train hard.

10km. Finish strong.

I really want to do this.
