# Monday, November 11, 2024

Clear skies. Cool air.

---

Today was a 12km adjustment run.

A shorter day. Just pulling back on the distance.

---

Early morning.

Started running slow.

So much lighter than when I was doing 17km.

My body feels good.

---

Turned around at the 6km mark.

12km done.

One hour, ten minutes.

Didn't worry about pace today. Just ran easy.

---

School.

My body's in good shape.

Not tired at all.

Is this what conditioning adjustment feels like?

---

Lunchtime.

Talked to Minseo.

"Hey, you look good."

"Huh? Really?"

"Yeah. You look less tired than last week."

"Oh, maybe because I'm running shorter distances now."

"Seems like it's working."

"Yeah. Just doing what the coach told me to."

---

After school.

Met with the coach.

"Coach, my body feels so light these days."

"That's conditioning adjustment. The fatigue is dropping off."

"Oh."

"If you want to run your best at the race, you need to rest now."

"Okay."

---

"During race week, just do about 3km."

"That little? That's okay?"

"Yeah. You have to give your body a break. You've got all the fitness built up by now."

"Got it."

---

Evening.

Thinking while I stretched.

Thirteen days left.

I've been training hard up until now.

Now it's time to rest and let my body recover. That's part of the preparation too.

---

In my room writing this.

12km adjustment run complete.

Body feels light.

Fatigue dropping away.

Doing what the coach said.

Will cut back even more during race week.

Thirteen days to go.

Want to come to the race in peak condition.
