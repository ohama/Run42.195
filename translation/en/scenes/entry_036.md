# Monday, July 8, 2024

Cloudy. The wind had picked up.

---

A typhoon is coming.

I saw it on the weather forecast. "Typhoon No. 5 heading north. Expected to impact Jeju from Wednesday to Thursday."

Wednesday to Thursday. Two days.

*I won't be able to run.*

---

I was able to run this morning.

The typhoon wasn't here yet. The wind had gotten stronger, but no rain.

Left at 5:15 AM, ran 6 km, walked 1 km. It was fine.

But the whole time I was running, I kept thinking about the typhoon.

---

On Saturday, I ran with Minseo.

We met at the park at 7 PM. Right as the sun was setting.

Minseo showed up in workout clothes. Her shoes were just regular ones. Not running shoes.

"Hey, ready?"

"Yeah. But I'm nervous."

"It's okay. We'll go slow."

---

We ran 1 km together.

Minseo struggled. After 500 meters, she started huffing and puffing.

"Hey, want to walk?"

"No, just a little more..."

She gritted her teeth.

We stopped at the 1 km mark. Minseo bent over, hands on her knees, gasping for air.

"Hah... this is hard..."

"You did great. You ran 1 km."

"Really?"

"Yeah."

Even though she was exhausted, Minseo smiled.

"Dude, I actually ran 1 km."

---

We walked the next 500 meters.

Then ran another 500 meters. Minseo too.

Total 2 km. Running 1.5 km, walking 0.5 km.

Minseo's first run.

"How was it?"

"Hard. Really hard. But..."

"But?"

"Weirdly satisfying."

Minseo smiled. That expression—I knew it. That feeling after your first run.

---

Today I got a KakaoTalk from Minseo.

"Hey my legs still hurt from Saturday lol"
"But I want to do it again"
"Let's go again next week"

I smiled. Is Minseo getting hooked on running too?

"Lol sore legs are normal"
"Let's do it next week"

I sent back.

But next week. With the typhoon, who knows what'll happen.

---

At lunch, Minseo brought it up.

"Hey, there's a typhoon coming, right? Can you still run?"

"I don't know. They said Wednesday to Thursday."

"Then Saturday should be fine, right?"

"Probably."

"Then let's run again Saturday. I need to work out these sore legs."

Minseo grinned.

---

I'm writing in my diary.

A typhoon is coming.

Wednesday to Thursday. Probably can't run for two days.

Disappointing. But nothing I can do about it. I can't control a typhoon.

I keep thinking about Saturday, running with Minseo. Her struggling after that first kilometer.

I was the same way. Back in March, when I first started walking.

Will Minseo get hooked on running? I hope she does. It'd be nice to have someone to run with.

Let's wait for the typhoon to pass.

Until then, I'll rest.

I read somewhere that rest is part of training too.
