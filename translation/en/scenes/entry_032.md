# Monday, June 24, 2024

Clear skies, but the early morning was cool.

---

I left the house at 5:30.

The sky was still dim. The sun was just about to rise.

The air felt different.

Way cooler than when I used to leave at 6:30. Around 22 degrees? Perfect temperature for running.

*Grandpa was right.*

---

I started running.

The cool air made my body feel light. I wasn't gasping as much either. So much easier than Thursday.

But I still sweated.

When you run, you sweat. That's just how it works. Even when it's cool. Because your body generates heat.

But today, the sweat felt different.

---

By the time I hit 3km, sweat was pouring from everywhere.

Forehead, back, chest, legs.

Sweat streamed down my skin. My shirt was soaked.

But it wasn't unpleasant.

Actually, it felt refreshing. Like something was leaving my body. Like all the bad stuff was draining out.

*So this is what sweat means.*

---

I used to hate sweating.

When I'd sweat during PE, it felt gross. Like I smelled bad. Uncomfortable.

When I'd sweat, I just wanted to shower right away.

But now it's different.

When I sweat, I think, *I'm running.* It's proof that I'm working hard.

Sweat wasn't a bad thing after all.

---

I finished 7km.

Today I ran the whole way. The cool air made it possible.

42 minutes. A good time.

When I got to school, I sat on a bench. Sweat was pouring off me like rain. But I felt amazing.

I drank water and wiped away the sweat.

*This feels good.*

Sweating feels good.

---

Minseo asked me.

"Hey, you're here early. What time did you leave?"

"5:30."

"What? 5:30?"

Minseo's eyes went wide.

"You're crazy. Why so early?"

"To beat the heat. It's cooler at dawn."

"Oh..."

Minseo nodded.

"Hey, but you're dripping sweat. Don't you smell?"

"I don't know. Do I?"

Minseo sniffed around and said.

"You're fine. Just smells like sweat."

"Just sweat?"

"Yeah. Not a bad smell. Smells like you exercised."

---

Smells like you exercised.

I liked that.

The sweat smell wasn't "a bad smell" but "the smell of exercise."

It felt like some kind of recognition.

---

When I got home, I showered.

Washing off the sweat felt refreshing. My body felt light.

I looked in the mirror. My face was flushed. I looked healthy.

*Sweating is a good thing.*

I didn't know that before. Sweat just felt gross.

Now I understand. Sweat is proof that you worked hard.

---

Writing in my diary now.

Today I left at 5:30 and ran. The cool air felt great.

I sweated a lot. But it felt good.

I used to hate sweating. It was sticky and unpleasant.

Now it's different. Sweat is proof of effort. It feels like the bad stuff is leaving my body.

The meaning of sweat has changed for me.

When I used to take the bus, I hated the smell of other people's sweat. On the packed bus.

Now my own sweat smell is okay. Because it's the smell of exercise.

It's strange. It's the same sweat.

Have I changed? Or is the sweat different?

Probably me. I'm the one who changed.

Running has changed a lot about me.

Even how I think about sweat.
