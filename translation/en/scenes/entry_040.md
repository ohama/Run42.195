# Monday, July 22, 2024

Clear skies. Scorching heat.

---

I took the bus today.

On purpose.

---

Things have been weird since Thursday.

Friday. I ran, but it just felt hard.

Saturday. I was supposed to run with Minseo. But I didn't want to go. I went anyway. Because Minseo showed up in new running shoes.

Sunday. Did nothing.

And then today.

---

When the alarm went off, I thought:

*Let's just take the bus today.*

I didn't want to run. Didn't want to get up. My body felt heavy.

The bus is easier, right? I can just sit and ride.

Motion sickness? It's summer break, so there'll be fewer people. I'll be able to get a seat.

---

I left the house at 7:20.

Went to the bus stop. Waited for the bus.

Strange feeling. My first morning bus since March.

The bus came. I got on.

---

It was emptier than I expected inside.

Summer break meant fewer students. I got a seat.

I sat by the window. The AC felt cool.

*This is comfortable.*

That's what I thought. Way more comfortable than running.

---

But after about ten minutes, something felt off.

I felt suffocated.

The AC was cool, but something felt stifling. Like I couldn't breathe.

I looked out the window. Familiar scenery rushed past. The tangerine orchards. The road I run every day.

*Wonder if the old man's there.*

Couldn't see from the bus. It went by too fast.

---

I arrived at school.

Forty minutes. Running takes forty-five.

Only a five-minute difference.

But it felt completely different.

---

I sat on the bench.

I wasn't sweating. I wasn't out of breath. I was comfortable.

But.

Something felt missing.

That refreshed feeling after a morning run. That clean sensation after sweating. None of it.

*Ah.*

I think I got it.

---

I think I understood why I run.

I don't run to be comfortable.

The bus is comfortable. There's AC, I can sit.

But running feels good. It's hard, but it feels good.

Comfortable and good are different things.

I wanted what felt good.

---

I messaged Minseo.

"Hey, I took the bus today."

"Why? Something wrong?"

"No, just wanted to try it."

"How was it?"

"... Not great."

"Lol, I figured."

Minseo sent a laughing emoji.

"Hey, let's run Saturday. You can't skip."

---

I'm writing in my diary.

I took the bus today.

Because I was in a slump. Because I didn't want to run. Because I wanted to be comfortable.

But taking the bus made me realize.

I didn't want comfort. I wanted something good.

Running is hard. But it's good.

The bus is comfortable. But it's not good.

I think my slump is lifting a bit.

Taking the bus showed me. I need to run after all.

I'll run again tomorrow.

5:15 in the morning.
