# Thursday, February 6, 2025

Cloudy, getting warmer.

---

Thought about a year ago.

---

Morning.

10km run.

---

While running, I thought about it.

One year ago. February.

Middle school entrance prep.

Worried about the bus commute.

*7km every day on the bus...*

Motion sickness anxiety.

---

Now.

7km every day on foot.

No bus.

No motion sickness.

---

So much has changed.

---

School.

Talked with Minseo during lunch.

"Hey, remember a year ago?"

"What?"

"I had really bad motion sickness on the bus, remember?"

"Oh yeah. You were struggling every day."

"Yeah."

"You don't have that problem now."

"Yeah. Because I'm not taking the bus anymore."

Minseo laughed.

"Running instead of the bus. That was a good choice."

---

After school.

Ran into my teacher.

"Teacher, it's been a year."

"What has?"

"Since I started running. From last March."

"Oh, that's right."

"I've changed a lot."

The teacher smiled.

"Yeah. You've grown a lot."

---

Got back to my room and started writing this entry.

Thought about a year ago.

The me who was worried about bus motion sickness.

Now I'm preparing for a full marathon.

So much has changed.

24 days until the race.
