# Monday, July 29, 2024

Clear skies. Scorching hot.

---

Summer break.

No school. No need to run the 7km to get there.

But I run anyway. Because I want to.

---

I made a training plan for the break.

Goal: 10km nonstop

Right now I can run 7km. I want to push it to 10km by the end of summer.

Schedule:
- Wake up at 5 AM every day
- Leave at 5:15
- Mon/Wed/Fri: Long distance (gradually increase from 7km to 10km)
- Tue/Thu: Short distance + fast pace (3-5km)
- Sat: Run with Minseo
- Sun: Rest

---

Day one. Today.

Left at 5:15 and ran 8km.

Changed my route. Not the 7km to school, but a big loop around the neighborhood.

8km. 48 minutes.

It was hard. My legs felt stiff for the last kilometer.

But I did it.

---

Got home, showered, ate breakfast.

Mom asked.

"You already went running?"

"Yeah."

"How many kilometers?"

"8km."

Her eyes went wide.

"8km? Wasn't it 7 before?"

"Trying to increase it over the break."

"..."

She sighed. But she was smiling.

"Our Doyun's going to become an athlete."

"I'm not an athlete."

"Close enough."

---

In the afternoon, I messaged Minseo.

'Hey I made a training plan for summer'

'Oh what is it?'

'Running every day. Six days a week.'

'You're crazy lol'

'What about you?'

'Me... three days a week? Tue Thu Sat?'

'Cool. Let's run together on Saturdays.'

'Sure'

Looks like Minseo's planning to keep running over the break too.

---

In the evening, I searched for posts from people who'd finished 10km races.

'Anyone can do 10km.'
'Stick with it and you can get there in 2-3 months.'
'Focus on distance, not pace.'

Reading them made me feel confident.

I can do this too. I've already come 8km. Just a little more.

---

Writing in my diary.

Summer break has begun.

I made a training plan.

Goal: 10km nonstop

Currently at 8km. Just need to add 2 more.

Running every day. Six days a week. One day off.

It's going to be tough. In this summer heat.

But I want to try.

In March, I missed the bus and started walking. Even 1km was hard.

Now I run 8km.

What will I be doing three months from now?

10km? Or more?

A marathon is 42.195km.

Still far. So, so far.

But someday.

First, 10km.

By the end of summer break, I really want to run 10km nonstop.

Let's do this.
