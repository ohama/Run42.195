## Narrative Perspective

**POV:** [first / second / third_limited / third_omniscient]

> - first: "I walked down the road"
> - second: "You walk down the road" (rare)
> - third_limited: "She walked down the road, wondering if..."
> - third_omniscient: "She walked down the road. Across town, he was thinking..."

**POV Character(s):** [Whose perspective? If multiple, list all]

**Tense:** [past / present]

> - past: "She walked down the road"
> - present: "She walks down the road"

**Narrative Distance:** [close / medium / far]

> - close: Deep in character's head, limited vocabulary to what they know, stream of consciousness
> - medium: Character's perspective but some authorial voice allowed
> - far: Distant, observational, broader vocabulary, narrator distinct from character

## Forbidden Elements

### Banned Phrases

These should never appear in the prose:

- "suddenly"
- "all at once"
- "couldn't help but"
- "let out a breath"
- "found herself [verb]ing"
- [Add more as needed]

### Cliche Watchlist

Flag these for review (warn but don't auto-reject):

- "eyes widened"
- "heart pounded"
- "let out a breath she didn't realize she was holding"
- "time seemed to slow"
- "a single tear rolled down"
- [Add more as needed]

### Other Restrictions

- [ ] No adverbs in dialogue tags (said quietly -> spoke in a whisper)
- [ ] No passive voice except when deliberate
- [ ] No filter words (she saw, he felt, they heard) - show directly
- [ ] Limit exclamation points (max 1 per chapter)
