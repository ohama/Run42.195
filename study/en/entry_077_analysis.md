# PART 1: EVERYDAY EXPRESSIONS (일상 표현)

## 1.1 Running & Recovery Vocabulary

### "I started running again after a week off."

**Original Korean:** "일주일 쉰 후 다시 달리기를 시작했다"

**Analysis:**
- "Running again"은 재개를 강조
- 시간 표현 "after a week off"로 휴식 기간 명시
- 간단하면서도 효과적인 문장 구조

**Variations by Context:**

| Full Expression | Context | Nuance |
|-----------------|---------|--------|
| "I started running again after a week off." | 휴식 후 재개 | 계획된 휴식 |
| "I got back to running after a week off." | 휴식 후 복귀 | 자연스러운 복귀 |
| "I returned to running after a week off." | 휴식 후 돌아옴 | 더 격식적 |
| "I resumed my runs after a week off." | 휴식 후 재개 | 매우 격식적 |

**Related Expressions:**

| Expression | Korean | Context |
|------------|--------|---------|
| "take a break from running" | 달리기를 쉬다 | 의도적 휴식 |
| "get back into running" | 달리기를 다시 시작하다 | 습관으로 돌아가기 |
| "return to the sport" | 스포츠로 돌아가다 | 더 격식적 |
| "be sidelined" | 활동 못하다 | 부상이나 문제로 |
| "ease back in" | 천천히 재개하다 | 안전하게 |

**Practice Dialogue:**

```
A: You haven't been running lately, right?
B: Yeah, I took a week off because of my leg.
A: When are you getting back to it?
B: I started again this morning. Just 3km, though.
A: Smart. Don't push yourself too hard.
```
