# Unit 14: Present Perfect and Past (2)

## A. 문법 설명 (Grammar Explanation)

### English
When we talk about something that **started in the past and continues to the present**, we use:
- **Present Perfect** or **Present Perfect Continuous**
- NOT Past Simple

Compare:
- "I **have lived** here for 10 years." (still living here)
- "I **lived** there for 10 years." (not anymore)

### Korean
**과거에 시작해서 현재까지 계속되는 것**을 말할 때:
- **현재완료** 또는 **현재완료진행형** 사용
- 과거시제 사용 안 함

비교:
- "I **have lived** here for 10 years." (지금도 살고 있음)
- "I **lived** there for 10 years." (더 이상 아님)

---

## B. 핵심 차이 (Key Difference)

| Present Perfect | Past Simple |
|-----------------|-------------|
| 과거 → 현재까지 계속 | 과거에 끝남 |
| I've worked here for 3 years. | I worked there for 3 years. |
| (지금도 여기서 일함) | (더 이상 거기서 안 일함) |

---

## C. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: 계속되는 상황

**Present Perfect (지금까지 계속):**
> **"I've been walking to school since March."**
> 3월부터 학교에 걸어다녔다. (지금도 계속)

**Past Simple (끝난 상황):**
> **"I walked to school for a month, then stopped."**
> 한 달 동안 걸어다녔다가, 그만뒀다. (더 이상 안 함)

### Example 2: 거주

**Present Perfect:**
> **"I've lived in Jeju all my life."**
> 평생 제주에서 살았다. (지금도 제주에 살고 있음)

**Past Simple:**
> **"I lived in Seoul for two years."**
> 서울에서 2년 살았다. (지금은 서울에 안 살음)

### Example 3: 알고 지낸 기간

**Present Perfect:**
> **"I've known Minseo since the first day of school."**
> 개학 첫날부터 민서를 알고 지냈다. (지금도 친구)

**Past Simple:**
> **"I knew a guy in elementary school who ran fast."**
> 초등학교 때 빨리 뛰는 애를 알았다. (지금은 모름)

### Example 4: 훈련 기간

**Present Perfect Continuous:**
> **"I've been training for a year."**
> 1년 동안 훈련해 왔다. (지금도 계속)

**Past Simple:**
> **"I trained for three months before the 10km race."**
> 10km 대회 전에 3개월 훈련했다. (그 대회를 위한 훈련 끝)

### Example 5: 감정/상태

**Present Perfect:**
> **"I've always loved the morning air."**
> 항상 아침 공기를 좋아했다. (지금도 좋아함)

**Past Simple:**
> **"I hated running when I was younger."**
> 어렸을 때 달리기가 싫었다. (과거의 감정)

---

## D. "It's been..." 표현

### It's been + 기간 + since...

> **"It's been three months since I started running."**
> 달리기 시작한 지 3개월이 됐다.

> **"It's been a year since I first walked to school."**
> 처음 걸어서 학교에 간 지 1년이 됐다.

### How long has it been since...?

> **"How long has it been since you started?"**
> 시작한 지 얼마나 됐어?

---

## E. "I haven't...for/since" 표현

### 부정문 + for/since = 그 기간 동안 안 했다

> **"I haven't taken the bus for three months."**
> 3개월 동안 버스를 안 탔다.

> **"I haven't seen Grandpa since last week."**
> 지난주 이후로 할아버지를 못 봤다.

### 소설 속 예문:

> **"I haven't gotten motion sickness since I started walking."**
> 걷기 시작한 이후로 멀미를 안 했다.

---

## F. 주의: "ago"와 "for"

### 과거시제 + ago:

> **"I started running six months ago."** (O)
> 6개월 전에 달리기를 시작했다.

### 현재완료 + for:

> **"I've been running for six months."** (O)
> 6개월 동안 달려왔다.

### 틀린 조합:

> ~~"I've started running six months ago."~~ (X)
> (ago는 과거시제와 함께)

## Exercises

### A. Present Perfect or Past Simple?

1. I (have lived / lived) in Jeju all my life. (지금도 제주)
2. She (has worked / worked) at that company for 5 years, but then she quit. (더 이상 안 함)
3. They (have been / were) friends since elementary school. (지금도 친구)
4. I (have trained / trained) hard before the race last year. (지난해 대회 전)
5. He (has run / ran) marathons since 2020. (지금도 계속)

**Answers:**
1. have lived (계속)
2. worked (끝남)
3. have been (계속)
4. trained (끝남)
5. has run (계속)

### B. 문장 완성

1. I _______ (walk) to school for three months now. (계속)
2. I _______ (walk) to school for a week, then stopped. (끝남)
3. She _______ (know) him since childhood. (지금도 알고 있음)
4. I _______ (know) a fast runner in elementary school. (과거)
5. It _______ (be) a year since I started training.

**Answers:**
1. have been walking / have walked
2. walked
3. has known
4. knew
5. has been

### C. It's been... since... 문장 만들기

Make sentences with "It's been... since...":

1. I started running (3 months ago).
   → It's been three months since I started running.

2. I last saw Grandpa (a week ago).
   → _______ since I last saw Grandpa.

3. I moved to Jeju (10 years ago).
   → _______ since I moved to Jeju.

**Answers:**
2. It's been a week since I last saw Grandpa.
3. It's been 10 years since I moved to Jeju.

### D. 부정문 만들기

Make negative sentences:

1. I / take the bus / for three months
   → I _______ the bus for three months.

2. She / run / since last week
   → She _______ since last week.

3. He / see / his friend / for ages
   → He _______ his friend for ages.

**Answers:**
1. haven't taken
2. hasn't run
3. hasn't seen

## Key Sentences (암기 문장)

| Present Perfect (계속) | Past Simple (끝남) |
|-----------------------|-------------------|
| I've lived here for 10 years. | I lived there for 10 years. |
| (지금도 살고 있음) | (더 이상 안 살음) |
| I've known him since school. | I knew someone who ran fast. |
| (지금도 알고 있음) | (과거의 사람) |
| I've been training for a year. | I trained for 3 months before the race. |
| (지금도 훈련 중) | (그 대회 위한 훈련 끝) |

## 정리: 언제 어떤 시제?

| 상황 | 시제 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| 과거에 시작, 지금도 계속 | Present Perfect | I've lived here since 2010. |
| 과거에 시작, 과거에 끝남 | Past Simple | I lived there for 2 years. |
| 기간 + still/now | Present Perfect | I've been running for 6 months (still). |
| 기간 + then stopped | Past Simple | I ran for 3 months, then stopped. |
| It's been... since | Present Perfect | It's been a year since I started. |
