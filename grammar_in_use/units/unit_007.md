# Unit 7: Present Perfect (1) (I have done)

## A. 문법 설명 (Grammar Explanation)

### English
**Present Perfect** (have/has + past participle) connects the **past to the present**.

We use it when:
1. A past action has a **result in the present**
2. Something happened in a **period that includes now** (today, this week, recently)
3. Talking about **life experiences** up to now

### Korean
**현재완료** (have/has + 과거분사)는 **과거와 현재를 연결**합니다.

다음 상황에서 사용합니다:
1. 과거의 행동이 **현재에 결과**를 남긴 경우
2. **현재를 포함하는 기간** (오늘, 이번 주, 최근)에 일어난 일
3. **지금까지의 경험**을 말할 때

---

## B. 현재완료의 형태 (Form)

| Subject | Have/Has | Past Participle |
|---------|----------|-----------------|
| I/You/We/They | have | done, walked, run |
| He/She/It | has | done, walked, run |

**부정문:** have/has + not + p.p.
**의문문:** Have/Has + 주어 + p.p.?

**축약형:**
- I have → I've
- He has → He's
- have not → haven't
- has not → hasn't

---

## C. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: 과거의 결과가 현재에 남아 있음

> **"I've always gotten carsick on long rides."**
> 나는 오래 타면 항상 차멀미를 했다.

- **I've gotten** = I have gotten (경험 + 현재도 해당)
- 과거부터 지금까지 계속 그런 특성

### Example 2: 현재까지의 변화

> **"I've changed."**
> 나는 변했다.

- **I've changed** = 과거와 비교해 지금은 다름
- The change happened and the result is now visible

### Example 3: 최근 일어난 일

> **"Summer break is over."**
> 여름 방학이 끝났다.

- 현재완료로 표현 가능: "Summer break **has ended**."
- 끝난 결과가 현재에 영향 (지금은 학교에 감)

### Example 4: 지금까지의 경험

> **"I've never walked this long before."**
> 이렇게 오래 걸어본 적이 없다.

- **I've never walked** = 지금까지의 인생에서 처음
- "before" = 지금까지

### Example 5: 완료된 일의 현재 결과

> **"I've been preparing for a whole year."**
> 1년 동안 준비해 왔다.

- **I've been preparing** = 과거에 시작해서 지금까지 계속
- The preparation connects past to present

## D. 현재완료와 자주 쓰는 표현

### Just (방금)
> **"I've just finished running."**
> 방금 달리기를 끝냈다.

### Already (이미, 벌써)
> **"I've already arrived at school."**
> 이미 학교에 도착했다.

### Yet (아직) - 부정문/의문문
> **"Have you eaten yet?"**
> 아직 먹었어?

> **"I haven't finished yet."**
> 아직 안 끝났어.

### Ever / Never (경험)
> **"Have you ever run a marathon?"**
> 마라톤 뛰어본 적 있어?

> **"I've never felt this good."**
> 이렇게 기분 좋은 적 없어.

---

## E. 비교: Present Perfect vs Past Simple

| Present Perfect | Past Simple |
|-----------------|-------------|
| 과거-현재 연결 | 과거만 언급 |
| 시간 특정 안 함 | 시간 특정 가능 |
| I've walked to school. | I walked to school yesterday. |
| (경험/결과 강조) | (과거 사실) |

### 예문 비교:

> **"I've finished the race."** (현재완료)
> 레이스를 완주했다. (지금 완주자)

> **"I finished the race yesterday."** (과거)
> 어제 레이스를 완주했다. (과거 사실)

## F. 불규칙 과거분사 (Irregular Past Participles)

| Base | Past | Past Participle |
|------|------|-----------------|
| run | ran | **run** |
| walk | walked | **walked** |
| see | saw | **seen** |
| get | got | **got/gotten** |
| do | did | **done** |
| go | went | **gone** |
| have | had | **had** |
| be | was/were | **been** |
| become | became | **become** |
| feel | felt | **felt** |

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. I (have walked / walked) to school many times.
2. She (has finished / finished) the race yesterday.
3. We (have just arrived / just arrived) at school.
4. (Have you ever run / Did you ever run) a marathon?
5. My stamina (has improved / improved) a lot this year.

**Answers:**
1. have walked (경험)
2. finished (yesterday = 과거)
3. have just arrived (just + 현재완료)
4. Have you ever run (경험)
5. has improved (this year = 현재 포함 기간)

### B. 빈칸 채우기

1. I _______ (never / walk) this far before.
2. She _______ (already / finish) her homework.
3. _______ you _______ (eat) yet?
4. They _______ (just / arrive) at the stadium.
5. He _______ (become) a marathoner.

**Answers:**
1. have never walked
2. has already finished
3. Have ... eaten
4. have just arrived
5. has become

### C. 현재완료 vs 과거시제

Choose the correct form:

1. I (have seen / saw) that movie last week.
2. I (have never seen / never saw) such beautiful flowers.
3. She (has started / started) running in March.
4. They (have just finished / just finished) - look, they're still sweating!

**Answers:**
1. saw (last week = 과거)
2. have never seen (경험)
3. started (in March = 과거 특정 시점)
4. have just finished (방금 + 현재 결과)

### D. 문장 완성하기

1. I _______ (walk) to school twice this week.
2. She _______ (not finish) her training yet.
3. _______ he ever _______ (run) a 10km race?
4. We _______ (become) good friends.

**Answers:**
1. have walked
2. hasn't finished
3. Has ... run
4. have become

## 한국어와 비교 (Compare with Korean)

한국어에서는 "~한 적 있다", "~해 왔다", "~했다"로 다양하게 번역됩니다.

| English Present Perfect | Korean |
|------------------------|--------|
| I have walked | 걸어본 적 있다 / 걸어왔다 / 걸었다 |
| I have changed | 변했다 / 변해왔다 |
| I have never run | 뛰어본 적 없다 |

영어에서는 "경험"과 "단순 과거"를 문법적으로 구분합니다!
