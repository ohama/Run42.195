# Unit 7: Present Perfect (1) (I have done)

## A. 현재완료의 형태 (Form)

**have/has + past participle (과거분사)**

| Subject | Have/Has | Past Participle | Full Example |
|---------|----------|-----------------|--------------|
| I/You/We/They | have | done, walked, run | I have finished. |
| He/She/It | has | done, walked, run | She has arrived. |

### Negative and Questions:

| Type | Structure | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Positive | have/has + p.p. | I have finished. |
| Negative | have/has + not + p.p. | I haven't finished. |
| Question | Have/Has + subject + p.p.? | Have you finished? |

### Contractions:
- I have → I've
- He has → He's
- have not → haven't
- has not → hasn't

---

## B. 현재완료의 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

**"I have done something" = The action is in the past, but it has a result NOW or a connection to NOW.**

현재완료는 **과거와 현재를 연결**합니다.

### From the Original Book:
> "Tom has lost his key." (= He doesn't have it now.)
> 톰이 열쇠를 잃어버렸다. (= 지금 열쇠가 없다)

> "**Have** you **read** this book?" (= Do you know the content now?)
> 이 책 읽어봤어? (= 지금 내용을 알아?)

---

## C. 현재완료 사용 상황 (When to Use)

### 1. 과거의 결과가 현재에 남아 있음 (Past action with present result)

| Past Action | Present Result |
|-------------|----------------|
| I've lost my key. | I don't have it now. |
| She's broken her leg. | Her leg is broken now. |
| I've finished my homework. | It's done now. |

### 2. 현재를 포함하는 기간에 일어난 일 (Period including now)

> "I've walked to school **this week**." (This week isn't finished.)
> "I've run **today**." (Today isn't finished.)

### 3. 지금까지의 경험 (Life experience up to now)

> "**Have** you **ever run** a marathon?"
> 마라톤 뛰어본 적 있어?

> "I've **never** walked this far before."
> 이렇게 멀리 걸어본 적이 없다.

---

## D. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: 과거의 결과가 현재에 남아 있음

> **"I've always gotten carsick on long rides."**
> 나는 오래 타면 항상 차멀미를 했다.

- **I've gotten** = I have gotten (경험 + 현재도 해당)
- 과거부터 지금까지 계속 그런 특성

### Example 2: 현재까지의 변화

> **"I've changed."**
> 나는 변했다.

- **I've changed** = 과거와 비교해 지금은 다름
- The change happened and the result is now visible

### Example 3: 지금까지의 경험

> **"I've never walked this long before."**
> 이렇게 오래 걸어본 적이 없다.

- **I've never walked** = 지금까지의 인생에서 처음
- "before" = 지금까지

### Example 4: 완료된 일의 현재 결과

> **"I've been preparing for a whole year."**
> 1년 동안 준비해 왔다.

- **I've been preparing** = 과거에 시작해서 지금까지 계속
- The preparation connects past to present

### Example 5: 최근 완료

> **"Summer break is over."** → "Summer break **has ended**."
> 여름 방학이 끝났다.

- 끝난 결과가 현재에 영향 (지금은 학교에 감)

---

## E. 현재완료와 자주 쓰는 표현 (Common Expressions)

### Just (방금)

> **"I've just finished running."**
> 방금 달리기를 끝냈다.

### Already (이미, 벌써) - 긍정문

> **"I've already arrived at school."**
> 이미 학교에 도착했다.

### Yet (아직) - 부정문/의문문

> **"Have you eaten yet?"**
> 아직 먹었어? (밥 먹었어?)

> **"I haven't finished yet."**
> 아직 안 끝났어.

### Ever / Never (경험)

> **"Have you ever run a marathon?"**
> 마라톤 뛰어본 적 있어?

> **"I've never felt this good."**
> 이렇게 기분 좋은 적 없어.

### Summary Table:

| Expression | Position | Use | Example |
|------------|----------|-----|---------|
| just | have + just + p.p. | 방금 | I've just arrived. |
| already | have + already + p.p. | 이미 (긍정) | I've already eaten. |
| yet | 문장 끝 | 아직 (부정/의문) | Have you finished yet? |
| ever | have + ever + p.p. | 경험 질문 | Have you ever run? |
| never | have + never + p.p. | 경험 부정 | I've never run. |

## F. 불규칙 과거분사 (Irregular Past Participles)

| Base | Past | Past Participle | Example |
|------|------|-----------------|---------|
| run | ran | **run** | I have run 10km. |
| walk | walked | **walked** | I have walked far. |
| see | saw | **seen** | I have seen that. |
| get | got | **got/gotten** | I've gotten better. |
| do | did | **done** | I've done it. |
| go | went | **gone** | She has gone home. |
| have | had | **had** | I've had breakfast. |
| be | was/were | **been** | I've been there. |
| become | became | **become** | I've become stronger. |
| feel | felt | **felt** | I've felt this before. |
| write | wrote | **written** | I've written a diary. |
| take | took | **taken** | I've taken the bus. |
| eat | ate | **eaten** | I've eaten lunch. |

---

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~I have went.~~ | I have **gone**. | 과거분사 사용 |
| ~~I have ran 10km.~~ | I have **run** 10km. | run-ran-run |
| ~~She have finished.~~ | She **has** finished. | 3인칭 단수 has |
| ~~Have you finish?~~ | **Have** you **finished**? | 과거분사 필요 |
| ~~I've seen him yesterday.~~ | I **saw** him yesterday. | 특정 과거 → 과거시제 |
| ~~Did you ever run?~~ | **Have** you **ever** run? | ever → 현재완료 |
| ~~I have ate.~~ | I have **eaten**. | eat-ate-eaten |

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. I (have walked / walked) to school many times.
2. She (has finished / finished) the race yesterday.
3. We (have just arrived / just arrived) at school.
4. (Have you ever run / Did you ever run) a marathon?
5. My stamina (has improved / improved) a lot this year.
6. I (have seen / saw) that movie last week.
7. They (haven't finished / didn't finish) yet.
8. She (has been / was) to Japan twice.

**Answers:**
1. have walked (경험)
2. finished (yesterday = 과거)
3. have just arrived (just + 현재완료)
4. Have you ever run (경험)
5. has improved (this year = 현재 포함 기간)
6. saw (last week = 특정 과거)
7. haven't finished (yet = 현재완료)
8. has been (경험)

### B. 빈칸 채우기

1. I _______ (never / walk) this far before.
2. She _______ (already / finish) her homework.
3. _______ you _______ (eat) yet?
4. They _______ (just / arrive) at the stadium.
5. He _______ (become) a marathoner.
6. I _______ (not / see) that movie yet.
7. We _______ (be) friends for ten years.
8. She _______ (write) three books.

**Answers:**
1. have never walked
2. has already finished
3. Have ... eaten
4. have just arrived
5. has become
6. haven't seen
7. have been
8. has written

### C. 과거분사 쓰기

Write the past participle:

1. go → _______
2. see → _______
3. do → _______
4. run → _______
5. be → _______
6. write → _______
7. eat → _______
8. take → _______

**Answers:**
1. gone
2. seen
3. done
4. run
5. been
6. written
7. eaten
8. taken

### D. 틀린 부분 고치기

Find and correct the errors:

1. I have went to that restaurant.
   → I have **gone** to that restaurant.

2. She have finished her work.
   → She **has** finished her work.

3. Have you ever saw the ocean?
   → Have you ever **seen** the ocean?

4. I've visited Seoul yesterday.
   → I **visited** Seoul yesterday.

5. We haven't ate lunch yet.
   → We haven't **eaten** lunch yet.

## 한국어와 비교 (Compare with Korean)

한국어에서는 "~한 적 있다", "~해 왔다", "~했다"로 다양하게 번역됩니다.

| English Present Perfect | Korean |
|------------------------|--------|
| I have walked | 걸어본 적 있다 / 걸어왔다 / 걸었다 |
| I have changed | 변했다 / 변해왔다 |
| I have never run | 뛰어본 적 없다 |
| I have just finished | 방금 끝냈다 |

**영어에서는 "경험"과 "단순 과거"를 문법적으로 구분합니다!**

|-----|---------|--------------|
