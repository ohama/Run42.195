# Unit 44: Passive (3) - Special Cases

## A. 동사 + 전치사 수동태 (Verb + Preposition Passive)

일부 동사는 **동사 + 전치사** 구조로 수동태가 됩니다. 전치사가 동사와 함께 남습니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "The doctor **was sent for**." (= Someone sent for the doctor)
> 의사를 부르러 사람을 보냈다.

> "I don't like **being looked at**."
> 쳐다보이는 게 싫다.

> "The children **were well looked after**."
> 아이들은 잘 돌봄을 받았다.

### 주요 동사 + 전치사 조합:

| 능동태 | 수동태 | 의미 |
|--------|--------|------|
| laugh at | be laughed at | 비웃음을 당하다 |
| look at | be looked at | 쳐다보이다 |
| look after | be looked after | 돌봄을 받다 |
| talk about | be talked about | 이야기되다 |
| look for | be looked for | 찾아지다 |
| send for | be sent for | 불려지다 |
| cheer on | be cheered on | 응원받다 |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was looked at by everyone."**
> 모두가 나를 쳐다봤다.

> **"The marathon was talked about by everyone at school."**
> 학교에서 모두가 마라톤에 대해 이야기했다.

> **"I was cheered on by my family."**
> 가족들이 나를 응원해줬다.

## B. 두 개의 목적어를 가진 동사 (Verbs with Two Objects)

**give, tell, show, send, offer, teach, pay** 등은 두 개의 목적어를 가집니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "They **gave me** a book." → "**I was given** a book." 또는 "**A book was given** to me."
> 나는 책을 받았다.

> "They **offered her** the job." → "**She was offered** the job."
> 그녀는 그 일자리 제안을 받았다.

### 두 가지 수동태 형태:

| 능동태 | 수동태 (사람 주어) | 수동태 (사물 주어) |
|--------|-------------------|-------------------|
| They gave **me** a medal. | **I** was given a medal. | A medal was given **to me**. |
| She told **me** the news. | **I** was told the news. | The news was told **to me**. |
| He showed **us** the photo. | **We** were shown the photo. | The photo was shown **to us**. |

### 사람 주어가 더 일반적:

영어에서는 **사람을 주어로 한 수동태**가 더 자연스럽습니다.

| 자연스러움 | 예문 |
|-----------|------|
| 더 자연스러움 | **I was given** a medal. |
| 덜 자연스러움 | A medal was given to me. |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was given the finisher's medal."**
> 완주 메달을 받았다.

> **"They gave me a training plan."**
> → **"I was given a training plan."**
> 훈련 계획을 받았다.

> **"The coach told me the good news."**
> → **"I was told the good news."**
> 좋은 소식을 들었다.

## C. It is said that... / He is said to... (전언 수동태)

**사람들이 ~라고 한다**는 표현의 수동태

### From the Original Book:

> "**It is said** that he is 110 years old."
> 그가 110살이라고 한다.

> "**He is said** to be 110 years old."
> 그가 110살이라고 한다.

### 두 가지 구조:

| 구조 | 예문 |
|------|------|
| **It is said that** + 주어 + 동사 | It is said that **he runs** every day. |
| **주어 + is said to** + 동사원형 | **He is said to run** every day. |

### 사용 가능한 동사들:

| 동사 | 의미 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| said | ~라고 한다 | It is said that... / He is said to... |
| believed | ~라고 믿어진다 | It is believed that... / He is believed to... |
| thought | ~라고 생각된다 | It is thought that... / He is thought to... |
| known | ~라고 알려져 있다 | It is known that... / He is known to... |
| reported | ~라고 보도된다 | It is reported that... / He is reported to... |
| expected | ~라고 예상된다 | It is expected that... / He is expected to... |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"It is said that running helps you think."**
> 달리기가 생각하는 데 도움이 된다고 한다.

> **"Running is said to help you think."**
> 달리기가 생각하는 데 도움이 된다고 한다.

> **"The course is known to be challenging."**
> 코스가 힘들다고 알려져 있다.

## D. Get + 과거분사 (구어체 수동태)

**get + p.p.**는 be + p.p.보다 더 구어적인 표현입니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "I **got stung** by a bee."
> 벌에 쏘였다.

> "Did you **get invited** to the party?"
> 파티에 초대받았어?

> "They **got married** last year."
> 그들은 작년에 결혼했다.

### be + p.p. vs get + p.p.:

| be + p.p. (격식) | get + p.p. (구어) | 의미 |
|------------------|-------------------|------|
| I was hurt. | I **got** hurt. | 다쳤다 |
| She was married. | She **got** married. | 결혼했다 |
| He was lost. | He **got** lost. | 길을 잃었다 |
| I was tired. | I **got** tired. | 피곤해졌다 |

### Get 수동태가 자주 쓰이는 표현:

| 표현 | 의미 |
|------|------|
| get married | 결혼하다 |
| get hurt/injured | 다치다 |
| get lost | 길을 잃다 |
| get tired | 피곤해지다 |
| get used to | ~에 익숙해지다 |
| get paid | 급여를 받다 |
| get invited | 초대받다 |
| get stuck | 막히다/갇히다 |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I got pushed into the bus."**
> 버스 안으로 밀렸다. (구어체)

> **"Don't get injured during training."**
> 훈련 중에 다치지 마. (구어체)

> **"I got tired after 30km."**
> 30km 후에 지쳤다. (구어체)

### Get + p.p.의 특징:

| 특징 | 설명 |
|------|------|
| 구어적 | 일상 대화에서 많이 사용 |
| 동적 | 상태 변화를 강조 |
| 비격식 | 격식체 글에서는 be 선호 |

## E. 능동태를 선호하는 경우

수동태보다 **능동태**가 더 자연스러운 경우가 있습니다.

### 1. 행위자가 명확할 때

| 어색함 (X) | 자연스러움 (O) |
|-----------|----------------|
| The bus was missed by me. | **I missed** the bus. |
| The alarm wasn't heard by me. | **I didn't hear** the alarm. |

### 2. 동작이 더 중요할 때

| 어색함 (X) | 자연스러움 (O) |
|-----------|----------------|
| The marathon was run by Doyun. | **Doyun ran** the marathon. |
| The race was won by Korea. | **Korea won** the race. |

### 3. 짧고 간결한 표현

| 수동태 (김) | 능동태 (간결) |
|-------------|---------------|
| Breakfast was made by me. | **I made** breakfast. |
| The door was opened by her. | **She opened** the door. |

### Examples from the Novel:

> (X) The bus was missed by me.
> (O) **I missed the bus.**

> (X) The alarm wasn't heard by me.
> (O) **I didn't hear the alarm.**

## F. Born (수동태 only)

**be born**은 항상 수동태로 사용합니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "**I was born** in London."
> 나는 런던에서 태어났다.

> "**Where were you born**?"
> 어디서 태어났어?

> "How many babies **are born** every day?"
> 매일 몇 명의 아기가 태어나나요?

### 주의사항:

| 틀린 표현 (X) | 올바른 표현 (O) |
|---------------|-----------------|
| My mother **born** me. | I **was born**. |
| I **born** in Jeju. | I **was born** in Jeju. |
| Where did you **born**? | Where **were you born**? |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was born in Jeju."**
> 나는 제주에서 태어났다.

> **"Where were you born?"**
> 어디서 태어났어?

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| 틀린 문장 (X) | 올바른 문장 (O) | 설명 |
|---------------|-----------------|------|
| I **born** in 2010. | I **was born** in 2010. | be born (수동태) |
| A medal **gave** to me. | A medal **was given** to me. | 수동태 형태 |
| I **was laugh at**. | I **was laughed at**. | 과거분사 형태 |
| The bus **got miss**. | I **got** my bus **missed**. | 구조 오류 |
| It **said** that he is rich. | It **is said** that he is rich. | be동사 필요 |
| He **is said run** fast. | He **is said to run** fast. | to 필요 |

### 전치사 위치 오류:

```
(X) I was looked. (at 누락)
(O) I was looked at.

(X) The problem was talked. (about 누락)
(O) The problem was talked about.
```

## Exercises

### A. 수동태로 바꾸세요.

1. They gave me a medal.
   → I _______________________.
   → A medal _______________________.

2. Everyone talked about the marathon.
   → The marathon _______________________.

3. My family looked after me.
   → I _______________________.

4. People say that running is good for health.
   → It is said that _______________________.
   → Running is said _______________________.

5. They showed us the new course.
   → We _______________________.

**Answers:**
1. was given a medal / was given to me
2. was talked about by everyone
3. was looked after by my family
4. running is good for health / to be good for health
5. were shown the new course

### B. be 또는 get을 사용해 빈칸을 채우세요.

1. I _______ hurt during the race. (구어체)
2. The race _______ held every year. (격식체)
3. Don't _______ injured! (구어체)
4. She _______ married last year. (구어체)
5. The medal _______ given to the winner. (격식체)
6. He _______ lost in the city. (구어체)
7. The door _______ opened by the manager. (격식체)
8. I _______ paid yesterday. (구어체)

**Answers:**
1. got
2. is
3. get
4. got
5. was
6. got
7. was
8. got

### C. 능동태로 바꾸세요.

1. The bus was missed by me.
   → _______________________

2. The marathon was completed by Doyun.
   → _______________________

3. The alarm wasn't heard by me.
   → _______________________

4. Breakfast was made by Mom.
   → _______________________

5. The door was opened by him.
   → _______________________

**Answers:**
1. I missed the bus.
2. Doyun completed the marathon.
3. I didn't hear the alarm.
4. Mom made breakfast.
5. He opened the door.

### D. It is said that... / ... is said to... 로 바꾸세요.

1. People say that the course is difficult.
   → It is said that _______________________.
   → The course is said _______________________.

2. People believe that running reduces stress.
   → It is believed that _______________________.
   → Running is believed _______________________.

3. People know that she is a good runner.
   → It is known that _______________________.
   → She is known _______________________.

4. People think that he trains every day.
   → It is thought that _______________________.
   → He is thought _______________________.

**Answers:**
1. the course is difficult / to be difficult
2. running reduces stress / to reduce stress
3. she is a good runner / to be a good runner
4. he trains every day / to train every day

## Summary: 특수 수동태 정리

### 1. 동사 + 전치사 수동태:
```
laugh at → be laughed at
look after → be looked after
talk about → be talked about
cheer on → be cheered on
```

### 2. 두 개의 목적어:
```
give me a medal
→ I was given a medal (사람 주어 - 더 자연스러움)
→ A medal was given to me (사물 주어)
```

### 3. It is said that... / ... is said to...
```
People say that he runs fast.
→ It is said that he runs fast.
→ He is said to run fast.
```

### 4. get + p.p. (구어체):
```
get hurt (다치다)
get married (결혼하다)
get lost (길을 잃다)
get tired (피곤해지다)
```

### 5. be born (항상 수동태):
```
I was born in 2010.
Where were you born?
```
