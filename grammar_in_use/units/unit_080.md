# Unit 80: Noun + Noun (a bus driver / a headache)

## A. 명사 + 명사 복합어

영어에서는 두 명사를 연결해서 **하나의 개념**을 만듭니다.

**명사 + 명사** = 앞 명사가 뒤 명사를 수식

| Compound | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| bus stop | 버스 정류장 |
| school building | 학교 건물 |
| running shoes | 러닝화 |
| morning bus | 아침 버스 |

---

## B. 소설 속 복합 명사

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I got off at the school stop."**
> 학교 정류장에서 내렸다.

- school + stop = 학교 정류장

> **"I looked up at the school building."**
> 학교 건물을 올려다봤다.

- school + building = 학교 건물

> **"The bus stop was crowded."**
> 버스 정류장이 붐볐다.

- bus + stop = 버스 정류장

> **"I put on my running shoes."**
> 러닝화를 신었다.

- running + shoes = 러닝화

> **"The morning bus is the problem."**
> 아침 버스가 문제다.

- morning + bus = 아침 버스

---

## C. 앞 명사는 보통 단수

복합 명사에서 **앞 명사**는 대부분 **단수형**을 씁니다.

| Correct | Incorrect |
|---------|-----------|
| a bus stop | a buses stop |
| a shoe store | a shoes store |
| a flower shop | a flowers shop |
| a sports field | a sport field (예외) |

### From the Novel:

> **"I saw the flower shop."** (NOT: flowers shop)
> 꽃집을 봤다.

> **"I was on the sports field."** (예외: sports)
> 운동장에 있었다.

---

## D. 일반적인 복합 명사 패턴

| Pattern | Examples |
|---------|----------|
| 장소 | bus stop, train station, school building |
| 시간 | morning bus, night train, summer vacation |
| 재료 | glass window, paper bag, leather shoes |
| 목적 | running shoes, sleeping bag, coffee cup |
| 종류 | sports car, horror movie, marathon race |

### From the Novel:

> **"I grabbed my gym clothes."**
> 체육복을 챙겼다.

- gym + clothes = 체육복

> **"The finish line was in sight."**
> 결승선이 보였다.

- finish + line = 결승선

> **"I looked at the sports field."**
> 운동장을 봤다.

- sports + field = 운동장

---

## E. 복합 명사 vs of 구문

| Noun + Noun | of 구문 |
|-------------|---------|
| a bus stop | the top of the bus |
| a school building | the roof of the building |
| morning coffee | a cup of coffee |

### When to use of:

1. **부분-전체**: the door **of** the bus
2. **소유**: the teacher **of** the class
3. **추상적 관계**: the end **of** the day

### From the Novel:

> **"The soles of my feet were burning."**
> 발바닥이 타는 듯했다.

- soles of my feet (부분-전체 관계)

> **"By the end of the day..."**
> 하루가 끝날 즈음...

- end of the day (추상적 관계)

## Exercises

### A. 두 명사를 연결하세요.

1. bus + stop = _______
2. school + building = _______
3. running + shoes = _______
4. finish + line = _______
5. gym + clothes = _______

**Answers:**
1. bus stop
2. school building
3. running shoes
4. finish line
5. gym clothes

### B. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. I waited at the (bus stop / buses stop).
2. I put on my (running shoes / runnings shoes).
3. The (finish line / finishes line) was in sight.
4. I went to the (flower shop / flowers shop).
5. I looked at the (sport field / sports field).

**Answers:**
1. bus stop
2. running shoes
3. finish line
4. flower shop
5. sports field

### C. of 또는 복합 명사로 바꾸세요.

1. the driver of the bus = _______
2. the door of the car = _______
3. the end of the race = _______
4. a stop for buses = _______
5. shoes for running = _______

**Answers:**
1. the bus driver
2. the car door
3. the race end / the end of the race
4. a bus stop
5. running shoes

| Pattern | Example |

| 예외: sports, clothes | sports field |
| of 구문 (부분-전체) | the door of the bus |
