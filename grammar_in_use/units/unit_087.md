# Unit 87: Much, many, little, few, a lot, plenty

## A. 형태와 기본 개념 (Form and Basic Concept)

| | Countable (셀 수 있음) | Uncountable (셀 수 없음) |
|-|----------------------|------------------------|
| 많이 | **many** | **much** |
| 적게 (부정적) | **few** | **little** |
| 약간 (긍정적) | **a few** | **a little** |
| 많이 (둘 다) | **a lot of / lots of / plenty of** |

### 핵심:
- **Countable**: many, few, a few
- **Uncountable**: much, little, a little
- **Both**: a lot of, lots of, plenty of

---

## B. many / much: 많은 (A lot of)

### many + 셀 수 있는 명사 (Countable):

> **"There were many people on the bus."**
> 버스에 사람이 많았다.

> **"I don't have many friends."**
> 친구가 많지 않다.

> **"How many kilometers did you run?"**
> 몇 킬로미터 뛰었어?

### much + 셀 수 없는 명사 (Uncountable):

> **"I don't have much time."**
> 시간이 별로 없다.

> **"I don't have much stamina."**
> 체력이 별로 없다.

> **"How much water did you drink?"**
> 물 얼마나 마셨어?

### From the Novel:

> **"Too many people."**
> 사람이 너무 많아.

> **"I don't have much experience."**
> 경험이 별로 없다.

---

## C. few / little: 적은 (부정적) - Almost none

**few**와 **little**은 "거의 없는"이라는 **부정적** 의미입니다.

### few + 셀 수 있는 명사:

> **"Few students came to school."**
> 학생이 거의 오지 않았다. (부정적)

> **"Few people understood him."**
> 그를 이해하는 사람이 거의 없었다.

### little + 셀 수 없는 명사:

> **"I have little time."**
> 시간이 거의 없다. (부정적)

> **"I have little stamina."**
> 체력이 거의 없다.

> **"There was little hope."**
> 희망이 거의 없었다.

---

## D. a few / a little: 약간의 (긍정적) - Some

**a few**와 **a little**은 "약간 있는"이라는 **긍정적** 의미입니다.

### a few + 셀 수 있는 명사:

> **"A few kids were waiting."**
> 몇몇 애들이 기다리고 있었다. (긍정적)

> **"I still have a few minutes."**
> 아직 몇 분 있다.

### a little + 셀 수 없는 명사:

> **"I need a little time."**
> 시간이 좀 필요하다. (긍정적)

> **"I felt a little better."**
> 좀 나아졌다.

### From the Novel:

> **"I felt a little nauseous."**
> 좀 메스꺼웠다.

> **"A few cherry trees lined the road."**
> 벚나무 몇 그루가 길가에 있었다.

---

## E. few vs a few / little vs a little (비교)

| Expression | Meaning | Nuance | Example |
|------------|---------|--------|---------|
| **few** | 거의 없음 | 부정적 | Few people came. (거의 안 옴) |
| **a few** | 약간 있음 | 긍정적 | A few people came. (몇 명 옴) |
| **little** | 거의 없음 | 부정적 | Little time left. (시간 거의 없음) |
| **a little** | 약간 있음 | 긍정적 | A little time left. (시간 좀 있음) |

### 시각화:

```
없음 <-------- few/little -------- a few/a little --------> 많음
              (거의 없음)           (약간 있음)
              [부정적]              [긍정적]
```

---

## F. a lot of / lots of / plenty of (Both Countable and Uncountable)

이 표현들은 셀 수 있는 명사와 셀 수 없는 명사 **둘 다** 사용 가능합니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"There were a lot of people."**
> 사람이 많았다. (countable)

> **"I have a lot of time."**
> 시간이 많다. (uncountable)

> **"Plenty of time to spare."**
> 시간이 충분히 남았다. (uncountable)

> **"Jeju has lots of hills."**
> 제주에는 언덕이 많다. (countable)

### 비교:

| Countable | Uncountable |
|-----------|-------------|
| many people | much time |
| a lot of people | a lot of time |
| lots of people | lots of time |
| plenty of people | plenty of time |

---

## G. 의문문, 부정문에서 much/many (Usage in Questions and Negatives)

긍정문에서는 **a lot of**를 더 자주 쓰고, 의문문/부정문에서는 **much/many**를 씁니다.

| Type | Expression | Example |
|------|------------|---------|
| 긍정문 | **a lot of** | I have **a lot of** time. |
| 부정문 | **much/many** | I don't have **much** time. |
| 의문문 | **much/many** | Do you have **many** friends? |

### From the Novel:

> **"I have a lot of energy today."** (긍정문)
> 오늘 힘이 넘친다.

> **"I don't have much energy."** (부정문)
> 힘이 별로 없다.

> **"How much water did you drink?"** (의문문)
> 물 얼마나 마셨어?

## H. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | 설명 |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| I have **much** friends. | I have **many** friends. | 셀 수 있는 명사 |
| **Many** water is needed. | **Much** water is needed. | 셀 수 없는 명사 |
| I have **few** time. | I have **little** time. | 셀 수 없는 명사 |
| **A little** people came. | **A few** people came. | 셀 수 있는 명사 |
| I have **lot of** money. | I have **a lot of** money. | a lot of (관사 필요) |

### 추가 실수 예시:

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Much** students failed. | **Many** students failed. |
| I need **a few** money. | I need **a little** money. |
| There is **many** water. | There is **much** water. |

## Exercises

### A. many, much, few, little 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. There were _______ people on the bus. (많은, 셀 수 있음)
2. I don't have _______ time. (많은, 셀 수 없음)
3. _______ students came to class today. (거의 안)
4. I have _______ stamina. (거의 없음, 셀 수 없음)
5. How _______ kilometers did you run?
6. How _______ water do you need?

**Answers:**
1. many
2. much
3. Few
4. little
5. many
6. much

### B. a few / a little / few / little 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. I have _______ money. I can buy lunch. (약간 있음)
2. I have _______ money. I can't buy anything. (거의 없음)
3. There are _______ students in class. (몇 명)
4. _______ people know about this. (거의 아무도 모름)
5. I need _______ more time. (좀 더)
6. _______ hope remained. (희망이 거의 없음)

**Answers:**
1. a little
2. little
3. a few
4. Few
5. a little
6. Little

### C. 빈칸을 채우세요.

1. I felt _______ (a little / a few) nauseous.
2. There were _______ (many / much) flowers along the road.
3. I don't have _______ (many / much) experience.
4. I have _______ (a lot of / much) time today.
5. Jeju has _______ (lots of / many) hills.
6. _______ (Few / A few) friends came to cheer me on. (몇 명 옴)
7. I have _______ (little / a little) energy left. (좀 남음)
8. _______ (Many / Much) people watched the race.

**Answers:**
1. a little
2. many
3. much
4. a lot of
5. lots of
6. A few
7. a little
8. Many

### D. 틀린 부분을 고치세요.

1. I have much friends.
2. There is many water in the bottle.
3. I need a few more information.
4. Few time is left.
5. I have lot of energy today.

**Answers:**
1. I have **many** friends.
2. There is **much** water in the bottle.
3. I need **a little** more information.
4. **Little** time is left.
5. I have **a lot of** energy today.

## Summary: 수량 표현 정리

| Word | Type | Meaning |
|------|------|---------|
| **many** | countable | 많은 |
| **much** | uncountable | 많은 |
| **few** | countable | 거의 없는 (부정적) |
| **little** | uncountable | 거의 없는 (부정적) |
| **a few** | countable | 약간의 (긍정적) |
| **a little** | uncountable | 약간의 (긍정적) |
| **a lot of** | both | 많은 |
| **lots of** | both | 많은 |
| **plenty of** | both | 충분한 |

### 용법 비교:

| | Countable | Uncountable |
|-|-----------|-------------|
| 많이 | many | much |
| 거의 없음 | few | little |
| 약간 | a few | a little |
| 긍정문 (많이) | a lot of | a lot of |
| 부정문/의문문 | many | much |
