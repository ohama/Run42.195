# Unit 112: Even

## A. even의 기본 의미 - ~조차, 심지어

**even**은 "~조차, 심지어"의 의미로, **예상 밖**의 일을 강조할 때 사용합니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Even a little running makes me gasp for air."**
> 조금만 달려도 숨이 찬다.

- even + 명사 (a little running)

> **"Even walking was hard at first."**
> 처음에는 걷기조차 힘들었다.

> **"I felt good even after 10 kilometers."**
> 10킬로미터 후에도 기분이 좋았다.

> **"Even my sister was impressed."**
> 여동생조차 감동받았다.

---

## B. even의 위치

**even**은 강조하려는 단어 **바로 앞**에 옵니다.

| 강조 대상 | 위치 | 예시 |
|-----------|------|------|
| 명사 | 명사 앞 | **even** Minseo |
| 동사 | 동사 앞 | I **even** ran |
| 형용사/부사 | 형용사/부사 앞 | **even** harder |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Even I was surprised."**
> 나조차 놀랐다. (나를 강조)

> **"I even ran when I didn't want to."**
> 하기 싫을 때조차 달렸다. (달렸다를 강조)

> **"The race was even harder than I expected."**
> 대회가 예상보다 훨씬 더 힘들었다. (harder를 강조)

---

## C. even + 비교급

**even**은 비교급 앞에서 "훨씬 더"의 의미를 강조합니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The full marathon was even harder than the half."**
> 풀마라톤이 하프보다 훨씬 더 힘들었다.

> **"I woke up even earlier today."**
> 오늘 더욱 일찍 일어났다.

> **"My legs felt even heavier after 35km."**
> 35km 후에 다리가 훨씬 더 무겁게 느껴졌다.

> **"I ran even faster than yesterday."**
> 어제보다 훨씬 더 빨리 달렸다.

> **"The second race was even more challenging."**
> 두 번째 대회가 훨씬 더 도전적이었다.

---

## D. even in/even when - ~에서도, ~할 때조차

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Even in summer, I wake up early to run."**
> 여름에도 일찍 일어나서 달린다.

> **"Even when I'm tired, I keep running."**
> 피곤할 때조차 계속 달린다.

> **"I run even in the rain."**
> 비가 와도 달린다.

> **"Even when I wanted to quit, I didn't."**
> 포기하고 싶을 때조차 포기하지 않았다.

---

## E. not even - ~조차 않다

**not even**은 "~조차 않다"의 강한 부정을 나타냅니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I couldn't even breathe."**
> 숨조차 쉴 수 없었다.

> **"I couldn't even walk after the race."**
> 대회 후에 걷지조차 못했다.

> **"He didn't even look tired."**
> 그는 피곤해 보이지조차 않았다.

> **"I didn't even know I could run this far."**
> 이렇게 멀리 달릴 수 있는지조차 몰랐다.

> **"I can't even imagine running 42km."**
> 42km를 달리는 건 상상조차 못한다.

---

## F. even if vs even though

| 표현 | 의미 | 사용 |
|------|------|------|
| even if | ~라도, 설령 ~하더라도 | 가정 (아직 일어나지 않음) |
| even though | ~에도 불구하고 | 사실 (이미 일어남) |

### Examples:

> **"Even if it rains, I'll run."** (가정)
> 비가 와도 달릴 거다.

> **"Even though it was raining, I ran."** (사실)
> 비가 왔지만 달렸다.

### From the Novel:

> **"Even if I fail, I'll try again."**
> 실패하더라도 다시 시도할 거다.

> **"Even though I was tired, I kept running."**
> 피곤했지만 계속 달렸다.

> **"Even if it's hard, I won't give up."**
> 힘들어도 포기하지 않을 거다.

## Exercises

### A. even을 알맞은 위치에 넣으세요.

1. My sister was impressed. (강조: sister)
   → **Even my sister was impressed.**

2. Walking was hard at first.
   → **Even walking was hard at first.**

3. I ran when I was tired.
   → **I even ran when I was tired.**

4. The race was harder than expected.
   → **The race was even harder than expected.**

### B. not even을 사용하여 문장을 완성하세요.

1. I couldn't breathe after running.
   → **I couldn't even breathe after running.**

2. He didn't look tired.
   → **He didn't even look tired.**

3. I can't imagine running 42km.
   → **I can't even imagine running 42km.**

### C. even if 또는 even though 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. _______ it rains tomorrow, I'll run. (가정)
   → **Even if**

2. _______ it was cold, I ran outside. (사실)
   → **Even though**

3. _______ I fail, I won't give up. (가정)
   → **Even if**

4. _______ I was exhausted, I finished the race. (사실)
   → **Even though**

### D. 문장을 완성하세요.

1. The full marathon was _______ harder than the half.
   → **even**

2. _______ I was tired, I kept running.
   → **Even though** / **Even when**

3. I couldn't _______ walk after the race.
   → **even**

4. _______ in summer, I wake up early.
   → **Even**
