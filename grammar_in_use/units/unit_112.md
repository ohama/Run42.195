# Unit 112: Even

## A. Even의 기본 의미와 형태 (Form and Meaning)

**Even**은 "~조차, 심지어"의 의미로, **예상 밖**의 일을 강조할 때 사용합니다.

### 기본 개념:
Even은 "예상하지 못한 극단적인 경우"를 나타냅니다. 듣는 사람이 놀랄 만한 정보를 강조합니다.

### From the Original Book:
> "She's very fit. **Even** at the age of 70, she runs marathons."
> 그녀는 매우 건강하다. 70세의 나이에도 마라톤을 뛴다.

> "I can't cook. I can't **even** boil an egg."
> 나는 요리를 못해. 달걀도 삶지 못해.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Even a little running makes me gasp for air."**
> 조금만 달려도 숨이 찬다.

- even + 명사 (a little running)

> **"Even walking was hard at first."**
> 처음에는 걷기조차 힘들었다.

> **"I felt good even after 10 kilometers."**
> 10킬로미터 후에도 기분이 좋았다.

> **"Even my sister was impressed."**
> 여동생조차 감동받았다.

---

## B. Even의 위치 (Position of Even)

**Even**은 강조하려는 단어 **바로 앞**에 옵니다.

| 강조 대상 | 위치 | 예시 | 의미 |
|-----------|------|------|------|
| 명사 | 명사 앞 | **even** Minseo | 민서조차 |
| 동사 | 동사 앞 | I **even** ran | 달리기까지 했다 |
| 형용사/부사 | 형용사/부사 앞 | **even** harder | 훨씬 더 힘들게 |
| 전치사구 | 전치사구 앞 | **even** in summer | 여름에도 |

### From the Original Book:
> "**Even** I understood." (나조차 이해했다 - 내가 이해할 줄은 몰랐다)
> "I **even** understood." (이해까지 했다 - 이해하는 것도 포함해서)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Even I was surprised."**
> 나조차 놀랐다. (나를 강조)

> **"I even ran when I didn't want to."**
> 하기 싫을 때조차 달렸다. (달렸다를 강조)

> **"The race was even harder than I expected."**
> 대회가 예상보다 훨씬 더 힘들었다. (harder를 강조)

---

## C. Even + 비교급 (Even + Comparative)

**Even**은 비교급 앞에서 "훨씬 더"의 의미를 강조합니다.

### 구조:
> even + 비교급 + than

### From the Original Book:
> "It's warm today, but it was **even** warmer yesterday."
> 오늘 따뜻하지만, 어제는 훨씬 더 따뜻했어.

> "I knew I was wrong. **Even** so, I didn't apologise."
> 내가 틀린 걸 알았어. 그래도 사과하지 않았어.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The full marathon was even harder than the half."**
> 풀마라톤이 하프보다 훨씬 더 힘들었다.

> **"I woke up even earlier today."**
> 오늘 더욱 일찍 일어났다.

> **"My legs felt even heavier after 35km."**
> 35km 후에 다리가 훨씬 더 무겁게 느껴졌다.

> **"I ran even faster than yesterday."**
> 어제보다 훨씬 더 빨리 달렸다.

> **"The second race was even more challenging."**
> 두 번째 대회가 훨씬 더 도전적이었다.

### 비교급 강조어 비교:

| 강조어 | 강도 | 예시 |
|--------|------|------|
| much | ★★★☆☆ | much harder |
| even | ★★★★☆ | even harder |
| a lot | ★★★☆☆ | a lot harder |
| far | ★★★★☆ | far harder |

---

## D. Even in/Even when - ~에서도, ~할 때조차

**Even**은 전치사구나 절과 함께 "~에서도, ~할 때조차"를 표현합니다.

### From the Original Book:
> "**Even in** winter, she goes swimming."
> 겨울에도 그녀는 수영을 간다.

> "**Even when** I'm tired, I go to the gym."
> 피곤할 때도 나는 체육관에 간다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Even in summer, I wake up early to run."**
> 여름에도 일찍 일어나서 달린다.

> **"Even when I'm tired, I keep running."**
> 피곤할 때조차 계속 달린다.

> **"I run even in the rain."**
> 비가 와도 달린다.

> **"Even when I wanted to quit, I didn't."**
> 포기하고 싶을 때조차 포기하지 않았다.

---

## E. Not even - ~조차 않다

**Not even**은 "~조차 않다"의 강한 부정을 나타냅니다.

### From the Original Book:
> "He didn't **even** say hello."
> 그는 인사조차 하지 않았다.

> "They have **not even** started yet."
> 그들은 아직 시작조차 하지 않았다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I couldn't even breathe."**
> 숨조차 쉴 수 없었다.

> **"I couldn't even walk after the race."**
> 대회 후에 걷지조차 못했다.

> **"He didn't even look tired."**
> 그는 피곤해 보이지조차 않았다.

> **"I didn't even know I could run this far."**
> 이렇게 멀리 달릴 수 있는지조차 몰랐다.

> **"I can't even imagine running 42km."**
> 42km를 달리는 건 상상조차 못한다.

## F. Even if vs Even though

| 표현 | 의미 | 시점 | 사용 |
|------|------|------|------|
| even if | ~라도, 설령 ~하더라도 | 가정 (아직 일어나지 않음) | 미래/가정 상황 |
| even though | ~에도 불구하고 | 사실 (이미 일어남) | 실제 상황 |

### From the Original Book:
> "**Even if** I get up at 6, I won't have time."
> 설령 6시에 일어나더라도, 시간이 없을 거야. (가정)

> "**Even though** I got up at 6, I didn't have time."
> 6시에 일어났는데도, 시간이 없었어. (사실)

### Examples:

> **"Even if it rains, I'll run."** (가정 - 비가 올지 안 올지 모름)
> 비가 와도 달릴 거다.

> **"Even though it was raining, I ran."** (사실 - 실제로 비가 왔음)
> 비가 왔지만 달렸다.

### From the Novel:

> **"Even if I fail, I'll try again."**
> 실패하더라도 다시 시도할 거다.

> **"Even though I was tired, I kept running."**
> 피곤했지만 계속 달렸다.

> **"Even if it's hard, I won't give up."**
> 힘들어도 포기하지 않을 거다.

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | 설명 |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| ~~Even it was hard, I continued.~~ | **Even though** it was hard, I continued. | even 단독 사용 불가 (접속사 필요) |
| ~~Although even I was tired~~ | **Even though** I was tired | although와 even 중복 |
| ~~I ran even more fast.~~ | I ran **even faster**. | 비교급 사용 |
| ~~Even he didn't said hello.~~ | He didn't **even** say hello. | even 위치 + 시제 |
| ~~I didn't eat even.~~ | I didn't **even** eat. | even은 동사 앞 |
| ~~Even I am tired, I will run.~~ | **Even if** I am tired, I will run. | if 필요 (가정) |

### Even if vs Even though 선택:

```
미래/가정 상황? → Even if
과거/현재 사실? → Even though
```

## Exercises

### A. even을 알맞은 위치에 넣으세요.

1. My sister was impressed. (강조: sister)
   → **Even my sister was impressed.**

2. Walking was hard at first.
   → **Even walking was hard at first.**

3. I ran when I was tired.
   → **I even ran when I was tired.**

4. The race was harder than expected.
   → **The race was even harder than expected.**

5. I understood the difficult explanation.
   → **I even understood the difficult explanation.** / **Even I understood the difficult explanation.**

6. She works on weekends.
   → **She even works on weekends.**

### B. not even을 사용하여 문장을 완성하세요.

1. I couldn't breathe after running.
   → **I couldn't even breathe after running.**

2. He didn't look tired.
   → **He didn't even look tired.**

3. I can't imagine running 42km.
   → **I can't even imagine running 42km.**

4. She didn't say goodbye.
   → **She didn't even say goodbye.**

5. They haven't started.
   → **They haven't even started.**

### C. even if 또는 even though 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. _______ it rains tomorrow, I'll run. (가정)
   → **Even if**

2. _______ it was cold, I ran outside. (사실)
   → **Even though**

3. _______ I fail, I won't give up. (가정)
   → **Even if**

4. _______ I was exhausted, I finished the race. (사실)
   → **Even though**

5. _______ you don't like running, try it once. (가정)
   → **Even if**

6. _______ he studied hard, he didn't pass. (사실)
   → **Even though**

### D. 틀린 부분을 고치세요.

1. Even it was raining, I ran.
   → **Even though** it was raining, I ran.

2. I ran even more fast today.
   → I ran **even faster** today.

3. I didn't finished even.
   → I didn't **even finish**.

4. Even I am not good, I will try.
   → **Even if** I am not good, I will try.

5. Although even she was tired, she continued.
   → **Even though** she was tired, she continued.

## Summary: Even 용법 정리표

| 표현 | 의미 | 구조 | 예시 |
|------|------|------|------|
| even + 명사/동사 | ~조차 | even + 강조 대상 | Even I was surprised. |
| even + 비교급 | 훨씬 더 | even + 비교급 | even harder |
| not even | ~조차 않다 | don't/can't + even + 동사 | couldn't even walk |
| even if | 설령 ~라도 (가정) | even if + 절 | Even if it rains |
| even though | ~에도 불구하고 (사실) | even though + 절 | Even though it was cold |
| even in/when | ~에서도/~할 때도 | even + 전치사구/when절 | even in summer |
