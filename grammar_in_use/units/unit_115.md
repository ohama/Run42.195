# Unit 115: Unless / As long as / Provided

## A. Unless의 형태와 용법 (Form and Usage)

**Unless**는 "~하지 않으면, ~하지 않는 한"의 의미로, **if ... not**과 같은 뜻입니다.

### 구조:
> Unless + 주어 + **현재시제** 동사, 주절

**중요:** 미래를 나타낼 때도 **현재시제**를 사용합니다.

### From the Original Book:
> "We'll be late **unless** we hurry."
> 서두르지 않으면 늦을 거야.

> "I can't understand what he says **unless** he speaks slowly."
> 그가 천천히 말하지 않으면 무슨 말인지 이해 못해.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I won't stop unless my body gives out."**
> 몸이 쓰러지지 않는 한 멈추지 않을 것이다.

> **"Unless I leave early, I'll miss the bus."**
> 일찍 출발하지 않으면 버스를 놓칠 것이다.
> = If I don't leave early, I'll miss the bus.

> **"Unless it rains, I'll run to school tomorrow."**
> 비가 오지 않으면 내일 학교까지 달릴 것이다.

---

## B. Unless vs If ... not

**Unless**는 대부분 **if ... not**으로 바꿀 수 있습니다.

| Unless | If ... not |
|--------|------------|
| Unless you hurry, you'll be late. | If you don't hurry, you'll be late. |
| Unless it rains, we'll go running. | If it doesn't rain, we'll go running. |
| Unless I get injured, I'll finish. | If I don't get injured, I'll finish. |

### From the Novel:

> **"Unless I get injured, I'll finish the race."**
> = **"If I don't get injured, I'll finish the race."**
> 부상을 당하지 않으면 경기를 완주할 것이다.

### 주의: Unless를 쓰지 못하는 경우

이미 부정적인 상황이나 결과에서는 unless를 쓰지 않습니다.

| Correct (O) | Awkward (X) |
|-------------|-------------|
| I'll be surprised **if he doesn't finish**. | ~~I'll be surprised unless he finishes.~~ |
| I'll be disappointed **if you don't come**. | ~~I'll be disappointed unless you come.~~ |

## C. As long as의 형태와 용법

**As long as**는 "~하는 한, ~하기만 하면"의 의미로, **조건**을 나타냅니다.
= so long as (같은 의미)

### From the Original Book:
> "You can borrow my car **as long as** you drive carefully."
> 조심히 운전하기만 하면 내 차를 빌려줄게.

> "**As long as** you're happy, that's all that matters."
> 네가 행복하기만 하면, 그게 가장 중요해.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"As long as I keep moving, I won't stop."**
> 움직이기만 하면, 멈추지 않을 것이다.

> **"As long as you don't give up, you'll finish."**
> 포기하지 않는 한, 완주할 수 있어.

> **"You can rest as long as you don't stop completely."**
> 완전히 멈추지만 않으면 쉬어도 돼.

---

## D. Provided / Providing (that)의 형태와 용법

**Provided (that)** / **Providing (that)**는 "~라면, ~한다는 조건 하에"의 의미로,
**if**보다 더 격식 있는 표현입니다.

### From the Original Book:
> "You can come to the party **provided** (that) you behave yourself."
> 예의 바르게 행동한다면 파티에 와도 돼.

> "**Providing** (that) nothing goes wrong, I'll see you next week."
> 아무 일이 없다면, 다음 주에 보자.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Provided the weather is good, I'll run the full distance."**
> 날씨가 좋다면, 전체 거리를 달릴 것이다.

> **"You can improve your time, provided you train regularly."**
> 규칙적으로 훈련한다면 기록을 향상시킬 수 있다.

> **"Providing that I don't get hurt, I'll participate in the race."**
> 다치지만 않으면, 경기에 참가할 것이다.

---

## E. 비교: Unless / As long as / Provided

| Expression | Meaning | Usage | Nuance |
|------------|---------|-------|--------|
| unless | ~하지 않으면 | 부정 조건 | 예외 상황 강조 |
| as long as | ~하는 한 | 긍정 조건 | 필수 조건 강조 |
| provided (that) | ~라면 (조건 하에) | 격식체 조건 | 조건 충족 강조 |

### Comparison Examples:

> **"Unless you stop, you'll finish."** (멈추지 않으면)
> **"As long as you keep going, you'll finish."** (계속 가기만 하면)
> **"Provided you don't give up, you'll finish."** (포기하지 않는다면)

모두 비슷한 의미이지만 뉘앙스가 다릅니다.

### 선택 가이드:

```
부정 조건 (~ 않으면) → Unless
필수 조건 (~ 하기만 하면) → As long as
격식적/계약적 조건 → Provided/Providing
```

## F. 시제 규칙 (Tense Rules)

**Unless, as long as, provided**는 미래를 나타낼 때도 **현재시제**를 사용합니다.

| Correct (O) | Incorrect (X) |
|-------------|---------------|
| Unless it **rains** tomorrow... | ~~Unless it will rain~~ |
| As long as you **try**... | ~~As long as you will try~~ |
| Provided she **comes**... | ~~Provided she will come~~ |

### Examples:

> **"I'll go running unless it rains."** (O)
> ~~"I'll go running unless it will rain."~~ (X)

> **"As long as you study hard, you'll pass."** (O)
> ~~"As long as you will study hard..."~~ (X)

---

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | 설명 |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| ~~Unless you will try~~ | Unless you **try** | 현재시제 사용 |
| ~~Unless you don't hurry~~ | **Unless** you hurry | 이중 부정 금지 |
| ~~As long as it will rain~~ | As long as it **rains** | 현재시제 사용 |
| ~~Provided that you will come~~ | Provided that you **come** | 현재시제 사용 |
| ~~I'll be sad unless you come.~~ | I'll be sad **if you don't come**. | unless 부적절한 경우 |

### Unless + not 이중 부정 주의:

> ~~Unless you don't try, you won't succeed.~~ (X)
> **Unless you try**, you won't succeed. (O) = If you don't try...
> **If you don't try**, you won't succeed. (O)

## Exercises

### A. unless 또는 if ... not을 사용해 문장을 완성하세요.

1. _______ you practice, you won't improve.
2. I'll be late _______ I leave now.
3. _______ it stops raining, we'll have to cancel.
4. The race won't start _______ everyone is ready.
5. I won't go _______ you come with me.

**Answers:**
1. Unless you practice / If you don't practice
2. if I don't leave / unless I leave
3. Unless it stops / If it doesn't stop
4. unless everyone is / if everyone isn't
5. unless you come / if you don't come

### B. as long as를 사용해 문장을 만드세요.

1. You can succeed. You must keep trying.
   → You can succeed **as long as you keep trying**.

2. I'll keep running. I must have energy.
   → I'll keep running **as long as I have energy**.

3. You can use my phone. You must be careful.
   → You can use my phone **as long as you're careful**.

4. I'll help you. You must ask nicely.
   → I'll help you **as long as you ask nicely**.

5. You'll improve. You must practice every day.
   → You'll improve **as long as you practice every day**.

### C. 적절한 표현을 고르세요 (unless / as long as / provided).

1. _______ you work hard, you'll achieve your goal.
2. _______ you hurry, you'll miss the train.
3. I'll help you, _______ you ask nicely.
4. _______ there's no traffic, we'll arrive on time.
5. _______ something unexpected happens, we'll be there.
6. You won't pass _______ you study.

**Answers:**
1. As long as / Provided
2. Unless
3. provided / as long as
4. Provided / As long as
5. Unless
6. unless

### D. 틀린 부분을 고치세요.

1. Unless you will try, you won't succeed.
   → Unless you **try**, you won't succeed.

2. As long as it will rain, we'll stay inside.
   → As long as it **rains**, we'll stay inside.

3. I'll go providing that you will come with me.
   → I'll go providing that you **come** with me.

4. Unless you don't study, you'll fail.
   → **Unless you study** / **If you don't study**, you'll fail.

5. The game will continue provided it will not rain.
   → The game will continue provided it **does not rain**.

## Summary: 조건 표현 비교표

| Expression | Meaning | Structure | Formality |
|------------|---------|-----------|-----------|
| unless | ~하지 않으면 | unless + 현재시제 | 중립 |
| as long as | ~하는 한 | as long as + 현재시제 | 중립 |
| so long as | ~하는 한 | so long as + 현재시제 | 약간 격식 |
| provided (that) | ~라면 | provided + 현재시제 | 격식 |
| providing (that) | ~라면 | providing + 현재시제 | 격식 |

---

## Unless vs As long as 뉘앙스 비교

| Unless (부정 조건) | As long as (긍정 조건) |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| 예외/방해 요소 강조 | 필수 조건 강조 |
| Unless it rains... | As long as it doesn't rain... |
| 비가 오지 않으면... | 비가 안 오기만 하면... |
| Unless you give up... | As long as you keep going... |
| 포기하지 않으면... | 계속 가기만 하면... |

## Compare with Korean

| English | Korean |
|---------|--------|
| unless | ~하지 않으면, ~하지 않는 한 |
| as long as | ~하는 한, ~하기만 하면 |
| so long as | ~하는 한 (as long as와 동일) |
| provided (that) | ~라면, ~한다는 조건으로 |
| providing (that) | ~라면 (provided와 동일) |

한국어에서는 "~지 않으면", "~하는 한", "~라면"으로 조건을 표현합니다.
