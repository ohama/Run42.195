# Unit 115: Unless / As long as / Provided

## A. Unless의 의미와 용법

**Unless**는 "~하지 않으면, ~하지 않는 한"의 의미로, **if ... not**과 같은 뜻입니다.

**구조:** unless + 주어 + 현재시제 동사

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I won't stop unless my body gives out."**
> 몸이 쓰러지지 않는 한 멈추지 않을 것이다.

> **"Unless I leave early, I'll miss the bus."**
> 일찍 출발하지 않으면 버스를 놓칠 것이다.
> = If I don't leave early, I'll miss the bus.

> **"Unless it rains, I'll run to school tomorrow."**
> 비가 오지 않으면 내일 학교까지 달릴 것이다.

---

## B. Unless vs If ... not

**Unless**는 대부분 **if ... not**으로 바꿀 수 있습니다.

| Unless | If ... not |
|--------|------------|
| Unless you hurry, you'll be late. | If you don't hurry, you'll be late. |
| Unless it rains, we'll go running. | If it doesn't rain, we'll go running. |

### From the Novel:

> **"Unless I get injured, I'll finish the race."**
> = **"If I don't get injured, I'll finish the race."**
> 부상을 당하지 않으면 경기를 완주할 것이다.

**주의:** 이미 부정적인 상황에서는 unless를 쓰지 않습니다.
- I'll be surprised **if he doesn't finish**. (O)
- I'll be surprised **unless he finishes**. (어색함)

---

## C. As long as의 의미와 용법

**As long as**는 "~하는 한, ~하기만 하면"의 의미로, **조건**을 나타냅니다.
= so long as (같은 의미)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"As long as I keep moving, I won't stop."**
> 움직이기만 하면, 멈추지 않을 것이다.

> **"As long as you don't give up, you'll finish."**
> 포기하지 않는 한, 완주할 수 있어.

> **"You can rest as long as you don't stop completely."**
> 완전히 멈추지만 않으면 쉬어도 돼.

---

## D. Provided / Providing (that)의 의미와 용법

**Provided (that)** / **Providing (that)**는 "~라면, ~한다는 조건 하에"의 의미로,
**if**보다 더 격식 있는 표현입니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Provided the weather is good, I'll run the full distance."**
> 날씨가 좋다면, 전체 거리를 달릴 것이다.

> **"You can improve your time, provided you train regularly."**
> 규칙적으로 훈련한다면 기록을 향상시킬 수 있다.

> **"Providing that I don't get hurt, I'll participate in the race."**
> 다치지만 않으면, 경기에 참가할 것이다.

---

## E. 비교: Unless / As long as / Provided

| Expression | Meaning | Usage |
|------------|---------|-------|
| unless | ~하지 않으면 | 부정 조건 |
| as long as | ~하는 한 | 긍정 조건 |
| provided (that) | ~라면 (조건 하에) | 격식체 조건 |

### Comparison Examples:

> **"Unless you stop, you'll finish."** (멈추지 않으면)
> **"As long as you keep going, you'll finish."** (계속 가기만 하면)
> **"Provided you don't give up, you'll finish."** (포기하지 않는다면)

모두 비슷한 의미이지만 뉘앙스가 다릅니다.

---

## F. 시제 규칙

**Unless, as long as, provided**는 미래를 나타낼 때도 **현재시제**를 사용합니다.

| Correct | Incorrect |
|---------|-----------|
| Unless it **rains** tomorrow... | Unless it **will rain** tomorrow... |
| As long as you **try**... | As long as you **will try**... |
| Provided she **comes**... | Provided she **will come**... |

## Exercises

### A. unless 또는 if ... not을 사용해 문장을 완성하세요.

1. _______ you practice, you won't improve. (unless)
2. I'll be late _______ I leave now. (if ... not)
3. _______ it stops raining, we'll have to cancel.
4. The race won't start _______ everyone is ready.

**Answers:**
1. Unless you practice, you won't improve.
2. I'll be late if I don't leave now.
3. Unless it stops raining, we'll have to cancel.
4. unless / if not

### B. as long as를 사용해 문장을 만드세요.

1. You can succeed. You must keep trying.
   → You can succeed _______.

2. I'll keep running. I must have energy.
   → I'll keep running _______.

**Answers:**
1. as long as you keep trying
2. as long as I have energy

### C. 적절한 표현을 고르세요 (unless / as long as / provided).

1. _______ you work hard, you'll achieve your goal.
2. _______ you hurry, you'll miss the train.
3. I'll help you, _______ you ask nicely.
4. _______ there's no traffic, we'll arrive on time.

**Answers:**
1. As long as / Provided
2. Unless
3. provided / as long as
4. Provided / As long as

### D. 틀린 부분을 고치세요.

1. Unless you will try, you won't succeed.
2. As long as it will rain, we'll stay inside.
3. I'll go providing that you will come with me.

**Answers:**
1. Unless you try, you won't succeed.
2. As long as it rains, we'll stay inside.
3. I'll go providing that you come with me.
