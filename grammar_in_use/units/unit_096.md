# Unit 96: Relative Clauses (5) - Extra Information Clauses (2)

## A. which로 앞 절 전체를 받기

비한정적 관계절에서 **which**는 앞의 명사뿐만 아니라 **앞 절 전체**를 받을 수 있습니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I finished the 42km race, which made my family very proud."**
> 나는 42km 대회를 완주했고, 그것이 가족을 매우 자랑스럽게 했다.

- which = "I finished the 42km race" 전체를 받음

> **"I ran 10km without stopping, which was my summer goal."**
> 나는 10km를 쉬지 않고 달렸고, 그것이 내 여름 목표였다.

> **"I woke up before the alarm, which surprised me."**
> 알람보다 먼저 일어났는데, 그게 나를 놀라게 했다.

> **"He said I was doing well, which made me happy."**
> 그가 내가 잘하고 있다고 말했고, 그것이 나를 행복하게 했다.

### Pattern:
```
문장, which + 동사 (앞 문장 전체에 대한 코멘트)
```

## B. all/most/some/none of which/whom

**all of which, most of whom, some of which** 등의 표현으로 부분을 나타낼 수 있습니다.

| 표현 | 의미 |
|------|------|
| all of which/whom | 그것들/그들 모두 |
| most of which/whom | 그것들/그들 대부분 |
| some of which/whom | 그것들/그들 일부 |
| none of which/whom | 그것들/그들 아무것도/아무도 |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I made several friends, all of whom encouraged me to run."**
> 나는 여러 친구를 사귀었는데, 그들 모두가 나에게 달리라고 격려했다.

> **"I ran many races, most of which were in Jeju."**
> 나는 많은 대회에 나갔는데, 대부분이 제주에서 열렸다.

> **"I tried different routes, some of which were very hilly."**
> 나는 다양한 경로를 시도했는데, 그중 일부는 언덕이 많았다.

> **"There were many runners, none of whom I knew."**
> 많은 주자들이 있었는데, 아는 사람이 한 명도 없었다.

## C. 전치사 + which/whom

전치사가 관계대명사 앞에 올 수 있습니다. 격식체에서 많이 사용됩니다.

| 비격식 | 격식 |
|--------|------|
| the race which I trained **for** | the race **for which** I trained |
| the friend who I run **with** | the friend **with whom** I run |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The race for which I had been preparing for a year finally arrived."**
> 1년간 준비해 온 대회가 드디어 왔다.

> **"Minseo, with whom I run every Saturday, came to cheer."**
> 매주 토요일마다 함께 달리는 민서가 응원하러 왔다.

> **"The bench on which I always rest is near the school gate."**
> 내가 항상 쉬는 벤치는 학교 정문 근처에 있다.

> **"The coach, from whom I learned a lot, believed in me."**
> 내가 많이 배운 코치님은 나를 믿어 주셨다.

---

## D. which/who + 동사 + 형용사/명사

비한정적 관계절에서 관계대명사 뒤에 be동사가 생략된 형태도 많이 씁니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"My legs, (which were) heavy like lead, kept moving."**
> 납처럼 무거웠던 다리가 계속 움직였다.

> **"The rapeseed flowers, (which were) yellow and bright, lined the road."**
> 노랗고 밝은 유채꽃이 길가에 늘어서 있었다.

> **"My coach, (who was) proud of my progress, smiled."**
> 내 발전을 자랑스러워하시는 코치님이 미소 지었다.

---

## E. 비한정적 관계절의 위치

비한정적 관계절은 문장 중간이나 끝에 올 수 있습니다.

### 문장 중간:

> **"Minseo, who was my first friend, helped me a lot."**
> 나의 첫 친구였던 민서가 나를 많이 도와주었다.

### 문장 끝:

> **"I finished the race with Minseo, who had been cheering from the sidelines."**
> 나는 옆에서 응원하던 민서와 함께 대회를 마쳤다.

## Exercises

### A. which가 무엇을 받는지 고르세요.

1. I finished the marathon, which made me proud.
   - a) the marathon
   - b) I finished the marathon (전체 문장)
   **Answer: b**

2. The medal, which was heavy, hung on my wall.
   - a) the medal
   - b) the medal hung on my wall
   **Answer: a**

3. He encouraged me, which helped a lot.
   - a) me
   - b) He encouraged me (전체 문장)
   **Answer: b**

### B. 알맞은 표현을 고르세요.

1. I have three medals, (all of which / all of whom) are from Jeju.
   → **all of which**

2. I met many runners, (some of which / some of whom) were from Seoul.
   → **some of whom**

3. There were many races, (none of which / none of whom) were easy.
   → **none of which**

4. My friends, (most of which / most of whom) run with me, came to cheer.
   → **most of whom**

### C. 두 문장을 한 문장으로 만드세요.

1. I finished 42km. This made my family proud.
   → **I finished 42km, which made my family proud.**

2. I met several runners. All of them were friendly.
   → **I met several runners, all of whom were friendly.**

3. I tried many routes. Most of them were scenic.
   → **I tried many routes, most of which were scenic.**

4. I woke up early. This surprised my mom.
   → **I woke up early, which surprised my mom.**

### D. 격식체로 바꾸세요.

1. The race which I trained for was 42km.
   → **The race for which I trained was 42km.**

2. The friend who I run with is Minseo.
   → **The friend with whom I run is Minseo.**

3. The bench which I rest on is near the gate.
   → **The bench on which I rest is near the gate.**

## Summary

| 구조 | 예시 | 의미 |
|------|------|------|
| 문장, which... | I won, which surprised me. | 앞 문장 전체를 받음 |
| all/most/some of which | races, most of which... | 부분 표현 |
| all/most/some of whom | runners, all of whom... | 부분 표현 (사람) |
| 전치사 + which/whom | for which, with whom | 격식체 |
