# Unit 96: Relative Clauses (5) - Extra Information Clauses (2)

## A. which로 앞 절 전체를 받기 (which referring to a whole clause)

비한정적 관계절에서 **which**는 앞의 명사뿐만 아니라 **앞 절 전체**를 받을 수 있습니다.

### Structure:

```
문장, which + 동사 (앞 문장 전체에 대한 코멘트)

I finished the race, which made my family proud.
        ↑                    ↑
   (전체 문장)          (which = 내가 대회를 완주한 것)
```

### From the Original Book:

> "Joe got the job, **which** surprised everybody."
> 조가 그 일자리를 얻었는데, 그건 모두를 놀라게 했다.

> "Sarah couldn't meet us, **which** was a shame."
> 사라가 우리를 만나지 못했는데, 그건 아쉬웠다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I finished the 42km race, which made my family very proud."**
> 나는 42km 대회를 완주했고, 그것이 가족을 매우 자랑스럽게 했다.

- which = "I finished the 42km race" 전체를 받음

> **"I ran 10km without stopping, which was my summer goal."**
> 나는 10km를 쉬지 않고 달렸고, 그것이 내 여름 목표였다.

> **"I woke up before the alarm, which surprised me."**
> 알람보다 먼저 일어났는데, 그게 나를 놀라게 했다.

> **"He said I was doing well, which made me happy."**
> 그가 내가 잘하고 있다고 말했고, 그것이 나를 행복하게 했다.

> **"I beat my personal best, which was unexpected."**
> 개인 기록을 경신했는데, 그건 예상치 못한 일이었다.

## B. all/most/some/none of which/whom

**all of which, most of whom, some of which** 등의 표현으로 부분을 나타낼 수 있습니다.

### Pattern Table:

| 표현 | 의미 | 사용 대상 |
|------|------|-----------|
| all of which/whom | 그것들/그들 모두 | which: 사물 / whom: 사람 |
| most of which/whom | 그것들/그들 대부분 | which: 사물 / whom: 사람 |
| some of which/whom | 그것들/그들 일부 | which: 사물 / whom: 사람 |
| none of which/whom | 그것들/그들 아무것도/아무도 | which: 사물 / whom: 사람 |
| many of which/whom | 그것들/그들 많은 것 | which: 사물 / whom: 사람 |
| both of which/whom | 둘 다 | which: 사물 / whom: 사람 |

### From the Original Book:

> "Kate has two sisters, **both of whom** are married."
> 케이트는 두 자매가 있는데, 둘 다 결혼했다.

> "We tried ten hotels, **all of which** were full."
> 호텔 10개를 시도했는데, 모두 다 찼다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I made several friends, all of whom encouraged me to run."**
> 나는 여러 친구를 사귀었는데, 그들 모두가 나에게 달리라고 격려했다.

> **"I ran many races, most of which were in Jeju."**
> 나는 많은 대회에 나갔는데, 대부분이 제주에서 열렸다.

> **"I tried different routes, some of which were very hilly."**
> 나는 다양한 경로를 시도했는데, 그중 일부는 언덕이 많았다.

> **"There were many runners, none of whom I knew."**
> 많은 주자들이 있었는데, 아는 사람이 한 명도 없었다.

> **"I bought two pairs of shoes, both of which were blue."**
> 신발 두 켤레를 샀는데, 둘 다 파란색이었다.

## C. 전치사 + which/whom (Preposition + which/whom)

전치사가 관계대명사 앞에 올 수 있습니다. 격식체에서 많이 사용됩니다.

### Pattern Comparison:

| 비격식 (Informal) | 격식 (Formal) |
|-------------------|---------------|
| the race which I trained **for** | the race **for which** I trained |
| the friend who I run **with** | the friend **with whom** I run |
| the bench that I sat **on** | the bench **on which** I sat |
| the goal that I worked **toward** | the goal **toward which** I worked |

### From the Original Book:

> "I didn't get the job **for which** I applied." (formal)
> "I didn't get the job **that** I applied **for**." (informal)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The race for which I had been preparing for a year finally arrived."**
> 1년간 준비해 온 대회가 드디어 왔다.

> **"Minseo, with whom I run every Saturday, came to cheer."**
> 매주 토요일마다 함께 달리는 민서가 응원하러 왔다.

> **"The bench on which I always rest is near the school gate."**
> 내가 항상 쉬는 벤치는 학교 정문 근처에 있다.

> **"The coach, from whom I learned a lot, believed in me."**
> 내가 많이 배운 코치님은 나를 믿어 주셨다.

> **"The goal toward which I had been working was finally within reach."**
> 내가 노력해 온 목표가 드디어 손에 닿을 것 같았다.

## D. which/who + 분사 축약 (Reduced relative clauses)

비한정적 관계절에서 관계대명사 뒤에 be동사가 오면 둘 다 생략할 수 있습니다.

### Pattern:

```
Full: My legs, which were heavy like lead, kept moving.
Reduced: My legs, heavy like lead, kept moving.
```

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"My legs, (which were) heavy like lead, kept moving."**
> 납처럼 무거웠던 다리가 계속 움직였다.

> **"The rapeseed flowers, (which were) yellow and bright, lined the road."**
> 노랗고 밝은 유채꽃이 길가에 늘어서 있었다.

> **"My coach, (who was) proud of my progress, smiled."**
> 내 발전을 자랑스러워하시는 코치님이 미소 지었다.

> **"The medal, (which was) heavier than expected, hung around my neck."**
> 예상보다 무거웠던 메달이 목에 걸렸다.

> **"Minseo, (who was) waiting at the finish line, cheered loudly."**
> 결승선에서 기다리던 민서가 크게 응원했다.

## E. 비한정적 관계절의 위치 (Position)

비한정적 관계절은 문장 중간이나 끝에 올 수 있습니다.

### 문장 중간 (Mid-sentence):

> **"Minseo, who was my first friend, helped me a lot."**
> 나의 첫 친구였던 민서가 나를 많이 도와주었다.

> **"The marathon, which took 5 hours, was exhausting."**
> 5시간이 걸린 마라톤은 지쳤다.

### 문장 끝 (End of sentence):

> **"I finished the race with Minseo, who had been cheering from the sidelines."**
> 나는 옆에서 응원하던 민서와 함께 대회를 마쳤다.

> **"I finally achieved my goal, which I had been working toward for a year."**
> 드디어 1년간 노력해 온 목표를 달성했다.

## F. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | 설명 |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| ~~I finished the race, that made me proud~~ | I finished the race, **which** made me proud | 절 전체 수식에 that 불가 |
| ~~I met many runners, most of who were fast~~ | I met many runners, most of **whom** were fast | 전치사 뒤에는 whom |
| ~~The race for that I trained~~ | The race **for which** I trained | 전치사 뒤에는 which |
| ~~All of them were my friends, which helped me~~ | All of **whom** were my friends... | 사람에게는 whom |
| ~~I have three medals, all of them are gold~~ | I have three medals, **all of which** are gold | 관계대명사 필요 |

### 특히 주의:

**1. 절 전체를 받을 때 that 사용 불가:**
```
✗ He passed the exam, that surprised everyone.
✓ He passed the exam, which surprised everyone.
```

**2. of + 관계대명사 구조에서 알맞은 형태 사용:**
```
✗ I have two brothers, both of which are doctors.
✓ I have two brothers, both of whom are doctors. (사람)

✗ I tried three routes, all of whom were hilly.
✓ I tried three routes, all of which were hilly. (사물)
```

## Exercises

### A. which가 무엇을 받는지 고르세요 (What does 'which' refer to?)

1. I finished the marathon, which made me proud.
   - a) the marathon
   - b) I finished the marathon (전체 문장)
   **Answer: b**

2. The medal, which was heavy, hung on my wall.
   - a) the medal
   - b) the medal hung on my wall
   **Answer: a**

3. He encouraged me, which helped a lot.
   - a) me
   - b) He encouraged me (전체 문장)
   **Answer: b**

4. I woke up early, which surprised my mom.
   - a) early
   - b) I woke up early (전체 문장)
   **Answer: b**

5. The race, which was 42km, was exhausting.
   - a) the race
   - b) the race was exhausting
   **Answer: a**

---

### B. 알맞은 표현을 고르세요 (Choose the correct expression)

1. I have three medals, (all of which / all of whom) are from Jeju.
   → **all of which** (사물)

2. I met many runners, (some of which / some of whom) were from Seoul.
   → **some of whom** (사람)

3. There were many races, (none of which / none of whom) were easy.
   → **none of which** (사물)

4. My friends, (most of which / most of whom) run with me, came to cheer.
   → **most of whom** (사람)

5. I tried several routes, (both of which / all of which) were scenic.
   → **all of which** (여러 개면 all, 두 개면 both)

6. I have two coaches, (both of which / both of whom) are excellent.
   → **both of whom** (사람)

---

### C. 두 문장을 한 문장으로 만드세요 (Combine sentences)

1. I finished 42km. This made my family proud.
   → **I finished 42km, which made my family proud.**

2. I met several runners. All of them were friendly.
   → **I met several runners, all of whom were friendly.**

3. I tried many routes. Most of them were scenic.
   → **I tried many routes, most of which were scenic.**

4. I woke up early. This surprised my mom.
   → **I woke up early, which surprised my mom.**

5. I have three medals. None of them are gold.
   → **I have three medals, none of which are gold.**

6. I beat my personal record. This was unexpected.
   → **I beat my personal record, which was unexpected.**

---

### D. 격식체로 바꾸세요 (Change to formal style)

1. The race which I trained for was 42km.
   → **The race for which I trained was 42km.**

2. The friend who I run with is Minseo.
   → **The friend with whom I run is Minseo.**

3. The bench which I rest on is near the gate.
   → **The bench on which I rest is near the gate.**

4. The goal that I worked toward was finally achieved.
   → **The goal toward which I worked was finally achieved.**

5. The coach who I learned from believed in me.
   → **The coach from whom I learned believed in me.**

## Summary

### Quick Reference Table:

| 구조 | 예시 | 의미 |
|------|------|------|
| 문장, which... | I won, which surprised me. | 앞 문장 전체를 받음 |
| all/most/some of which | races, most of which... | 부분 표현 (사물) |
| all/most/some of whom | runners, all of whom... | 부분 표현 (사람) |
| 전치사 + which | for which, on which | 격식체 |
| 전치사 + whom | with whom, from whom | 격식체 (사람) |
