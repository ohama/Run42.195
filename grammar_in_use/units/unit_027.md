# Unit 27: Could (do) and could have (done)

## A. 문법 설명 (Grammar Explanation)

### Could (do): 가능성/제안 (현재/미래)
- ~할 수 있을 텐데 (가능성)
- ~하면 어때? (제안)

### Could have (done): 과거의 가능성/후회
- ~할 수 있었는데 (안 했음)
- ~했을 수도 있어 (과거 가능성)

---

## B. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: Could (제안)

> **"You could try running with me sometime."**
> 언젠가 나랑 같이 달려볼 수도 있어.

- 제안/가능성

### Example 2: Could (가능성)

> **"I could walk, but it would take too long."**
> 걸을 수도 있지만, 너무 오래 걸릴 거야.

- 현재 가능한 선택지

### Example 3: Couldn't (불가능)

> **"I couldn't have imagined this a year ago."**
> 1년 전에는 이걸 상상도 못 했을 거야.

- 과거에 불가능했던 것

### Example 4: Could have done (과거 가능성)

> **"I could have taken the bus, but I chose to walk."**
> 버스를 탈 수도 있었는데, 걷기로 했어.

- 하지 않은 과거 선택

### Example 5: Could have done (후회)

> **"I could have trained harder."**
> 더 열심히 훈련할 수 있었는데.

- 과거에 대한 후회

---

## C. 추가 설명 (Additional Points)

### Could vs Could have done

| Could (do) | Could have (done) |
|------------|-------------------|
| 현재/미래 가능성 | 과거 가능성 |
| You could walk. | You could have walked. |
| (지금 걸을 수 있어) | (과거에 걸을 수 있었는데) |

### Could have done의 용법

1. **하지 않은 과거 선택**:
   > "I could have given up, but I didn't."
   > 포기할 수 있었지만, 안 했어.

2. **과거에 대한 추측**:
   > "He could have been tired."
   > 그가 피곤했을 수도 있어.

3. **과거에 대한 비난/후회**:
   > "You could have told me earlier!"
   > 더 일찍 말해줄 수도 있었잖아!

---

## D. 비교 (Comparison)

| 형태 | 의미 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| could | 현재 가능 | You **could** run with me. |
| could have done | 과거 가능 (안 함) | You **could have** run with me. |
| couldn't | 현재 불가능 | I **couldn't** do it. |
| couldn't have done | 과거 불가능 | I **couldn't have** done it. |

### Could vs Was able to (복습)

| Could | Was able to |
|-------|-------------|
| 일반 과거 능력 | 특정 성취 |
| I could run fast. | I was able to finish the race. |

## Exercises

### A. Could vs Could have 선택

1. It's nice weather. We _______ go for a run. (지금)
2. I _______ take the bus yesterday, but I walked instead. (과거)
3. You _______ tell me you were tired! (과거 - 비난)
4. He _______ be at the park now. (현재 가능성)
5. She _______ finish earlier if she had tried. (과거 가능성)

**Answers:**
1. could
2. could have taken
3. could have told
4. could
5. could have finished

### B. 문장 완성

1. 1년 전에는 상상도 못 했을 거야.
   → I _______ this a year ago.

2. 버스 탈 수도 있었는데, 걷기로 했어.
   → I _______ the bus, but I chose to walk.

3. 더 열심히 훈련할 수 있었는데.
   → I _______ harder.

**Answers:**
1. couldn't have imagined
2. could have taken
3. could have trained

### C. 상황에 맞게 작성

1. (제안) 같이 달릴 수도 있어.
   → You _______ with me.

2. (후회) 포기할 수 있었는데, 안 했어.
   → I _______, but I didn't.

**Answers:**
1. could run
2. could have given up
