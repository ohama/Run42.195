# Unit 28: Must and can't

## A. 핵심 개념 (Key Concept)

**이 Unit에서 must와 can't는 추측의 의미입니다. (의무 아님)**

### Must (추측): ~임에 틀림없다

**강한 확신**을 가지고 추측할 때 사용합니다. (90% 확신)

> "You **must** be tired after running 10km."
> 10km 달리고 나서 피곤할 게 **틀림없어**.

### Can't (추측): ~일 리가 없다

**불가능**하다고 생각할 때 사용합니다. (0% 가능성)

> "That **can't** be true."
> 그건 사실일 **리가 없어**.

---

## B. 형태 (Form)

### Must (현재 추측)

| Subject | must be | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| He/She/It | must be | He must be tired. |
| They | must be | They must be at home. |

### Can't (현재 추측)

| Subject | can't be | Example |
|---------|----------|---------|
| He/She/It | can't be | It can't be true. |
| They | can't be | They can't be at school. |

### Must have done (과거 추측)

| Subject | must have + p.p. | Example |
|---------|------------------|---------|
| He/She | must have run | He must have run fast. |
| They | must have trained | They must have trained hard. |

### Can't have done (과거 추측)

| Subject | can't have + p.p. | Example |
|---------|-------------------|---------|
| He/She | can't have been | It can't have been easy. |
| They | can't have finished | They can't have finished. |

## C. Must (현재 추측)

### From the Original Book:

> "You've been traveling all day. You **must** be tired."
> 하루 종일 여행했잖아. 피곤할 게 틀림없어.

> "Jim is a hard worker. He **must** be good at his job."
> 짐은 열심히 일해. 일을 잘할 게 틀림없어.

> "I'm sure Sally gave me her address. It **must** be somewhere."
> 샐리가 주소를 줬을 텐데. 어딘가에 있을 게 틀림없어.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"You must be tired."**
> 피곤할 게 틀림없어.

- 상대방의 상태를 확신 있게 추측

> **"He must be at the orchard. He's always there in the morning."**
> 과수원에 계실 거야. 아침에 항상 거기 계시니까.

- 근거를 바탕으로 한 추측

> **"He must be waiting at the finish line."**
> 결승선에서 기다리고 계실 거야.

- 진행 중인 상태 추측 (must be + -ing)

---

## D. Can't (현재 추측)

### From the Original Book:

> "Tom's been awake all day. He **can't** be tired." (X) → This doesn't make sense.
> "Tom's been sleeping all day. He **can't** be tired." (O)
> 톰은 하루 종일 잤어. 피곤할 리가 없어.

> "She **can't** be at home. Her car isn't there."
> 그녀가 집에 있을 리가 없어. 차가 없잖아.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"That can't be right. The race is 10km, not 5km."**
> 그건 맞을 리가 없어. 대회는 10km야, 5km가 아니라.

- 불가능하다고 확신

> **"He can't be at school. It's Sunday."**
> 학교에 있을 리가 없어. 일요일이잖아.

---

## E. Must have done vs Can't have done (과거 추측)

### Must have done: 과거에 ~했을 게 틀림없다

> **"He must have trained really hard."**
> 정말 열심히 훈련했나 보다.

> "I can't find my keys. I **must have left** them at home."
> 열쇠를 못 찾겠어. 집에 두고 왔나 봐.

### Can't have done: 과거에 ~했을 리가 없다

> **"That can't have been easy."**
> 쉬웠을 리가 없어.

> "She **can't have seen** me. I was hiding."
> 그녀가 나를 봤을 리가 없어. 숨어 있었거든.

### Comparison Table:

| Form | Meaning | Time | Example |
|------|---------|------|---------|
| must be | ~임에 틀림없다 | 현재 | You must be tired. |
| can't be | ~일 리가 없다 | 현재 | He can't be at home. |
| must have done | ~했을 게 틀림없다 | 과거 | He must have trained hard. |
| can't have done | ~했을 리가 없다 | 과거 | It can't have been easy. |

---

## F. 확신의 정도 (Degrees of Certainty)

### 확신도 스펙트럼:

| Expression | Certainty | Korean | Example |
|------------|-----------|--------|---------|
| must | 90% | ~임에 틀림없다 | He **must** be tired. |
| may/might | 50% | ~일 수도 있다 | He **might** be tired. |
| could | 50% | ~일 수도 있다 | He **could** be tired. |
| can't | 0% | ~일 리가 없다 | He **can't** be tired. |

---

## G. Must (추측) vs Must (의무) 구분

| Must (추측) | Must (의무) |
|-------------|-------------|
| ~임에 틀림없다 | ~해야 한다 |
| You must be tired. | You must rest. |
| (피곤할 거야) | (쉬어야 해) |
| He must be at home. | He must go home. |
| (집에 있을 거야) | (집에 가야 해) |

### Can't (추측) vs Can't (능력)

| Can't (추측) | Can't (능력) |
|--------------|--------------|
| ~일 리가 없다 | ~할 수 없다 |
| That can't be true. | I can't run anymore. |
| (사실일 리가 없어) | (더 못 뛰겠어) |

---

## H. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~He must is tired.~~ | He **must be** tired. | must + 동사원형 |
| ~~It must be not true.~~ | It **can't be** true. | 추측의 부정 = can't |
| ~~He must has trained.~~ | He **must have trained**. | must have + p.p. |
| ~~It can't been easy.~~ | It **can't have been** easy. | can't have + p.p. |
| ~~You must to be tired.~~ | You **must be** tired. | to 없음 |
| ~~She musts be home.~~ | She **must be** home. | must는 변하지 않음 |

### 추측 vs 의무 구분 팁:

| 문맥 | Must 의미 | 예문 |
|------|----------|------|
| 상태 판단 | 추측 | You must be tired. (피곤할 거야) |
| 행동 지시 | 의무 | You must rest. (쉬어야 해) |
| 이유 있는 결론 | 추측 | He must be at home. The lights are on. |
| 규칙/명령 | 의무 | You must be quiet in the library. |

## Exercises

### A. Must vs Can't 선택 (Choose must or can't)

1. He ran 42km. He _______ be exhausted. (확실히 피곤할 것)
2. She finished in 3 hours? That _______ be right. She's a beginner. (불가능)
3. You've been training for a year. You _______ be ready. (확실히 준비됨)
4. He _______ be at school. It's Sunday. (불가능)
5. They're not answering the phone. They _______ be out. (확실히)
6. You passed the exam? That _______ be a mistake! (불가능 - 놀람)
7. She's 90 years old. She _______ be tired easily. (확실히)
8. It's 3 a.m. He _______ be sleeping. (확실히)

**Answers:**
1. must
2. can't
3. must
4. can't
5. must
6. can't
7. must
8. must

### B. 현재 vs 과거 추측 (Present vs Past)

Fill in with the correct form.

1. You look tired. You _______ (be) exhausted.
2. He finished the marathon. He _______ (train) really hard.
3. She _______ (be) at the finish line now. The race is over.
4. That _______ (be) easy. Running 42km is tough.
5. I can't find my phone. I _______ (leave) it at home.
6. She's not here. She _______ (go) home already.
7. He's smiling. He _______ (be) happy.
8. The cake is gone. Someone _______ (eat) it.

**Answers:**
1. must be
2. must have trained
3. must be
4. can't have been
5. must have left
6. must have gone
7. must be
8. must have eaten

### C. 문장 완성 (Complete the sentences)

1. 피곤할 게 틀림없어. → You _______ tired.
2. 그건 맞을 리가 없어. → That _______ right.
3. 열심히 훈련했나 보다. → He _______ really hard.
4. 쉬웠을 리가 없어. → It _______ easy.
5. 결승선에서 기다리고 계실 거야. → He _______ at the finish line.

**Answers:**
1. must be
2. can't be
3. must have trained
4. can't have been
5. must be waiting

### D. 추측 vs 의무 구분 (Identify the meaning)

Choose: (a) 추측 (b) 의무

1. You must be tired. → **(a) 추측**
2. You must rest before the race. → **(b) 의무**
3. He must be at home. → **(a) 추측**
4. You must arrive by 8 a.m. → **(b) 의무**
5. She must have been happy. → **(a) 추측**
6. Students must wear uniforms. → **(b) 의무**
7. It must be nice to live by the beach. → **(a) 추측**
8. You mustn't be late. → **(b) 의무**
