# Unit 59: Prefer and would rather

## A. prefer의 형태 (Form)

**prefer**는 ~을 더 좋아하다라는 의미로, 여러 형태로 쓸 수 있습니다.

| Structure | Example | Korean |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| prefer + -ing | I prefer walking. | 걷는 게 더 좋아. |
| prefer + to | I prefer to walk. | 걷는 게 더 좋아. |
| prefer A to B | I prefer walking to running. | 달리는 것보다 걷는 게 좋아. |
| prefer to A rather than B | I prefer to walk rather than run. | 달리느니 걷는 게 더 좋아. |

### From the Original Book:

> "I prefer **driving** to **traveling** by train."
> 기차 여행보다 운전하는 게 더 좋아.

> "I prefer **to drive** rather than **travel** by train."
> 기차로 여행하느니 운전하는 게 더 좋아.

---

## B. prefer + -ing / prefer + to (Primary Usage)

두 형태 모두 가능하며, 의미 차이가 거의 없습니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I prefer walking to taking the bus."**
> 버스 타는 것보다 걷는 게 더 좋다.

- **prefer A to B**: A를 B보다 더 좋아하다

> **"I prefer to run in the morning."**
> 아침에 달리는 게 더 좋다.

> **"Now I prefer running to sitting on the bus."**
> 이제 버스에 앉아 있는 것보다 달리는 게 더 좋다.

### 선호도 표현:

```
prefer A to B:
A ████████████ (더 좋음)
B ████ (덜 좋음)
```

---

## C. prefer A to B (Comparison)

**A를 B보다 더 좋아하다**

| Structure | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| prefer 명사 to 명사 | I prefer tea to coffee. |
| prefer -ing to -ing | I prefer walking to riding. |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I prefer walking to taking the bus."**
> 버스 타는 것보다 걷는 게 더 좋다.

> **"He prefers running to walking."**
> 그는 걷기보다 달리기를 더 좋아한다.

**주의:** to 뒤에도 -ing가 옵니다 (to부정사 아님!)

### 문법 구조:

```
prefer [A: -ing] to [B: -ing]
       ↓              ↓
    running    to   walking
    (O)              (O)
```

| Correct | Incorrect |
|---------|-----------|
| prefer walking **to** running | ~~prefer walking **than** running~~ |
| prefer tea **to** coffee | ~~prefer tea **than** coffee~~ |

---

## D. would rather + 동사원형 (Special Pattern)

**would rather**는 ~하는 게 낫겠다는 의미로, **동사원형**이 옵니다.

| Structure | Example | Korean |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| would rather + 동사원형 | I'd rather walk. | 걷는 게 낫겠어. |
| would rather A than B | I'd rather walk than take the bus. | 버스 타느니 걷는 게 낫겠어. |
| would rather not | I'd rather not go. | 안 가는 게 낫겠어. |

### From the Original Book:

> "I'd rather **stay** at home tonight."
> 오늘 밤은 집에 있는 게 낫겠어.

> "Would you rather **have** tea or coffee?"
> 차와 커피 중 뭐가 낫겠어?

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I'd rather walk than take the bus."**
> 버스 타느니 걷는 게 낫겠어.

> **"I'd rather run to school."**
> 학교까지 뛰어가는 게 낫겠어.

> **"Would you rather rest today?"**
> 오늘은 쉬는 게 낫겠어?

---

## E. prefer vs would rather (Important Distinction)

| Expression | Meaning | Usage | Structure |
|------------|---------|-------|-----------|
| prefer | 일반적인 선호 | 항상 그런 취향 | -ing / to |
| would rather | 특정 상황에서의 선호 | 지금 이 상황에서 | 동사원형만 |

### Examples:

> **"I prefer walking."** (일반적으로 걷는 게 좋아)
> **"I'd rather walk today."** (오늘은 걷는 게 낫겠어)

### From the Novel:

> **"The bus is comfortable. But I'd rather run."**
> 버스가 편하긴 해. 하지만 달리는 게 낫겠어.

- 이 상황에서 달리는 것을 선택

### 시간적 차이:

```
prefer:       일반적 선호 (항상)
              ──────────────────────────→

would rather: 특정 상황 선호 (지금/오늘)
              ────┐
                  └→ (이 순간의 선택)
```

---

## F. would rather... than... (Detailed Usage)

**~하느니 차라리 ~하겠다**

### From the Original Book:

> "I'd rather **go** out **than** **stay** at home."
> 집에 있느니 나가는 게 낫겠어.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I'd rather walk for an hour than feel sick on the bus for 40 minutes."**
> 버스에서 40분 동안 멀미하느니 1시간 걷는 게 낫겠어.

> **"I'd rather run slow than stop."**
> 멈추느니 천천히 달리는 게 낫겠어.

> **"I'd rather train hard than regret later."**
> 나중에 후회하느니 열심히 훈련하는 게 낫겠어.

### 부정형:

> **"I'd rather not take the bus."**
> 버스 타지 않는 게 낫겠어.

> **"I'd rather not talk about it."**
> 그것에 대해 말하지 않는 게 낫겠어.

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~I prefer walking **than** running.~~ | I prefer walking **to** running. | prefer A **to** B |
| ~~I'd rather **to walk**.~~ | I'd rather **walk**. | would rather + 동사원형 |
| ~~I'd rather **walking**.~~ | I'd rather **walk**. | would rather + 동사원형 |
| ~~I prefer **walk** to run.~~ | I prefer **walking** to **running**. | prefer + -ing |
| ~~I'd rather not **to go**.~~ | I'd rather not **go**. | would rather + 동사원형 |
| ~~Would you rather **to stay**?~~ | Would you rather **stay**? | would rather + 동사원형 |

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. I prefer (walk / walking) to running.
2. I'd rather (walk / walking) than run.
3. She prefers (take / to take) the bus.
4. Would you rather (rest / to rest) today?
5. I prefer running (to / than) walking.
6. I'd rather not (go / to go) out tonight.
7. He prefers coffee (to / than) tea.
8. Would you rather (stay / staying) home?

**Answers:**
1. walking
2. walk
3. taking / to take
4. rest
5. to
6. go
7. to
8. stay

### B. 문장을 완성하세요.

1. I prefer _______ (run) to _______ (walk).
2. I'd rather _______ (train) than _______ (rest).
3. She prefers _______ (ride) the bus to _______ (walk).
4. Would you rather _______ (stay) home?
5. I'd rather _______ (not go) out today.
6. He prefers _______ (swim) to _______ (run).
7. I'd rather _______ (run) slowly than _______ (stop).
8. She prefers _______ (work) alone.

**Answers:**
1. running, walking
2. train, rest
3. riding, walking
4. stay
5. not go
6. swimming, running
7. run, stop
8. working / to work

### C. prefer 또는 would rather를 사용해 문장을 만드세요.

1. (일반적으로) 걷는 게 더 좋아.
   → I _______ walking.

2. (오늘은) 달리는 게 낫겠어.
   → I _______ run today.

3. 버스 타느니 걷는 게 낫겠어.
   → I _______ walk _______ take the bus.

4. 차보다 커피가 더 좋아.
   → I _______ coffee _______ tea.

5. 오늘 밤은 집에 있는 게 낫겠어.
   → I _______ stay home tonight.

**Answers:**
1. prefer
2. 'd rather
3. 'd rather, than
4. prefer, to
5. 'd rather

### D. 틀린 부분을 고치세요.

1. I prefer walking than running.
   → _______

2. I'd rather to stay home.
   → _______

3. She prefers walk to run.
   → _______

4. Would you rather resting today?
   → _______

**Answers:**
1. I prefer walking **to** running.
2. I'd rather **stay** home.
3. She prefers **walking** to **running**.
4. Would you rather **rest** today?

## Summary: prefer vs would rather

| prefer | would rather |
|--------|--------------|
| prefer to walk / prefer walking | would rather walk |
| prefer A to B | would rather A than B |
| 일반적 선호 | 특정 상황 선호 |
| -ing 또는 to 가능 | **동사원형만** |

### Quick Grammar Check:

| Expression | Following Structure |
|------------|---------------------|
| prefer | -ing / to + 동사원형 |
| prefer A to B | A(-ing) to B(-ing) |
| would rather | **동사원형** |
| would rather A than B | A(동사원형) than B(동사원형) |

### Comparison Prepositions:

| Expression | Preposition |
|------------|-------------|
| prefer A **to** B | to |
| would rather A **than** B | than |
