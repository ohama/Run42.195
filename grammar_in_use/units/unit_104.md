# Unit 104: Quite, Pretty, Rather, and Fairly

## A. 정도를 나타내는 부사들

**quite, pretty, rather, fairly**는 모두 "꽤, 상당히"의 의미이지만, 강도와 뉘앙스가 다릅니다.

강도 순서 (약 → 강):
```
fairly < quite < rather/pretty < very
```

---

## B. fairly - 어느 정도, 꽤 (약한 긍정)

**fairly**는 가장 약한 표현으로, "예상보다 나쁘지 않은" 정도입니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The first kilometer was fairly easy."**
> 첫 킬로미터는 어느 정도 쉬웠다.

> **"The morning was fairly cool."**
> 아침은 꽤 서늘했다.

> **"I felt fairly confident before the race."**
> 대회 전에 어느 정도 자신감이 있었다.

---

## C. quite - 꽤, 상당히

**quite**는 "꽤, 상당히"의 의미로, fairly보다 강합니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"7 kilometers is quite a distance."**
> 7킬로미터는 꽤 먼 거리다.

- quite + a/an + 명사 = 상당한

> **"The view was quite beautiful."**
> 경치가 꽤 아름다웠다.

> **"I was quite tired after 10 kilometers."**
> 10킬로미터 후에 꽤 피곤했다.

> **"The race was quite challenging."**
> 대회가 꽤 도전적이었다.

### quite + a/an + 명사:

> **"It was quite a surprise."**
> 꽤 놀라운 일이었다.

> **"It was quite an achievement."**
> 상당한 성취였다.

---

## D. rather - 꽤, 다소 (예상보다/부정적 뉘앙스)

**rather**는 "예상보다" 또는 "다소 부정적"인 뉘앙스가 있습니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The bus was rather crowded."**
> 버스가 다소 붐볐다. (부정적)

> **"It was rather cold this morning."**
> 오늘 아침은 꽤 추웠다.

> **"The hill was rather steep."**
> 언덕이 꽤 가팔랐다.

> **"I felt rather nervous before the race."**
> 대회 전에 다소 긴장됐다.

### rather + 긍정적 형용사 = 예상보다 좋음:

> **"The race was rather enjoyable."**
> 대회가 예상보다 즐거웠다.

---

## E. pretty - 꽤 (구어체)

**pretty**는 **rather**와 비슷하지만 더 **구어체**입니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Pretty far."**
> 꽤 멀어.

> **"That's pretty impressive."**
> 그거 꽤 인상적이네.

> **"I felt pretty good after the run."**
> 달리고 나서 기분이 꽤 좋았다.

> **"The first 5km was pretty easy."**
> 처음 5km는 꽤 쉬웠다.

---

## F. 강도 비교

| 단어 | 강도 | 뉘앙스 | 격식 |
|------|------|--------|------|
| fairly | ★★☆☆☆ | 약한 긍정 | 중립 |
| quite | ★★★☆☆ | 상당히 | 중립 |
| rather | ★★★★☆ | 예상보다/부정적 | 격식 |
| pretty | ★★★★☆ | 꽤 | 비격식 |
| very | ★★★★★ | 매우 | 중립 |

### Comparison Examples:

| | 문장 | 의미 |
|---|------|------|
| fairly | The race was fairly hard. | 어느 정도 힘들었다 |
| quite | The race was quite hard. | 꽤 힘들었다 |
| rather | The race was rather hard. | 예상보다/다소 힘들었다 |
| pretty | The race was pretty hard. | 꽤 힘들었다 (구어) |
| very | The race was very hard. | 매우 힘들었다 |

---

## G. quite의 두 가지 의미

**quite**는 형용사에 따라 의미가 달라질 수 있습니다.

### 1. 점진적 형용사 + quite = 꽤, 상당히

> **"It was quite cold."** = 꽤 추웠다.
> **"I was quite tired."** = 꽤 피곤했다.

### 2. 절대적 형용사 + quite = 완전히

> **"I quite agree."** = 완전히 동의한다.
> **"It was quite amazing."** = 정말 놀라웠다.
> **"You're quite right."** = 완전히 맞아.

## Exercises

### A. fairly, quite, rather, pretty 중 가장 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. The bus ride was _______ uncomfortable. (부정적 뉘앙스)
   → **rather**

2. The view was _______ beautiful. (상당히)
   → **quite**

3. The race was _______ hard, but I expected it. (약한 정도)
   → **fairly**

4. That's _______ impressive! (구어체)
   → **pretty**

### B. 문장의 강도 순서를 매기세요 (1 = 가장 약함).

a) The race was quite difficult.
b) The race was fairly difficult.
c) The race was very difficult.
d) The race was rather difficult.

**Answer:** b (1) → a (2) → d (3) → c (4)

### C. 빈칸에 알맞은 단어를 넣으세요.

1. 7 kilometers is _______ a distance. (상당한)
   → **quite**

2. The morning was _______ cool. (어느 정도)
   → **fairly**

3. The hill was _______ steep. (예상보다)
   → **rather**

4. I felt _______ good after running. (구어체)
   → **pretty**

### D. 문맥에 맞게 골라 쓰세요.

1. (Speaking casually) The race was _______ hard.
   → **pretty**

2. (Surprised it was hard) The race was _______ hard.
   → **rather**

3. (As expected, moderately hard) The race was _______ hard.
   → **fairly/quite**
