# Unit 104: Quite, Pretty, Rather, and Fairly

## A. 정도를 나타내는 부사들 (Degree Adverbs)

**quite, pretty, rather, fairly**는 모두 "꽤, 상당히"의 의미이지만, 강도와 뉘앙스가 다릅니다.

### Intensity Scale (강도 순서):

```
약 ←────────────────────────────────→ 강
fairly < quite < rather/pretty < very
```

### From the Original Book:

> "I'm **fairly** sure I can come." (not very sure)
> "I'm **quite** sure I can come." (more certain)
> "I'm **pretty** sure I can come." (informal, quite certain)
> "I'm **very** sure I can come." (certain)

---

## B. fairly - 어느 정도, 꽤 (약한 긍정)

**fairly**는 가장 약한 표현으로, "예상보다 나쁘지 않은" 정도입니다. 조금 소극적인 평가.

### Nuance:

- 강도: ★★☆☆☆
- 뉘앙스: "나쁘지 않은 편" (not bad, but not great either)
- 격식: 중립적

### From the Original Book:

> "I know her **fairly** well."
> 그녀를 어느 정도 알아. (아주 잘 아는 건 아님)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The first kilometer was fairly easy."**
> 첫 킬로미터는 어느 정도 쉬웠다.

> **"The morning was fairly cool."**
> 아침은 꽤 서늘했다.

> **"I felt fairly confident before the race."**
> 대회 전에 어느 정도 자신감이 있었다.

> **"The route was fairly flat."**
> 경로가 꽤 평평했다.

---

## C. quite - 꽤, 상당히 (중간 강도)

**quite**는 "꽤, 상당히"의 의미로, fairly보다 강합니다.

### Nuance:

- 강도: ★★★☆☆
- 뉘앙스: "상당히" (to a fairly large degree)
- 격식: 중립적

### From the Original Book:

> "It's **quite** cold today. You should wear a coat."
> 오늘 꽤 춥네. 코트 입어야겠다.

> "The exam was **quite** difficult."
> 시험이 꽤 어려웠다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"7 kilometers is quite a distance."**
> 7킬로미터는 꽤 먼 거리다.

- quite + a/an + 명사 = 상당한

> **"The view was quite beautiful."**
> 경치가 꽤 아름다웠다.

> **"I was quite tired after 10 kilometers."**
> 10킬로미터 후에 꽤 피곤했다.

> **"The race was quite challenging."**
> 대회가 꽤 도전적이었다.

### quite + a/an + 명사:

> **"It was quite a surprise."**
> 꽤 놀라운 일이었다.

> **"It was quite an achievement."**
> 상당한 성취였다.

---

## D. rather - 꽤, 다소 (예상보다/부정적 뉘앙스)

**rather**는 "예상보다" 또는 "다소 부정적"인 뉘앙스가 있습니다. 예상과 다를 때 자주 사용.

### Nuance:

- 강도: ★★★★☆
- 뉘앙스: "예상보다" 또는 "다소 부정적"
- 격식: 다소 격식적

### From the Original Book:

> "The exam was **rather** difficult." (unexpectedly/unpleasantly difficult)
> 시험이 생각보다 어려웠다.

> "I think she was **rather** offended."
> 그녀가 꽤 기분 나빴던 것 같아.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The bus was rather crowded."**
> 버스가 다소 붐볐다. (부정적)

> **"It was rather cold this morning."**
> 오늘 아침은 꽤 추웠다.

> **"The hill was rather steep."**
> 언덕이 꽤 가팔랐다.

> **"I felt rather nervous before the race."**
> 대회 전에 다소 긴장됐다.

### rather + 긍정적 형용사 = 예상보다 좋음:

> **"The race was rather enjoyable."**
> 대회가 예상보다 즐거웠다.

> **"The result was rather good."**
> 결과가 예상보다 좋았다.

---

## E. pretty - 꽤 (구어체)

**pretty**는 **rather**와 비슷하지만 더 **구어체**입니다.

### Nuance:

- 강도: ★★★★☆
- 뉘앙스: "꽤" (informal)
- 격식: 비격식적/구어체

### From the Original Book:

> "I'm **pretty** sure I left my keys in the car."
> 차에 열쇠 두고 온 거 거의 확실해.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Pretty far."**
> 꽤 멀어.

> **"That's pretty impressive."**
> 그거 꽤 인상적이네.

> **"I felt pretty good after the run."**
> 달리고 나서 기분이 꽤 좋았다.

> **"The first 5km was pretty easy."**
> 처음 5km는 꽤 쉬웠다.

> **"I'm pretty sure I can finish."**
> 완주할 수 있다고 꽤 확신해.

---

## F. 강도 비교 (Intensity Comparison)

### Comparison Table:

| 단어 | 강도 | 뉘앙스 | 격식 |
|------|------|--------|------|
| fairly | ★★☆☆☆ | 약한 긍정, 소극적 | 중립 |
| quite | ★★★☆☆ | 상당히 | 중립 |
| rather | ★★★★☆ | 예상보다/부정적 | 격식적 |
| pretty | ★★★★☆ | 꽤 | 비격식 |
| very | ★★★★★ | 매우 | 중립 |

### Same Sentence, Different Intensity:

| 강도 | 문장 | 의미 |
|------|------|------|
| fairly | The race was **fairly** hard. | 어느 정도 힘들었다 (소극적) |
| quite | The race was **quite** hard. | 꽤 힘들었다 |
| rather | The race was **rather** hard. | 예상보다/다소 힘들었다 |
| pretty | The race was **pretty** hard. | 꽤 힘들었다 (구어) |
| very | The race was **very** hard. | 매우 힘들었다 |

## G. quite의 두 가지 의미 (Two meanings of quite)

**quite**는 형용사에 따라 의미가 달라질 수 있습니다.

### 1. 점진적 형용사 + quite = 꽤, 상당히 (fairly)

> **"It was quite cold."** = 꽤 추웠다.
> **"I was quite tired."** = 꽤 피곤했다.

### 2. 절대적 형용사 + quite = 완전히 (completely)

> **"I quite agree."** = 완전히 동의한다.
> **"It was quite amazing."** = 정말 놀라웠다.
> **"You're quite right."** = 완전히 맞아.
> **"The answer is quite wrong."** = 완전히 틀렸다.

### From the Original Book:

> "It's **quite** impossible." (= completely impossible)
> "I **quite** agree with you." (= completely agree)

## H. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | 설명 |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| ~~It's pretty formal document~~ | It's a **fairly** formal document | 격식 문맥에 pretty 부적합 |
| ~~The food was rather delicious~~ | The food was **quite/very** delicious | 긍정 형용사에 rather 어색 |
| ~~I'm quite agree~~ | I **quite** agree | quite는 동사 앞에 |
| ~~fairly quite hard~~ | **fairly** hard / **quite** hard | 둘 다 사용 불가 |

## Exercises

### A. fairly, quite, rather, pretty 중 가장 알맞은 것을 고르세요 (Choose the best option)

1. The bus ride was _______ uncomfortable. (부정적 뉘앙스)
   → **rather**

2. The view was _______ beautiful. (상당히)
   → **quite**

3. The race was _______ hard, but I expected it. (약한 정도)
   → **fairly**

4. That's _______ impressive! (구어체)
   → **pretty**

5. The morning was _______ cold. (예상보다)
   → **rather**

6. The route was _______ flat. (나쁘지 않은)
   → **fairly**

7. I'm _______ sure I can finish. (구어체)
   → **pretty**

8. The result was _______ good. (상당히)
   → **quite**

---

### B. 문장의 강도 순서를 매기세요 (Rank by intensity: 1 = weakest)

a) The race was quite difficult.
b) The race was fairly difficult.
c) The race was very difficult.
d) The race was rather difficult.

**Answer:** b (1) → a (2) → d (3) → c (4)

---

### C. 빈칸에 알맞은 단어를 넣으세요 (Fill in the blanks)

1. 7 kilometers is _______ a distance. (상당한)
   → **quite**

2. The morning was _______ cool. (어느 정도)
   → **fairly**

3. The hill was _______ steep. (예상보다)
   → **rather**

4. I felt _______ good after running. (구어체)
   → **pretty**

5. You're _______ right. (완전히)
   → **quite**

6. The exam was _______ difficult. (상당히)
   → **quite**

---

### D. 문맥에 맞게 골라 쓰세요 (Choose based on context)

1. (Speaking casually to a friend) The race was _______ hard.
   → **pretty**

2. (Surprised it was hard) The race was _______ hard.
   → **rather**

3. (As expected, moderately hard) The race was _______ hard.
   → **fairly/quite**

4. (In a formal report) The project was _______ successful.
   → **fairly/quite** (not pretty)

5. (Expressing complete agreement) I _______ agree.
   → **quite**

## Summary

### Comparison Table:

| 단어 | 강도 | 뉘앙스 | 사용 상황 |
|------|------|--------|----------|
| fairly | 약 | 나쁘지 않은 | 격식/비격식 |
| quite | 중 | 상당히 | 격식/비격식 |
| rather | 강 | 예상보다/부정적 | 더 격식적 |
| pretty | 강 | 꽤 | 비격식/구어체 |

### quite의 두 가지 의미:

| 형용사 유형 | quite의 의미 | 예시 |
|-------------|-------------|------|
| 점진적 (gradable) | 꽤, 상당히 | quite cold, quite tired |
| 절대적 (absolute) | 완전히 | quite right, quite agree |

### Usage Guidelines:

| 상황 | 추천 |
|------|------|
| 캐주얼한 대화 | pretty |
| 예상과 다를 때 | rather |
| 중립적 표현 | quite, fairly |
| 소극적 평가 | fairly |
| 완전한 동의 | quite (agree) |
