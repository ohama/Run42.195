# Unit 79: Singular and Plural

## A. 규칙 복수형

대부분의 명사는 **-s** 또는 **-es**를 붙여 복수형을 만듭니다.

| Rule | Singular | Plural |
|------|----------|--------|
| +s | bus | buses |
| +es (s, x, ch, sh) | bus | buses |
| 자음+y → ies | body | bodies |
| 모음+y → +s | day | days |
| f/fe → ves | life | lives |

---

## B. 소설 속 규칙 복수형

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Four days, eight bus rides."**
> 나흘, 버스 8번.

- day → days, ride → rides

> **"I could see people crammed inside."**
> 안에 사람들이 빽빽이 들어찬 게 보였다.

- person → people (불규칙)

> **"There were flowers along the road."**
> 길가에 꽃들이 있었다.

- flower → flowers

> **"I set three alarms."**
> 알람을 세 개 맞췄다.

- alarm → alarms

---

## C. 불규칙 복수형

| Singular | Plural |
|----------|--------|
| man | men |
| woman | women |
| child | children |
| person | people |
| foot | feet |
| tooth | teeth |
| mouse | mice |
| life | lives |

### From the Novel:

> **"People poured off the bus."**
> 사람들이 버스에서 쏟아져 나왔다.

> **"My feet were sore."**
> 발이 아팠다.

> **"Kids were running around."**
> 애들이 뛰어다녔다.

- kid → kids (규칙)
- child → children (불규칙)

---

## D. 단복수 동형

일부 명사는 **단수와 복수가 같습니다**.

| Word | Singular | Plural |
|------|----------|--------|
| sheep | a sheep | three sheep |
| fish | a fish | many fish |
| deer | a deer | two deer |
| series | a series | two series |
| species | a species | several species |

---

## E. 항상 복수형인 명사

일부 명사는 **항상 복수형**으로 쓰입니다.

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| 두 부분 | pants, shorts, glasses, scissors |
| 항상 복수 | clothes, goods, stairs |

### From the Novel:

> **"I grabbed my gym clothes."**
> 체육복을 챙겼다.

- clothes = 항상 복수

> **"The stairs were hard to climb."**
> 계단 오르기가 힘들었다.

- stairs = 항상 복수

---

## F. 집합 명사

**집합 명사**는 단수/복수 동사 모두 가능합니다 (의미에 따라).

| Word | As unit (단수) | As members (복수) |
|------|---------------|------------------|
| family | My family is big. | My family are all runners. |
| team | The team is strong. | The team are wearing new uniforms. |
| class | The class is large. | The class are taking their seats. |

### From the Novel:

> **"My family came to the finish line."**
> 우리 가족이 결승선에 왔다.

> **"Minseo's family were all cheering."**
> 민서네 가족 모두가 응원하고 있었다.

---

## G. 수량 + 복수 명사

**숫자 + 명사** = 복수형:

### From the Novel:

> **"Seven kilometers from home to school."**
> 집에서 학교까지 7킬로미터.

> **"40 minutes by bus."**
> 버스로 40분.

> **"Four days, eight bus rides."**
> 나흘, 버스 8번.

## Exercises

### A. 복수형을 쓰세요.

1. bus → _______
2. day → _______
3. life → _______
4. child → _______
5. foot → _______
6. person → _______

**Answers:**
1. buses
2. days
3. lives
4. children
5. feet
6. people

### B. 빈칸을 채우세요.

1. I saw many _______ (flower) along the road.
2. My _______ (foot) were sore after walking.
3. _______ (Person) were waiting at the bus stop.
4. I set three _______ (alarm).
5. I grabbed my gym _______ (cloth).

**Answers:**
1. flowers
2. feet
3. People
4. alarms
5. clothes

### C. 틀린 부분을 고치세요.

1. I saw many peoples. → _______
2. My foots hurt. → _______
3. I have two childs. → _______
4. I walked for 7 kilometer. → _______

**Answers:**
1. people
2. feet
3. children
4. kilometers

## Summary: 복수형 규칙

| Type | Rule | Example |
|------|------|---------|
| 규칙 | +s | flowers, days |
| 규칙 | +es | buses, watches |
| 규칙 | y → ies | bodies, stories |
| 불규칙 | 변형 | feet, children, people |
| 동형 | 변화 없음 | sheep, fish |
| 항상 복수 | - | clothes, stairs |
