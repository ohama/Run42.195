# Unit 49: Questions (1) - Basic Question Forms

## A. 의문문의 기본 구조 (Basic Question Structure)

영어 의문문은 **조동사/be동사**가 주어 앞으로 이동합니다.

### From the Original Book:

| 평서문 | 의문문 |
|--------|--------|
| You **are** tired. | **Are** you tired? |
| He **can** run. | **Can** he run? |
| She **is** coming. | **Is** she coming? |
| They **have** finished. | **Have** they finished? |

### 의문문 형성 규칙:
```
조동사/be동사 + 주어 + (동사) + ...?
```

---

## B. Be동사 의문문 (Questions with Be)

**Be + 주어 + ...?**

### From the Original Book:

> "**Are** you tired?"
> 피곤해?

> "**Is** he a student?"
> 그는 학생이니?

> "**Was** the exam difficult?"
> 시험이 어려웠어?

### 시제별 Be동사 의문문:

| 시제 | 의문문 | 예문 |
|------|--------|------|
| 현재 | Am/Is/Are + 주어? | **Are** you tired? |
| 과거 | Was/Were + 주어? | **Was** it difficult? |
| 현재진행 | Am/Is/Are + 주어 + -ing? | **Are** you running? |
| 과거진행 | Was/Were + 주어 + -ing? | **Were** you sleeping? |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Are you nervous?"**
> 긴장돼?

> **"Is that your bus?"**
> 그거 네 버스야?

> **"Was the race difficult?"**
> 대회 힘들었어?

> **"Were you tired after running?"**
> 달리고 나서 피곤했어?

---

## C. Do/Does/Did 의문문 (Questions with Do)

일반동사는 **Do/Does/Did + 주어 + 동사원형?**

### From the Original Book:

> "**Do** you like coffee?"
> 커피 좋아해?

> "**Does** she work here?"
> 그녀가 여기서 일해?

> "**Did** you see him?"
> 그를 봤어?

### 시제별 Do 의문문:

| 시제 | 의문문 | 예문 |
|------|--------|------|
| 현재 (I/you/we/they) | Do + 주어 + 동사원형? | **Do** you run? |
| 현재 (he/she/it) | Does + 주어 + 동사원형? | **Does** he run? |
| 과거 | Did + 주어 + 동사원형? | **Did** you run? |

### 주의: 동사원형 사용!

| 틀림 (X) | 올바름 (O) |
|----------|-----------|
| Does he **runs**? | Does he **run**? |
| Did you **went**? | Did you **go**? |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Do you run every day?"**
> 매일 달려?

> **"Does the bus come at 7:30?"**
> 버스가 7시 30분에 와?

> **"Did you miss the bus?"**
> 버스 놓쳤어?

> **"Did you catch the next one?"**
> 다음 버스 탔어?

> **"Did I see you this morning?"**
> 오늘 아침에 너 봤나?

## D. Wh- 의문문 (Wh- Questions)

**의문사 + 조동사/be + 주어 + ...?**

### From the Original Book:

| 의문사 | 의미 | 예문 |
|--------|------|------|
| **What** | 무엇 | **What** do you want? |
| **Where** | 어디 | **Where** do you live? |
| **When** | 언제 | **When** did you arrive? |
| **Why** | 왜 | **Why** are you late? |
| **Who** | 누구 | **Who** is he? |
| **Which** | 어느 | **Which** one do you want? |
| **How** | 어떻게 | **How** do you feel? |
| **How long** | 얼마나 오래 | **How long** did it take? |
| **How far** | 얼마나 멀리 | **How far** is it? |
| **How much** | 얼마나 많이 | **How much** does it cost? |
| **How many** | 몇 개 | **How many** people came? |
| **How often** | 얼마나 자주 | **How often** do you run? |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Where do you catch the bus?"**
> 어디서 버스 타?

> **"What do you want for dinner?"**
> 저녁에 뭐 먹고 싶어?

> **"How long did that take?"**
> 그거 얼마나 걸렸어?

> **"Why are you late?"**
> 왜 늦었어?

> **"How far is it from your house to school?"**
> 집에서 학교까지 얼마나 멀어?

---

## E. 주어를 묻는 의문문 (Subject Questions)

주어를 물을 때는 **의문사가 주어 역할**을 하므로 어순이 바뀌지 않습니다.

### From the Original Book:

| 일반 의문문 (목적어) | 주어 의문문 |
|---------------------|-------------|
| **Who** did you see? (너는 누구를 봤어?) | **Who** saw you? (누가 널 봤어?) |
| **What** did you eat? (너는 뭘 먹었어?) | **What** happened? (무슨 일이 있었어?) |

### 핵심 차이:

| 질문 유형 | 어순 | 예문 |
|-----------|------|------|
| 목적어를 물음 | 의문사 + 조동사 + 주어 + 동사? | **Who** did you **see**? |
| 주어를 물음 | 의문사 + 동사? | **Who** **saw** you? |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Who runs with you?"**
> 누가 너랑 같이 달려? (주어)

> **"What makes you tired?"**
> 뭐가 널 피곤하게 해? (주어)

> **"Who finished first?"**
> 누가 1등으로 끝냈어? (주어)

### 비교:

| 목적어 의문문 | 주어 의문문 |
|---------------|-------------|
| **Who** did you **meet**? | **Who** **met** you? |
| (너는 누구를 만났어?) | (누가 널 만났어?) |
| **What** did you **buy**? | **What** **fell**? |
| (너는 뭘 샀어?) | (뭐가 떨어졌어?) |

## F. 부정 의문문 (Negative Questions)

**Don't/Doesn't/Didn't + 주어 + 동사?** 또는 **Isn't/Aren't/Wasn't + 주어?**

### From the Original Book:

> "**Don't** you want to come?"
> 오고 싶지 않아?

> "**Didn't** you hear me?"
> 못 들었어?

> "**Isn't** it cold?"
> 춥지 않아?

### 부정 의문문의 용도:

| 용도 | 예문 |
|------|------|
| **놀람** | Didn't you hear your alarm? (알람 못 들었어?) |
| **확인** | Don't you feel sick? (속 안 좋아?) |
| **제안** | Wouldn't you like some coffee? (커피 드실래요?) |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Doesn't that tire them out?"**
> 그거 안 힘들어? (놀람)

> **"Don't you feel sick on the bus?"**
> 버스에서 안 속 안 좋아? (확인)

> **"Didn't you hear your alarm?"**
> 알람 못 들었어? (놀람)

> **"Isn't it too far to walk?"**
> 걸어가기엔 너무 멀지 않아? (확인)

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| 틀린 문장 (X) | 올바른 문장 (O) | 설명 |
|---------------|-----------------|------|
| **Does** he **runs**? | **Does** he **run**? | 동사원형 사용 |
| **Did** you **went**? | **Did** you **go**? | 동사원형 사용 |
| **Where you live**? | **Where do you live**? | do 필요 |
| **What happened** to you? | **What happened** to you? (O) | 주어 질문은 do 불필요 |
| You **are** tired? | **Are** you tired? | 어순 변화 |
| **Who** did **saw** you? | **Who** **saw** you? | 주어 질문은 did 불필요 |

### Do/Does/Did + 동사원형 규칙:

```
(X) Does he runs every day?
(O) Does he run every day?

(X) Did you went there?
(O) Did you go there?
```

### 주어 질문 오류:

```
(X) Who did win the race?
(O) Who won the race?

주어를 물을 때는 do/does/did 사용 안 함
```

## Exercises

### A. 의문문으로 바꾸세요.

1. You are tired. → ______________________?
2. He runs every day. → ______________________?
3. She can help me. → ______________________?
4. They missed the bus. → ______________________?
5. The race was difficult. → ______________________?
6. You have finished. → ______________________?
7. It will rain tomorrow. → ______________________?
8. She is coming. → ______________________?

**Answers:**
1. Are you tired?
2. Does he run every day?
3. Can she help me?
4. Did they miss the bus?
5. Was the race difficult?
6. Have you finished?
7. Will it rain tomorrow?
8. Is she coming?

### B. Wh- 의문문을 만드세요.

1. You catch the bus **at Jocheon**. (where)
   → ______________________?

2. The bus comes **at 7:30**. (what time)
   → ______________________?

3. It takes **40 minutes** to get to school. (how long)
   → ______________________?

4. He is tired **because he ran**. (why)
   → ______________________?

5. She wants **pasta** for dinner. (what)
   → ______________________?

6. He runs **every day**. (how often)
   → ______________________?

7. It is **10 kilometers** to school. (how far)
   → ______________________?

8. She bought **three** books. (how many)
   → ______________________?

**Answers:**
1. Where do you catch the bus?
2. What time does the bus come?
3. How long does it take to get to school?
4. Why is he tired?
5. What does she want for dinner?
6. How often does he run?
7. How far is it to school?
8. How many books did she buy?

### C. 주어를 묻는 의문문과 목적어를 묻는 의문문을 구분하세요.

1. Who did you meet? (목적어 / 주어)
2. Who came first? (목적어 / 주어)
3. What happened? (목적어 / 주어)
4. What did you see? (목적어 / 주어)
5. Who called you? (목적어 / 주어)
6. Who did you call? (목적어 / 주어)

**Answers:**
1. 목적어
2. 주어
3. 주어
4. 목적어
5. 주어
6. 목적어

### D. 틀린 부분을 고치세요.

1. Does he runs every day?
   → ______________________

2. Where you live?
   → ______________________

3. Who did won the race?
   → ______________________

4. Did you went to school?
   → ______________________

5. What did happened?
   → ______________________

**Answers:**
1. Does he **run** every day?
2. Where **do you** live?
3. Who **won** the race?
4. Did you **go** to school?
5. What **happened**?

## Summary: 의문문 구조 정리

### 의문문 유형별 구조:

```
1. Be동사: Be + 주어 + ...?
   Are you tired?

2. 조동사: 조동사 + 주어 + 동사원형?
   Can you help me?

3. 일반동사: Do/Does/Did + 주어 + 동사원형?
   Do you run?

4. Wh-: 의문사 + 조동사/be + 주어 + ...?
   Where do you live?

5. 주어 의문: 의문사 + 동사 + ...?
   Who came first?

6. 부정 의문: Don't/Doesn't/Didn't + 주어 + ...?
   Don't you feel tired?
```

### 핵심 규칙:

| 규칙 | 예시 |
|------|------|
| Do/Does/Did 뒤는 동사원형 | Does he **run**? (runs X) |
| 주어 질문은 do 불필요 | Who **won**? (did win X) |
| Be동사/조동사가 앞으로 | **Are** you tired? |
