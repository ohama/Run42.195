# Unit 19: Present Tenses for the Future

## A. 형태 (Form)

영어에서는 **확정된 미래 일정**이나 **시간표**를 말할 때 현재시제를 사용합니다.

### Present Simple for Timetables (시간표/일정)

버스, 비행기, 영화, 학교 수업 등 **정해진 일정**은 현재시제(Present Simple)로 표현합니다.

| Subject | Verb | Time | Example |
|---------|------|------|---------|
| The bus | comes | at 7:30 | The bus comes at 7:30. |
| School | starts | at 8:30 | School starts at 8:30. |
| The race | begins | at 8 a.m. | The race begins at 8 a.m. |
| The movie | ends | at 10 | The movie ends at 10. |

### Present Continuous for Arrangements (약속/계획)

**개인적인 약속이나 계획**은 현재진행형(Present Continuous)으로 표현합니다.

| Subject | Be + -ing | Time | Example |
|---------|-----------|------|---------|
| I | am running | tomorrow | I'm running tomorrow. |
| We | are meeting | on Saturday | We're meeting on Saturday. |
| She | is coming | this weekend | She's coming this weekend. |

## B. 시간표/일정을 위한 현재시제 (Present Simple for Timetables)

### From the Original Book:

> "The train **leaves** Plymouth at 11:30 and **arrives** in London at 14:45."
> 기차는 11시 30분에 플리머스를 떠나 14시 45분에 런던에 도착한다.

> "What time **does** the film **start**?"
> 영화 몇 시에 시작해?

> "Tomorrow **is** Wednesday."
> 내일은 수요일이야.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The bus comes at 7:30."**
> 버스는 7시 30분에 온다.

- 매일 정해진 버스 시간표 - Present Simple 사용

> **"School starts at 8:30."**
> 학교는 8시 30분에 시작한다.

- 고정된 학교 일정 - Present Simple 사용

> **"The next bus isn't until 8:05."**
> 다음 버스는 8시 5분까지 없다.

- 미래의 버스이지만 시간표이므로 - Present Simple 사용

### Commonly Used with:
- **Transportation**: arrive, leave, depart, get in
- **Events**: start, begin, end, finish, open, close
- **Time words**: tomorrow, next week, on Monday

## C. 약속/계획을 위한 현재진행형 (Present Continuous for Arrangements)

**이미 정해진 개인적인 약속이나 계획**은 현재진행형을 사용합니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "I**'m playing** tennis with Julia tomorrow."
> 내일 줄리아랑 테니스 쳐.

> "What **are** you **doing** on Saturday evening?"
> 토요일 저녁에 뭐 해?

> "**Are** you **meeting** David tonight?"
> 오늘 밤 데이비드 만나?

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Saturday, Minseo and I are running together."**
> 토요일에 민서랑 같이 달려.

- 개인적인 약속/계획 - Present Continuous 사용

> **"My family is coming to cheer me on."**
> 가족들이 응원하러 올 거야.

- 약속된 계획 - Present Continuous 사용

### Key Point:
Present Continuous for future는 **이미 정해진 구체적인 계획**에 사용합니다.
- 언제, 어디서, 누구와 하는지 대략 정해져 있음
- 달력에 표시할 수 있는 약속

## D. 시간 표현과 함께 사용 (Time Expressions)

### Time Expressions Table:

| Expression | Present Simple (시간표) | Present Continuous (약속) |
|------------|------------------------|--------------------------|
| tomorrow | The race **starts** tomorrow. | I**'m running** tomorrow. |
| next week | School **begins** next week. | I**'m meeting** him next week. |
| on Saturday | The bus **leaves** at 7 on Saturday. | We**'re running** on Saturday. |
| this weekend | The store **opens** at 9 this weekend. | I**'m training** this weekend. |
| at 3 o'clock | The movie **starts** at 3. | I**'m seeing** him at 3. |

---

## E. 현재시제를 미래에 쓰지 않는 경우 (When NOT to use)

다음 상황에서는 현재시제를 쓰지 않습니다:

| Situation | Use | Example |
|-----------|-----|---------|
| Predictions (예측) | will | I think it **will** rain. |
| Decisions made now (즉석 결정) | will | I**'ll** take this one. |
| Intentions (의도) | be going to | I**'m going to** train harder. |
| Offers (제안) | will | I**'ll** help you. |

### Wrong vs Correct:

| Wrong | Correct | Reason |
|-------|---------|--------|
| ~~I think it rains tomorrow.~~ | I think it **will rain** tomorrow. | 예측 = will |
| ~~I take this one.~~ (즉석 결정) | I**'ll take** this one. | 즉석 결정 = will |

## F. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~The bus will come at 7:30.~~ | The bus **comes** at 7:30. | 시간표는 현재시제 |
| ~~What time will the movie start?~~ | What time **does** the movie **start**? | 시간표는 현재시제 |
| ~~I run tomorrow.~~ (약속) | I**'m running** tomorrow. | 개인 약속은 진행형 |
| ~~I'm meeting him tomorrow.~~ (시간표) | The meeting **is** tomorrow. | 시간표는 현재시제 |
| ~~The bus is coming at 7:30 every day.~~ | The bus **comes** at 7:30 every day. | 습관/시간표는 현재시제 |
| ~~School will start at 8:30.~~ | School **starts** at 8:30. | 고정된 일정 = 현재시제 |

### 헷갈리기 쉬운 상황:

| 상황 | 사용 시제 | 이유 |
|------|-----------|------|
| 버스 시간표 | Present Simple | 외부에서 정한 일정 |
| 친구와의 약속 | Present Continuous | 개인적으로 정한 계획 |
| 영화 상영 시간 | Present Simple | 극장에서 정한 시간표 |
| 주말 계획 | Present Continuous | 내가 정한 계획 |

## Exercises

### A. 빈칸 채우기 (Fill in the blanks)

Put the verb into the correct form (Present Simple or Present Continuous).

1. The race _______ (start) at 8 a.m. tomorrow.
2. I _______ (run) with Minseo this Saturday.
3. What time _______ the bus _______ (come)?
4. We _______ (meet) at the park at 7 p.m.
5. School _______ (end) at 3:30 every day.
6. The train _______ (leave) at 9:15 tomorrow morning.
7. _______ you _______ (do) anything special this weekend?
8. The movie _______ (begin) at 7:30.

**Answers:**
1. starts
2. am running
3. does ... come
4. are meeting
5. ends
6. leaves
7. Are ... doing
8. begins

### B. 문장 변환 (Sentence Transformation)

Change the sentences as indicated.

1. The bus arrives at 8:05. (의문문으로)
   → **What time does the bus arrive?**

2. I will run tomorrow. (현재진행형으로 - 약속된 계획)
   → **I'm running tomorrow.**

3. The race will start at 8 a.m. (현재시제로 - 정해진 일정)
   → **The race starts at 8 a.m.**

4. We will meet at the park. (현재진행형으로 - 약속)
   → **We're meeting at the park.**

5. The movie begins at 9. (부정문으로)
   → **The movie doesn't begin at 9.**

### C. 올바른 형태 고르기 (Choose the correct form)

1. "What time (does the train leave / is the train leaving)?" - 시간표
   → **does the train leave**

2. "I (meet / 'm meeting) Sarah for lunch tomorrow." - 약속
   → **'m meeting**

3. "The concert (starts / is starting) at 8 p.m." - 공연 일정
   → **starts**

4. "We (fly / 're flying) to Jeju next week." - 여행 계획
   → **'re flying**

5. "(Do you work / Are you working) tomorrow?" - 개인 일정
   → **Are you working**

### D. 틀린 부분 고치기 (Error Correction)

Correct the mistakes in the following sentences.

1. The bus will come at 7:30 every morning.
   → The bus **comes** at 7:30 every morning.

2. I run with my friend tomorrow. (약속)
   → I**'m running** with my friend tomorrow.

3. What time is the movie starting?
   → What time **does** the movie **start**?

4. School is beginning next Monday.
   → School **begins** next Monday.

5. The train will leave at 9:15.
   → The train **leaves** at 9:15.

## Present Simple vs Present Continuous for Future

| Present Simple | Present Continuous |
|----------------|-------------------|
| 시간표, 일정, 프로그램 | 개인적 약속, 계획 |
| 외부에서 정한 것 | 내가 정한 것 |
| The train **leaves** at 9. | I**'m meeting** him at 9. |
| The movie **starts** at 7. | We**'re having** dinner at 7. |
| School **begins** next week. | I**'m starting** training next week. |
