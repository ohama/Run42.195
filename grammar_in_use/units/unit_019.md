# Unit 19: Present Tenses for the Future

## A. 문법 설명 (Grammar Explanation)

영어에서는 **확정된 미래 일정**이나 **시간표**를 말할 때 현재시제를 사용합니다.

### Present Simple for Timetables (시간표/일정)
버스, 비행기, 영화, 학교 수업 등 **정해진 일정**은 현재시제(Present Simple)로 표현합니다.

### Present Continuous for Arrangements (약속/계획)
**개인적인 약속이나 계획**은 현재진행형(Present Continuous)으로 표현합니다.

---

## B. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: Present Simple for Timetables

> **"The bus comes at 7:30."**
> 버스는 7시 30분에 온다.

- 매일 정해진 버스 시간표 - Present Simple 사용

### Example 2: Present Simple for Schedules

> **"School starts at 8:30."**
> 학교는 8시 30분에 시작한다.

- 고정된 학교 일정 - Present Simple 사용

### Example 3: Present Simple for Future Schedule

> **"The next bus isn't until 8:05."**
> 다음 버스는 8시 5분까지 없다.

- 미래의 버스이지만 시간표이므로 - Present Simple 사용

### Example 4: Present Continuous for Personal Plans

> **"Saturday, Minseo and I are running together."**
> 토요일에 민서랑 같이 달려.

- 개인적인 약속/계획 - Present Continuous 사용

### Example 5: Present Continuous for Arrangements

> **"My family is coming to cheer me on."**
> 가족들이 응원하러 올 거야.

- 약속된 계획 - Present Continuous 사용

---

## C. 추가 설명 (Additional Points)

### Time Expressions (시간 표현)

미래를 나타내는 시간 표현과 함께 현재시제를 사용:

| Expression | Example |
|------------|---------|
| tomorrow | The race **starts** at 8 a.m. tomorrow. |
| next week | School **begins** next week. |
| on Saturday | We**'re meeting** at the park on Saturday. |
| this weekend | I**'m running** 10km this weekend. |

### When NOT to use Present for Future

다음 상황에서는 현재시제를 쓰지 않습니다:
- Predictions (예측): **will** 사용
- Decisions made now (즉석 결정): **will** 사용
- Intentions (의도): **be going to** 사용

## D. 비교 (Comparison)

| Situation | Tense | Example |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| 시간표/일정 (timetable) | Present Simple | The bus **comes** at 7:30. |
| 개인 약속/계획 (arrangement) | Present Continuous | I**'m running** tomorrow. |
| 확정된 미래 (fixed future) | Present Simple | School **starts** at 8:30. |
| 준비된 계획 (arranged plan) | Present Continuous | We**'re meeting** at the park. |

### Wrong vs Correct

| Wrong | Correct |
|-------|---------|
| The bus is coming at 7:30 every day. | The bus **comes** at 7:30 every day. |
| I run to school tomorrow. | I**'m running** to school tomorrow. |

## Exercises

### A. 빈칸 채우기

1. The race _______ (start) at 8 a.m. tomorrow.
2. I _______ (run) with Minseo this Saturday.
3. What time _______ the bus _______ (come)?
4. We _______ (meet) at the park at 7 p.m.
5. School _______ (end) at 3:30 every day.

**Answers:**
1. starts
2. am running
3. does ... come
4. are meeting
5. ends

### B. 문장 변환

1. The bus arrives at 8:05. (의문문으로)
   → **What time does the bus arrive?**

2. I will run tomorrow. (현재진행형으로 - 약속된 계획)
   → **I'm running tomorrow.**

3. The race will start at 8 a.m. (현재시제로 - 정해진 일정)
   → **The race starts at 8 a.m.**
