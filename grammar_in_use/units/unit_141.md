# Unit 141: Phrasal Verbs (5) - on/off (2)

## A. 개념 설명 (Grammar Explanation)

### English
This unit focuses on phrasal verbs with **off**. **Off** generally means "away," "disconnected," "stopped," or "removed." It is often the opposite of **on**.

### Korean
이 유닛에서는 **off**가 포함된 구동사를 다룹니다. **off**는 일반적으로 "떨어져," "분리," "멈춤," "제거"를 의미하며, 종종 **on**의 반대입니다.

---

## B. OFF의 주요 의미 (Main Meanings of OFF)

### 1. 분리/떨어짐 (Separation/Removal)

| Phrasal Verb | Meaning | Separable? |
|--------------|---------|------------|
| take off | 벗다 | Yes |
| get off | 내리다 | No |
| drop off | 내려주다/떨어지다 | Yes |
| cut off | 자르다/차단하다 | Yes |
| fall off | 떨어지다 | No |

> **"When I got off at the school stop, I collapsed onto a bench."**
> 학교 정류장에서 내렸을 때, 나는 벤치에 털썩 주저앉았다.

> **"I took off my shoes."**
> 신발을 벗었다.

### 2. 작동 중지 (Stopping)

| Phrasal Verb | Meaning | Separable? |
|--------------|---------|------------|
| turn off | 끄다 | Yes |
| switch off | 끄다 | Yes |

> **"I turned off the alarm."**
> 알람을 껐다.

### 3. 출발 (Departing)

| Phrasal Verb | Meaning |
|--------------|---------|
| take off | 출발하다/이륙하다 |
| set off | 출발하다 |
| head off | 출발하다 |

> **"I took off at 5:20 a.m."**
> 오전 5시 20분에 출발했다.

> **"The plane took off on time."**
> 비행기가 정시에 이륙했다.

### 4. 갑자기 작동/울림 (Sudden activation)

| Phrasal Verb | Meaning |
|--------------|---------|
| go off | (알람이) 울리다, (폭탄이) 터지다 |

> **"The alarm went off at 6:20."**
> 알람이 6시 20분에 울렸다.

**매우 중요:** go off ≠ 끄다!
| go off | turn off |
|--------|----------|
| 울리다 (자동) | 끄다 (의도적) |
| The alarm **went off**. | I **turned off** the alarm. |
| 알람이 울렸다. | 알람을 껐다. |

### 5. 줄이다 (Reducing)

| Phrasal Verb | Meaning |
|--------------|---------|
| cut back | 줄이다 |

> **"You have to cut back the distance before the race."**
> 대회 전에 거리를 줄여야 해.

## C. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: get off (내리다)

> **"When I got off at the school stop, I collapsed onto a bench."**
> 학교 정류장에서 내렸을 때, 나는 벤치에 털썩 주저앉았다.

> **"Every time I get off the bus, I have to sit and catch my breath first."**
> 버스에서 내릴 때마다, 먼저 앉아서 숨을 골라야 한다.

### Example 2: go off (울리다)

> **"The alarm went off at 6:20."**
> 알람이 6시 20분에 울렸다.

> **"When the alarm went off, I thought: Let's just take the bus today."**
> 알람이 울렸을 때, 나는 생각했다: 오늘은 그냥 버스 타자.

### Example 3: take off (출발하다/벗다)

> **"I drank plenty of water and took off at 5:15."**
> 물을 충분히 마시고 5시 15분에 출발했다.

> **"I took off my running shoes."**
> 러닝화를 벗었다.

### Example 4: cut back (줄이다)

> **"Tapering. You have to cut back the distance before the race."**
> 테이퍼링. 대회 전에 거리를 줄여야 해.

> **"Will cut back even more during race week."**
> 대회 주간에는 더 줄일 거야.

## D. ON vs OFF 비교 (Comparison)

| ON (켜다/시작/착용) | OFF (끄다/중지/제거) |
|--------------------|---------------------|
| put on (입다) | take off (벗다) |
| get on (타다) | get off (내리다) |
| turn on (켜다) | turn off (끄다) |
| switch on (켜다) | switch off (끄다) |
| keep on (계속하다) | - |
| - | go off (울리다) |
| - | cut back (줄이다) |

### Example pairs:

| ON | OFF |
|----|-----|
| I **got on** the bus. | I **got off** the bus. |
| I **put on** my shoes. | I **took off** my shoes. |
| I **turned on** the TV. | I **turned off** the TV. |

## E. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~The alarm went on.~~ (울림) | The alarm **went off**. | 울리다 = go off |
| ~~I got the bus off.~~ | I **got off the bus**. | get off는 분리 불가 |
| ~~I took it off.~~ (벗다) | ✓ (Correct!) | take off는 분리 가능 |
| ~~I turned off it.~~ | I **turned it off**. | 대명사는 중간에 |
| ~~cut down~~ (줄이다, 거리) | **cut back** | 거리/양 줄이기 = cut back |

### go off vs turn off 혼동 방지:

| Situation | Use |
|-----------|-----|
| 알람이 (자동으로) 울렸다 | The alarm **went off**. |
| 알람을 (내가) 껐다 | I **turned off** the alarm. |

### cut back vs cut down vs cut off:

| Phrasal Verb | Meaning | Example |
|--------------|---------|---------|
| cut back | 줄이다 (양/거리) | Cut back the distance. |
| cut down | 줄이다 (나무 베다) | Cut down the tree. / Cut down on sugar. |
| cut off | 자르다/차단하다 | The electricity was cut off. |

## Exercises

### A. on 또는 off 채우기

1. I got _______ at the school stop.
2. I put _______ my uniform.
3. The alarm went _______ at 6:20.
4. I turned _______ the light. (끄다)
5. I took _______ at 5:15. (출발)
6. Keep _______ running!
7. I took _______ my shoes.
8. Cut _______ the distance before the race.

**Answers:**
1. off
2. on
3. off
4. off
5. off
6. on
7. off
8. back

### B. 알맞은 구동사 선택하기

1. 버스에서 내렸다.
   - a) got on  b) got off  c) took off

2. 알람이 울렸다.
   - a) turned off  b) went off  c) cut off

3. 신발을 벗었다.
   - a) put off  b) got off  c) took off

4. 거리를 줄여야 해.
   - a) cut back  b) cut off  c) go off

5. 5시 15분에 출발했다.
   - a) got off  b) went off  c) took off

**Answers:**
1. b) got off
2. b) went off
3. c) took off
4. a) cut back
5. c) took off

---

### C. 반대 구동사 쓰기 (Write the opposite)

1. put on ↔ _______
2. get on ↔ _______
3. turn on ↔ _______
4. switch on ↔ _______

**Answers:**
1. take off
2. get off
3. turn off
4. switch off

### D. 틀린 부분 고치기 (Error Correction)

1. The alarm went on at 6 a.m.
2. I got the bus off.
3. I turned off it.
4. You should cut down the distance.

**Answers:**
1. The alarm **went off** at 6 a.m.
2. I **got off the bus**.
3. I **turned it off**.
4. You should **cut back** the distance.

## ON vs OFF 요약표 (Summary)

| Category | ON | OFF |
|----------|-----|------|
| 착용/제거 | put on (입다) | take off (벗다) |
| 승차/하차 | get on (타다) | get off (내리다) |
| 작동/중지 | turn on (켜다) | turn off (끄다) |
| 계속 | keep on (계속) | - |
| 울림 | - | go off (울리다) |
| 출발 | - | take off, set off |
| 줄이기 | - | cut back |
