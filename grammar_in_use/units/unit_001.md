# Unit 1: Present Continuous (I am doing)

## A. 현재진행형의 형태 (Form)

**am/is/are + -ing**

| Subject | Be | -ing form | Full Example |
|---------|-----|-----------|--------------|
| I | am | running | I am running. |
| He/She/It | is | walking | She is walking. |
| We/You/They | are | riding | They are riding. |

### Negative and Questions:

| Type | Structure | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Positive | am/is/are + -ing | I am writing. |
| Negative | am/is/are + not + -ing | I am not writing. |
| Question | Am/Is/Are + subject + -ing? | Are you writing? |

### Contractions:
- I am → I'm
- He is → He's
- We are → We're
- is not → isn't
- are not → aren't

## B. 지금 진행 중인 동작 (Action happening now)

**"I am doing something" = I started doing it, and I haven't finished yet. I'm in the middle of doing it.**

현재진행형은 **말하는 순간에 진행 중인 동작**을 나타냅니다.

### From the Original Book:
> "Please don't make so much noise. **I'm working.**"
> 시끄럽게 하지 마. 일하는 중이야.

> "Let's go out now. **It isn't raining** anymore."
> 지금 나가자. 비 더 이상 안 와.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I'm writing this diary now that I'm home."**
> 집에 와서 지금 일기를 쓰고 있다.

- "I'm writing" = 지금 이 순간 일기를 쓰는 중

> **"The bus was approaching in the distance."**
> 버스가 멀리서 다가오고 있었다.

- "was approaching" = 그 순간 진행 중이던 동작

> **"Kids in gym clothes were running around."**
> 체육복 입은 애들이 뛰어다니고 있었다.

---

## C. 요즘/이 시기에 진행 중인 일 (Around now, not necessarily at this moment)

정확히 "지금 이 순간"이 아니더라도, **요즘/최근에 진행 중인 상황**을 표현합니다.

### From the Original Book:
> "**I'm reading** an interesting book at the moment."
> 요즘 재미있는 책을 읽고 있어. (지금 이 순간 읽는 게 아니라, 읽는 중인 상태)

> "You can borrow my umbrella. **I'm not using** it right now."
> 내 우산 빌려가. 지금 안 쓰고 있어.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I'm training for a marathon."**
> 마라톤 훈련 중이야.

- 지금 이 순간 달리고 있는 건 아니지만, 요즘 훈련 중

> **"These days I'm walking to school instead of taking the bus."**
> 요즘 버스 대신 걸어서 학교에 가고 있어.

### Use with "today / this week / this year":

| Period | Example |
|--------|---------|
| today | "You're working hard **today**." |
| this week | "I'm training extra hard **this week**." |
| this year | "He's running his first marathon **this year**." |

## D. 변화를 나타낼 때 (Describing changes)

**변화가 시작되어 진행 중인 상황**을 표현할 때 현재진행형을 씁니다.

### From the Original Book:
> "The population of the world **is increasing** very fast."
> 세계 인구가 매우 빠르게 증가하고 있다.

> "Your English **is improving**."
> 네 영어가 늘고 있어.

> "**Is it getting** dark?"
> 어두워지고 있어?

### Common verbs for change:
- get (getting better, getting worse, getting dark)
- become (becoming popular)
- change (changing rapidly)
- increase / decrease
- improve / grow / rise / fall

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The rapeseed flowers are starting to bloom."**
> 유채꽃이 피기 시작하고 있다.

> **"My stamina is getting better."**
> 체력이 좋아지고 있다.

> **"The weather is getting warmer."**
> 날씨가 따뜻해지고 있다.

> **"I'm becoming a runner."**
> 나는 러너가 되어가고 있다.

## E. 현재진행형을 쓰지 않는 동사 (Stative verbs)

일부 동사는 보통 현재진행형을 쓰지 않습니다 (상태를 나타내는 동사들):

### Categories:

| Category | Verbs |
|----------|-------|
| 감정/선호 | like, love, hate, want, need, prefer |
| 인지/사고 | know, understand, believe, remember, realize, suppose, mean |
| 감각 | see, hear, smell, taste, feel |
| 소유 | have, own, belong, possess |
| 기타 | seem, appear, contain, consist of, depend on |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I don't really like running."** (NOT: ~~I'm not really liking~~)
> 나는 달리기를 별로 좋아하지 않는다.

> **"I know that."** (NOT: ~~I'm knowing~~)
> 나도 알아.

> **"I don't know what kind of bird it is."** (NOT: ~~I'm not knowing~~)
> 무슨 새인지는 모르겠다.

> **"I understand how you feel."** (NOT: ~~I'm understanding~~)
> 네 기분 이해해.

### Exceptions: Some verbs can be both!

| Stative (현재시제) | Action (진행형 가능) |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| I **have** a car. (소유) | I'**m having** lunch. (먹는 중) |
| This soup **tastes** good. (맛) | I'**m tasting** the soup. (맛보는 중) |
| I **think** it's good. (의견) | I'**m thinking** about it. (고민 중) |
| I **see** you. (시각) | I'**m seeing** the doctor tomorrow. (만남) |

## F. 형태 주의 (Spelling rules for -ing)

| Rule | Base | -ing form | More Examples |
|------|------|-----------|---------------|
| 대부분 | walk | walk**ing** | work → working, read → reading |
| -e 삭제 | write | writ**ing** | make → making, come → coming |
| 자음 중복 | run | run**n**ing | sit → sitting, stop → stopping |
| -ie → -y | lie | l**y**ing | die → dying, tie → tying |

### Consonant doubling rule details:
단모음 + 단자음으로 끝나는 동사 → 자음 중복
- run → ru**nn**ing (u = 단모음, n = 단자음)
- sit → si**tt**ing
- begin → begi**nn**ing

### From the Novel:
- write → writing: "I'm **writing** this diary"
- walk → walking: "I started **walking** to school"
- run → running: "I kept **running** even when my legs hurt"
- get → getting: "It's **getting** dark"

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) |
|-----------|-------------|
| ~~I'm know~~ | I **know** |
| ~~I'm liking this~~ | I **like** this |
| ~~Where you are going?~~ | Where **are you going**? |
| ~~He working now~~ | He **is working** now |
| ~~I'm agree~~ | I **agree** |
| ~~It's cost too much~~ | It **costs** too much |

## Exercises

### A. 현재진행형으로 문장 완성하기

Put the verb into the correct form.

1. The bus (come) _______. Hurry up!
2. I (write) _______ my diary right now.
3. The flowers (start) _______ to bloom.
4. The students (run) _______ on the field.
5. Listen! Someone (knock) _______ at the door.
6. "Where's Mom?" "She (cook) _______ in the kitchen."
7. Please be quiet. I (try) _______ to concentrate.
8. My English (improve) _______ these days.

**Answers:**
1. is coming
2. am writing
3. are starting
4. are running
5. is knocking
6. is cooking
7. am trying
8. is improving

### B. 현재시제 vs 현재진행형 선택하기

Choose the correct form.

1. I (don't like / am not liking) running in the rain.
2. What (do you do / are you doing) right now?
3. The weather (gets / is getting) colder.
4. He usually (walks / is walking) to school.
5. I (know / am knowing) the answer.
6. Look! The bus (comes / is coming)!
7. Water (boils / is boiling) at 100°C.
8. I (think / am thinking) about joining the marathon.

**Answers:**
1. don't like (상태동사)
2. are you doing (지금 진행 중)
3. is getting (변화)
4. walks (습관)
5. know (상태동사)
6. is coming (지금 진행 중)
7. boils (일반적 사실)
8. am thinking (고민하는 중 - 동작)

### C. 틀린 부분 고치기

Correct the mistakes if there are any.

1. I'm understanding your problem.
   → I **understand** your problem.

2. The world's population is increase.
   → The world's population **is increasing**.

3. Where you are running to?
   → **Where are you running** to?

4. It's get dark. Let's go home.
   → **It's getting** dark. Let's go home.

5. I'm agreeing with you.
   → I **agree** with you.

### D. 상황에 맞게 문장 만들기

Complete the sentences using the present continuous.

1. (the weather / get / warmer)
   → The weather is getting warmer.

2. (my running / improve)
   → My running is improving.

3. (what / you / do / these days?)
   → What are you doing these days?

4. (I / train / for a marathon)
   → I'm training for a marathon.

## Present Continuous vs Present Simple

| Present Continuous | Present Simple |
|-------------------|----------------|
| 지금/요즘 진행 중인 일 | 습관, 반복, 일반적 사실 |
| I **am running** to school. (지금) | I **run** to school every day. (매일) |
| The bus **is coming**. (지금 오는 중) | The bus **comes** at 7:30. (매일) |
| I **am reading** a book. (요즘) | I **read** books. (습관) |
| It **is raining**. (지금) | It **rains** a lot in summer. (일반적) |

## Compare with Korean

영어와 한국어의 진행형 비교:

| English | Korean |
|---------|--------|
| I am writing | 쓰**고 있다** / 쓰**는 중이다** |
| He is running | 달리**고 있다** |
| They are waiting | 기다리**고 있다** |
| It's getting better | 좋아지**고 있다** |

한국어에서는 "~고 있다" 또는 "~는 중이다"로 진행형을 표현합니다.
