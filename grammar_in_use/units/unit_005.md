# Unit 5: Past Simple (I did)

## A. 과거시제의 형태

**규칙 동사:** 동사원형 + -ed
**불규칙 동사:** 각각 다른 형태 (see → saw, go → went, etc.)

| Type | Base | Past |
|------|------|------|
| Regular | walk | walked |
| Regular | start | started |
| Irregular | see | saw |
| Irregular | go | went |
| Irregular | get | got |
| Irregular | take | took |
| Irregular | have | had |

---

## B. 과거의 완료된 동작 (Completed actions in the past)

과거시제는 **과거에 일어나서 끝난 일**을 표현합니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I missed the bus today."**
> 오늘 버스를 놓쳤다.

> **"I jumped out of bed."**
> 침대에서 벌떡 일어났다.

> **"I started walking."**
> 걷기 시작했다.

> **"I slurped down the soup and grabbed my bag."**
> 국을 후루룩 마시고 가방을 집었다.

> **"The teacher looked at me."**
> 선생님이 나를 쳐다봤다.

---

## C. 과거의 연속된 동작 (Sequence of past events)

과거시제는 **연속으로 일어난 일들**을 나열할 때 사용합니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I put on my new uniform and looked in the mirror."**
> 새 교복을 입고 거울을 봤다.

> **"The bus stopped. The doors opened. People poured off."**
> 버스가 멈췄다. 문이 열렸다. 사람들이 쏟아져 나왔다.

> **"I opened the map on my phone."**
> 폰에서 지도를 열었다.

> **"I sat on the bench and looked up at the school building."**
> 벤치에 앉아서 학교 건물을 올려다봤다.

---

## D. 부정문과 의문문 (Negatives and Questions)

### 부정문: did not (didn't) + 동사원형

> **"I didn't say anything."**
> 나는 아무 말도 안 했다.

> **"I don't think they heard."**
> 그들이 들은 것 같지 않다.

> **"I didn't hear my alarm."**
> 알람 소리를 못 들었다.

### 의문문: Did + 주어 + 동사원형?

> **"Did you catch the next one?"**
> 다음 버스 탔어?

> **"Did I see you this morning?"**
> 오늘 아침에 널 봤나?

> **"Miss the bus?"**
> 버스 놓쳤어? (Did you 생략)

---

## E. 불규칙 동사 (Irregular verbs from the novel)

| Base | Past | Example from Novel |
|------|------|-------------------|
| see | saw | "I **saw** the school" |
| get | got | "I **got** off at the school stop" |
| go | went | "Minseo's eyes **went** wide" |
| take | took | "It only **took** an hour" |
| sit | sat | "Seaweed soup **sat** on the table" |
| have | had | "I **had** to squeeze my way through" |
| come | came | "Cool air **came** in" |
| feel | felt | "I **felt** nauseous four times" |
| hear | heard | "I **heard** birds singing" |
| think | thought | "That **thought** made it worse" |
| know | knew | "I never **knew** this was here" |
| stand | stood | "I just **stood** there" |

## F. -ed 발음 규칙

| Ending | Pronunciation | Examples |
|--------|---------------|----------|
| /t/ or /d/ | /ɪd/ | started, waited |
| voiceless | /t/ | walked, stopped, watched |
| voiced | /d/ | opened, arrived, pulled |

### From the Novel:
- start**ed** /ɪd/ - "I started walking"
- walk**ed** /t/ - "I walked all the way"
- open**ed** /d/ - "When I opened my eyes"

## Exercises

### A. 과거형으로 바꾸세요.

1. I (miss) _______ the bus.
2. The bus (stop) _______ at the station.
3. She (look) _______ at me.
4. We (start) _______ walking.
5. He (grab) _______ his bag.

**Answers:**
1. missed
2. stopped
3. looked
4. started
5. grabbed

### B. 불규칙 동사 과거형을 쓰세요.

1. see → _______
2. get → _______
3. go → _______
4. take → _______
5. feel → _______

**Answers:**
1. saw
2. got
3. went
4. took
5. felt

### C. 부정문으로 바꾸세요.

1. I heard the alarm.
   → I **didn't hear** the alarm.

2. She said anything.
   → She **didn't say** anything.
