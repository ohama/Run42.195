# Unit 15: Past Perfect (I had done)

## A. 과거완료의 형태 (Form)

**had + past participle (과거분사)**

| Subject | Had | Past Participle | Full Example |
|---------|-----|-----------------|--------------|
| I/You/He/She/It/We/They | had | done, walked, run | I had finished. |

### Negative and Questions:

| Type | Structure | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Positive | had + p.p. | I had finished. |
| Negative | had + not + p.p. | I hadn't finished. |
| Question | Had + subject + p.p.? | Had you finished? |

### Contractions:
- I had → I'd
- He had → He'd
- had not → hadn't

---

## B. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

**"I had done something" = Something happened BEFORE another past action.**

과거완료는 **과거의 어떤 시점보다 더 이전에** 일어난 일을 표현합니다.
"과거의 과거" - 과거 시점에서 더 이전을 돌아볼 때 사용합니다.

### From the Original Book:
> "When I arrived at the party, Tom **had already gone** home."
> 파티에 도착했을 때, 톰은 이미 집에 갔었다.
> (도착 = 과거, 톰이 간 것 = 더 이전)

---

## C. 시간 순서 이해하기 (Timeline)

```
더 이전 ─────────── 과거 ─────────── 현재
   |                  |                |
 (먼저 일어난 일)    (나중 일어난 일)
   Past Perfect       Past Simple
   had done           did
```

### Example:
```
1. Tom left (먼저)        2. I arrived (나중)         Now
       |                        |                      |
   had left                  arrived
   Past Perfect             Past Simple
```

> "When I **arrived**, Tom **had** already **left**."

---

## D. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: 과거보다 더 이전의 사건

> **"When I got to school, I realized I had forgotten my lunch."**
> 학교에 도착했을 때, 도시락을 잊어버렸다는 걸 깨달았다.

- **got** (Past Simple) = 도착한 과거 시점
- **had forgotten** (Past Perfect) = 그보다 더 이전에 잊어버림

### Example 2: 과거의 경험 언급

> **"I had never walked this long before."**
> 이렇게 오래 걸어본 적이 없었다.

- 걷기 시작하기 **전까지** 그런 경험이 없었음
- **before** = 그 과거 시점 이전에

### Example 3: 연속된 과거 사건

> **"Mom had already left for work when I woke up."**
> 내가 일어났을 때, 엄마는 이미 출근하셨다.

- **woke up** = 일어난 시점 (과거)
- **had left** = 그보다 먼저 출근함 (과거완료)

### Example 4: 과거 상황의 배경

> **"The race started at 8 AM. I had trained for a whole year for this moment."**
> 대회가 8시에 시작했다. 이 순간을 위해 1년 동안 훈련해 왔었다.

- **started** = 대회 시작 (과거)
- **had trained** = 대회 전 1년간의 훈련 (과거완료)

### Example 5: 상태 변화

> **"By the time I finished the race, I had run 42 kilometers."**
> 레이스를 끝냈을 때, 나는 42km를 달렸다.

- **finished** = 끝낸 시점 (과거)
- **had run** = 그 시점까지 달린 거리 (과거완료)

## E. 과거완료 vs 과거시제 비교

### Past Perfect + Past Simple (시간 순서 다름):

> **"When I arrived, the bus had already left."**
> 도착했을 때, 버스는 이미 떠났다.
> (버스가 먼저 떠남 → 내가 도착)

### 두 과거시제 (시간 순서대로 나열):

> **"The bus left, and then I arrived."**
> 버스가 떠났고, 그 다음 내가 도착했다.
> (순서대로 나열)

### Comparison Table:

| Past Perfect + Past | Two Past Simple |
|--------------------|-----------------|
| 시간 순서 강조 (먼저/나중) | 시간 순서대로 나열 |
| When I arrived, the bus had left. | The bus left, then I arrived. |
| 더 이전 일 + 나중 일 | 순서대로 |

---

## F. 과거완료를 자주 쓰는 상황

### 1. Before/After와 함께:

> **"I had never felt this good before."**
> 전에 이렇게 기분 좋은 적 없었다.

> **"After I had finished eating, I left."**
> 다 먹은 후에 떠났다.

### 2. When/By the time과 함께:

> **"When I got there, everyone had already started."**
> 도착했을 때, 모두 이미 시작했었다.

> **"By the time I arrived, the race had begun."**
> 내가 도착했을 때쯤, 대회가 시작했었다.

### 3. Already/Just/Never와 함께:

> **"I had just finished eating when Mom called."**
> 엄마가 전화했을 때 방금 다 먹었다.

> **"I had already decided before he asked."**
> 그가 물어보기 전에 이미 결정했었다.

### 4. Realize/Discover/Notice와 함께:

> **"I realized I had made a mistake."**
> 실수했다는 걸 깨달았다.

> **"I noticed that someone had taken my bag."**
> 누군가 내 가방을 가져갔다는 걸 알아챘다.

---

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~When I arrived, the bus left.~~ | When I arrived, the bus **had** left. | 먼저 일어난 일 = 과거완료 |
| ~~I had went there.~~ | I **had gone** there. | 과거분사 사용 |
| ~~I had never saw it.~~ | I **had never seen** it. | 과거분사 사용 |
| ~~I realized I forget my bag.~~ | I realized I **had forgotten** my bag. | 과거완료 필요 |
| ~~He had ate dinner.~~ | He **had eaten** dinner. | eat-ate-eaten |

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. When I arrived, the race (started / had started) already.
2. I realized I (forgot / had forgotten) my water bottle.
3. By the time I finished, I (ran / had run) 10km.
4. She (trained / had trained) for months before the race.
5. The bus (left / had left) before I got to the stop.
6. I (never saw / had never seen) such beautiful flowers before.
7. When I woke up, Mom (already left / had already left).
8. I (just finished / had just finished) eating when she called.

**Answers:**
1. had started (도착 전에 시작)
2. had forgotten (깨닫기 전에 잊어버림)
3. had run (끝낼 때까지 달린 양)
4. had trained (대회 전에 훈련)
5. had left (도착 전에 떠남)
6. had never seen (그 전까지 경험 없음)
7. had already left
8. had just finished

### B. 문장 연결하기

Connect using Past Perfect:

1. I ate breakfast. Then I left for school.
   → I **had eaten** breakfast before I left for school.

2. The bus left. Then I arrived at the stop.
   → The bus **had left** before I arrived.

3. She finished training. Then the race started.
   → She **had finished** training before the race started.

4. I ran 5km. Then I took a break.
   → I **had run** 5km before I took a break.

5. He went home. Then I arrived at the party.
   → He **had gone** home by the time I arrived.

### C. 빈칸 채우기

1. When I woke up, Mom _______ (already / leave) for work.
2. I _______ (never / run) a marathon before that day.
3. By the time I arrived, the race _______ (start).
4. I realized I _______ (forget) to stretch.
5. She _______ (just / finish) eating when I called.
6. Before I started running, I _______ (never / exercise) regularly.
7. When the teacher asked, I _______ (already / do) my homework.
8. The bus _______ (leave) by the time I got there.

**Answers:**
1. had already left
2. had never run
3. had started
4. had forgotten
5. had just finished
6. had never exercised
7. had already done
8. had left

Find and correct the errors:

   → I **had never seen** the ocean before.

## Summary: 과거완료 사용 공식

| 상황 | 공식 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| ~했을 때, 이미 | When + Past, had + p.p. | When I arrived, it had started. |
| ~하기 전에 | had + p.p. + before + Past | I had finished before he came. |
| ~할 때까지 | By the time + Past, had + p.p. | By the time I finished, I had run 42km. |
| ~한 적 없었다 | had never + p.p. | I had never felt this good. |
| 깨달았다 ~했다는 것을 | realized + had + p.p. | I realized I had forgotten. |
| 방금 ~했었다 | had just + p.p. | I had just arrived when... |
| 이미 ~했었다 | had already + p.p. | She had already left. |
