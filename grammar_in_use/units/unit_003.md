# Unit 3: Present Continuous and Present Simple (1)

## A. 기본 개념 (Basic Concepts)

### Present Continuous (am/is/are + -ing)
**"I am doing something"** = I'm in the middle of doing it; I've started but haven't finished.

### Present Simple (verb / verb-s)
**"I do something"** = I do it regularly, or it's generally true.

| Tense | Use | Signal Words |
|-------|-----|--------------|
| Present Continuous | 지금/요즘 진행 중 | now, at the moment, today, this week |
| Present Simple | 습관, 반복, 일반적 사실 | always, usually, every day, never |

---

## B. 핵심 차이점 (Key Differences)

### From the Original Book:

> "**I'm working** hard today." (오늘은 열심히 일하는 중)
> "**I work** hard every day." (매일 열심히 일함)

> "Look! That man **is wearing** a funny hat." (지금 쓰고 있음)
> "I usually **wear** jeans." (보통 청바지를 입음)

### Comparison Table:

| Present Continuous | Present Simple |
|-------------------|----------------|
| 지금 일어나는 일 | 항상/반복되는 일 |
| I **am writing** now. | I **write** every day. |
| 일시적인 상황 | 영구적인 상황 |
| Today I **am walking** to school. | I usually **take** the bus. |
| 변화 중인 상황 | 변하지 않는 사실 |
| My English **is improving**. | Water **boils** at 100°C. |

## C. 소설 속 예문 비교 (Novel Examples)

### Example 1: 지금 하는 일 vs 습관

**Present Continuous (지금 하는 일):**
> **"I'm writing this diary now that I'm home."**
> 집에 와서 지금 일기를 쓰고 있다.

**Present Simple (습관):**
> **"I write in my diary every night."**
> 나는 매일 밤 일기를 쓴다.

### Example 2: 스케줄 vs 지금 상황

**Present Simple (반복 스케줄):**
> **"The bus comes at 7:30."**
> 버스는 7시 30분에 온다.

**Present Continuous (지금 오는 중):**
> **"Look! The bus is coming!"**
> 봐! 버스가 오고 있어!

### Example 3: 변화 vs 일반적 사실

**Present Continuous (요즘 변화 중):**
> **"The rapeseed flowers are starting to bloom."**
> 유채꽃이 피기 시작하고 있다.

**Present Simple (매일의 일정):**
> **"School starts at 8:30."**
> 학교는 8시 30분에 시작한다.

### Example 4: 일시적 vs 영구적

**Present Continuous (일시적 - 오늘만):**
> **"Today I'm walking to school on purpose."**
> 오늘 일부러 걸어서 학교에 간다.

**Present Simple (영구적 습관):**
> **"I take the bus every day."**
> 나는 매일 버스를 탄다.

### Example 5: 지금 이 순간

**Present Continuous (신체 상태):**
> **"My stomach is churning."**
> 속이 울렁거리고 있다.

## D. 시간 표현에 따른 구분 (Time Expressions)

### Present Continuous와 함께:
| Expression | Korean | Example |
|------------|--------|---------|
| now | 지금 | I'm running now. |
| right now | 바로 지금 | I'm writing right now. |
| at the moment | 이 순간에 | I'm resting at the moment. |
| today | 오늘 | I'm walking to school today. |
| this week | 이번 주 | I'm training hard this week. |
| these days | 요즘 | I'm walking a lot these days. |
| currently | 현재 | I'm currently preparing for a race. |

### Present Simple과 함께:
| Expression | Korean | Example |
|------------|--------|---------|
| every day/week | 매일/매주 | I run every morning. |
| always | 항상 | I always wake up early. |
| usually | 보통 | I usually take the bus. |
| often | 자주 | I often walk to school. |
| sometimes | 가끔 | I sometimes miss the bus. |
| never | 절대 ~안 함 | I never give up. |
| on Mondays | 월요일마다 | I run on Mondays. |

## E. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~I writing now.~~ | I **am writing** now. | be동사 필요 |
| ~~He is run every day.~~ | He **runs** every day. | 습관은 현재시제 |
| ~~I am go to school.~~ | I **go** to school. / I **am going** to school. | 형태 혼합 안 됨 |
| ~~The bus is come now.~~ | The bus **is coming** now. | -ing 형태 필요 |
| ~~I work right now.~~ | I **am working** right now. | 지금=진행형 |
| ~~She is walks to school.~~ | She **walks** to school. | is+walks 불가 |

## F. 특별한 경우: 변화 표현 (Changes)

변화가 진행 중일 때는 **현재진행형**을 씁니다.

### From the Original Book:
> "The population of the world **is increasing** very fast."
> "Your English **is getting** better."
> "**Is** your new job **going** well?"

### From the Novel:
> **"My stamina is getting better."**
> 체력이 좋아지고 있다.

> **"The weather is getting warmer."**
> 날씨가 따뜻해지고 있다.

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. The bus (comes / is coming) at 7:30 every morning.
2. Look! The bus (comes / is coming) now.
3. I (write / am writing) my diary right now.
4. I usually (write / am writing) before bed.
5. The cherry blossoms (bloom / are blooming) this week.
6. Water (boils / is boiling) at 100 degrees Celsius.
7. Listen! Someone (talks / is talking) outside.
8. She always (walks / is walking) to school.

**Answers:**
1. comes (매일 반복)
2. is coming (지금 오는 중)
3. am writing (지금 쓰는 중)
4. write (습관)
5. are blooming (요즘 진행 중)
6. boils (과학적 사실)
7. is talking (지금 말하는 중)
8. walks (항상=습관)

### B. 빈칸 채우기 (Fill in the blanks)

1. Right now, I _______ (walk) to school.
2. School _______ (start) at 8:30 every day.
3. My stomach _______ (churn) - I feel nauseous.
4. The rapeseed flowers _______ (start) to bloom these days.
5. Mom always _______ (make) seaweed soup on my birthday.
6. What _______ you _______ (do) at the moment?
7. He _______ (not/like) running in the rain.
8. The sun _______ (rise) in the east.

**Answers:**
1. am walking
2. starts
3. is churning
4. are starting
5. makes
6. are ... doing
7. doesn't like
8. rises

### C. 문장 변환 (Sentence Transformation)

Change to present continuous (지금 일어나는 일로):
1. I take the bus. → I _______ the bus (right now).
2. She runs every morning. → She _______ (now).
3. The bus comes at 7:30. → Look! The bus _______!

Change to present simple (습관으로):
4. I'm walking to school today. → I _______ every Monday.
5. He is writing a diary. → He _______ a diary every night.
6. They are running now. → They _______ every morning.

**Answers:**
1. am taking
2. is running
3. is coming
4. walk
5. writes
6. run

### D. 틀린 부분 고치기

Find and correct the errors:

1. She is work hard today.
   → She **is working** hard today.

2. I walking to school right now.
   → I **am walking** to school right now.

3. The sun is rise in the east.
   → The sun **rises** in the east.

4. He is usually take the bus.
   → He **usually takes** the bus.

5. Look! It rains!
   → Look! It **is raining**!

## Key Sentences (암기 문장)

| English | Korean | Grammar Point |
|---------|--------|---------------|
| I'm writing this diary now. | 지금 일기를 쓰고 있다. | 지금 하는 일 |
| The bus comes at 7:30. | 버스는 7시 30분에 온다. | 매일의 일정 |
| The flowers are starting to bloom. | 꽃이 피기 시작하고 있다. | 요즘 진행 중 |
| I usually take the bus. | 보통 버스를 탄다. | 습관 |
| Today I'm walking to school. | 오늘은 걸어서 학교에 간다. | 오늘의 계획 |
| My English is improving. | 내 영어가 늘고 있다. | 변화 |
| Water boils at 100°C. | 물은 100도에서 끓는다. | 과학적 사실 |
| Look! The bus is coming! | 봐! 버스가 오고 있어! | 지금 일어나는 일 |

## 한국어와 비교 (Compare with Korean)

한국어에서는 현재시제와 현재진행형의 구분이 덜 명확합니다.

| English | Korean |
|---------|--------|
| I write (습관) | 나는 쓴다 |
| I am writing (지금) | 나는 쓰고 있다 / 나는 쓴다 |

**영어에서는 "지금 하는 일"과 "습관적으로 하는 일"을 반드시 구분해야 합니다!**

## Summary Comparison Table

| Aspect | Present Simple | Present Continuous |
|--------|---------------|-------------------|
| Use | Habits, facts, routines | Actions happening now |
| Time words | always, usually, every day | now, at the moment, today |
| Form | verb / verb-s | am/is/are + -ing |
| Example | I walk to school. | I'm walking to school. |
| Focus | 반복, 일반적 | 진행, 임시적 |
