# Unit 62: Verb + preposition + -ing

## A. 동사 + 전치사 + -ing

특정 동사는 전치사와 함께 쓰이며, 전치사 뒤에는 **-ing**가 옵니다.

**주요 패턴:**
- think about / dream about / talk about + -ing
- apologize for / thank someone for + -ing
- succeed in / believe in + -ing
- insist on / depend on + -ing

---

## B. think about / dream about + -ing

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I'm thinking about running a marathon."**
> 마라톤을 뛰는 것에 대해 생각하고 있어.

- **think about + -ing**: ~에 대해 생각하다

> **"I never dreamed about finishing a marathon."**
> 마라톤을 완주하는 건 꿈에도 생각 못했다.

- **dream about + -ing**: ~에 대해 꿈꾸다

> **"Have you thought about joining the track team?"**
> 육상부에 들어가는 것에 대해 생각해 봤어?

---

## C. thank someone for / apologize for + -ing

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Thank you for coming to the race."**
> 레이스에 와줘서 고마워요.

- **thank someone for + -ing**: ~해줘서 고맙다

> **"Thank you for cheering me on."**
> 응원해줘서 고마워요.

> **"I apologize for being late."**
> 늦어서 죄송합니다.

- **apologize for + -ing**: ~해서 사과하다

---

## D. succeed in / believe in + -ing

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I succeeded in finishing the marathon."**
> 마라톤을 완주하는 데 성공했다.

- **succeed in + -ing**: ~하는 데 성공하다

> **"I believe in training hard."**
> 열심히 훈련하는 것을 믿는다.

- **believe in + -ing**: ~하는 것을 믿다

---

## E. insist on / depend on + -ing

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Mom insisted on driving me to the race."**
> 엄마가 레이스까지 차로 데려다주겠다고 고집했다.

- **insist on + -ing**: ~하겠다고 고집하다

> **"Success depends on training consistently."**
> 성공은 꾸준히 훈련하는 것에 달려 있다.

- **depend on + -ing**: ~에 달려 있다

---

## F. 주요 동사 + 전치사 + -ing 표현

| Verb + Preposition | Meaning | Example |
|-------------------|---------|---------|
| think about | ~에 대해 생각하다 | I'm thinking about running. |
| dream about | ~을 꿈꾸다 | I dreamed about finishing. |
| talk about | ~에 대해 말하다 | We talked about training. |
| apologize for | ~에 대해 사과하다 | Sorry for being late. |
| thank for | ~에 감사하다 | Thanks for coming. |
| succeed in | ~에 성공하다 | I succeeded in finishing. |
| believe in | ~을 믿다 | I believe in hard work. |
| insist on | ~을 고집하다 | He insisted on helping. |
| depend on | ~에 달려있다 | It depends on practicing. |
| prevent from | ~을 막다 | Nothing prevented me from running. |

---

## G. stop from / prevent from + -ing

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Nothing could stop me from running."**
> 아무것도 나를 달리는 것에서 멈추게 할 수 없었다.

- **stop from + -ing**: ~하는 것을 막다

> **"The rain didn't prevent me from training."**
> 비가 와도 훈련하는 걸 막지 못했다.

- **prevent from + -ing**: ~하는 것을 막다

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 전치사를 고르세요.

1. I'm thinking (about / of / for) running a marathon.
2. Thank you (about / of / for) coming to the race.
3. I succeeded (about / in / for) finishing the marathon.
4. He apologized (about / in / for) being late.
5. She insisted (about / on / for) helping me train.

**Answers:**
1. about
2. for
3. in
4. for
5. on

### B. 문장을 완성하세요.

1. I dreamed _______ (finish) a marathon.
2. Thank you _______ (cheer) me on.
3. He succeeded _______ (break) his record.
4. She apologized _______ (miss) the race.
5. They talked _______ (train) together.

**Answers:**
1. about finishing
2. for cheering
3. in breaking
4. for missing
5. about training

### C. 문장을 만드세요.

1. think / about / I'm / running / a marathon
   → _______________________________

2. thank / for / you / coming / to the race
   → _______________________________

3. succeeded / I / in / the marathon / finishing
   → _______________________________

**Answers:**
1. I'm thinking about running a marathon.
2. Thank you for coming to the race.
3. I succeeded in finishing the marathon.
