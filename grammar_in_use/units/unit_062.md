# Unit 62: Verb + preposition + -ing

## A. 동사 + 전치사 + -ing (Form)

특정 동사는 전치사와 함께 쓰이며, 전치사 뒤에는 **-ing**가 옵니다.

| Verb + Preposition | Meaning | Structure |
|-------------------|---------|-----------|
| think about | ~에 대해 생각하다 | think about + -ing |
| dream about | ~을 꿈꾸다 | dream about + -ing |
| talk about | ~에 대해 말하다 | talk about + -ing |
| apologize for | ~에 대해 사과하다 | apologize for + -ing |
| thank for | ~에 감사하다 | thank for + -ing |

### From the Original Book:

> "I'm **thinking about buying** a new car."
> 새 차를 사는 것에 대해 생각 중이야.

> "She **apologized for being** late."
> 그녀는 늦은 것에 대해 사과했어.

---

## B. think about / dream about / talk about + -ing (Primary Usage)

### From the Original Book:

> "Are you **thinking about leaving** your job?"
> 직장을 그만두는 것에 대해 생각 중이야?

> "I **dreamed about flying** last night."
> 어젯밤 나는 꿈에 날았어.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I'm thinking about running a marathon."**
> 마라톤을 뛰는 것에 대해 생각하고 있어.

- **think about + -ing**: ~에 대해 생각하다

> **"I never dreamed about finishing a marathon."**
> 마라톤을 완주하는 건 꿈에도 생각 못했다.

- **dream about + -ing**: ~에 대해 꿈꾸다

> **"Have you thought about joining the track team?"**
> 육상부에 들어가는 것에 대해 생각해 봤어?

### Related expressions:

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| think about | ~을 고려하다 | thinking about running |
| dream about | ~을 꿈꾸다 | dream about winning |
| talk about | ~에 대해 말하다 | talked about training |
| worry about | ~을 걱정하다 | worried about failing |
| complain about | ~에 대해 불평하다 | complained about waiting |

---

## C. thank someone for / apologize for + -ing (Gratitude & Apology)

### From the Original Book:

> "**Thank you for helping** me."
> 도와줘서 고마워.

> "He **apologized for keeping** me waiting."
> 그가 기다리게 해서 미안하다고 했어.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Thank you for coming to the race."**
> 레이스에 와줘서 고마워요.

- **thank someone for + -ing**: ~해줘서 고맙다

> **"Thank you for cheering me on."**
> 응원해줘서 고마워요.

> **"I apologize for being late."**
> 늦어서 죄송합니다.

- **apologize for + -ing**: ~해서 사과하다

### 감사/사과 표현:

| Expression | Formality | Example |
|------------|-----------|---------|
| Thanks for | 비격식 | Thanks for coming. |
| Thank you for | 표준 | Thank you for helping me. |
| I apologize for | 격식 | I apologize for being late. |
| Sorry for | 비격식 | Sorry for keeping you waiting. |

---

## D. succeed in / believe in + -ing (Achievement & Belief)

### From the Original Book:

> "She **succeeded in passing** the exam."
> 그녀는 시험에 합격하는 데 성공했어.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I succeeded in finishing the marathon."**
> 마라톤을 완주하는 데 성공했다.

- **succeed in + -ing**: ~하는 데 성공하다

> **"I believe in training hard."**
> 열심히 훈련하는 것을 믿는다.

- **believe in + -ing**: ~하는 것을 믿다

### Related expressions with 'in':

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| succeed in | ~에 성공하다 | succeeded in finishing |
| believe in | ~을 믿다 | believe in working hard |
| take part in | ~에 참여하다 | took part in running |
| participate in | ~에 참가하다 | participated in racing |

---

## E. insist on / depend on + -ing (Determination & Reliance)

### From the Original Book:

> "He **insisted on paying** for dinner."
> 그가 저녁값을 내겠다고 고집했어.

> "It **depends on what** you want." (명사절)
> 네가 뭘 원하느냐에 달려 있어.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Mom insisted on driving me to the race."**
> 엄마가 레이스까지 차로 데려다주겠다고 고집했다.

- **insist on + -ing**: ~하겠다고 고집하다

> **"Success depends on training consistently."**
> 성공은 꾸준히 훈련하는 것에 달려 있다.

- **depend on + -ing**: ~에 달려 있다

### Related expressions with 'on':

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| insist on | ~을 고집하다 | insisted on helping |
| depend on | ~에 달려있다 | depends on practicing |
| concentrate on | ~에 집중하다 | concentrate on running |
| count on | ~에 의지하다 | count on winning |
| rely on | ~에 의존하다 | rely on training |

---

## F. stop from / prevent from + -ing (Prevention)

### From the Original Book:

> "The bad weather **prevented us from going** out."
> 안 좋은 날씨가 우리가 나가는 것을 막았어.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Nothing could stop me from running."**
> 아무것도 나를 달리는 것에서 멈추게 할 수 없었다.

- **stop from + -ing**: ~하는 것을 막다

> **"The rain didn't prevent me from training."**
> 비가 와도 훈련하는 걸 막지 못했다.

- **prevent from + -ing**: ~하는 것을 막다

### Prevention expressions:

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| stop from | ~하는 것을 막다 | stop me from running |
| prevent from | ~하는 것을 막다 | prevent me from training |
| keep from | ~하지 못하게 하다 | keep me from giving up |

## H. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~I'm thinking about **to run**.~~ | I'm thinking about **running**. | about + -ing |
| ~~Thank you for **to come**.~~ | Thank you for **coming**. | for + -ing |
| ~~He succeeded in **to finish**.~~ | He succeeded in **finishing**. | in + -ing |
| ~~I apologized for **be** late.~~ | I apologized for **being** late. | for + -ing |
| ~~She insisted on **to help**.~~ | She insisted on **helping**. | on + -ing |
| ~~They prevented me from **to go**.~~ | They prevented me from **going**. | from + -ing |

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 전치사를 고르세요.

1. I'm thinking (about / of / for) running a marathon.
2. Thank you (about / of / for) coming to the race.
3. I succeeded (about / in / for) finishing the marathon.
4. He apologized (about / in / for) being late.
5. She insisted (about / on / for) helping me train.
6. Nothing prevented me (about / on / from) running.
7. I'm worried (about / in / for) failing the race.
8. She blamed me (about / on / for) losing.

**Answers:**
1. about
2. for
3. in
4. for
5. on
6. from
7. about
8. for

2. Thank you _______ (cheer) me on.

5. They talked _______ (train) together.
6. I insisted _______ (pay) for dinner.
7. He prevented me _______ (give) up.
8. I'm thinking _______ (join) the team.

**Answers:**
1. about finishing
2. for cheering
3. in breaking
4. for missing
5. about training
6. on paying
7. from giving
8. about joining

## Summary: 전치사별 동사 정리

### about (생각, 감정 관련)
- think about, dream about, talk about, worry about, complain about

### for (이유, 감사, 사과)
- thank for, apologize for, forgive for, blame for

### in (성취, 참여)
- succeed in, believe in, take part in, participate in

### on (고집, 의존)
- insist on, depend on, concentrate on, count on, rely on

### from (방지, 막음)
- prevent from, stop from, keep from

### of (비난, 고발)
- accuse of, suspect of, convict of
