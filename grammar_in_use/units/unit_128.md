# Unit 128: By

## A. By + 행위자 (수동태에서) - Form/Structure

**By**는 수동태에서 **행위자(동작을 하는 사람/것)**를 나타냅니다.

### Structure:
```
Subject + be + past participle + by + agent
```

| Active | Passive with BY |
|--------|-----------------|
| My coach made the plan. | The plan was made **by my coach**. |
| Grandpa encouraged me. | I was encouraged **by Grandpa**. |
| A professional won the race. | The race was won **by a professional**. |

### From the Original Book:
> "This house was built **by my grandfather**."
> 이 집은 할아버지가 지으셨다.

> "The letter was written **by a friend of mine**."
> 그 편지는 내 친구가 썼다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The plan was made by my coach."**
> 계획은 코치님이 세웠다.

> **"I was encouraged by Grandpa Yeongcheol."**
> 영철 할아버지에게 격려받았다.

> **"The race was won by a professional runner."**
> 경기는 프로 선수가 우승했다.

---

## B. By + 교통수단/방법 - Transportation and Means

**By**는 "~로, ~에 의해"의 의미로 **교통수단이나 방법**을 나타냅니다.

### 주의: 관사 없이 씁니다!
- by bus (O) / by the bus (X)
- by car (O) / by a car (X)
- by hand (O) / by the hand (X)

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| 교통수단 | by bus, by car, by train, by plane, by bike, by taxi |
| 통신수단 | by phone, by email, by text, by fax |
| 방법 | by hand, by mistake, by chance, by credit card |

### From the Original Book:
> "Do you usually go to work **by car** or **by bus**?"
> 보통 차로 출근하세요, 버스로 출근하세요?

> "You can reserve your tickets **by phone**."
> 전화로 예약할 수 있습니다.

### BUT: on foot (걸어서)
- I go to school **on foot**. (NOT: ~~by foot~~)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I usually go to school by bus."**
> 보통 버스로 학교에 간다.

> **"Forty minutes by bus from home to school."**
> 집에서 학교까지 버스로 40분.

> **"I contacted my coach by phone."**
> 코치님께 전화로 연락했다.

## C. By + 시간 (기한) - Deadline

**By**는 "~까지 (그 전에)"의 의미로 **기한/마감**을 나타냅니다.
= no later than (늦어도 ~까지)

### By vs Until 비교:

| By (~까지) | Until (~까지 계속) |
|-----------|-------------------|
| 기한/마감 (동작 완료 시점) | 지속 (동작이 계속됨) |
| I need to finish **by** 5. | I'll work **until** 5. |
| 5시까지 끝내야 해. (마감) | 5시까지 일할 거야. (계속) |

### From the Original Book:
> "I'll be back **by** 10:30."
> 10시 30분까지 돌아올게. (늦어도)

> "**By** the time I got to the party, everyone had gone home."
> 내가 파티에 도착했을 때, 모두 집에 가버렸다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I need to arrive by 8 o'clock."**
> 8시까지 도착해야 한다.

> **"By the time I finished, I was exhausted."**
> 끝났을 때, 나는 지쳐 있었다.

> **"By March, I had been running for a year."**
> 3월까지, 1년 동안 달리기를 해왔다.

---

## D. By + 장소 (옆에) - Position

**By**는 "~ 옆에, ~ 곁에"의 의미로 **위치**를 나타냅니다.
= beside, next to, near

### From the Original Book:
> "Come and sit **by** me."
> 와서 내 옆에 앉아.

> "Our house is **by** the sea."
> 우리 집은 바다 옆에 있다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I sat by the window."**
> 창가에 앉았다.

> **"Mom sat by my bed."**
> 엄마가 내 침대 옆에 앉았다.

> **"I passed by the tangerine orchard."**
> 감귤 과수원 옆을 지나갔다.

---

## E. By + 동명사 (방법) - Method with -ing

**By + -ing**는 "~함으로써"의 의미로 **방법/수단**을 나타냅니다.

### Structure:
```
Verb + by + verb-ing
```

### From the Original Book:
> "You can improve your English **by reading** more."
> 더 많이 읽음으로써 영어를 향상시킬 수 있어.

> "She made money **by selling** things online."
> 그녀는 온라인에서 물건을 팔아서 돈을 벌었다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I improved by running every day."**
> 매일 달림으로써 나아졌다.

> **"You can succeed by not giving up."**
> 포기하지 않음으로써 성공할 수 있다.

> **"I avoided motion sickness by walking."**
> 걸음으로써 멀미를 피했다.

---

## F. By oneself (혼자서) - Alone

**By oneself**는 "혼자서, 스스로"의 의미입니다.
= alone, on one's own

| Person | By + reflexive |
|--------|---------------|
| I | by myself |
| You | by yourself |
| He | by himself |
| She | by herself |
| We | by ourselves |
| They | by themselves |

### From the Original Book:
> "Did you do this **by yourself**?"
> 이거 혼자 한 거야?

> "He lives **by himself**."
> 그는 혼자 산다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I trained by myself during summer."**
> 여름 동안 혼자 훈련했다.

> **"I ran the first marathon by myself."**
> 첫 마라톤은 혼자 뛰었다.

---

## G. 관용 표현 (Idiomatic Expressions with BY)

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| by the way | 그런데 | **By the way**, are you coming tomorrow? |
| by far | 단연, 훨씬 | This is **by far** the best. |
| by accident | 우연히 | I met her **by accident**. |
| by chance | 우연히 | I found it **by chance**. |
| by mistake | 실수로 | I took it **by mistake**. |
| step by step | 단계별로 | Learn **step by step**. |
| little by little | 조금씩 | I improved **little by little**. |
| day by day | 하루하루 | Things got better **day by day**. |
| one by one | 하나씩 | They entered **one by one**. |

### From the Novel:

> **"Little by little, I got stronger."**
> 조금씩 더 강해졌다.

> **"Step by step, I increased my distance."**
> 단계별로 거리를 늘렸다.

## H. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~by the bus~~ | **by bus** | 교통수단 앞에 관사 없음 |
| ~~by foot~~ | **on foot** | 걸어서는 on foot |
| ~~by himself~~ (여자일 때) | **by herself** | 성별에 맞게 |
| ~~I'll finish until 5~~ | **I'll finish by 5** | 마감은 by, 지속은 until |
| ~~by chance meeting~~ | **by chance** | 명사 앞에는 by 안 씀 |
| ~~The book written by.~~ | **The book was written by him.** | by 뒤에 행위자 필요 |

### More Common Errors:

> ~~I go to school by a bus.~~
> ✓ I go to school **by bus**. (관사 없이)

> ~~I sent it by the email.~~
> ✓ I sent it **by email**. (관사 없이)

> ~~The report must be finished until Friday.~~
> ✓ The report must be finished **by Friday**. (마감)

## Exercises

### A. By의 용법 구분하기 (Identify the usage)

각 문장에서 by의 의미를 구분하세요: 행위자/교통수단/기한/위치/방법/혼자서

1. I go to school by bus. → _______
2. I need to arrive by 8. → _______
3. I sat by the window. → _______
4. The book was written by her. → _______
5. I improved by practicing daily. → _______
6. I did it by myself. → _______

**Answers:**
1. 교통수단
2. 기한
3. 위치 (옆에)
4. 행위자
5. 방법 (~함으로써)
6. 혼자서

3. I'll wait _______ you come back.
4. She sat _______ the fire.
5. I go to school _______ foot.
6. I improved _______ practicing every day.
7. I trained _______ myself.
8. He worked _______ midnight. (계속)

**Answers:**
1. by
2. by
3. until
4. by

7. by

### C. 틀린 부분 고치기 (Error Correction)

다음 문장에서 틀린 부분을 고치세요.

1. I go to school by the bus.
2. I'll finish this report until tomorrow.
3. She went there by foot.
4. The window was broken by.
5. I learned it step to step.

**Answers:**
1. I go to school **by bus**. (관사 제거)
2. I'll finish this report **by tomorrow**. (마감은 by)
3. She went there **on foot**. (걸어서)
4. The window was broken **by someone**. (행위자 필요)
5. I learned it **step by step**. (to → by)

## By의 다양한 용법 요약표 (Summary)

| By + | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| 행위자 | ~에 의해 (수동태) | made by my coach |
| 교통수단 | ~로 (관사 없이) | by bus, by train |
| 시간 | ~까지 (마감) | by 8 o'clock |
| 장소 | ~ 옆에 | by the window |
| -ing | ~함으로써 | by practicing |
| oneself | 혼자서 | by myself |

## By vs Until vs On 비교표

| Preposition | Usage | Example |
|-------------|-------|---------|
| **by** (마감) | 동작 완료 시점 | I'll finish **by** 5. |
| **until** (지속) | 동작이 계속됨 | I'll work **until** 5. |
| **on foot** | 걸어서 | I go **on foot**. |
| **by bus** | 버스로 | I go **by bus**. |
