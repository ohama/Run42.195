# Unit 128: By

## A. By + 행위자 (수동태)

**By**는 수동태에서 **행위자**를 나타냅니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The plan was made by my coach."**
> 계획은 코치님이 세웠다.

> **"I was encouraged by Grandpa Yeongcheol."**
> 영철 할아버지에게 격려받았다.

> **"The race was won by a professional runner."**
> 경기는 프로 선수가 우승했다.

---

## B. By + 교통수단/방법

**By**는 "~로, ~에 의해"의 의미로 **교통수단이나 방법**을 나타냅니다.
관사 없이 씁니다 (by bus, not by the bus).

| Usage | Examples |
|-------|----------|
| 교통수단 | by bus, by car, by train, by plane |
| 통신수단 | by phone, by email, by text |
| 방법 | by hand, by mistake, by chance |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I usually go to school by bus."**
> 보통 버스로 학교에 간다.

> **"Forty minutes by bus from home to school."**
> 집에서 학교까지 버스로 40분.

> **"I contacted my coach by phone."**
> 코치님께 전화로 연락했다.

---

## C. By + 시간 (기한)

**By**는 "~까지 (그 전에)"의 의미로 **기한**을 나타냅니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I need to arrive by 8 o'clock."**
> 8시까지 도착해야 한다.

> **"By the time I finished, I was exhausted."**
> 끝났을 때, 나는 지쳐 있었다.

> **"By March, I had been running for a year."**
> 3월까지, 1년 동안 달리기를 해왔다.

---

## D. By + 장소 (옆에)

**By**는 "~ 옆에, ~ 곁에"의 의미로 **위치**를 나타냅니다.
= beside, next to

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I sat by the window."**
> 창가에 앉았다.

> **"Mom sat by my bed."**
> 엄마가 내 침대 옆에 앉았다.

> **"I passed by the tangerine orchard."**
> 감귤 과수원 옆을 지나갔다.

---

## E. By + 동명사 (방법)

**By + -ing**는 "~함으로써"의 의미로 **방법**을 나타냅니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I improved by running every day."**
> 매일 달림으로써 나아졌다.

> **"You can succeed by not giving up."**
> 포기하지 않음으로써 성공할 수 있다.

> **"I avoided motion sickness by walking."**
> 걸음으로써 멀미를 피했다.

---

## F. By oneself (혼자서)

**By oneself**는 "혼자서, 스스로"의 의미입니다.
= alone, on one's own

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I trained by myself during summer."**
> 여름 동안 혼자 훈련했다.

> **"I ran the first marathon by myself."**
> 첫 마라톤은 혼자 뛰었다.

---

## G. 관용 표현

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| by the way | 그런데 |
| by far | 단연 |
| by accident | 우연히 |
| by chance | 우연히 |
| step by step | 단계별로 |
| little by little | 조금씩 |
| day by day | 하루하루 |

### From the Novel:

> **"Little by little, I got stronger."**
> 조금씩 더 강해졌다.

> **"Step by step, I increased my distance."**
> 단계별로 거리를 늘렸다.

## Exercises

### A. by를 사용해 빈칸을 채우세요.

1. I go to school _______ bus.
2. I need to finish _______ Friday.
3. I sat _______ the window.
4. I improved _______ practicing every day.
5. I trained _______ myself.

**Answers:**
1. by
2. by
3. by
4. by
5. by

### B. 문장을 완성하세요.

1. The plan was made _______.
2. I contacted him _______ email.
3. Little _______ little, I got better.
4. _______ the time I arrived, the race had started.

**Answers:**
1. by my coach
2. by
3. by
4. By

### C. by의 의미를 구분하세요.

1. I go to school by bus. → _______
2. I need to arrive by 8. → _______
3. I sat by the window. → _______
4. The book was written by her. → _______

**Answers:**
1. 교통수단
2. 기한
3. 위치 (옆에)
4. 행위자
