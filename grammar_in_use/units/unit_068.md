# Unit 68: -ing clauses (Feeling tired, I went to bed early.)

## A. -ing 분사구문

**-ing 분사구문**은 두 문장을 하나로 연결하는 방법입니다.
주절과 같은 주어일 때 사용합니다.

| Full sentence | -ing clause |
|---------------|-------------|
| Because I was tired, I went to bed. | **Feeling tired**, I went to bed. |
| While I was running, I listened to music. | **Running**, I listened to music. |

---

## B. 동시 동작 (While/As)

두 동작이 동시에 일어날 때 사용합니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Walking to school, I listened to the birds."**
> 학교에 걸어가면서, 새소리를 들었다.

- = While I was walking to school, I listened to the birds.

> **"Running through the tangerine groves, I felt happy."**
> 귤밭을 달리면서, 행복했다.

- = While I was running through the tangerine groves, I felt happy.

> **"Gasping for breath, I crossed the finish line."**
> 숨을 헐떡이며, 결승선을 통과했다.

---

## C. 이유 (Because/Since)

이유를 나타낼 때 사용합니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Feeling nauseous, I got off the bus."**
> 속이 메스꺼워서, 버스에서 내렸다.

- = Because I felt nauseous, I got off the bus.

> **"Being tired, I decided to rest."**
> 피곤해서, 쉬기로 했다.

- = Because I was tired, I decided to rest.

> **"Not wanting to be late, I ran to school."**
> 늦고 싶지 않아서, 학교까지 뛰었다.

- = Because I didn't want to be late, I ran to school.

---

## D. 결과 (So/And)

결과를 나타낼 때도 사용합니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I finished the race, collapsing at the finish line."**
> 레이스를 끝내고, 결승선에서 쓰러졌다.

- = I finished the race and collapsed at the finish line.

> **"I kept running, ignoring the pain."**
> 통증을 무시하면서 계속 달렸다.

- = I kept running and ignored the pain.

---

## E. 부정형 (Not + -ing)

부정은 **not**을 -ing 앞에 둡니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Not wanting to give up, I kept going."**
> 포기하고 싶지 않아서, 계속 갔다.

> **"Not having breakfast, I felt hungry."**
> 아침을 먹지 않아서, 배가 고팠다.

> **"Not knowing what to do, I just stood there."**
> 뭘 해야 할지 몰라서, 그냥 서 있었다.

---

## F. 완료형 분사구문 (Having + 과거분사)

먼저 일어난 동작을 나타낼 때 사용합니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Having finished the race, I collapsed."**
> 레이스를 끝내고, 쓰러졌다.

- = After I had finished the race, I collapsed.

> **"Having trained for a year, I felt ready."**
> 1년간 훈련해서, 준비가 됐다고 느꼈다.

- = Because I had trained for a year, I felt ready.

> **"Having missed the bus, I decided to walk."**
> 버스를 놓쳐서, 걷기로 했다.

---

## G. 분사구문 위치

분사구문은 **문장 앞, 중간, 뒤**에 올 수 있습니다.

| Position | Example |
|----------|---------|
| 문장 앞 | **Feeling tired**, I went home. |
| 문장 중간 | I, **feeling tired**, went home. |
| 문장 뒤 | I went home, **feeling tired**. |

### From the Novel:

> **"Feeling good, I started running faster."** (앞)
> **"I kept running, feeling stronger."** (뒤)

---

## H. 주의사항

**주어가 같아야 합니다!**

| Correct | Incorrect |
|---------|-----------|
| **Walking home**, I saw a bird. | **Walking home**, a bird sang. |
| (내가 걸었고, 내가 봄) | (내가 걸었는데, 새가 노래?) |

### Correct usage:

> **"Walking to school, I heard birds singing."**
> 학교에 걸어가면서, 새들이 노래하는 것을 들었다.

- 주어: I (walking), I (heard)

## Exercises

### A. 분사구문으로 바꾸세요.

1. Because I was tired, I went to bed early.
   → _______, I went to bed early.

2. While I was running, I listened to music.
   → _______, I listened to music.

3. Because I didn't want to be late, I ran.
   → _______, I ran.

**Answers:**
1. Feeling tired / Being tired
2. Running
3. Not wanting to be late

### B. 문장을 완성하세요.

1. _______ (walk) to school, I enjoy the fresh air.
2. _______ (feel) nauseous, I got off the bus.
3. _______ (have) finished the race, I collapsed.
4. _______ (not know) what to do, I waited.
5. I kept running, _______ (ignore) the pain.

**Answers:**
1. Walking
2. Feeling
3. Having
4. Not knowing
5. ignoring

### C. 두 문장을 분사구문으로 연결하세요.

1. I was tired. I decided to rest.
   → _______, I decided to rest.

2. I finished the race. I cried with joy.
   → _______, I cried with joy.

3. I didn't have breakfast. I felt hungry.
   → _______, I felt hungry.

**Answers:**
1. Being tired / Feeling tired
2. Having finished the race / Finishing the race
3. Not having breakfast

## I. 분사구문 의미 정리

| -ing clause | Meaning | Example |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| Running, ... | ~하면서 (동시) | Running, I listened to music. |
| Feeling tired, ... | ~해서 (이유) | Feeling tired, I rested. |
| ..., collapsing. | ~하면서/그래서 (결과) | I finished, collapsing. |
| Not knowing, ... | ~하지 못해서 (부정) | Not knowing, I waited. |
| Having finished, ... | ~한 후에 (완료) | Having finished, I rested. |
