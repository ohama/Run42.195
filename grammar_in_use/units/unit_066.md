# Unit 66: To... (afraid to do) and preposition + -ing

## A. afraid to do vs afraid of doing (Form)

**afraid**는 to부정사와 전치사 + -ing 둘 다 쓸 수 있지만, 의미가 다릅니다.

| Structure | Meaning | Nuance |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| afraid **to do** | ~하기가 두렵다 | 하고 싶지 않다 (의도적 회피) |
| afraid **of doing** | ~할까봐 두렵다 | 결과가 걱정된다 (우연한 결과) |

### From the Original Book:

> "She was **afraid to tell** her father." (말하기가 두려웠다)
> "She was **afraid of telling** her father." (말하게 될까봐 두려웠다)

---

## B. afraid to do (Deliberate Avoidance - Primary Usage)

~하는 것이 두려워서 **하고 싶지 않다**는 의미입니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "He was **afraid to go** out alone."
> 그는 혼자 나가기가 두려웠다. (나가고 싶지 않음)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was afraid to try running."**
> 달리기를 시도하는 게 두려웠다.

- 달리기가 어려울 것 같아서 시도하기 싫었다

> **"I was afraid to tell Mom about missing the bus."**
> 버스 놓친 걸 엄마에게 말하기가 두려웠다.

- 말했을 때의 반응이 두려워서 말하기 싫었다

### 핵심: **의도적 회피**

```
afraid to do = "하기 싫어" (의식적 결정)
              → 두려워서 안 함
```

---

## C. afraid of doing (Worried About Possibility)

~하게 될까봐 **걱정된다**는 의미입니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "I'm **afraid of making** a mistake."
> 실수할까봐 두렵다. (의도치 않게 할 수도 있음)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I'm afraid of failing the race."**
> 레이스에서 실패할까봐 두렵다.

- 실패라는 결과가 걱정됨

> **"I was afraid of getting injured."**
> 다칠까봐 두려웠다.

- 부상이라는 결과가 걱정됨

> **"I'm afraid of not finishing the marathon."**
> 마라톤을 완주하지 못할까봐 두렵다.

### 핵심: **결과에 대한 걱정**

```
afraid of doing = "~될까봐 걱정" (우연한 결과)
                 → 통제할 수 없는 결과 걱정
```

---

## D. 비교: afraid to vs afraid of (Detailed Comparison)

| afraid to do | afraid of doing |
|--------------|-----------------|
| 의도적으로 안 함 | 결과가 걱정됨 |
| 의식적 선택 | 우연한 가능성 |
| I was afraid **to jump**. | I was afraid **of falling**. |
| (점프하기 싫었다) | (떨어질까봐 걱정됐다) |

### From the Original Book:

> "I was **afraid to walk** home alone."
> (혼자 걷기 싫었다 - 의도적 회피)

> "I was **afraid of being** attacked."
> (공격당할까봐 두려웠다 - 결과 걱정)

### From the Novel:

> **"I was afraid to run 42km."** (뛰기가 두려웠다 - 시도 자체가 두려움)
> **"I was afraid of not finishing."** (완주 못 할까봐 두려웠다 - 결과가 걱정됨)

### 시각적 이해:

```
afraid to do:     [나] ──X──→ [행동]
                  (하기 싫음)

afraid of doing:  [나] ──?──→ [결과]
                  (결과 걱정)
```

## E. interested in doing vs interested to do (Different Meanings)

| Structure | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| interested **in doing** | ~에 관심이 있다 (일반적) |
| interested **to do** | ~해서 흥미롭다 (반응) |

### From the Original Book:

> "I'm **interested in learning** French."
> 프랑스어를 배우는 데 관심이 있어.

> "I was **interested to hear** your opinion."
> 네 의견을 들어서 흥미로웠어.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I'm interested in running marathons."**
> 마라톤을 뛰는 데 관심이 있다.

- **interested in + -ing**: ~에 관심이 있다

> **"I was interested to hear about your training."**
> 네 훈련에 대해 들어서 흥미로웠다.

- **interested to + 동사**: ~해서 흥미롭다

## F. sorry for doing vs sorry to do (Apology vs Sympathy)

| Structure | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| sorry **for doing** | ~해서 미안하다 (과거 행동) |
| sorry **to do** | ~하게 되어 유감이다 (공식적/동정) |

### From the Original Book:

> "I'm **sorry for shouting** at you."
> 소리 질러서 미안해.

> "I'm **sorry to hear** about your accident."
> 사고 소식을 듣게 되어 유감이야.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I'm sorry for being late."**
> 늦어서 미안해.

- 과거에 한 행동에 대해 미안함

> **"I'm sorry to hear that."**
> 그 소식을 듣게 되어 유감이야.

- 공식적인 유감 표현 / 동정

### 비교:

| sorry for + -ing | sorry to + 동사 |
|------------------|-----------------|
| 내 행동에 사과 | 상황에 유감 표현 |
| 과거 지향 | 현재/미래 지향 |

---

## G. 기타 형용사 + to / 전치사 + -ing (Additional Patterns)

| Adjective | + to | + preposition + -ing |
|-----------|------|---------------------|
| good | good to see you | good **at** running |
| bad | - | bad **at** swimming |
| tired | tired to hear | tired **of** walking |
| keen | keen to try | keen **on** running |
| fond | - | fond **of** walking |
| bored | - | bored **with** waiting |

### From the Novel:

> **"I'm good at running now."**
> 이제 달리기를 잘한다.

> **"I got tired of taking the bus."**
> 버스 타는 게 지겨워졌다.

> **"He was keen on running."**
> 그는 달리기에 열심이었다.

### 자주 쓰이는 조합:

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| good/bad **at** | ~을 잘하다/못하다 |
| tired **of** | ~에 지치다 |
| keen **on** | ~에 열심인 |
| fond **of** | ~을 좋아하는 |
| bored **with** | ~에 지루한 |
| worried **about** | ~을 걱정하는 |

## H. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~I'm interested **to run**.~~ (관심) | I'm interested **in running**. | 관심 = in + -ing |
| ~~I'm afraid **of tell** her.~~ | I'm afraid **of telling** her. | 전치사 + -ing |
| ~~I'm sorry **to being** late.~~ | I'm sorry **for being** late. | 사과 = for + -ing |
| ~~I'm good **in** running.~~ | I'm good **at** running. | good at |
| ~~I'm tired **with** waiting.~~ | I'm tired **of** waiting. | tired of |
| ~~I'm keen **to run**.~~ (일반적 관심) | I'm keen **on running**. | keen on |

## Exercises

### A. to 또는 전치사 + -ing를 고르세요.

1. I'm afraid (to fail / of failing) the race. (결과 걱정)
2. I was afraid (to tell / of telling) her. (말하기 싫었다)
3. I'm interested (to hear / in hearing) about your training. (관심)
4. I'm sorry (to hear / for hearing) that. (유감)
5. I'm tired (to take / of taking) the bus.
6. I'm good (to run / at running) now.
7. She's keen (to try / on trying) the new route.
8. I was afraid (to jump / of jumping). (점프하기 싫었다)

**Answers:**
1. of failing (결과 걱정)
2. to tell (하기 싫음)
3. in hearing
4. to hear
5. of taking
6. at running
7. on trying (일반적 관심)
8. to jump (의도적 회피)

### B. 문장을 완성하세요.

1. I was afraid _______ (run) 42km for the first time. (하기 싫었다)
2. I'm afraid _______ (get) injured during the race. (결과 걱정)
3. She's interested _______ (join) the track team.
4. I'm sorry _______ (be) late for training.
5. He got tired _______ (walk) the same route.
6. I'm good _______ (pace) myself now.
7. We're keen _______ (start) training together.
8. I was worried _______ (finish) on time.

**Answers:**
1. to run (의도적 회피)
2. of getting (결과 걱정)
3. in joining
4. for being
5. of walking
6. at pacing
7. on starting
8. about finishing

### C. 의미에 맞게 고르세요.

1. 나는 실패할까봐 두려웠다. (결과 걱정)
   → I was afraid _______.
   a) to fail  b) of failing

2. 나는 시도하기가 두려웠다. (하기 싫음)
   → I was afraid _______.
   a) to try  b) of trying

3. 마라톤에 관심이 있다. (일반적 관심)
   → I'm interested _______.
   a) to run marathons  b) in running marathons

4. 소식을 들어서 유감이다. (동정)
   → I'm sorry _______.
   a) to hear  b) for hearing

5. 늦어서 미안해. (사과)
   → I'm sorry _______.
   a) to be late  b) for being late

**Answers:**
1. b) of failing
2. a) to try
3. b) in running marathons
4. a) to hear
5. b) for being late

### D. 틀린 부분을 고치세요.

1. I'm interested to learn Korean.
   → _______

2. I'm sorry to being late.
   → _______

3. I'm good in running.
   → _______

4. I'm tired with waiting.
   → _______

**Answers:**
1. I'm interested **in learning** Korean.
2. I'm sorry **for being** late.
3. I'm good **at** running.
4. I'm tired **of** waiting.

## Summary: to vs 전치사 + -ing 비교

| Adjective | + to | + preposition + -ing |
|-----------|------|---------------------|
| **afraid** | 하기 싫어서 두려움 | 결과가 걱정됨 (**of**) |
| **interested** | ~해서 흥미로움 | ~에 관심 있음 (**in**) |
| **sorry** | ~하게 되어 유감 | ~해서 미안함 (**for**) |
| **tired** | - | 지겨움 (**of**) |
| **good/bad** | - | 잘/못함 (**at**) |
| **keen** | - | 열심임 (**on**) |

### Quick Decision:
- **afraid**: 의도적 회피? → **to** / 결과 걱정? → **of -ing**
- **interested**: 반응? → **to** / 관심? → **in -ing**
- **sorry**: 유감/동정? → **to** / 사과? → **for -ing**
