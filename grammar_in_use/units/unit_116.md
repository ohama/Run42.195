# Unit 116: As (reason and time)

## A. As - 이유를 나타낼 때 (Reason)

**As**는 "~이므로, ~이기 때문에"의 의미로 **이유**를 나타냅니다.
= because, since

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"As I was tired, I walked instead of running."**
> 피곤했기 때문에 달리는 대신 걸었다.

> **"As it was my first day, I was nervous."**
> 첫날이었기 때문에 긴장되었다.

> **"As the bus was crowded, I couldn't get a seat."**
> 버스가 붐볐기 때문에 자리를 잡을 수 없었다.

---

## B. As vs Because vs Since

| Expression | Meaning | Usage |
|------------|---------|-------|
| as | ~이므로 | 이유가 이미 알려진 경우, 문장 앞에 자주 위치 |
| because | ~이기 때문에 | 이유 강조, 문장 뒤에 자주 위치 |
| since | ~이므로 | as와 비슷, 격식체 |

### Comparison:

> **"As you know, I started running in March."** (이미 알려진 정보)
> 알다시피, 나는 3월에 달리기를 시작했다.

> **"I started running because I hated the bus."** (이유 강조)
> 버스가 싫어서 달리기를 시작했다.

> **"Since it was raining, we trained indoors."** (격식체)
> 비가 왔기 때문에 실내에서 훈련했다.

---

## C. As - 시간을 나타낼 때 (Time)

**As**는 "~할 때, ~하면서"의 의미로 **동시에 일어나는 동작**을 나타냅니다.
= while, when

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"As I walked, my mindset started to shift."**
> 걸으면서 마음가짐이 바뀌기 시작했다.

> **"As I ran, the scenery flew by."**
> 달리면서 풍경이 빠르게 지나갔다.

> **"As the sun rose, it got hotter."**
> 해가 뜨면서 더 더워졌다.

> **"As I approached the finish line, I heard the crowd cheering."**
> 결승선에 가까워지면서 관중들의 환호성이 들렸다.

---

## D. As - 변화를 나타낼 때

**As**는 시간이 지남에 따라 변화하는 상황을 나타낼 때도 사용됩니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"As the weeks went by, I got stronger."**
> 몇 주가 지나면서 나는 더 강해졌다.

> **"As I continued running, it got easier."**
> 계속 달리면서 점점 더 쉬워졌다.

> **"As summer approached, the mornings got hotter."**
> 여름이 다가오면서 아침이 더 더워졌다.

---

## E. As의 위치

**이유의 as**는 보통 문장 **앞**에 오는 것이 자연스럽습니다.
**시간의 as**는 문장 **앞**이나 **뒤** 모두 가능합니다.

### Examples:

**이유 (Reason):**
> **"As I was late, I had to run."** (자연스러움)
> **"I had to run as I was late."** (가능하지만 덜 일반적)

**시간 (Time):**
> **"As I ran, I felt better."** (문장 앞)
> **"I felt better as I ran."** (문장 뒤)

---

## F. As vs While vs When

| Expression | Usage |
|------------|-------|
| as | 동시 동작, 변화 (짧은 동작) |
| while | 동시 진행 (긴 동작) |
| when | 특정 시점 |

### Comparison:

> **"As I crossed the finish line, everyone cheered."** (짧은 순간)
> **"While I was running, I thought about my goal."** (긴 시간 동안)
> **"When I finished, I felt exhausted."** (끝난 시점)

## Exercises

### A. as를 사용해 두 문장을 연결하세요 (이유).

1. I was tired. I took a rest.
   → _______, _______.

2. The bus was full. I had to stand.
   → _______, _______.

**Answers:**
1. As I was tired, I took a rest.
2. As the bus was full, I had to stand.

### B. as를 사용해 두 문장을 연결하세요 (시간).

1. I walked to school. I listened to birds singing.
   → As _______, _______.

2. The sun set. The temperature dropped.
   → As _______, _______.

**Answers:**
1. As I walked to school, I listened to birds singing.
2. As the sun set, the temperature dropped.

### C. as, because, while 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. _______ you know, I started running in March.
2. I run every morning _______ it keeps me healthy.
3. _______ I was running, I saw a beautiful sunrise.
4. _______ the weeks passed, my stamina improved.

**Answers:**
1. As
2. because
3. While / As
4. As

### D. as가 이유인지 시간인지 구분하세요.

1. As I was sick, I stayed home. → _______
2. As I ran, the wind brushed my face. → _______
3. As it was getting late, we had to hurry. → _______
4. As summer came, I started running earlier. → _______

**Answers:**
1. 이유 (reason)
2. 시간 (time)
3. 이유 (reason)
4. 시간 (time) / 변화

## As의 두 가지 의미 요약

| As = Reason | As = Time |
|-------------|-----------|
| "~이므로, ~이기 때문에" | "~하면서, ~할 때" |
| As I was late, I ran. | As I ran, I felt better. |
| 늦어서 달렸다. | 달리면서 기분이 좋아졌다. |
