# Unit 116: As (reason and time)

## A. As - 이유를 나타낼 때 (Reason)

**As**는 "~이므로, ~이기 때문에"의 의미로 **이유**를 나타냅니다.
= because, since

### 구조:
> As + 주어 + 동사, 주절
> 주절 + as + 주어 + 동사

### From the Original Book:
> "**As** I was feeling tired, I went to bed early."
> 피곤했기 때문에, 일찍 잠자리에 들었다.

> "We watched TV all evening **as** we had nothing better to do."
> 딱히 할 일이 없어서, 저녁 내내 TV를 봤다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"As I was tired, I walked instead of running."**
> 피곤했기 때문에 달리는 대신 걸었다.

> **"As it was my first day, I was nervous."**
> 첫날이었기 때문에 긴장되었다.

> **"As the bus was crowded, I couldn't get a seat."**
> 버스가 붐볐기 때문에 자리를 잡을 수 없었다.

---

## B. As vs Because vs Since 비교

| Expression | Meaning | Usage | Position |
|------------|---------|-------|----------|
| as | ~이므로 | 이유가 이미 알려진 경우 | 주로 문장 앞 |
| because | ~이기 때문에 | 이유 강조 | 주로 문장 뒤 |
| since | ~이므로 | as와 비슷, 약간 격식체 | 주로 문장 앞 |

### From the Original Book:
> "**As** you know, I've already done this." (이미 알려진 정보)
> 알다시피, 나는 이미 이것을 했다.

> "I did it **because** I wanted to." (이유 강조)
> 하고 싶어서 했다.

> "**Since** you're not interested, I won't tell you." (격식체)
> 관심 없으니까, 말 안 할게.

### Comparison:

> **"As you know, I started running in March."** (이미 알려진 정보)
> 알다시피, 나는 3월에 달리기를 시작했다.

> **"I started running because I hated the bus."** (이유 강조)
> 버스가 싫어서 달리기를 시작했다.

> **"Since it was raining, we trained indoors."** (격식체)
> 비가 왔기 때문에 실내에서 훈련했다.

### 이유 강조 정도:

| 강조 정도 | 접속사 |
|-----------|--------|
| 약함 ★☆☆ | as |
| 중간 ★★☆ | since |
| 강함 ★★★ | because |

---

## C. As - 시간을 나타낼 때 (Time)

**As**는 "~할 때, ~하면서"의 의미로 **동시에 일어나는 동작**을 나타냅니다.
= while, when

### From the Original Book:
> "**As** I walked along the street, I looked in the shop windows."
> 거리를 걸으면서, 상점 창문을 들여다봤다.

> "**As** the day went on, the weather got worse."
> 하루가 지나면서, 날씨가 나빠졌다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"As I walked, my mindset started to shift."**
> 걸으면서 마음가짐이 바뀌기 시작했다.

> **"As I ran, the scenery flew by."**
> 달리면서 풍경이 빠르게 지나갔다.

> **"As the sun rose, it got hotter."**
> 해가 뜨면서 더 더워졌다.

> **"As I approached the finish line, I heard the crowd cheering."**
> 결승선에 가까워지면서 관중들의 환호성이 들렸다.

---

## D. As - 변화를 나타낼 때 (Change)

**As**는 시간이 지남에 따라 변화하는 상황을 나타낼 때도 사용됩니다.

### From the Original Book:
> "**As** the conversation went on, I got more interested."
> 대화가 진행되면서, 더 흥미로워졌다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"As the weeks went by, I got stronger."**
> 몇 주가 지나면서 나는 더 강해졌다.

> **"As I continued running, it got easier."**
> 계속 달리면서 점점 더 쉬워졌다.

> **"As summer approached, the mornings got hotter."**
> 여름이 다가오면서 아침이 더 더워졌다.

---

## E. As의 위치 (Position)

### 이유의 as:
보통 문장 **앞**에 오는 것이 자연스럽습니다.

### 시간의 as:
문장 **앞**이나 **뒤** 모두 가능합니다.

### Examples:

**이유 (Reason):**
> **"As I was late, I had to run."** (자연스러움 ★★★)
> **"I had to run as I was late."** (가능 ★★☆)

**시간 (Time):**
> **"As I ran, I felt better."** (문장 앞 ★★★)
> **"I felt better as I ran."** (문장 뒤 ★★★)

---

## F. As vs While vs When 비교

| Expression | Usage | Duration |
|------------|-------|----------|
| as | 동시 동작, 변화 | 짧은/진행 중인 동작 |
| while | 동시 진행 | 긴 시간 동안 |
| when | 특정 시점 | 순간/시점 |

### From the Original Book:
> "**As** I was leaving, the phone rang." (짧은 순간)
> "**While** I was away, the weather was nice." (긴 기간)
> "**When** I got home, I made dinner." (특정 시점)

### Comparison:

> **"As I crossed the finish line, everyone cheered."** (짧은 순간)
> **"While I was running, I thought about my goal."** (긴 시간 동안)
> **"When I finished, I felt exhausted."** (끝난 시점)

---

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | 설명 |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| ~~As because I was tired~~ | **As** I was tired / **Because** I was tired | 중복 사용 금지 |
| ~~As I was walking, but I saw a bird~~ | **As** I was walking, I saw a bird | but 중복 금지 |
| ~~Like I said~~ (격식체) | **As** I said | 격식체에서는 as |
| ~~As you are knowing~~ | **As you know** | 상태동사는 진행형 불가 |

### As의 두 가지 의미 구분하기:

문맥을 통해 이유인지 시간인지 구분해야 합니다.

> "As I was sick, I stayed home." → **이유** (아팠기 때문에)
> "As I was walking, I saw a bird." → **시간** (걷고 있을 때)

## Exercises

### A. as를 사용해 두 문장을 연결하세요 (이유).

1. I was tired. I took a rest.
   → **As I was tired, I took a rest.**

2. The bus was full. I had to stand.
   → **As the bus was full, I had to stand.**

3. It was raining. I stayed inside.
   → **As it was raining, I stayed inside.**

4. He was late. He ran to school.
   → **As he was late, he ran to school.**

### B. as를 사용해 두 문장을 연결하세요 (시간).

1. I walked to school. I listened to birds singing.
   → **As I walked to school, I listened to birds singing.**

2. The sun set. The temperature dropped.
   → **As the sun set, the temperature dropped.**

3. I approached the finish. The crowd cheered.
   → **As I approached the finish, the crowd cheered.**

4. Time passed. I got better at running.
   → **As time passed, I got better at running.**

### C. as, because, while 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. _______ you know, I started running in March.
2. I run every morning _______ it keeps me healthy.
3. _______ I was running, I saw a beautiful sunrise.
4. _______ the weeks passed, my stamina improved.
5. I missed the bus _______ I woke up late.
6. _______ I was studying, the phone rang.

**Answers:**
1. As
2. because
3. While / As
4. As
5. because
6. While

### D. as가 이유인지 시간인지 구분하세요.

1. As I was sick, I stayed home. → _______
2. As I ran, the wind brushed my face. → _______
3. As it was getting late, we had to hurry. → _______
4. As summer came, I started running earlier. → _______
5. As you requested, I'm sending the report. → _______
6. As the race progressed, I felt more confident. → _______

**Answers:**
1. 이유 (reason)
2. 시간 (time)
3. 이유 (reason)
4. 시간 (time) / 변화
5. 이유 (reason) - "요청하셨으므로"
6. 시간 (time) / 변화

## Summary: As의 두 가지 의미

| As = Reason (이유) | As = Time (시간) |
|-------------------|------------------|
| "~이므로, ~이기 때문에" | "~하면서, ~할 때" |
| As I was late, I ran. | As I ran, I felt better. |
| 늦어서 달렸다. | 달리면서 기분이 좋아졌다. |
| 이유 강조 (약함) | 동시 동작/변화 |

---

## As / Because / Since 비교표

| 접속사 | 이유 강조 | 위치 | 격식 | 사용 상황 |
|--------|----------|------|------|----------|
| as | 약함 | 문장 앞 | 중립 | 이미 알려진 이유 |
| because | 강함 | 문장 뒤 | 중립 | 새로운/중요한 이유 |
| since | 중간 | 문장 앞 | 격식 | 알려진 이유 (격식체) |

---

## As / While / When 비교표

| 접속사 | 의미 | 사용 상황 |
|--------|------|----------|
| as | ~하면서, ~할 때 | 짧은 순간, 동시 동작, 변화 |
| while | ~하는 동안 | 긴 시간, 배경 동작 |
| when | ~할 때 | 특정 시점, 순간 |

---

## Compare with Korean

| English | Korean |
|---------|--------|
| as (reason) | ~이므로, ~이기 때문에, ~라서 |
| as (time) | ~하면서, ~할 때 |
| because | ~때문에 (강조) |
| since | ~이므로 (격식) |
| while | ~하는 동안 |
| when | ~할 때 |

한국어에서는 "~이므로", "~하면서"로 as를 표현합니다.
