# Unit 24: Will be doing and will have done

## A. 형태 (Form)

### Future Continuous: will be + -ing

**미래 특정 시점에 진행 중일 동작**을 나타냅니다.

| Subject | will be | -ing | Example |
|---------|---------|------|---------|
| I | will be | running | I will be running. |
| She | will be | waiting | She will be waiting. |
| They | will be | training | They will be training. |

### Future Perfect: will have + 과거분사

**미래 특정 시점까지 완료될 동작**을 나타냅니다.

| Subject | will have | Past Participle | Example |
|---------|-----------|-----------------|---------|
| I | will have | run | I will have run 10km. |
| He | will have | finished | He will have finished. |
| We | will have | trained | We will have trained for a year. |

### 부정문과 의문문:

| Type | Future Continuous | Future Perfect |
|------|-------------------|----------------|
| 긍정 | I will be running. | I will have finished. |
| 부정 | I won't be running. | I won't have finished. |
| 의문 | Will you be running? | Will you have finished? |

## B. Future Continuous (미래 진행형)

### 용법 1: 미래 특정 시점에 진행 중인 동작

**"At a particular time in the future, something will be in progress."**

### From the Original Book:

> "This time tomorrow I**'ll be lying** on the beach."
> 내일 이 시간에는 해변에 누워 있을 거야.

> "Don't phone me between 7 and 8. We**'ll be having** dinner."
> 7시에서 8시 사이에 전화하지 마. 저녁 먹고 있을 거야.

> "What **will** you **be doing** at 10 o'clock tomorrow?"
> 내일 10시에 뭐 하고 있을 거야?

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"This time tomorrow, I'll be running the race."**
> 내일 이 시간에는 대회를 달리고 있을 거야.

- 미래 특정 시점에 진행 중인 동작

> **"At 10 a.m. on Sunday, I'll be crossing the finish line."**
> 일요일 오전 10시에는 결승선을 지나고 있을 거야.

- 특정 시간에 하고 있을 동작

> **"Don't call me at 7 a.m. I'll be running."**
> 오전 7시에 전화하지 마. 달리고 있을 거야.

### Time Expressions for Future Continuous:

| Expression | Example |
|------------|---------|
| this time tomorrow | This time tomorrow, I'll be running. |
| at + time | At 8 a.m., I'll be training. |
| between + times | Between 6 and 7, I'll be stretching. |
| when + clause | When you arrive, I'll be waiting. |

---

## C. Future Continuous 추가 용법 (Additional Uses)

### 용법 2: 자연스럽게 일어날 미래 사건

**당연히 그때 하고 있을 일:**

> **"I'll be seeing him tomorrow anyway."**
> 어차피 내일 그를 볼 거야.

- 특별한 약속 없이 자연스럽게 일어날 일

> **"Will you be passing the post office?"**
> 우체국 지나갈 거야?

### 용법 3: 정중한 질문

**상대방의 계획을 물을 때 더 정중하게:**

| Direct | Polite |
|--------|--------|
| Will you come? | Will you be coming? |
| Are you going? | Will you be going? |

> **"Will you be coming to the race?"**
> 대회에 오실 건가요? (정중)

> **"Will you be staying for dinner?"**
> 저녁까지 계실 건가요? (정중)

## D. Future Perfect (미래 완료)

### 용법: 미래 특정 시점까지 완료될 것

**"By a certain time in the future, something will be complete."**

### From the Original Book:

> "By the time you get home, I**'ll have cooked** dinner."
> 네가 집에 올 때쯤이면 저녁 다 만들어 놨을 거야.

> "Next month, we**'ll have been married** for 20 years."
> 다음 달이면 결혼한 지 20년이 돼.

> "I**'ll have finished** this report by tomorrow."
> 내일까지 이 보고서 끝내 놓을 거야.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"By March, I will have trained for a whole year."**
> 3월이면 1년간 훈련한 게 될 거야.

- 미래 특정 시점까지 완료될 것

> **"By the end of 2024, I will have run approximately 1,500km."**
> 2024년 말까지 약 1,500km를 달렸을 거야.

- 특정 시점까지의 누적 결과

> **"By the time I finish, I will have run 42km."**
> 내가 끝낼 때쯤이면 42km를 달린 게 될 거야.

### Time Expressions for Future Perfect:

| Expression | Example |
|------------|---------|
| by + time | By 10 a.m., I'll have finished. |
| by + date | By March, I'll have trained for a year. |
| by the time + clause | By the time you arrive, I'll have left. |
| by the end of | By the end of this year, I'll have run 1,000km. |
| in + period | In two weeks, I'll have completed the training. |

## E. Future Perfect Continuous

### will have been + -ing

**미래 특정 시점까지 계속 진행되어 온 것**을 강조:

> **"By March, I will have been running for a whole year."**
> 3월이면 1년간 달려 온 게 될 거야. (계속 진행됨을 강조)

> "By next month, she**'ll have been training** for six months."
> 다음 달이면 6개월간 훈련해 온 게 돼.

### Future Perfect vs Future Perfect Continuous:

| Future Perfect | Future Perfect Continuous |
|----------------|---------------------------|
| 완료 강조 | 기간/계속 강조 |
| I'll have run 100km. | I'll have been running for 3 hours. |
| (100km 달린 게 됨) | (3시간째 달리고 있는 것) |

---

## F. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~I will running.~~ | I **will be running**. | will be + -ing |
| ~~I will be run.~~ | I **will be running**. | -ing 필요 |
| ~~I will have run by tomorrow.~~ (시점 없음) | **By tomorrow**, I will have run 10km. | by + 시점 필요 |
| ~~By 10, I will finish.~~ | By 10, I **will have finished**. | 완료 = will have done |
| ~~I will have been run.~~ | I **will have run** / I **will have been running**. | 형태 주의 |
| ~~When will you have doing?~~ | What **will** you **be doing**? | 의문문 형태 |

### 시제 선택 가이드:

| 상황 | 시제 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| 특정 시점에 하고 있을 것 | Future Continuous | I'll be running at 8. |
| 특정 시점까지 끝날 것 | Future Perfect | I'll have finished by 10. |
| 특정 시점까지 계속해 온 것 | Future Perfect Cont. | I'll have been running for 2 hours. |
| 단순 미래 | Will | I'll run tomorrow. |

## Exercises

### A. 빈칸 채우기 - Future Continuous (will be doing)

1. This time tomorrow, I _______ (run) the marathon.
2. At 3 p.m. on Sunday, he _______ (wait) at the finish line.
3. Don't call me at 8 a.m. I _______ (run).
4. What _______ you _______ (do) at this time tomorrow?
5. When you arrive, I _______ (train) at the park.

**Answers:**
1. will be running
2. will be waiting
3. will be running
4. will ... be doing
5. will be training

### B. 빈칸 채우기 - Future Perfect (will have done)

1. By the end of this year, I _______ (run) over 1,000km.
2. By March, he _______ (train) for a whole year.
3. By the time I finish, I _______ (complete) 42km.
4. I _______ (finish) this book by tonight.
5. By next week, they _______ (arrive) in Jeju.

**Answers:**
1. will have run
2. will have trained
3. will have completed
4. will have finished
5. will have arrived

### C. 올바른 형태 선택 (Choose the correct form)

1. "What (will you do / will you be doing) at 8 a.m. tomorrow?"
   → **will you be doing** (특정 시점에 진행 중인 동작)

2. "By December, I (will run / will have run) my first marathon."
   → **will have run** (특정 시점까지 완료)

3. "I (will wait / will be waiting) for you at the finish line."
   → **will be waiting** (그 시점에 하고 있을 동작)

4. "By the time you get here, I (will finish / will have finished) dinner."
   → **will have finished** (특정 시점까지 완료)

5. "Don't phone at 7. We (will eat / will be eating) dinner."
   → **will be eating** (그 시간에 진행 중)

### D. 문장 완성하기 (Complete the sentences)

1. (내일 이 시간에 달리고 있을 것)
   → This time tomorrow, I _______.

2. (3월까지 1년간 훈련한 게 될 것)
   → By March, I _______ for a year.

3. (10시에 뭐 하고 있을 거야?)
   → What _______ at 10 o'clock?

4. (끝낼 때쯤이면 42km 달린 게 될 것)
   → By the time I finish, I _______ 42km.

**Answers:**
1. will be running
2. will have trained
3. will you be doing
4. will have run

## Summary Table (요약표)

| 형태 | 의미 | 시간 표현 | 예문 |
|------|------|----------|------|
| will do | 단순 미래 | tomorrow, next week | I **will run** tomorrow. |
| will be doing | 미래 특정 시점 진행 | at 8, this time tomorrow | I **will be running** at 8 a.m. |
| will have done | 미래 특정 시점까지 완료 | by 10, by March | I **will have run** 10km by then. |
| will have been doing | 미래까지 계속 진행 | for + 기간, by + 시점 | I **will have been running** for 2 hours. |
