# Unit 124: In/at/on (place) (2)

## A. At home / at work / at school

**At**은 **일상적인 장소**에서 활동하고 있음을 나타냅니다.

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| at home | 집에서 |
| at work | 직장에서 |
| at school | 학교에서 |
| at the station | 역에서 |
| at the airport | 공항에서 |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I'm writing this diary at home."**
> 집에서 이 일기를 쓰고 있다.

> **"Mom was at work when I left."**
> 내가 나갈 때 엄마는 직장에 있었다.

> **"I met Minseo at school."**
> 학교에서 민서를 만났다.

---

## B. In bed / In hospital / In prison

특정 상황에서는 **in**을 사용합니다.

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| in bed | 침대에 누워서 |
| in hospital | 병원에 입원해서 (영국식) |
| in prison | 감옥에 수감되어 |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was in bed when the alarm went off."**
> 알람이 울렸을 때 침대에 있었다.

> **"I stayed in bed until 7:23."**
> 7시 23분까지 침대에 있었다.

---

## C. Arrive at / Arrive in

**Arrive**와 함께 쓰는 전치사:

| arrive at | arrive in |
|-----------|-----------|
| 특정 장소/지점 | 도시/나라 |
| arrive at school | arrive in Jeju |
| arrive at the station | arrive in Korea |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I arrived at school at 8:40."**
> 8시 40분에 학교에 도착했다.

> **"We arrived in Jeju last year."**
> 작년에 제주에 도착했다.

> **"When I arrived at the finish line, everyone cheered."**
> 결승선에 도착했을 때, 모두가 환호했다.

---

## D. On a bus / On a train / On a plane

**대중교통**에는 **on**을 사용합니다.

| Expression | Example |
|------------|---------|
| on a bus | I was on a bus. |
| on a train | She's on the train. |
| on a plane | They're on a plane. |

### But: In a car / In a taxi

**개인 차량**에는 **in**을 사용합니다.

| Expression | Example |
|------------|---------|
| in a car | I got in the car. |
| in a taxi | She's in a taxi. |

### From the Novel:

> **"I was on the bus for 40 minutes."**
> 40분 동안 버스에 타고 있었다.

> **"Dad drove me in the car."**
> 아빠가 차로 데려다 주셨다.

---

## E. On the way / On the left / On the right

**On**은 길이나 방향을 나타낼 때도 사용합니다.

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| on the way | 가는 길에 |
| on the left | 왼쪽에 |
| on the right | 오른쪽에 |
| on the corner | 모퉁이에 |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"On the way to school, I saw rapeseed flowers."**
> 학교 가는 길에 유채꽃을 봤다.

> **"On the way home, I thought about the bus."**
> 집에 가는 길에 버스에 대해 생각했다.

> **"The bus stop is on the left."**
> 버스 정류장은 왼쪽에 있다.

---

## F. At the top / At the bottom / In the middle

**위치**를 나타내는 표현:

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| at the top | 맨 위에 |
| at the bottom | 맨 아래에 |
| in the middle | 가운데에 |
| at the front | 앞에 |
| at the back | 뒤에 |

### From the Novel:

> **"In elementary school, I was near the bottom in the endurance run."**
> 초등학교 때 오래달리기에서 거의 꼴찌였다.

> **"The classroom is at the end of the hall."**
> 교실은 복도 끝에 있다.

## Exercises

### A. at, in, on 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. I was _______ home when you called.
2. She arrived _______ school on time.
3. We arrived _______ Jeju yesterday.
4. I was _______ the bus for an hour.
5. He got _______ the car and drove away.

**Answers:**
1. at
2. at
3. in
4. on
5. in

### B. 빈칸을 채우세요.

1. I stayed _______ bed until 7 a.m.
2. _______ the way to school, I saw birds.
3. The school is _______ the right.
4. I arrived _______ the finish line exhausted.
5. She works _______ a hospital.

**Answers:**
1. in
2. On
3. on
4. at
5. at / in (at = 일함, in = 병원 안에)

### C. arrive at 또는 arrive in을 사용해 문장을 완성하세요.

1. We _______ the airport at 6 a.m.
2. They _______ Korea last week.
3. I _______ school late.
4. When did you _______ Jeju?

**Answers:**
1. arrived at
2. arrived in
3. arrived at
4. arrive in
