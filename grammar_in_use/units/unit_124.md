# Unit 124: In/at/on (place) (2)

## A. At home / at work / at school (일상 장소)

**At**은 **일상적인 장소**에서 활동하고 있음을 나타냅니다.

### From the Original Book:
> "I'll be **at home** all evening."
> 저녁 내내 집에 있을 거야.

> "She's **at work** right now."
> 그녀는 지금 직장에 있다.

### At 사용 장소:

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| at home | 집에서 | I'm at home now. |
| at work | 직장에서 | She's at work. |
| at school | 학교에서 | The kids are at school. |
| at college/university | 대학에서 | He's at university. |
| at the station | 역에서 | I'll meet you at the station. |
| at the airport | 공항에서 | Pick me up at the airport. |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I'm writing this diary at home."**
> 집에서 이 일기를 쓰고 있다.

> **"Mom was at work when I left."**
> 내가 나갈 때 엄마는 직장에 있었다.

> **"I met Minseo at school."**
> 학교에서 민서를 만났다.

---

## B. In bed / In hospital / In prison (특정 상태)

특정 상황에서는 **in**을 사용합니다.

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| in bed | 침대에 누워서 | I was in bed all day. |
| in hospital | 병원에 입원해서 (영국식) | He's in hospital. |
| in the hospital | 병원 안에 (미국식) | She's in the hospital. |
| in prison/jail | 감옥에 수감되어 | He's in prison. |
| in church | 교회에서 (예배 중) | They're in church. |

### From the Original Book:
> "I stayed **in bed** until 10 o'clock."
> 10시까지 침대에 있었다.

> "Jack's father is **in hospital**." (영국식)
> Jack의 아버지는 병원에 입원해 있다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was in bed when the alarm went off."**
> 알람이 울렸을 때 침대에 있었다.

> **"I stayed in bed until 7:23."**
> 7시 23분까지 침대에 있었다.

---

## C. Arrive at / Arrive in (도착)

**Arrive**와 함께 쓰는 전치사:

| arrive at | arrive in |
|-----------|-----------|
| 특정 장소/지점 | 도시/나라 |
| arrive at school | arrive in Jeju |
| arrive at the station | arrive in Korea |
| arrive at the airport | arrive in Seoul |

### From the Original Book:
> "What time did you **arrive at** the party?"
> 파티에 몇 시에 도착했어?

> "We **arrived in** Paris at 3 o'clock."
> 우리는 3시에 파리에 도착했다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I arrived at school at 8:40."**
> 8시 40분에 학교에 도착했다.

> **"We arrived in Jeju last year."**
> 작년에 제주에 도착했다.

> **"When I arrived at the finish line, everyone cheered."**
> 결승선에 도착했을 때, 모두가 환호했다.

---

## D. On a bus / On a train / On a plane (대중교통)

**대중교통**에는 **on**을 사용합니다.

| Expression | Example |
|------------|---------|
| on a bus | I was on a bus. |
| on a train | She's on the train. |
| on a plane | They're on a plane. |
| on a ship | We're on a ship. |
| on a bike | He's on a bike. |

### But: In a car / In a taxi (개인 차량)

**개인 차량**에는 **in**을 사용합니다.

| Expression | Example |
|------------|---------|
| in a car | I got in the car. |
| in a taxi | She's in a taxi. |

### From the Original Book:
> "I met her **on** the train."
> 기차에서 그녀를 만났다.

> "She arrived **in** a taxi."
> 그녀는 택시로 도착했다.

### From the Novel:

> **"I was on the bus for 40 minutes."**
> 40분 동안 버스에 타고 있었다.

> **"Dad drove me in the car."**
> 아빠가 차로 데려다 주셨다.

---

## E. On the way / On the left / On the right (방향/경로)

**On**은 길이나 방향을 나타낼 때도 사용합니다.

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| on the way | 가는 길에 | On the way to school... |
| on the left | 왼쪽에 | The school is on the left. |
| on the right | 오른쪽에 | Turn right. It's on the right. |
| on the corner | 모퉁이에 | The shop is on the corner. |

### From the Original Book:
> "I bought this **on the way** home."
> 집에 오는 길에 이걸 샀다.

> "The bank is **on the left**."
> 은행은 왼쪽에 있다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"On the way to school, I saw rapeseed flowers."**
> 학교 가는 길에 유채꽃을 봤다.

> **"On the way home, I thought about the bus."**
> 집에 가는 길에 버스에 대해 생각했다.

> **"The bus stop is on the left."**
> 버스 정류장은 왼쪽에 있다.

---

## F. At the top / At the bottom / In the middle (위치)

**위치**를 나타내는 표현:

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| at the top | 맨 위에 | At the top of the hill... |
| at the bottom | 맨 아래에 | At the bottom of the page... |
| in the middle | 가운데에 | In the middle of the road... |
| at the front | 앞에 | At the front of the line... |
| at the back | 뒤에 | At the back of the room... |

### From the Original Book:
> "Write your name **at the top** of the page."
> 페이지 맨 위에 이름을 적어.

### From the Novel:

> **"In elementary school, I was near the bottom in the endurance run."**
> 초등학교 때 오래달리기에서 거의 꼴찌였다.

> **"The classroom is at the end of the hall."**
> 교실은 복도 끝에 있다.

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | 설명 |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| ~~I arrived in school.~~ | I arrived **at** school. | 특정 장소는 at |
| ~~I arrived at Korea.~~ | I arrived **in** Korea. | 나라/도시는 in |
| ~~I was in the bus.~~ | I was **on** the bus. | 대중교통은 on |
| ~~in the way home~~ | **on** the way home | 가는 길에는 on |
| ~~at home's~~ | **at** home / **at** my house | at home (관사 없음) |
| ~~I got on the car.~~ | I got **in** the car. | 작은 차량은 in |

## Exercises

### A. at, in, on 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. I was _______ home when you called.
2. She arrived _______ school on time.
3. We arrived _______ Jeju yesterday.
4. I was _______ the bus for an hour.
5. He got _______ the car and drove away.
6. She's _______ work right now.
7. I stayed _______ bed until noon.
8. _______ the way to school, I saw birds.

**Answers:**
1. at
2. at
3. in
4. on
5. in
6. at
7. in
8. On

### B. 빈칸을 채우세요.

1. I stayed _______ bed until 7 a.m.
2. _______ the way to school, I saw birds.
3. The school is _______ the right.
4. I arrived _______ the finish line exhausted.
5. She works _______ a hospital.
6. The answer is _______ the bottom of the page.
7. I met her _______ the airport.
8. We were _______ a plane for 3 hours.

**Answers:**
1. in
2. On
3. on
4. at
5. at / in (at = 일함, in = 병원 안에)
6. at
7. at
8. on

### C. arrive at 또는 arrive in을 사용해 문장을 완성하세요.

1. We _______ the airport at 6 a.m.
2. They _______ Korea last week.
3. I _______ school late.
4. When did you _______ Jeju?
5. She _______ the party early.
6. We finally _______ Seoul.

**Answers:**
1. arrived at
2. arrived in
3. arrived at
4. arrive in
5. arrived at
6. arrived in

### D. 틀린 부분을 고치세요.

1. I arrived in school at 8.
   → I arrived **at** school at 8.

2. She's in work now.
   → She's **at** work now.

3. I was in the bus for an hour.
   → I was **on** the bus for an hour.

4. In the way home, I bought a snack.
   → **On** the way home, I bought a snack.

5. He got on the car.
   → He got **in** the car.

## Summary: 장소 전치사 정리표

| 표현 | 전치사 | 예문 |
|------|--------|------|
| home / work / school | at | at home, at work, at school |
| 침대 / 병원 | in | in bed, in hospital |
| 버스 / 기차 | on | on the bus, on the train |
| 차 / 택시 | in | in the car, in a taxi |
| 가는 길에 | on | on the way |
| 왼쪽/오른쪽 | on | on the left, on the right |
| 도착 (장소) | at | arrive at school |
| 도착 (도시/나라) | in | arrive in Jeju |

## 선택 가이드

```
일상 장소 (home, work, school)? → At
"I'm at work."

침대/병원에 (상태)? → In
"I was in bed."

대중교통 (bus, train)? → On
"I was on the bus."

작은 차량 (car, taxi)? → In
"I got in the car."

가는 길에? → On the way
"On the way to school..."

도착 + 건물/장소? → Arrive at
"I arrived at the station."

도착 + 도시/나라? → Arrive in
"I arrived in Seoul."
```
