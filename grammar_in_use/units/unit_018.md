# Unit 18: Used to (do)

## A. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

**used to + 동사원형**: 과거에는 그랬지만 **지금은 더 이상 아닌** 상태나 습관

### From the Original Book:
> "I **used to play** tennis. I don't play now."
> 예전에는 테니스를 쳤다. 지금은 안 친다.

> "David **used to live** in London, but now he lives in Manchester."
> 데이비드는 예전에 런던에 살았지만, 지금은 맨체스터에 산다.

---

## B. 형태 (Form)

| Type | Structure | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Positive | used to + verb | I used to run. |
| Negative | didn't use to + verb | I didn't use to like running. |
| Question | Did + subject + use to + verb? | Did you use to run? |

### Pronunciation:
- **used to** = /juːst tə/ (fast speech: /juːstə/)

---

## C. 과거의 습관 (Past Habits)

### From the Novel:

> **"I've always gotten carsick on long rides."**
> 나는 오래 타면 항상 차멀미를 했다.
> = I **used to get** carsick on long rides. (지금은 덜하다면)

> **"In elementary school, I was near the bottom in the endurance run."**
> 초등학교 때 오래달리기에서 거의 꼴찌였다.
> = I **used to be** near the bottom. (지금은 아님)

> **"Going up to our third-floor classroom used to leave me breathless."**
> 3층 교실까지 올라가면 숨이 찼다.
> = It **used to** make me breathless. (지금은 쉽게 올라감)

---

## D. 과거의 상태 (Past States)

과거에는 그랬던 **상태**를 표현합니다.

### From the Novel:

> **"I hate endurance runs. No, hated."**
> 오래달리기가 싫다. 아니, 싫**었다**.
> = I **used to hate** endurance runs. (지금은 아님)

> **"I used to dread opening my eyes in the morning."**
> 예전에는 아침에 눈 뜨는 게 싫었다.

> **"The bus used to make me feel sick every morning."**
> 버스 타면 매일 아침 속이 안 좋았다.

---

## E. 부정문과 의문문 (Negatives and Questions)

### 부정문: didn't use to

> **"I didn't use to like walking."**
> 예전에는 걷는 걸 좋아하지 않았다.

> **"I didn't use to have much stamina."**
> 예전에는 체력이 별로 없었다.

> **"She didn't use to exercise at all."**
> 그녀는 예전에 운동을 전혀 안 했다.

### 의문문: Did ... use to?

> **"Did you use to take the bus every day?"**
> 예전에 매일 버스 탔어?

> **"Did you use to get motion sickness?"**
> 예전에 멀미했어?

> **"Where did you use to live?"**
> 예전에 어디 살았어?

---

## F. Used to vs Past Simple

| Aspect | Used to | Past Simple |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Focus | 과거 습관/상태 강조 | 단순한 과거 사실 |
| Contrast | 지금과의 대비 암시 | 대비 없음 |
| Duration | 어느 정도 기간 동안 | 특정 시점/일회성 |

### From the Novel:

> "I **used to hate** endurance runs." (과거 습관, 지금은 아님)
> "I **hated** the bus this morning." (그날의 사실, 한 번)

> "He **used to walk** slowly." (과거 습관)
> "He **walked** slowly yesterday." (어제의 사실)

---

## G. Used to vs Be used to vs Get used to

**주의:** 완전히 다른 표현입니다!

| Expression | Meaning | Structure | Example |
|------------|---------|-----------|---------|
| used to + verb | 예전에는 ~했다 | used to + 동사원형 | I **used to take** the bus. |
| be used to + noun/-ing | ~에 익숙하다 | am/is/are used to + 명사/-ing | I **am used to walking** now. |
| get used to + noun/-ing | ~에 익숙해지다 | get used to + 명사/-ing | I **got used to** the pain. |

### From the Novel:

> "I **used to get** carsick." (예전에 차멀미를 했다)
> "Now I **am used to walking** long distances." (이제 오래 걷는 데 익숙하다)
> "I **got used to** waking up early." (일찍 일어나는 데 익숙해졌다)

## H. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~I use to run.~~ | I **used to** run. | used (과거형) |
| ~~I didn't used to like it.~~ | I **didn't use to** like it. | didn't + use |
| ~~Did you used to run?~~ | **Did** you **use to** run? | did + use |
| ~~I'm used to run.~~ | I'm used to **running**. | be used to + -ing |
| ~~I used to running.~~ | I **used to run**. | used to + 동사원형 |
| ~~He is used to drive.~~ | He is used to **driving**. | be used to + -ing |

## I. 소설 속 변화 (Character's Transformation)

도윤이의 변화를 used to로 표현:

| Before (Used to) | Now |
|------------------|-----|
| I used to hate exercise. | I enjoy running now. |
| I used to get motion sick. | I rarely feel sick now. |
| I used to be near the bottom in running. | I can run 10km now. |
| I used to dread the bus. | I walk to school happily. |
| I used to have no stamina. | I've built up my stamina. |
| I used to avoid PE class. | I look forward to running. |

## Exercises

### A. used to로 문장 만들기

Write sentences with "used to":

1. Before: I hated running. Now: I like it.
   → I **used to hate** running.

2. Before: The bus made me sick. Now: I walk.
   → The bus **used to make** me sick.

3. Before: I was weak. Now: I'm stronger.
   → I **used to be** weak.

4. Before: She lived in Seoul. Now: She lives in Jeju.
   → She **used to live** in Seoul.

5. Before: He smoked. Now: He doesn't smoke.
   → He **used to smoke**.

### B. 빈칸 채우기

1. I _______ (used to / get) carsick on long rides.
2. She _______ (didn't / use to) like walking.
3. _______ (Did / you / use to) take the bus?
4. He _______ (used to / be) near the bottom in running.
5. They _______ (didn't / use to) exercise regularly.
6. Where _______ (did / she / use to) live?
7. I _______ (used to / hate) mornings.
8. He _______ (used to / have) a lot of friends.

**Answers:**
1. used to get
2. didn't use to
3. Did you use to
4. used to be
5. didn't use to
6. did she use to
7. used to hate
8. used to have

### C. Used to vs Be used to

Choose the correct form:

1. I _______ walk to school. (예전에 걸어다녔다)
   → **used to**

2. I _______ walking now. (이제 걷는 게 익숙하다)
   → **am used to**

3. She _______ live in Seoul. (예전에 서울에 살았다)
   → **used to**

4. He _______ the early mornings. (이른 아침에 익숙하다)
   → **is used to**

5. I _______ hate running. (예전에 싫어했다)
   → **used to**

6. She _______ running long distances. (익숙하다)
   → **is used to**

### D. 부정문과 의문문 만들기

Make negative or question forms:

1. I used to like running. (부정)
   → I **didn't use to** like running.

2. He used to take the bus. (의문)
   → **Did he use to** take the bus?

3. She used to live here. (부정)
   → She **didn't use to** live here.

4. They used to exercise. (의문)
   → **Did they use to** exercise?

5. You used to get up early. (의문)
   → **Did you use to** get up early?

## Story Summary with "Used to"

> Doyun **used to hate** taking the bus. He **used to get** motion sickness every morning. He **used to think** he had no stamina. He **used to be** near the bottom in the endurance run.
>
> But things changed. He started walking to school. Now he doesn't get sick anymore. He **used to dread** mornings, but now he enjoys the fresh air and the rapeseed flowers along the way.
>
> He **used to avoid** exercise, but now he runs marathons. He **used to hate** his body, but now he's proud of what it can do.

## Comparison: Used to vs Would

Both can describe past habits, but:

| Used to | Would |
|---------|-------|
| 과거 상태와 습관 모두 | 반복된 과거 행동만 |
| I used to live in Seoul. (O) | ~~I would live in Seoul.~~ (X - 상태) |
| I used to run every day. (O) | I would run every day. (O - 행동) |

**Note:** "Would" for past habits is more formal and literary.
