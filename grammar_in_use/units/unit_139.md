# Unit 139: Phrasal Verbs (3) - out (continued)

## A. 개념 설명 (Grammar Explanation)

### English
The particle **out** has many meanings beyond "going outside." It can indicate **completion**, **distribution**, **discovery**, **exhaustion**, or **loudness**. This unit focuses on these extended meanings of "out."

### Korean
**out**은 "밖으로 나가다"라는 의미 외에도 다양한 뜻을 가집니다. **완료**, **배포**, **발견**, **소진**, **크게/소리 높여** 등의 의미로 확장됩니다. 이 유닛에서는 이러한 확장된 의미를 다룹니다.

---

## B. OUT의 확장 의미 (Extended Meanings of OUT)

### 1. 완료/끝까지 (Completion)

**Out**이 "완전히, 끝까지"의 의미를 더할 때

| Phrasal Verb | Meaning |
|--------------|---------|
| work out | 해결하다, 완성하다 |
| figure out | 알아내다, 이해하다 |
| carry out | 수행하다 |
| fill out | (양식을) 작성하다 |
| sort out | 정리하다 |
| clean out | 깨끗이 치우다 |

> **"I worked out a training plan."**
> 훈련 계획을 세웠다. (완성했다)

> **"I figured out what to do."**
> 어떻게 해야 할지 알아냈다.

### 2. 배포/나눠줌 (Distribution)

| Phrasal Verb | Meaning |
|--------------|---------|
| hand out | 나눠주다 |
| give out | 나눠주다, 배포하다 |
| pass out | 나눠주다/기절하다 |
| send out | 발송하다 |

> **"The teacher handed out the papers."**
> 선생님이 종이를 나눠주셨다.

> **"They gave out water at the checkpoint."**
> 체크포인트에서 물을 나눠줬다.

### 3. 발견/드러남 (Discovery/Revelation)

| Phrasal Verb | Meaning |
|--------------|---------|
| find out | 알아내다 |
| turn out | 밝혀지다, ~인 것으로 드러나다 |
| point out | 지적하다 |
| check out | 확인하다 |

> **"It turns out I can run!"**
> 알고 보니 나도 달릴 수 있구나!

> **"I found out about the race online."**
> 온라인에서 그 대회에 대해 알게 됐다.

### 4. 소멸/소진 (Exhaustion/Ending)

| Phrasal Verb | Meaning |
|--------------|---------|
| run out (of) | 다 떨어지다 |
| wear out | 닳다, 지치다 |
| burn out | 소진되다, 타버리다 |
| die out | 멸종하다 |
| fade out | 서서히 사라지다 |

> **"I'm worn out after running 10km."**
> 10km를 달리고 나니 완전히 지쳤다.

> **"My shoes are worn out."**
> 신발이 다 닳았다.

### 5. 크게/소리 높여 (Loudness)

| Phrasal Verb | Meaning |
|--------------|---------|
| cry out | 외치다 |
| call out | 크게 부르다 |
| shout out | 소리치다 |
| read out | 소리 내어 읽다 |

> **"She called out my name."**
> 그녀가 내 이름을 큰 소리로 불렀다.

## C. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: work out (해결하다/운동하다)

> **"I worked out a training plan with my teacher."**
> 선생님과 훈련 계획을 세웠다.

> **"I need to work out these sore legs."**
> 뻐근한 다리를 풀어야 겠다.

**주의:** work out의 두 가지 의미
| Meaning | Example |
|---------|---------|
| 해결하다/계획하다 | I worked out a plan. |
| 운동하다 | I work out every day. |

### Example 2: turn out (결과가 ~로 나오다)

> **"Here, try these. They turned out really good this year."**
> 여기, 이거 먹어봐. 올해 정말 맛있게 나왔어.

> **"It turned out I could run further than I thought."**
> 생각보다 더 멀리 달릴 수 있다는 게 밝혀졌다.

### Example 3: find out (알아내다)

> **"I'll find out when I do it."**
> 해보면 알게 될 거야.

> **"I found out about the marathon race."**
> 마라톤 대회에 대해 알게 됐다.

### Example 4: break out (발생하다)

> **"Cold sweat broke out on my forehead. I closed my eyes."**
> 이마에 식은땀이 났다. 나는 눈을 감았다.

> **"I broke out of the slump."**
> 슬럼프에서 벗어났다.

### Example 5: run out (다 떨어지다)

> **"I ran out of water during the run."**
> 달릴 때 물이 다 떨어졌다.

## E. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~turn off well~~ (결과) | **turn out well** | 결과는 turn out |
| ~~find up~~ | **find out** | 알아내다는 find out |
| ~~I work up every day.~~ (운동) | I **work out** every day. | 운동하다는 work out |
| ~~The water ran up.~~ | The water **ran out**. | 소진은 run out |
| ~~It turned up he was right.~~ | It **turned out** he was right. | 드러남은 turn out |
| ~~run out water~~ | **run out of** water | run out **of** + 명사 |

### work out vs work up 비교:

| work out | work up |
|----------|---------|
| 해결하다/운동하다 | (감정을) 고조시키다 |
| I worked out a plan. | I worked up the courage. |
| 계획을 세웠다. | 용기를 냈다. |

## Exercises

### A. 알맞은 구동사 선택하기

1. I need to _______ a training schedule. (만들다/해결하다)
   - a) find out  b) work out  c) run out

2. The tangerines _______ really good this year. (결과가 나오다)
   - a) turned out  b) broke out  c) went out

3. I _______ of water during the race. (다 떨어지다)
   - a) worked out  b) found out  c) ran out

4. Cold sweat _______ on my forehead. (나다/발생하다)
   - a) turned out  b) broke out  c) went out

5. I _______ about the race online. (알게 되다)
   - a) found out  b) turned out  c) ran out

**Answers:**
1. b) work out
2. a) turned out
3. c) ran out
4. b) broke out
5. a) found out

### C. 틀린 부분 고치기 (Error Correction)

1. I run out water during the race.
2. It turned off that he was right.
3. I find up the answer.
4. I need to work up a plan.
5. She broke in to the song.

**Answers:**
1. I **ran out of** water during the race.
2. It **turned out** that he was right.
3. I **found out** the answer.
4. I need to **work out** a plan.
5. She **broke out** into the song. (갑자기 노래하기 시작했다)

## OUT의 다양한 의미 요약 (Summary)

| Meaning | Phrasal Verbs |
|---------|---------------|
| **완료** (completion) | work out, figure out, carry out, sort out |
| **발견** (discovery) | find out, turn out, check out, point out |
| **소멸** (exhaustion) | run out, wear out, burn out, die out |
| **발생** (occurrence) | break out, come out |
| **나가다** (exit) | go out, step out, get out |
| **배포** (distribution) | hand out, give out, pass out, send out |
| **크게** (loudness) | call out, shout out, cry out |

## 비교: 유사한 OUT 구동사

| Phrasal Verb | Similar to | Difference |
|--------------|------------|------------|
| work out | figure out | work out (해결) vs figure out (이해) |
| find out | turn out | find out (알아내다) vs turn out (드러나다) |
| hand out | give out | 거의 같음 (나눠주다) |
| run out | wear out | run out (소진) vs wear out (닳다/지치다) |
