# Unit 47: Reported Speech (1) - Statements

## A. 직접화법과 간접화법

| 직접화법 (Direct) | 간접화법 (Indirect) |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| He said, "**I run** every day." | He said (that) he **ran** every day. |
| She said, "**I am** tired." | She said (that) she **was** tired. |

---

## B. 시제 변화 (Tense Changes)

직접화법에서 간접화법으로 바꿀 때 **시제가 한 단계 뒤로** 갑니다.

| 직접화법 | 간접화법 |
|----------|----------|
| Present Simple (run) | Past Simple (**ran**) |
| Present Continuous (am running) | Past Continuous (**was running**) |
| Present Perfect (have run) | Past Perfect (**had run**) |
| Past Simple (ran) | Past Perfect (**had run**) |
| will | **would** |
| can | **could** |
| may | **might** |

---

## C. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### 1. Present → Past

**직접화법:**
> Mom said, **"Nervous? First day and all?"**

**간접화법:**
> Mom asked if I **was** nervous about the first day.

**직접화법:**
> Minseo said, **"I get on at Jocheon."**
> 민서가 "나 조천에서 타"라고 말했다.

**간접화법:**
> Minseo said (that) he **got** on at Jocheon.
> 민서가 조천에서 탄다고 말했다.

### 2. Present Perfect → Past Perfect

**직접화법:**
> The coach said, **"You have improved a lot."**
> 코치가 "너 많이 늘었다"라고 말했다.

**간접화법:**
> The coach said (that) I **had improved** a lot.
> 코치가 내가 많이 늘었다고 말했다.

### 3. Will → Would

**직접화법:**
> Dad said, **"I will wait at the finish line."**
> 아빠가 "결승선에서 기다릴게"라고 말했다.

**간접화법:**
> Dad said (that) he **would wait** at the finish line.
> 아빠가 결승선에서 기다리겠다고 말했다.

### 4. Can → Could

**직접화법:**
> Mom said, **"You can do it."**
> 엄마가 "넌 할 수 있어"라고 말했다.

**간접화법:**
> Mom said (that) I **could** do it.
> 엄마가 내가 할 수 있다고 말했다.

## D. 대명사 변화 (Pronoun Changes)

직접화법에서 간접화법으로 바꿀 때 **대명사**도 바꿉니다.

| 직접화법 | 간접화법 |
|----------|----------|
| I | he/she |
| you | I/he/she/they |
| my | his/her |
| we | they |
| us | them |

### Examples from the Novel:

**직접화법:**
> Minseo said, **"I'll** wait for **you**."

**간접화법:**
> Minseo said (that) **he** would wait for **me**.

**직접화법:**
> Mom said, "**I'm** proud of **you**."

**간접화법:**
> Mom said (that) **she** was proud of **me**.

---

## E. 시간/장소 표현 변화

| 직접화법 | 간접화법 |
|----------|----------|
| today | that day |
| tomorrow | the next day / the following day |
| yesterday | the day before / the previous day |
| now | then |
| here | there |
| this | that |

### Examples from the Novel:

**직접화법:**
> She said, "I'll see you **tomorrow**."

**간접화법:**
> She said (that) she would see me **the next day**.

**직접화법:**
> He said, "I ran **yesterday**."

**간접화법:**
> He said (that) he had run **the day before**.

---

## F. Say vs Tell

| Say | Tell |
|-----|------|
| say (that)... | tell + 사람 + (that)... |
| say something | tell someone something |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **He said** (that) he would come.
> 그가 오겠다고 말했다.

> **He told me** (that) he would come.
> 그가 나에게 오겠다고 말했다.

> The coach **said** (that) I had improved.
> The coach **told me** (that) I had improved.

## Exercises

### A. 간접화법으로 바꾸세요.

1. He said, "I run every day."
   → He said (that) he _______ every day.

2. She said, "I am tired."
   → She said (that) she _______ tired.

3. Mom said, "I will wait for you."
   → Mom said (that) she _______ wait for me.

4. The coach said, "You have improved."
   → The coach said (that) I _______.

5. Minseo said, "I can help you."
   → Minseo said (that) he _______ help me.

**Answers:**
1. ran
2. was
3. would
4. had improved
5. could

### B. 대명사를 바꾸어 간접화법으로 쓰세요.

1. She said to me, "I like your shoes."
   → She told me (that) she _______ _______ shoes.

2. He said, "I'll see you tomorrow."
   → He said (that) he _______ see _______ _______.

3. Mom said, "You can do it."
   → Mom said (that) I _______ do it.

**Answers:**
1. liked my
2. would / me / the next day
3. could

### C. Say 또는 Tell을 골라 빈칸을 채우세요.

1. He _______ (that) he was tired.
2. He _______ me (that) he was tired.
3. She _______ (that) she would come.
4. She _______ us (that) she would come.

**Answers:**
1. said
2. told
3. said
4. told

### D. 시간 표현을 바꿔 간접화법으로 쓰세요.

1. He said, "I will run tomorrow."
   → He said (that) he would run _______.

2. She said, "I ran yesterday."
   → She said (that) she had run _______.

3. He said, "I am running now."
   → He said (that) he was running _______.

**Answers:**
1. the next day / the following day
2. the day before / the previous day
3. then

## Key Sentences (암기 문장)

| Direct | Indirect |
|--------|----------|
| "I run every day." | He said he ran every day. |
| "I am tired." | She said she was tired. |
| "I will wait for you." | He said he would wait for me. |
| "You have improved." | The coach said I had improved. |
| "I can help you." | She said she could help me. |

---

## Summary

```
간접화법 시제 변화:
Present → Past
Present Perfect → Past Perfect
Past → Past Perfect
will → would
can → could
may → might

대명사 변화:
I → he/she
you → I/me
my → his/her

시간 표현 변화:
today → that day
tomorrow → the next day
yesterday → the day before
now → then
```
