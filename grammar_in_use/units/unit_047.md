# Unit 47: Reported Speech (1) - Statements

## A. 직접화법과 간접화법 (Direct and Reported Speech)

**직접화법:** 말한 내용을 그대로 인용 (" " 사용)
**간접화법:** 말한 내용을 전달하는 형태로 바꿈

### From the Original Book:

| 직접화법 (Direct) | 간접화법 (Reported) |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| "**I'm tired**," she said. | She said (that) she **was tired**. |
| "**I don't like** it," he said. | He said (that) he **didn't like** it. |
| "**I'm going** home," she said. | She said (that) she **was going** home. |

### 기본 변화:
- 시제가 한 단계 뒤로
- 대명사가 바뀜
- 시간/장소 표현이 바뀔 수 있음

---

## B. 시제 변화 (Tense Changes)

직접화법에서 간접화법으로 바꿀 때 **시제가 한 단계 뒤로** 갑니다.

### From the Original Book:

| 직접화법 | 간접화법 | 명칭 |
|----------|----------|------|
| "I **run**" | He said he **ran** | 현재→과거 |
| "I **am running**" | He said he **was running** | 현재진행→과거진행 |
| "I **have run**" | He said he **had run** | 현재완료→과거완료 |
| "I **ran**" | He said he **had run** | 과거→과거완료 |
| "I **will** run" | He said he **would** run | will→would |
| "I **can** run" | He said he **could** run | can→could |
| "I **may** run" | He said he **might** run | may→might |
| "I **must** go" | He said he **had to** go | must→had to |

---

## C. 시제 변화 예문 (Examples)

### 1. Present → Past

**직접화법:**
> Mom said, **"Nervous? First day and all?"**

**간접화법:**
> Mom asked if I **was** nervous about the first day.

**직접화법:**
> Minseo said, **"I get on at Jocheon."**
> 민서가 "나 조천에서 타"라고 말했다.

**간접화법:**
> Minseo said (that) he **got** on at Jocheon.
> 민서가 조천에서 탄다고 말했다.

### 2. Present Perfect → Past Perfect

**직접화법:**
> The coach said, **"You have improved a lot."**
> 코치가 "너 많이 늘었다"라고 말했다.

**간접화법:**
> The coach said (that) I **had improved** a lot.
> 코치가 내가 많이 늘었다고 말했다.

### 3. Will → Would

**직접화법:**
> Dad said, **"I will wait at the finish line."**
> 아빠가 "결승선에서 기다릴게"라고 말했다.

**간접화법:**
> Dad said (that) he **would wait** at the finish line.
> 아빠가 결승선에서 기다리겠다고 말했다.

### 4. Can → Could

**직접화법:**
> Mom said, **"You can do it."**
> 엄마가 "넌 할 수 있어"라고 말했다.

**간접화법:**
> Mom said (that) I **could** do it.
> 엄마가 내가 할 수 있다고 말했다.

## D. 대명사 변화 (Pronoun Changes)

직접화법에서 간접화법으로 바꿀 때 **대명사**도 바꿉니다.

### From the Original Book:

| 직접화법 | 간접화법 |
|----------|----------|
| **I** | he/she |
| **you** | I/he/she/they |
| **my** | his/her |
| **we** | they |
| **us** | them |
| **our** | their |

### Examples from the Novel:

**직접화법:**
> Minseo said, **"I'll** wait for **you**."

**간접화법:**
> Minseo said (that) **he** would wait for **me**.

**직접화법:**
> Mom said, "**I'm** proud of **you**."

**간접화법:**
> Mom said (that) **she** was proud of **me**.

**직접화법:**
> They said, "**We** will help **you**."

**간접화법:**
> They said (that) **they** would help **me**.

---

## E. 시간/장소 표현 변화 (Time and Place Changes)

### From the Original Book:

| 직접화법 | 간접화법 |
|----------|----------|
| today | **that day** |
| tomorrow | **the next day** / the following day |
| yesterday | **the day before** / the previous day |
| now | **then** |
| here | **there** |
| this | **that** |
| these | **those** |
| ago | **before** |
| last week | **the week before** |
| next week | **the following week** |

### Examples from the Novel:

**직접화법:**
> She said, "I'll see you **tomorrow**."

**간접화법:**
> She said (that) she would see me **the next day**.

**직접화법:**
> He said, "I ran **yesterday**."

**간접화법:**
> He said (that) he had run **the day before**.

**직접화법:**
> She said, "I'm running **now**."

**간접화법:**
> She said (that) she was running **then**.

## F. Say vs Tell

| Say | Tell |
|-----|------|
| say (that)... | tell + **사람** + (that)... |
| say something | tell **someone** something |
| "He said..." | "He told me..." |

### From the Original Book:

> "He **said** (that) he would come."
> 그가 오겠다고 말했다.

> "He **told me** (that) he would come."
> 그가 나에게 오겠다고 말했다.

### 주의:
- **Say**는 사람 목적어 없이 사용
- **Tell**은 반드시 사람 목적어와 함께 사용

| 올바름 (O) | 틀림 (X) |
|-----------|----------|
| He **said** he was tired. | He **said me** he was tired. |
| He **told me** he was tired. | He **told** he was tired. |

### Examples from the Novel:

> The coach **said** (that) I had improved.
> The coach **told me** (that) I had improved.

---

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| 틀린 문장 (X) | 올바른 문장 (O) | 설명 |
|---------------|-----------------|------|
| He said **me** he was tired. | He **told me** he was tired. | say + me (X) |
| She **told** she was happy. | She **said** she was happy. | tell + 목적어 필요 |
| He said he **is** tired. | He said he **was** tired. | 시제 변화 필요 |
| She said **"I am"** tired. | She said she **was** tired. | 간접화법 형태 |
| He said I **will** come. | He said he **would** come. | 대명사+시제 변화 |
| She told that she was happy. | She said that she was happy. | tell + 사람 필요 |

### 시제 변화 누락:

```
(X) He said he is coming tomorrow.
(O) He said he was coming the next day.

직접화법→간접화법: 시제 한 단계 뒤로
```

## H. 시제를 바꾸지 않아도 되는 경우

### 일반적인 사실이나 여전히 사실인 내용:

> He said that the earth **is** round.
> 그가 지구가 둥글다고 말했다. (사실)

> She said she **likes** coffee.
> 그녀가 커피를 좋아한다고 말했다. (여전히 사실)

### From the Original Book:

> "He told me his name **is** Tom."
> (아직도 Tom이므로)

   → He said (that) he _______ every day.

2. She said, "I am tired."
   → She said (that) she _______ tired.

3. Mom said, "I will wait for you."
   → Mom said (that) she _______ wait for me.

4. The coach said, "You have improved."
   → The coach said (that) I _______.

   → Minseo said (that) he _______ help me.

6. She said, "I'm going home."
   → She said (that) she _______ home.

   → He said (that) he _______ come the next day.

| Direct | Reported | 변화 |
|--------|----------|------|
| "I run every day." | He said he ran every day. | run→ran |
| "I am tired." | She said she was tired. | am→was |
| "I will wait for you." | He said he would wait for me. | will→would |
| "You have improved." | The coach said I had improved. | have→had |

| "I'm coming tomorrow." | He said he was coming the next day. | tomorrow→the next day |
| "I saw him yesterday." | She said she had seen him the day before. | saw→had seen |

## Summary: 간접화법 시제/대명사/시간 변화표

### 시제 변화:
```
Present → Past
Present Perfect → Past Perfect
Past → Past Perfect
will → would
can → could
may → might
must → had to
```

### 대명사 변화:
```
I → he/she
you → I/me
my → his/her
we → they
us → them
```

### 시간 표현 변화:
```
today → that day
tomorrow → the next day
yesterday → the day before
now → then
here → there
this → that
```

### Say vs Tell:
```
say + (that) + 절: He said that he was tired.
tell + 사람 + (that) + 절: He told me that he was tired.
```
