# Unit 86: No/none/any / Nothing/nobody, etc.

## A. 형태와 기본 개념 (Form and Basic Concept)

**no**로 시작하는 단어들은 **부정**을 나타냅니다.

| Word | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **no** + 명사 | ~이 없다 | no time, no money |
| **nothing** | 아무것도 ~ 않다 | nothing happened |
| **nobody/no one** | 아무도 ~ 않다 | nobody came |
| **nowhere** | 아무 데도 ~ 않다 | nowhere to go |
| **none** | 하나도 없다 (대명사) | none left |

### no vs not any:
- **no** + 명사 = **not any** + 명사
- "I have **no** money" = "I don't have **any** money"

---

## B. 소설 속 예문: no, nothing, nobody, nowhere

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"There was no space to move."**
> 움직일 공간이 없었다.
- no space = 공간이 없다

> **"I said nothing."**
> 아무 말도 안 했다.
- nothing = 아무것도 아닌 것

> **"Nobody was there."**
> 아무도 없었다.
- nobody = 아무도

> **"I had no experience."**
> 경험이 없었다.
- no experience

> **"There was nowhere to sit."**
> 앉을 곳이 없었다.
- nowhere = 아무 데도

---

## C. no vs not any (Equivalence)

**no + 명사** = **not any + 명사** (같은 의미)

| no | not any |
|----|---------|
| I have **no** time. | I **don't have any** time. |
| There is **no** water. | There **isn't any** water. |
| She has **no** friends. | She **doesn't have any** friends. |

### From the Novel:

> **"There was no space."** = **"There wasn't any space."**
> 공간이 없었다.

> **"I had no stamina."** = **"I didn't have any stamina."**
> 체력이 없었다.

> **"No one came."** = **"Not anyone came."**
> 아무도 안 왔다.

### 뉘앙스 차이:
- **no**: 더 강조, 더 직접적
- **not any**: 좀 더 일반적

---

## D. none: 대명사 (Pronoun)

**none**은 명사 없이 단독으로 쓰입니다.

### As a pronoun:

> **"How many friends came?" - "None."**
> 친구 몇 명 왔어? - 한 명도 안 왔어.

> **"Is there any water left?" - "None."**
> 물 남았어? - 하나도 없어.

> **"I wanted some cake, but there was none."**
> 케이크 먹고 싶었는데 하나도 없었다.

### none of + 복수명사/대명사:

> **"None of my friends came."**
> 내 친구들 중 아무도 안 왔다.

> **"None of them knew."**
> 그들 중 아무도 몰랐다.

> **"None of the runners gave up."**
> 러너들 중 아무도 포기하지 않았다.

---

## E. nothing, nobody/no one, nowhere (Compound Negatives)

| Word | Question | Answer |
|------|----------|--------|
| **nothing** | What happened? | Nothing. (아무것도) |
| **nobody/no one** | Who came? | Nobody. / No one. (아무도) |
| **nowhere** | Where did you go? | Nowhere. (아무 데도) |

### From the Novel:

> **"Nothing changed."**
> 아무것도 안 변했다.

> **"No one heard me."**
> 아무도 내 말을 못 들었다.

> **"I had nowhere to go."**
> 갈 곳이 없었다.

### 주의: nobody vs no one
- 의미는 같음
- **no one**이 좀 더 격식체
- **nobody**가 좀 더 일상적

---

## F. 이중 부정 금지 (No Double Negatives)

영어에서는 **이중 부정**을 쓰지 않습니다.

| Wrong (X) | Right (O) |
|-----------|-----------|
| I don't have **nothing**. | I don't have **anything**. / I have **nothing**. |
| Nobody **didn't** come. | Nobody came. |
| I can't see **nothing**. | I can't see **anything**. / I can see **nothing**. |
| She never says **nothing**. | She never says **anything**. |

### From the Novel:

> **"I didn't say anything."** (O)
> **"I said nothing."** (O)
> ~~"I didn't say nothing."~~ (X)

### 규칙:
- **부정어 (not, never) + any/anyone/anything**
- **긍정 동사 + no/nobody/nothing**
- 둘 다 쓰면 안 됨!

---

## G. any vs no 비교 (Comparison)

| Positive context (긍정문) | Negative context |
|--------------------------|------------------|
| **any** = 어떤 것이든 | **no** = 전혀 없음 |
| Any bus is fine. | I have no bus ticket. |
| Anyone can do it. | Nobody can do it. |

### 같은 의미, 다른 구조:

| no/nothing/nobody | not any/anything/anyone |
|-------------------|-------------------------|
| I have **no** time. | I **don't have any** time. |
| **Nothing** happened. | **Didn't anything** happen? |
| **Nobody** knows. | **Doesn't anyone** know? |

---

## H. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | 설명 |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| I don't have **nothing**. | I don't have **anything**. | 이중 부정 |
| **Nobody** didn't come. | **Nobody** came. | 이중 부정 |
| I have **any** money. | I have **no** money. / I don't have **any** money. | 긍정문 + any (오류) |
| **None** time left. | **No** time left. | none은 대명사 |
| **None of** problem. | **No** problem. / **None of the** problems. | none of + the/복수 |

### 추가 실수 예시:

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) |
|-----------|-------------|
| I never see **nobody**. | I never see **anybody**. |
| He didn't go **nowhere**. | He didn't go **anywhere**. |
| **None** student came. | **No** student came. |

## Exercises

### A. no, nothing, nobody, none 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. There was _______ space to sit.
2. _______ happened during the race.
3. _______ came to see me.
4. I have _______ of the candy left.
5. I said _______.
6. "How many people came?" - "_______."
7. _______ of them knew the answer.
8. I have _______ time to waste.

**Answers:**
1. no
2. Nothing
3. Nobody/No one
4. none
5. nothing
6. None
7. None
8. no

### B. not any로 바꾸세요.

1. I have no money. → _______
2. There is no water. → _______
3. She has no friends. → _______
4. I saw no one. → _______
5. I did nothing. → _______

**Answers:**
1. I don't have any money.
2. There isn't any water.
3. She doesn't have any friends.
4. I didn't see anyone.
5. I didn't do anything.

### C. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. I didn't see (nothing / anything).
2. (Nobody / Anybody) came to the race.
3. There was (no / any) space left.
4. I have (no / none) experience.
5. "How much water?" - "(No / None)."
6. (Nothing / Anything) is impossible.
7. I never said (nothing / anything).
8. (None / No) of my friends were there.

**Answers:**
1. anything
2. Nobody
3. no
4. no
5. None
6. Nothing (아무것도 불가능하지 않다)
7. anything
8. None

### D. 틀린 부분을 고치세요.

1. I don't have no money.
2. Nobody didn't call me.
3. None student passed the test.
4. I can't find nothing.
5. She didn't go nowhere.
6. I don't never give up.

**Answers:**
1. I don't have **any** money. / I have **no** money.
2. **Nobody** called me.
3. **No** student passed the test.
4. I can't find **anything**.
5. She didn't go **anywhere**.
6. I **never** give up. / I don't **ever** give up.

## Summary: 부정 표현 정리

| Word | Usage | Example |
|------|-------|---------|
| **no** + 명사 | 형용사처럼 | no time, no space |
| **nothing** | 대명사 (것) | I said nothing. |
| **nobody/no one** | 대명사 (사람) | Nobody came. |
| **nowhere** | 부사 (장소) | Nowhere to go. |
| **none** | 대명사 (수량) | "How many?" - "None." |
| **none of** | + the/복수/대명사 | None of them. |

### no vs not any:
- **no + 명사** = **not any + 명사**
- I have no time = I don't have any time

### 이중 부정 금지:
- 부정어 (not/never) + any/anyone/anything (O)
- 부정어 + no/nobody/nothing (X - 이중 부정)
