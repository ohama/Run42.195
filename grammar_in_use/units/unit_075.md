# Unit 75: The (3) (children / the children)

## A. 일반적인 것 vs 특정한 것

**복수 명사**나 **셀 수 없는 명사**가 **일반적인 의미**일 때는 the 없이, **특정한 것**을 가리킬 때는 the를 씁니다.

| General (일반) | Specific (특정) |
|---------------|-----------------|
| Children like candy. | The children in my class are nice. |
| I like music. | I like the music in this movie. |
| Life is short. | The life of a student is busy. |

---

## B. 일반적인 의미 (no the)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I don't really like running."**
> 달리기를 별로 안 좋아한다.

- running = 달리기 일반 (NOT: the running)

> **"Kids in gym clothes were running around."**
> 체육복 입은 애들이 뛰어다니고 있었다.

- kids = 일반적인 아이들

> **"Birds were singing."**
> 새들이 노래하고 있었다.

- birds = 새 일반

> **"I learned something today."**
> 오늘 뭔가 배웠다.

- something = 일반적인 무언가

---

## C. 특정한 의미 (the)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The kids outside finished a lap around the field."**
> 밖에 있는 애들이 운동장을 한 바퀴 돌았다.

- the kids = 특정한 아이들 (outside = 수식어)

> **"The students on the bus looked tired."**
> 버스에 탄 학생들이 피곤해 보였다.

- the students = 특정한 학생들 (on the bus = 수식어)

> **"The flowers along the road were beautiful."**
> 길가의 꽃들이 예뻤다.

- the flowers = 특정한 꽃들 (along the road = 수식어)

---

## D. 추상 명사: 일반 vs 특정

### 일반적인 의미:

> **"Life is short."**
> 인생은 짧다.

> **"Time flies."**
> 시간이 빨리 간다.

### 특정한 의미:

> **"The life of a runner is tough."**
> 러너의 삶은 힘들다.

> **"The time I spent walking was not wasted."**
> 걸으면서 보낸 시간은 헛되지 않았다.

---

## E. 소설 속 비교

| General | Specific |
|---------|----------|
| "I saw **flowers**." (꽃들 일반) | "**The flowers** along the road." (특정 꽃들) |
| "**Students** were talking." (학생들 일반) | "**The students** in my class." (특정 학생들) |
| "I heard **birds**." (새 일반) | "**The birds** in the tree." (특정 새들) |
| "I like **running**." (달리기 일반) | "**The running** this morning was hard." (특정 달리기) |

---

## F. the + 형용사 = 집단

**the + 형용사**는 "~한 사람들"이라는 집단을 나타냅니다.

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| the rich | 부자들 |
| the poor | 가난한 사람들 |
| the young | 젊은이들 |
| the old | 노인들 |
| the homeless | 노숙자들 |
| the unemployed | 실업자들 |

**Note:** 복수 취급합니다.
> **"The young are energetic."** (NOT: The young is)

---

## G. 국적 + the

**국적 형용사** + the = 그 나라 국민 전체

> **"The Korean enjoy running."** (X)
> **"The Koreans enjoy running."** (O) 또는 "Korean people..."

일부 국적은 복수형이 없어서 the + 형용사 형태:
> **"The Japanese are polite."**
> **"The French love food."**

## Exercises

### A. the가 필요하면 넣으세요.

1. I like ___ music.
2. ___ music in this cafe is nice.
3. ___ students are hardworking.
4. ___ students in my class are nice.
5. ___ life is beautiful.
6. ___ life of a runner is tough.

**Answers:**
1. (nothing)
2. The
3. (nothing)
4. The
5. (nothing)
6. The

### B. 일반(G)인지 특정(S)인지 표시하세요.

1. I saw birds singing. ___
2. The birds in the tree were loud. ___
3. Kids were playing soccer. ___
4. The kids on the field looked happy. ___
5. I don't like running. ___

**Answers:**
1. G
2. S
3. G
4. S
5. G

### C. 빈칸을 채우세요.

2. ___ children in this school are polite. (특정)
3. I enjoy ___ walking. (일반)
4. ___ walking I did this morning was tiring. (특정)
5. ___ young are energetic. (집단)

**Answers:**
1. (nothing)
2. The
3. (nothing)
4. The
5. The
