# Unit 77: Names with and without the (1)

## A. 사람/장소 이름: the 없음

**사람 이름**과 대부분의 **장소 이름**에는 the를 쓰지 않습니다.

### 사람 이름:

> **"I'm Kang Minseo."** (NOT: I'm the Kang Minseo)
> 나는 강민서야.

> **"Kim Doyun, you're late?"**
> 김도윤, 지각이야?

### 장소 이름 (도시, 나라, 대륙):

> **"I live in Jeju."** (NOT: the Jeju)
> 나는 제주에 산다.

> **"I was born in Korea."**
> 나는 한국에서 태어났다.

## D. the가 필요한 지명

### 복수형 국가/섬:

> **"the United States"** (복수)
> **"the Philippines"** (복수)
> **"the Netherlands"** (복수)

### 공화국, 왕국 등:

> **"the Republic of Korea"**
> 대한민국

> **"the United Kingdom"**
> 영국

### 바다, 강, 산맥, 사막:

> **"the Pacific Ocean"** (바다)
> **"the Han River"** (강)
> **"the Alps"** (산맥)
> **"the Sahara"** (사막)

## Exercises

2. I go to ___ Jeju Middle School.
3. I get on at ___ Jocheon.
4. ___ Pacific Ocean is huge.
5. ___ Korea is in Asia.

**Answers:**
1. (nothing)
2. (nothing)

4. The

### B. 틀린 부분을 고치세요.

1. I was born in the Korea. → _______
2. The Minseo is my friend. → _______
3. I climbed the Hallasan. → _______
4. I crossed Pacific Ocean. → _______

**Answers:**
1. Korea
2. Minseo
3. Hallasan
4. the Pacific Ocean

1. ___ Grandpa Yeongcheol works in the orchard.
2. I studied at ___ Seoul National University.
3. We visited ___ Alps last summer.
4. ___ Han River flows through Seoul.
5. I live near ___ Central Park.

**Answers:**
1. (nothing)
2. (nothing)
3. the
4. The
5. (nothing)
