# Unit 108: Superlatives - the longest, the most enjoyable, etc.

## A. 최상급 만들기 (Forming superlatives)

셋 이상을 비교하여 **가장 ~한**을 나타낼 때 최상급을 사용합니다.

### Basic Rules:

| 형용사 유형 | 최상급 형태 | 예시 |
|-------------|-------------|------|
| 짧은 형용사 (1음절) | the + 형용사 + -est | the fast**est** |
| -e로 끝남 | the + 형용사 + -st | the larg**est** |
| -y로 끝남 | the + 형용사 (y→i) + -est | the eas**iest** |
| 긴 형용사 (2음절+) | the most + 형용사 | the **most** difficult |

### From the Original Book:

> "Yesterday was **the hottest** day of the year."
> 어제가 올해 가장 더운 날이었다.

> "What is **the most important** thing in life?"
> 인생에서 가장 중요한 것은 무엇인가?

---

## B. 짧은 형용사 + -est (Short adjectives)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Mondays are the hardest."**
> 월요일이 가장 힘들다.

- hard → the hardest

> **"The last 2 kilometers were the longest."**
> 마지막 2킬로미터가 가장 길었다.

- long → the longest

> **"That was the fastest I ever ran."**
> 그때가 내가 달린 것 중 가장 빨랐다.

> **"The finish line was the happiest place."**
> 결승선이 가장 행복한 곳이었다.

> **"December was the coldest month."**
> 12월이 가장 추운 달이었다.

---

## C. the most + 긴 형용사 (Long adjectives)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"It was the most exhausting day of my life."**
> 내 인생에서 가장 지치는 날이었다.

- exhausting → the most exhausting

> **"The 42km was the most challenging race."**
> 42km가 가장 도전적인 대회였다.

- challenging → the most challenging

> **"Finishing the marathon was the most satisfying moment."**
> 마라톤 완주가 가장 만족스러운 순간이었다.

> **"The rapeseed fields were the most beautiful scenery."**
> 유채꽃 밭이 가장 아름다운 경치였다.

> **"Running became the most enjoyable part of my day."**
> 달리기가 하루 중 가장 즐거운 시간이 됐다.

---

## D. 불규칙 최상급 (Irregular superlatives)

| 원급 | 비교급 | 최상급 |
|------|--------|--------|
| good | better | the **best** |
| bad | worse | the **worst** |
| far | farther | the **farthest** |
| little | less | the **least** |
| much/many | more | the **most** |
| old | older/elder | the **oldest/eldest** |

### From the Original Book:

> "It was **the worst** experience of my life."
> 내 인생 최악의 경험이었다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"That was the best day of my life."**
> 그날이 내 인생 최고의 날이었다.

> **"The worst part was the motion sickness."**
> 가장 나빴던 건 멀미였다.

> **"This was the farthest I had ever run."**
> 이게 내가 달린 것 중 가장 먼 거리였다.

> **"He is the best coach I've ever had."**
> 그는 내가 가진 최고의 코치다.

---

## E. 최상급 + in/of (Prepositions)

### Usage Rules:

| 전치사 | 사용 | 예시 |
|--------|------|------|
| **in** | 장소, 그룹 | the tallest **in** the class |
| **of** | 범위, 기간, 수량 | the best **of** all, the longest **of** the year |

### From the Original Book:

> "What's **the longest** river **in** the world?"
> 세계에서 가장 긴 강은 뭐야?

> "It was **the happiest** day **of** my life."
> 내 인생에서 가장 행복한 날이었다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"He was the fastest runner in the race."**
> 그가 대회에서 가장 빠른 주자였다.

> **"It was the best moment of the day."**
> 그날의 가장 좋은 순간이었다.

> **"March was the hardest month of all."**
> 3월이 모든 달 중 가장 힘들었다.

> **"That was the longest run of my life."**
> 그게 내 인생에서 가장 긴 달리기였다.

> **"She is the kindest person in our school."**
> 그녀가 우리 학교에서 가장 친절한 사람이다.

---

## F. 최상급 + 현재완료 (ever) (Superlative + Present Perfect)

"지금까지 ~한 것 중 가장"을 표현할 때 **have/has + ever + 과거분사**를 사용합니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "This is **the best** coffee **I've ever tasted**."
> 이게 내가 맛본 것 중 최고의 커피야.

> "What's **the most interesting** place **you've ever visited**?"
> 네가 방문한 것 중 가장 흥미로운 곳은 어디야?

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"This is the hardest thing I've ever done."**
> 이게 내가 해본 것 중 가장 힘든 일이다.

> **"It was the proudest moment I've ever felt."**
> 지금까지 느낀 것 중 가장 자랑스러운 순간이었다.

> **"That was the farthest I had ever run."**
> 그게 내가 달린 것 중 가장 먼 거리였다.

> **"He is the kindest person I've ever met."**
> 그는 내가 만난 사람 중 가장 친절하다.

> **"This was the most exhausting race I've ever completed."**
> 이게 내가 완주한 것 중 가장 지치는 대회였다.

---

## G. one of the + 최상급 + 복수명사 (One of the superlatives)

"가장 ~한 것들 중 하나"를 표현합니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "It's **one of the oldest** buildings **in** the city."
> 그 건물은 도시에서 가장 오래된 건물 중 하나다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Jeju is one of the most beautiful islands in Korea."**
> 제주는 한국에서 가장 아름다운 섬 중 하나다.

> **"Running is one of the best exercises."**
> 달리기는 최고의 운동 중 하나다.

> **"That was one of the happiest days of my life."**
> 그날은 내 인생에서 가장 행복한 날들 중 하나였다.

> **"This is one of the hardest routes in the area."**
> 이 지역에서 가장 힘든 경로 중 하나다.

## H. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | 설명 |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| ~~the most fastest~~ | the **fastest** | most + 규칙 최상급 불가 |
| ~~the most good~~ | the **best** | 불규칙 사용 |
| ~~the hardest in all~~ | the hardest **of** all | of all 패턴 |
| ~~one of the best runner~~ | one of the best **runners** | 복수명사 필요 |
| ~~most beautiful~~ (정관사 없이) | **the** most beautiful | the 필수 |
| ~~the longer river~~ (3개 이상 비교) | **the longest** river | 최상급 사용 |

## Exercises

### A. 최상급을 쓰세요 (Write the superlative)

1. fast → _______
2. difficult → _______
3. good → _______
4. easy → _______
5. challenging → _______
6. bad → _______
7. far → _______
8. happy → _______

**Answers:**
1. the fastest
2. the most difficult
3. the best
4. the easiest
5. the most challenging
6. the worst
7. the farthest/furthest
8. the happiest

---

### B. 문장을 완성하세요 (Complete the sentences)

1. Mondays are _______ (hard) day of the week.
   → **the hardest**

2. That was _______ (good) race of my life.
   → **the best**

3. It was _______ (exhausting) day ever.
   → **the most exhausting**

4. He is _______ (fast) runner in our school.
   → **the fastest**

5. This is _______ (difficult) race I've ever done.
   → **the most difficult**

6. December was _______ (cold) month.
   → **the coldest**

7. She is _______ (kind) person I've met.
   → **the kindest**

8. It was _______ (bad) experience of my life.
   → **the worst**

---

### C. in 또는 of를 고르세요 (Choose in or of)

1. He is the best runner _______ the team.
   → **in**

2. That was the longest race _______ my life.
   → **of**

3. She is the fastest _______ all the runners.
   → **of**

4. It was the hottest day _______ the year.
   → **of**

5. This is the biggest building _______ the city.
   → **in**

6. He is the tallest student _______ our class.
   → **in**

---

### D. "one of the + 최상급"을 사용하여 문장을 완성하세요 (Complete with "one of the...")

1. Jeju / beautiful / island / Korea
   → Jeju is **one of the most beautiful islands** in Korea.

2. Running / good / exercise
   → Running is **one of the best exercises**.

3. That / happy / day / my life
   → That was **one of the happiest days** of my life.

4. This / hard / route / the area
   → This is **one of the hardest routes** in the area.

5. He / kind / person / I've met
   → He is **one of the kindest people** I've ever met.

## Summary

### Formation Rules:

| 형용사 | 최상급 규칙 | 예시 |
|--------|-------------|------|
| 1음절 | the + -est | the fastest |
| 1음절 (자음중복) | the + 자음 + est | the biggest |
| -y로 끝남 | the + -iest | the happiest |
| 2음절+ | the most + 형용사 | the most difficult |
| 불규칙 | 외워야 함 | the best |

### Prepositions:

| 전치사 | 사용 | 예시 |
|--------|------|------|
| **in** | 장소, 그룹 | in the class, in the world |
| **of** | 범위, 기간 | of all, of my life, of the year |

### Key Patterns:

```
최상급 + I've ever + 과거분사
the best book I've ever read

one of the + 최상급 + 복수명사
one of the best runners
```

### Comparison vs Superlative:

| 비교급 (2개) | 최상급 (3개 이상) |
|--------------|-------------------|
| A is taller than B | A is the tallest of the three |
| harder | the hardest |
| more difficult | the most difficult |
