# Unit 132: Verb + Preposition (1): at and to

## A. 형태 (Form/Structure)

특정 동사들은 특정 전치사와 함께 사용됩니다. 동사 뒤에 오는 전치사에 따라 의미가 달라지기도 합니다.

### Structure:
```
Verb + preposition + object
```

| Verb + at | Verb + to |
|-----------|-----------|
| look **at** | talk **to** |
| stare **at** | listen **to** |
| laugh **at** | write **to** |

---

## B. Look at / Stare at / Glance at (시선 관련)

| Expression | Meaning | Intensity |
|------------|---------|-----------|
| glance at | ~을 흘끗 보다 | 짧게 ★☆☆ |
| look at | ~을 보다 | 보통 ★★☆ |
| stare at | ~을 응시하다 | 길게 ★★★ |
| gaze at | ~을 바라보다 | 꿈꾸듯이 ★★★ |

### From the Original Book:
> "Don't **look at** me like that!"
> 그렇게 나를 쳐다보지 마!

> "Why are you **staring at** me?"
> 왜 나를 빤히 쳐다봐?

> "She **glanced at** her watch."
> 그녀는 시계를 흘끗 봤다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The teacher looked at me."**
> 선생님이 나를 봤다.

> **"I looked at the finish line."**
> 결승선을 바라봤다.

> **"I glanced at my watch."**
> 시계를 흘끗 봤다.

---

## C. Laugh at / Smile at (표정 관련)

| Expression | Meaning | Nuance |
|------------|---------|--------|
| laugh at | ~을 보고 웃다 | 조롱/즐거움 |
| smile at | ~에게 미소 짓다 | 친근함 |

### From the Original Book:
> "Don't **laugh at** me!"
> 나 비웃지 마!

> "She **smiled at** me warmly."
> 그녀가 나에게 따뜻하게 미소 지었다.

### 주의: laugh at vs laugh with

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| laugh **at** someone | ~을 비웃다, ~을 보고 웃다 (부정적일 수 있음) |
| laugh **with** someone | ~와 함께 웃다 (긍정적) |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Minseo laughed at my joke."**
> 민서가 내 농담에 웃었다.

> **"Grandpa smiled at me."**
> 할아버지가 나에게 미소 지었다.

---

## D. Shout at / Throw at / Aim at (방향/공격 관련)

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| shout at | ~에게 소리치다 |
| aim at | ~을 겨냥하다 |
| throw at | ~을 향해 던지다 |
| point at | ~을 가리키다 |
| shoot at | ~을 향해 쏘다 |

### From the Original Book:
> "He **shouted at** me angrily."
> 그가 화나서 나에게 소리쳤다.

> "Don't **point at** people."
> 사람을 가리키지 마.

---

## E. Talk to / Speak to / Listen to (의사소통 관련)

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| talk to | ~와 이야기하다 |
| speak to | ~에게 말하다 |
| listen to | ~을 듣다 |

### From the Original Book:
> "Can I **talk to** you for a minute?"
> 잠깐 얘기 좀 할 수 있을까?

> "I need to **speak to** the manager."
> 매니저와 이야기해야 해요.

> "She **listened to** what I had to say."
> 그녀는 내가 하는 말을 들었다.

### 주의: listen to (전치사 필수!)

> ~~I listened music.~~
> ✓ I **listened to** music. (to 필수)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I talked to Minseo about running."**
> 민서와 달리기에 대해 이야기했다.

> **"I listened to the birds singing."**
> 새들이 노래하는 것을 들었다.

> **"Mom spoke to me about the race."**
> 엄마가 나에게 경기에 대해 말씀하셨다.

---

## F. Write to / Reply to / Belong to (기타 to 동사)

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| write to | ~에게 편지를 쓰다 |
| reply to | ~에게 답장하다 |
| belong to | ~에 속하다 |
| happen to | ~에게 일어나다 |
| explain to | ~에게 설명하다 |
| describe to | ~에게 묘사하다 |
| apologize to | ~에게 사과하다 |

### From the Original Book:
> "Who does this book **belong to**?"
> 이 책 누구 거야?

> "I **apologized to** her for being late."
> 늦은 것에 대해 그녀에게 사과했다.

### 주의: explain to + 사람 + what/how...

> ~~I explained him the problem.~~
> ✓ I **explained to** him the problem.
> ✓ I **explained** the problem **to** him.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I belong to the school track team now."**
> 이제 학교 육상부에 속해 있다.

> **"I explained to Minseo why I walk."**
> 민서에게 왜 걷는지 설명했다.

> **"What happened to you this morning?"**
> 오늘 아침에 무슨 일이 있었어?

---

## G. Talk to vs Talk with / Talk about (비교)

| Expression | Meaning | Usage |
|------------|---------|-------|
| talk **to** someone | ~에게 말하다 | 일방적 느낌 |
| talk **with** someone | ~와 대화하다 | 쌍방 느낌 |
| talk **about** something | ~에 대해 말하다 | 주제 |

### Examples:

> **"I talked to Minseo about the race."**
> 민서에게 경기에 대해 말했다.

> **"We talked about running for hours."**
> 달리기에 대해 몇 시간 동안 이야기했다.

> **"I talked with my coach about training."**
> 코치님과 훈련에 대해 대화했다.

---

## H. Shout at vs Shout to (중요 비교)

| Shout at | Shout to |
|----------|----------|
| 화나서 소리치다 | 멀리서 부르다 |
| (부정적) | (중립적/긍정적) |

### From the Original Book:
> "She **shouted at** me for being late." (화남)
> 늦어서 나한테 소리쳤다.

> "She **shouted to** me from across the street." (부름)
> 길 건너편에서 나를 불렀다.

### From the Novel:

> **"Mom shouted to me from the door."** (부름)
> 엄마가 문에서 나를 불렀다.

## I. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~listen music~~ | **listen to** music | listen 뒤에 to 필수 |
| ~~explain him~~ | **explain to** him | explain 뒤에 to + 사람 |
| ~~look to the picture~~ | **look at** the picture | look + at |
| ~~belong~~ him | **belong to** him | belong 뒤에 to 필수 |
| ~~I talked her~~ | I **talked to** her | talk 뒤에 to/with 필요 |
| ~~shout to~~ (화났을 때) | **shout at** (화났을 때) | 화나면 at |
| ~~reply him~~ | **reply to** him | reply 뒤에 to 필수 |

### 동사별 전치사 필수 여부:

| Always needs prep | Can be without prep |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| listen **to** ✓ | look (+ at) |
| belong **to** ✓ | write (+ to) |
| explain **to** + person ✓ | |
| reply **to** ✓ | |

## Exercises

### A. at 또는 to 선택하기

1. I looked _______ the finish line.
2. I talked _______ Minseo about running.
3. He laughed _______ my joke.
4. I listened _______ music on the bus.
5. She smiled _______ me kindly.
6. I explained _______ her why I was late.
7. Who does this book belong _______?
8. What happened _______ you this morning?

**Answers:**
1. at
2. to
3. at
4. to
5. at
6. to
7. to
8. to

### B. 알맞은 동사 + 전치사 쓰기

1. I _______ the teacher explain the rules. (듣다)
2. She _______ me when I made a mistake. (비웃다)
3. He _______ his watch to check the time. (흘끗 보다)
4. I _______ my coach about my progress. (말하다)
5. I _______ the school track team. (속하다)
6. She _______ me from across the room. (부르다)

**Answers:**
1. listened to
2. laughed at
3. glanced at
4. talked to
5. belong to
6. shouted to

---

### C. 틀린 부분 고치기 (Error Correction)

1. I listen music every day.
2. Can you explain me this problem?
3. She looked to the window.
4. Who does this belong?
5. I talked him yesterday.

**Answers:**
1. I listen **to** music every day.
2. Can you explain **to** me this problem? / Can you explain this problem **to** me?
3. She looked **at** the window.
4. Who does this belong **to**?
5. I talked **to/with** him yesterday.

---

### D. shout at vs shout to 구분하기

1. He shouted _______ me angrily. (화남)
2. He shouted _______ me from the car. (부름)
3. Don't shout _______ your mother! (무례)
4. She shouted _______ us to come back. (부름)

**Answers:**
1. at
2. to
3. at
4. to

## At vs To 선택 팁 (Tips)

| Use AT when... | Use TO when... |
|----------------|----------------|
| 시선의 대상 (look, stare) | 의사소통 대상 (talk, speak) |
| 표정 대상 (smile, laugh) | 소리 듣기 (listen) |
| 공격/방향 (throw, aim, shout) | 소속 (belong) |
| | 설명/응답 (explain, reply) |
