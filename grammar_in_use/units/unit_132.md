# Unit 132: Verb + preposition (1): at and to

## A. Verb + at

### Look at / Stare at / Glance at

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| look at | ~을 보다 |
| stare at | ~을 응시하다 |
| glance at | ~을 흘끗 보다 |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The teacher looked at me."**
> 선생님이 나를 봤다.

> **"I looked at the finish line."**
> 결승선을 바라봤다.

> **"I glanced at my watch."**
> 시계를 흘끗 봤다.

### Laugh at / Smile at

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| laugh at | ~을 보고 웃다 |
| smile at | ~에게 미소 짓다 |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Minseo laughed at my joke."**
> 민서가 내 농담에 웃었다.

> **"Grandpa smiled at me."**
> 할아버지가 나에게 미소 지었다.

### Shout at / Aim at / Throw at

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| shout at | ~에게 소리치다 |
| aim at | ~을 겨냥하다 |
| throw at | ~을 향해 던지다 |

## B. Verb + to

### Talk to / Speak to / Listen to

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| talk to | ~와 이야기하다 |
| speak to | ~에게 말하다 |
| listen to | ~을 듣다 |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I talked to Minseo about running."**
> 민서와 달리기에 대해 이야기했다.

> **"I listened to the birds singing."**
> 새들이 노래하는 것을 들었다.

> **"Mom spoke to me about the race."**
> 엄마가 나에게 경기에 대해 말씀하셨다.

### Write to / Reply to / Belong to

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| write to | ~에게 편지를 쓰다 |
| reply to | ~에게 답장하다 |
| belong to | ~에 속하다 |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I belong to the school track team now."**
> 이제 학교 육상부에 속해 있다.

### Explain to / Describe to / Happen to

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| explain to | ~에게 설명하다 |
| describe to | ~에게 묘사하다 |
| happen to | ~에게 일어나다 |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I explained to Minseo why I walk."**
> 민서에게 왜 걷는지 설명했다.

> **"What happened to you this morning?"**
> 오늘 아침에 무슨 일이 있었어?

---

## C. Talk to vs Talk with / Talk about

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| talk **to** someone | ~에게 말하다 (일방적) |
| talk **with** someone | ~와 대화하다 (쌍방) |
| talk **about** something | ~에 대해 말하다 |

### Examples:

> **"I talked to Minseo about the race."**
> 민서에게 경기에 대해 말했다.

> **"We talked about running for hours."**
> 달리기에 대해 몇 시간 동안 이야기했다.

---

## D. Shout at vs Shout to

| Shout at | Shout to |
|----------|----------|
| 화나서 소리치다 | 멀리서 부르다 |
| She shouted at me. (화남) | She shouted to me across the street. (부름) |

### From the Novel:

> **"Mom shouted to me from the door."** (부름)
> 엄마가 문에서 나를 불렀다.

## Exercises

### A. at 또는 to를 사용해 빈칸을 채우세요.

1. I looked _______ the finish line.
2. I talked _______ Minseo about running.
3. He laughed _______ my joke.
4. I listened _______ music on the bus.
5. She smiled _______ me kindly.

**Answers:**
1. at
2. to
3. at
4. to
5. at

### B. 알맞은 동사 + 전치사를 쓰세요.

1. I _______ the teacher explain the rules. (listen)
2. She _______ me when I made a mistake. (laugh)
3. He _______ his watch to check the time. (glance)
4. I _______ my coach about my progress. (talk)

**Answers:**
1. listened to
2. laughed at
3. glanced at
4. talked to

### C. 다음 문장에서 shout at과 shout to의 차이를 설명하세요.

1. He shouted at me angrily.
2. He shouted to me from the car.

**Answers:**
1. 화가 나서 나에게 소리쳤다. (부정적)
2. 차에서 나를 불렀다. (멀리서)
