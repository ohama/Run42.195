# Unit 119: For, during, and while

## A. For + 기간 (시간의 길이)

**For**는 "~동안"의 의미로, **기간의 길이**를 나타냅니다.
뒤에 **시간 표현 (숫자 + 시간 단위)**이 옵니다.

### 구조:
> for + 숫자 + 시간 단위 (minutes, hours, days, weeks, months, years)

### From the Original Book:
> "I've been waiting **for** an hour."
> 한 시간 동안 기다리고 있었다.

> "We watched TV **for** two hours."
> 우리는 두 시간 동안 TV를 봤다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I ran for 40 minutes."**
> 40분 동안 달렸다.

> **"I walked for an hour and ten minutes."**
> 1시간 10분 동안 걸었다.

> **"I've been running for six months."**
> 6개월 동안 달리기를 해왔다.

> **"We trained for twelve weeks."**
> 12주 동안 훈련했다.

### For와 함께 쓰이는 시간 표현:

| 시간 단위 | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| minutes | for 40 minutes |
| hours | for 2 hours |
| days | for 3 days |
| weeks | for 12 weeks |
| months | for 6 months |
| years | for 2 years |
| a while | for a while |
| ages | for ages |

---

## B. During + 명사 (특정 기간)

**During**은 "~ 동안에"의 의미로, **특정 기간/사건** 동안에 무슨 일이 일어났는지를 나타냅니다.
뒤에 **명사 (기간을 나타내는)**가 옵니다.

### 구조:
> during + 명사 (사건/기간)

### From the Original Book:
> "I fell asleep **during** the movie."
> 영화 보는 동안 잠들었다.

> "Nobody spoke **during** dinner."
> 저녁 식사 동안 아무도 말하지 않았다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I felt nauseous during the bus ride."**
> 버스 타는 동안 속이 울렁거렸다.

> **"During summer break, I trained every day."**
> 여름 방학 동안 매일 훈련했다.

> **"I thought about giving up during the race."**
> 경기 중에 포기할까 생각했다.

> **"During PE class, we ran laps around the track."**
> 체육 시간 동안 트랙을 돌았다.

### During과 함께 쓰이는 명사:

| 사건/기간 | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| the race | during the race |
| summer break | during summer break |
| the movie | during the movie |
| class | during class |
| the night | during the night |
| the meeting | during the meeting |

---

## C. While + 절 (동시 동작)

**While**은 "~하는 동안"의 의미로, **두 가지 일이 동시에 일어남**을 나타냅니다.
뒤에 **주어 + 동사 (절)**가 옵니다.

### 구조:
> while + 주어 + 동사

### From the Original Book:
> "I'll wait **while** you get ready."
> 네가 준비하는 동안 기다릴게.

> "**While** I was cooking, the phone rang."
> 요리하는 동안 전화가 울렸다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"While I was running, I thought about my goal."**
> 달리는 동안 목표에 대해 생각했다.

> **"While the bus was moving, my stomach churned."**
> 버스가 움직이는 동안 속이 울렁거렸다.

> **"I listened to birds singing while I walked."**
> 걸으면서 새들이 노래하는 소리를 들었다.

---

## D. For vs During vs While 비교

| For | During | While |
|-----|--------|-------|
| + 기간 (숫자) | + 명사 (사건/기간) | + 절 (주어+동사) |
| for 40 minutes | during the race | while I was running |
| 40분 동안 | 경기 동안 | 달리는 동안 |
| 얼마나 오래? | 언제? (어떤 사건 중) | 무엇을 하는 동안? |

### 구분 방법:

```
숫자 + 시간 단위? → For
명사 (사건/기간)? → During
주어 + 동사 (절)? → While
```

### Comparison Examples:

> **"I ran for two hours."** (2시간 동안 - 기간)
> **"I felt pain during the run."** (달리기 동안 - 사건)
> **"While I was running, it started to rain."** (달리는 동안 - 동시 동작)

### From the Novel:

> **"For forty minutes, I endured the crowded bus."** (40분 동안)
> **"During the forty-minute ride, I felt sick."** (40분 타는 동안)
> **"While I was riding the bus, my stomach churned."** (버스 타는 동안)

---

## E. During vs While 주의점

**During** 다음에는 **명사**만, **While** 다음에는 **절**만 올 수 있습니다.

| Correct (O) | Incorrect (X) |
|-------------|---------------|
| during the race | ~~during I was racing~~ |
| while I was racing | ~~while the race~~ |
| during summer | ~~during it was summer~~ |
| while it was summer | (덜 자연스러움) |

### 변환:

| During + 명사 | While + 절 |
|---------------|------------|
| during the race | while the race was happening |
| during the movie | while I was watching the movie |
| during dinner | while we were having dinner |

---

## F. For의 생략 (Omitting For)

**how long** 질문에 대답할 때, for는 생략 가능합니다.

> "How long did you run?" - "**For** an hour." / "An hour."
> "얼마나 달렸어?" - "한 시간 동안." / "한 시간."

### 생략 가능한 경우:

> I waited **(for)** three hours.
> She's been here **(for)** two weeks.
> We talked **(for)** a long time.

---

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | 설명 |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| ~~I felt sick during I was on the bus.~~ | I felt sick **while** I was on the bus. | during + 절 불가 |
| ~~While the race, I got tired.~~ | **During** the race, I got tired. | while + 명사 불가 |
| ~~I trained during six months.~~ | I trained **for** six months. | 기간에는 for |
| ~~I ran for the race.~~ | I ran **during** the race. | 사건에는 during |
| ~~I slept during three hours.~~ | I slept **for** three hours. | 숫자+시간에는 for |

## Exercises

### A. for, during, while 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. I trained _______ the summer.
2. I ran _______ 58 minutes.
3. _______ I was stretching, Mom called me.
4. I felt tired _______ the race.
5. We practiced _______ three months.
6. _______ I was running, I saw a beautiful sunrise.
7. I couldn't sleep _______ the night.
8. I've been running _______ six months.

**Answers:**
1. during
2. for
3. While
4. during
5. for
6. While
7. during
8. for

### B. 문장을 완성하세요.

1. I ran for _______.
2. During _______, I felt nervous.
3. While _______, I heard birds singing.
4. I walked for _______.
5. She didn't speak during _______.
6. I was thinking about my goal while _______.

**Answers (예시):**
1. an hour / 30 minutes / two hours
2. the race / PE class / the exam
3. I was walking / running / studying
4. 7 kilometers / one hour / a long time
5. the meeting / dinner / class
6. I was running / walking / resting

### C. 틀린 문장을 고치세요.

1. I felt sick during I was on the bus.
   → I felt sick **while** I was on the bus. / I felt sick **during** the bus ride.

2. While the race, I thought about quitting.
   → **During** the race, I thought about quitting. / **While** I was racing, I thought about quitting.

3. I trained during six months.
   → I trained **for** six months.

4. I ran for the morning.
   → I ran **during** the morning. / I ran **in** the morning.

5. While dinner, we talked about the race.
   → **During** dinner, we talked about the race. / **While** we were having dinner...

### D. 같은 의미로 문장을 바꾸세요.

1. I felt tired during the race.
   → I felt tired **while I was racing**.

2. While I was sleeping, someone called.
   → **During** my sleep, someone called.

3. I trained for two hours.
   → I trained **for a couple of hours**.

4. During the flight, I read a book.
   → **While** I was on the flight, I read a book.

## Summary: For vs During vs While 비교표

| Expression | Structure | Example | Korean |
|------------|-----------|---------|--------|
| for | + 기간 (숫자+시간) | for 2 hours | 2시간 동안 |
| during | + 명사 (사건/기간) | during the race | 경기 동안 |
| while | + 절 (주어+동사) | while running | 달리면서 |

---

## 선택 가이드

```
"얼마나 오래?" (How long?)
→ For + 숫자 + 시간

"어떤 사건 중에?" (During what event?)
→ During + 명사

"무엇을 하는 동안?" (While doing what?)
→ While + 주어 + 동사
```

## Compare with Korean

| English | Korean |
|---------|--------|
| for + 기간 | ~동안 (기간) |
| during + 명사 | ~동안(에), ~중(에) |
| while + 절 | ~하는 동안, ~하면서 |

한국어에서는 모두 "~동안"으로 번역될 수 있지만, 영어에서는 뒤에 오는 것에 따라 구분합니다.
