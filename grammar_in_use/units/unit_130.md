# Unit 130: Adjective + preposition (1)

## A. Adjective + of

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| afraid of | ~을 무서워하는 | afraid of failing |
| scared of | ~이 무서운 | scared of the pain |
| tired of | ~에 지친 | tired of the bus |
| proud of | ~이 자랑스러운 | proud of myself |
| full of | ~으로 가득 찬 | full of energy |
| aware of | ~을 알고 있는 | aware of my limits |
| capable of | ~할 수 있는 | capable of finishing |
| fond of | ~을 좋아하는 | fond of running |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was afraid of the pain after 30km."**
> 30km 후의 고통이 두려웠다.

> **"I was tired of taking the bus."**
> 버스 타는 것에 지쳤다.

> **"Mom said she was proud of me."**
> 엄마가 나를 자랑스럽다고 말씀하셨다.

> **"I was full of energy after the run."**
> 달리기 후에 에너지가 넘쳤다.

## B. Adjective + at

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| good at | ~을 잘하는 | good at running |
| bad at | ~을 못하는 | bad at sports |
| amazed at | ~에 놀란 | amazed at my progress |
| surprised at | ~에 놀란 | surprised at the result |
| angry at | ~에 화난 | angry at myself |
| excellent at | ~에 뛰어난 | excellent at distance running |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was bad at running in elementary school."**
> 초등학교 때 달리기를 못했다.

> **"I was amazed at how much I had improved."**
> 얼마나 나아졌는지 놀랐다.

> **"Minseo was surprised at my marathon time."**
> 민서는 내 마라톤 기록에 놀랐다.

> **"I became good at long-distance running."**
> 장거리 달리기를 잘하게 되었다.

## C. Adjective + for

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| famous for | ~으로 유명한 | famous for its scenery |
| responsible for | ~에 책임이 있는 | responsible for my training |
| ready for | ~할 준비가 된 | ready for the race |
| sorry for | ~에 대해 미안한 | sorry for being late |
| grateful for | ~에 감사하는 | grateful for the support |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was ready for the marathon."**
> 마라톤 준비가 되었다.

> **"I was grateful for my family's support."**
> 가족의 지지에 감사했다.

> **"Jeju is famous for its beautiful scenery."**
> 제주는 아름다운 풍경으로 유명하다.

## D. Adjective + with

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| pleased with | ~에 기쁜 | pleased with my result |

| disappointed with | ~에 실망한 | disappointed with myself |
| bored with | ~에 지루한 | bored with the routine |
| busy with | ~으로 바쁜 | busy with training |

### Examples from the Novel:

> 완주 기록에 기뻤다.

> **"The coach was satisfied with my progress."**
> 코치님이 내 발전에 만족하셨다.

> **"I was busy with school and training."**
> 학교와 훈련으로 바빴다.

---

## E. 비교: At vs With

일부 형용사는 **at**과 **with** 둘 다 사용 가능합니다.

| At (사람의 행동) | With (사람/결과) |
|-----------------|------------------|
| angry **at** him (for what he did) | angry **with** him |
| disappointed **at** the news | disappointed **with** the result |

### From the Novel:

> **"I was angry at myself for missing the bus."**
> 버스를 놓친 것에 화가 났다.

## Exercises

### A. 알맞은 전치사를 고르세요 (of, at, for, with).

1. I was afraid _______ the pain.
2. I'm good _______ running now.
3. I was ready _______ the marathon.
4. I was pleased _______ my time.
5. Mom was proud _______ me.

**Answers:**
1. of
2. at
3. for
4. with
5. of

### B. 빈칸을 채워 문장을 완성하세요.

1. I was tired _______ taking the bus.
2. I was surprised _______ my improvement.
3. I'm grateful _______ your help.
4. I was satisfied _______ my result.
5. I was capable _______ finishing the race.

**Answers:**
1. of
2. at
3. for
4. with
5. of

### C. 문장을 완성하세요.

1. I'm good at _______.
2. I was proud of _______.
3. I'm ready for _______.
4. I was tired of _______.

**Answers (예시):**
1. long-distance running
2. finishing the marathon
3. the race
4. the crowded bus
