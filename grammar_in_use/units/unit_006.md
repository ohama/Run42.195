# Unit 6: Past Continuous (I was doing)

## A. 과거진행형의 형태 (Form)

**was/were + -ing**

| Subject | Was/Were | -ing form | Full Example |
|---------|----------|-----------|--------------|
| I | was | running | I was running. |
| He/She/It | was | walking | She was walking. |
| We/You/They | were | riding | They were riding. |

### Negative and Questions:

| Type | Structure | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Positive | was/were + -ing | I was writing. |
| Negative | was/were + not + -ing | I wasn't writing. |
| Question | Was/Were + subject + -ing? | Were you writing? |

### Contractions:
- was not → wasn't
- were not → weren't

---

## B. 과거진행형의 기본 용법 (Basic Usage)

**"I was doing something" = I was in the middle of doing it at a certain time in the past.**

과거진행형은 **과거의 특정 시점에 진행 중이던 동작**을 표현합니다.

### From the Original Book:
> "This time last year I **was living** in Brazil."
> 작년 이맘때 나는 브라질에 살고 있었다.

> "What **were** you **doing** at 10 o'clock last night?"
> 어젯밤 10시에 뭐 하고 있었어?

---

## C. 과거진행형 사용 상황 (When to Use)

### 1. 과거 특정 시점에 진행 중 (Action in progress at a specific past time)

> **"At 7:30, I was waiting at the bus stop."**
> 7시 30분에, 나는 버스 정류장에서 기다리고 있었다.

> **"What were you doing at 8 AM yesterday?"**
> 어제 오전 8시에 뭐 하고 있었어?

### 2. 배경 동작 - when + 단순과거 (Background action when something happened)

> **"I was walking to school when I saw Grandpa."**
> 학교에 걷고 있을 때 할아버지를 봤다.

더 긴 동작 (진행형) + 짧은 동작 (과거)

### 3. 두 동작의 동시 진행 (Two actions happening at the same time)

> **"While I was running, I was thinking about the race."**
> 달리면서 대회에 대해 생각하고 있었다.

---

## D. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: 과거 특정 시점에 진행 중

> **"The bus was approaching in the distance."**
> 버스가 멀리서 다가오고 있었다.

- **was approaching** = 그 순간 진행 중이던 동작
- The bus was in the process of approaching when the narrator saw it

### Example 2: 배경 동작

> **"Kids in gym clothes were running around."**
> 체육복 입은 애들이 뛰어다니고 있었다.

- **were running** = 배경이 되는 진행 중인 동작
- The kids' running was the background scene Doyun observed

### Example 3: 과거의 진행 중인 활동

> **"I was passing by the tangerine orchard."**
> 귤 밭을 지나가고 있었다.

- **was passing** = 할아버지를 만났을 때 진행 중이던 동작

### Example 4: When + Past Continuous

> **"When I got to school, I was sweating a lot."**
> 학교에 도착했을 때, 나는 땀을 많이 흘리고 있었다.

- **was sweating** = 도착한 그 순간의 상태

### Example 5: 두 동작의 동시 진행

> **"While I was running, I kept thinking about it."**
> 달리는 동안, 나는 그것에 대해 계속 생각했다.

- **was running** = 배경 동작 (진행 중)

---

## E. Past Continuous vs Past Simple

| Past Continuous | Past Simple |
|-----------------|-------------|
| 진행 중이던 동작 | 완료된 동작 |
| I **was walking** when... | I **walked** to school. |
| 배경 동작 | 주요 동작 |
| 더 긴 동작 | 더 짧은 동작/방해 |

### Timeline Visualization:

```
Past Continuous: ─────────────────
Past Simple:              X
                "While I was walking, I saw him."
```

### 비교 예문:

| Past Continuous | Past Simple |
|-----------------|-------------|
| I **was walking** to school | when I **saw** Grandpa. |
| (진행 중, 더 긴 동작) | (갑자기 일어난 일) |
| The bus **was approaching** | and it **stopped**. |
| (진행 중) | (완료된 동작) |

### From the Original Book:
> "It **began** to rain while I **was walking** home."
> 집에 걸어가고 있을 때 비가 내리기 시작했다.

> "Tom **burnt** his hand while he **was cooking** dinner."
> 저녁을 요리하는 동안 톰은 손을 데였다.

---

## F. When vs While

### When + Past Simple (갑자기 일어난 일)

> "**When** I **opened** my eyes, it was 7:23."
> 눈을 떴을 때, 7시 23분이었다.

> "**When** the phone **rang**, I was having a shower."
> 전화가 울렸을 때, 나는 샤워 중이었다.

### While + Past Continuous (진행 중인 동작)

> "**While** I **was walking**, I thought about it."
> 걷는 동안, 나는 그것에 대해 생각했다.

> "**While** I **was running**, it started to rain."
> 달리는 동안, 비가 내리기 시작했다.

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~I was walk to school.~~ | I **was walking** to school. | -ing 형태 필요 |
| ~~He were running.~~ | He **was** running. | He → was |
| ~~While I walked, I saw him.~~ | **While** I **was walking**, I saw him. | while + 진행형 |
| ~~I was knowing the answer.~~ | I **knew** the answer. | know = 상태동사 |
| ~~When I was walk...~~ | **When** I **was walking**... | -ing 형태 필요 |
| ~~They was running.~~ | They **were** running. | They → were |

---

## H. 시간 표현 (Time Expressions)

### Past Continuous와 자주 쓰이는 표현:

| Expression | Korean | Example |
|------------|--------|---------|
| at 7 o'clock | 7시에 | At 7, I was running. |
| at that time | 그때 | At that time, I was sleeping. |
| when... | ~할 때 | When he called, I was eating. |
| while... | ~하는 동안 | While I was walking, I saw him. |
| as... | ~하면서 | As I was running, I felt happy. |
| all morning/day | 아침/하루 내내 | I was studying all morning. |
| this time yesterday | 어제 이맘때 | This time yesterday, I was running. |

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. The bus (approached / was approaching) when I saw it.
2. I (walked / was walking) to school when it started to rain.
3. The kids (ran / were running) around the field during PE.
4. When I (got / was getting) off the bus, I felt sick.
5. While I (waited / was waiting), Minseo arrived.
6. What (did you do / were you doing) at 8 o'clock?
7. It (rained / was raining) when I woke up.
8. She (read / was reading) a book when I called.

**Answers:**
1. was approaching (진행 중이던 동작)
2. was walking (배경 동작)
3. were running (진행 중인 장면)
4. got (완료된 짧은 동작)
5. was waiting (배경 동작)
6. were you doing (특정 시점의 활동)
7. was raining (상황)
8. was reading (배경 동작)

### C. When 또는 While로 문장 완성하기

1. _______ I was walking, I heard birds singing.
2. _______ the bus stopped, everyone got off.
3. _______ I was running, I felt great.
4. _______ I opened my eyes, it was already 7:23.
5. _______ I was studying, my phone rang.
6. _______ the phone rang, I was in the shower.

**Answers:**
1. While
2. When
3. While
4. When
5. While
6. When

### D. 문장 연결하기

Connect the sentences using when or while:

1. I was walking to school. + I saw my friend.
   → I was walking to school **when** I saw my friend.
   → **While** I was walking to school, I saw my friend.

2. It started to rain. + I was running.
   → It started to rain **while** I was running.

3. The phone rang. + I was having breakfast.
   → The phone rang **while** I was having breakfast.
   → I was having breakfast **when** the phone rang.

4. I was reading. + The power went out.
   → I was reading **when** the power went out.

## 한국어와 비교 (Compare with Korean)

영어 과거진행형은 한국어에서 "~하고 있었다" 또는 "~하던 중이었다"로 표현됩니다.

| English | Korean |
|---------|--------|
| I was walking | 걷고 있었다 / 걷던 중이었다 |
| They were running | 뛰고 있었다 / 뛰던 중이었다 |
| The bus was coming | 버스가 오고 있었다 |

한국어에서는 "~했다"로만 표현할 수 있는 상황도, 영어에서는 Past Continuous로 구분합니다.

## Summary: Past Simple vs Past Continuous

| Aspect | Past Simple | Past Continuous |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| Focus | 완료된 동작 | 진행 중이던 동작 |
| Duration | 짧은/순간적 동작 | 더 긴 배경 동작 |
| Time | 특정 시점에 일어난 일 | 특정 시점에 진행 중 |
| Example | I walked to school. | I was walking to school. |
| With "when" | When I saw him... (새로운 동작) | When I was walking... (배경) |
| With "while" | (덜 자연스러움) | While I was walking... |
