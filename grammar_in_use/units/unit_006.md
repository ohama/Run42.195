# Unit 6: Past Continuous (I was doing)

## A. 문법 설명 (Grammar Explanation)

### English
**Past Continuous** (was/were + -ing) describes an action that **was in progress at a particular time in the past**.

We use it for:
1. Actions in progress at a specific past time
2. Background actions when another event happened
3. Two actions happening at the same time

### Korean
**과거진행형** (was/were + -ing)은 **과거의 특정 시점에 진행 중이던 동작**을 표현합니다.

다음 상황에서 사용합니다:
1. 과거 특정 시점에 진행 중이던 일
2. 다른 사건이 일어났을 때 배경이 되는 동작
3. 동시에 일어나고 있던 두 동작

---

## B. 과거진행형의 형태 (Form)

| Subject | Was/Were | -ing form |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| I/He/She/It | was | running |
| We/You/They | were | walking |

**부정문:** was/were + not + -ing
**의문문:** Was/Were + 주어 + -ing?

---

## C. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: 과거 특정 시점에 진행 중

> **"The bus was approaching in the distance."**
> 버스가 멀리서 다가오고 있었다.

- **was approaching** = 그 순간 진행 중이던 동작
- The bus was in the process of approaching when the narrator saw it

### Example 2: 배경 동작 (when + 단순과거)

> **"Kids in gym clothes were running around."**
> 체육복 입은 애들이 뛰어다니고 있었다.

- **were running** = 배경이 되는 진행 중인 동작
- The kids' running was the background scene Doyun observed

### Example 3: 과거의 진행 중인 활동

> **"I was passing by the tangerine orchard."**
> 귤 밭을 지나가고 있었다.

- **was passing** = 할아버지를 만났을 때 진행 중이던 동작
- This action was in progress when something else happened

### Example 4: When + Past Continuous

> **"When I got to school, I was sweating a lot."**
> 학교에 도착했을 때, 나는 땀을 많이 흘리고 있었다.

- **was sweating** = 도착한 그 순간의 상태
- The sweating was already in progress when he arrived

### Example 5: 두 동작의 동시 진행

> **"While I was running, I kept thinking about it."**
> 달리는 동안, 나는 그것에 대해 계속 생각했다.

- **was running** = 배경 동작 (진행 중)
- "kept thinking" = 그 동안 일어난 일

## D. Past Continuous vs Past Simple

| Past Continuous | Past Simple |
|-----------------|-------------|
| 진행 중이던 동작 | 완료된 동작 |
| I **was walking** when... | I **walked** to school. |
| 배경 동작 | 주요 동작 |
| 더 긴 동작 | 더 짧은 동작 |

### 비교 예문:

| Past Continuous | Past Simple |
|-----------------|-------------|
| I **was walking** to school | when I **saw** Grandpa. |
| (진행 중, 더 긴 동작) | (갑자기 일어난 일) |
| The bus **was approaching** | and it **stopped**. |
| (진행 중) | (완료된 동작) |

---

## E. 시간 표현 (Time Expressions)

### Past Continuous와 자주 쓰이는 표현:
- at 7 o'clock yesterday (어제 7시에)
- when... (할 때)
- while... (하는 동안)
- as... (하면서)
- all morning/evening (아침/저녁 내내)
- at that time (그때)

### 예문:
> **"At 7:30, I was waiting at the bus stop."**
> 7시 30분에, 나는 버스 정류장에서 기다리고 있었다.

> **"While I was walking, I heard birds singing."**
> 걷는 동안, 새가 노래하는 소리가 들렸다.

---

## F. When vs While

### When + Past Simple (갑자기 일어난 일)
> "When I **opened** my eyes, it was 7:23."
> 눈을 떴을 때, 7시 23분이었다.

### While + Past Continuous (진행 중인 동작)
> "While I **was walking**, I thought about it."
> 걷는 동안, 나는 그것에 대해 생각했다.

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. The bus (approached / was approaching) when I saw it.
2. I (walked / was walking) to school when it started to rain.
3. The kids (ran / were running) around the field during PE.
4. When I (got / was getting) off the bus, I felt sick.
5. While I (waited / was waiting), Minseo arrived.

**Answers:**
1. was approaching (진행 중이던 동작)
2. was walking (배경 동작)
3. were running (진행 중인 장면)
4. got (완료된 짧은 동작)
5. was waiting (배경 동작)

### B. 빈칸 채우기 (was/were + -ing)

1. I _______ (walk) to school when I met the old man.
2. The rapeseed flowers _______ (start) to bloom.
3. At 7:30, I _______ (wait) for the bus.
4. The children _______ (run) around the field.
5. While I _______ (think), Mom called me.

**Answers:**
1. was walking
2. were starting
3. was waiting
4. were running
5. was thinking

### C. 문장 완성하기 (When/While)

1. _______ I was walking, I heard birds singing.
2. _______ the bus stopped, everyone got off.
3. _______ I was running, I felt great.
4. _______ I opened my eyes, it was already 7:23.

**Answers:**
1. While
2. When
3. While
4. When

### D. 문장 변환

Past Simple → Past Continuous (진행 중의 의미로):

1. I walked to school at that time.
   → I _______ to school at that time.
2. The bus approached the stop.
   → The bus _______ the stop (when I saw it).
3. Kids ran on the field.
   → Kids _______ on the field (when I looked).

**Answers:**
1. was walking
2. was approaching
3. were running

## Key Sentences (암기 문장)

| English | Korean | Grammar Point |
|---------|--------|---------------|
| The bus was approaching. | 버스가 다가오고 있었다. | 진행 중인 동작 |
| Kids were running around. | 애들이 뛰어다니고 있었다. | 배경 동작 |
| While I was walking, I heard birds. | 걷는 동안, 새 소리가 들렸다. | While + 진행형 |
| When I got there, I was sweating. | 도착했을 때, 땀을 흘리고 있었다. | 도착 시점의 상태 |
| I was thinking about it. | 그것에 대해 생각하고 있었다. | 생각 중 |

## 한국어와 비교 (Compare with Korean)

영어 과거진행형은 한국어에서 "~하고 있었다" 또는 "~하던 중이었다"로 표현됩니다.

| English | Korean |
|---------|--------|
| I was walking | 걷고 있었다 / 걷던 중이었다 |
| They were running | 뛰고 있었다 / 뛰던 중이었다 |
| The bus was coming | 버스가 오고 있었다 |

한국어에서는 "~했다"로만 표현할 수 있는 상황도, 영어에서는 Past Continuous로 구분합니다.
