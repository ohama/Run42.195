# Unit 82: Myself/yourself/themselves, etc.

## A. 재귀대명사 형태

| Subject | Reflexive |
|---------|-----------|
| I | myself |
| you (단수) | yourself |
| he | himself |
| she | herself |
| it | itself |
| we | ourselves |
| you (복수) | yourselves |
| they | themselves |

---

## B. 재귀 용법: 주어 = 목적어

주어와 목적어가 **같은 사람/것**일 때 재귀대명사를 씁니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I told myself to stay calm."**
> 침착하라고 스스로에게 말했다.

- I = myself (같은 사람)

> **"I pushed myself to keep going."**
> 계속 가라고 나 자신을 밀어붙였다.

- I = myself

> **"I looked at myself in the mirror."**
> 거울에 비친 나를 봤다.

- I (주어) = myself (목적어)

---

## C. 강조 용법: "직접, 스스로"

재귀대명사를 사용해 **강조**할 수 있습니다. 이 경우 생략해도 문법적으로 맞습니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I did it myself."**
> 내가 직접 했다.

- myself = 강조 (스스로)

> **"You should see it yourself."**
> 네가 직접 봐야 해.

- yourself = 강조

> **"The experience itself was valuable."**
> 경험 자체가 가치 있었다.

- itself = 강조

---

## D. by + 재귀대명사 = 혼자서

**by myself/yourself/etc.** = alone (혼자서)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I walked to school by myself."**
> 혼자 학교까지 걸어갔다.

> **"I run by myself every morning."**
> 매일 아침 혼자 달린다.

> **"I sat by myself on the bench."**
> 벤치에 혼자 앉았다.

---

## E. 재귀대명사 vs 목적격 대명사

| Reflexive (주어=목적어) | Object Pronoun (주어≠목적어) |
|------------------------|----------------------------|
| I hurt **myself**. | He hurt **me**. |
| She taught **herself**. | She taught **him**. |
| I looked at **myself**. | I looked at **him**. |

### From the Novel:

> **"I pushed myself."** (I = myself)
> 나 자신을 밀어붙였다.

> **"Mom looked at me."** (Mom ≠ me)
> 엄마가 나를 봤다.

---

## F. 재귀대명사가 필요한 동사

일부 동사는 재귀대명사와 자주 쓰입니다:

| Verb | Example |
|------|---------|
| enjoy oneself | I enjoyed myself. |
| help oneself | Help yourself to the food. |
| hurt oneself | I hurt myself. |
| teach oneself | I taught myself. |
| introduce oneself | I introduced myself. |

### From the Novel:

> **"I found myself thinking about the race."**
> 어느새 경기 생각을 하고 있었다.

> **"I asked myself why I was doing this."**
> 왜 이러고 있는지 스스로에게 물었다.

---

## G. 재귀대명사가 필요 없는 동사

일부 동사는 한국어와 달리 재귀대명사 없이 씁니다:

| English | Korean | Note |
|---------|--------|------|
| wash | 씻다 | NOT: wash myself |
| shave | 면도하다 | NOT: shave myself |
| dress | 옷 입다 | NOT: dress myself |
| feel + 형용사 | 느끼다 | NOT: feel myself tired |

### From the Novel:

> **"I washed and got dressed."** (NOT: washed myself)
> 씻고 옷을 입었다.

> **"I felt tired."** (NOT: felt myself tired)
> 피곤했다.

## Exercises

### A. 알맞은 재귀대명사를 넣으세요.

1. I told _______ to stay calm.
2. She looked at _______ in the mirror.
3. We pushed _______ to keep going.
4. He hurt _______ while running.
5. They enjoyed _______ at the race.

**Answers:**
1. myself
2. herself
3. ourselves
4. himself
5. themselves

### B. 재귀대명사 또는 목적격 대명사를 고르세요.

1. I pushed (myself / me) to run faster.
2. Mom looked at (herself / me) with worry.
3. I taught (myself / me) to run.
4. The coach helped (himself / me) with my form.

**Answers:**
1. myself
2. me
3. myself
4. me

### C. "by + 재귀대명사"로 바꾸세요.

1. I walked to school alone. → I walked to school _______.
2. He finished the race alone. → He finished the race _______.
3. We trained alone. → We trained _______.

**Answers:**
1. by myself
2. by himself
3. by ourselves
