# Unit 82: Myself/yourself/themselves, etc.

## A. 형태 (Form) - 재귀대명사

| Subject | Reflexive | Korean |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| I | **myself** | 나 자신 |
| you (단수) | **yourself** | 너 자신 |
| he | **himself** | 그 자신 |
| she | **herself** | 그녀 자신 |
| it | **itself** | 그것 자체 |
| we | **ourselves** | 우리 자신 |
| you (복수) | **yourselves** | 너희 자신 |
| they | **themselves** | 그들 자신 |
| one | **oneself** | 자기 자신 (일반) |

### 형태 규칙:
- 1/2인칭: 소유격 + self (my + self, your + self)
- 3인칭: 목적격 + self (him + self, her + self)
- 복수: -selves

---

## B. 재귀 용법: 주어 = 목적어 (Reflexive Use)

주어와 목적어가 **같은 사람/것**일 때 재귀대명사를 씁니다.

### From the Original Book:

> **"I cut myself with a knife."**
> 칼로 베였다. (I = myself)

> **"She looked at herself in the mirror."**
> 그녀는 거울에 비친 자신을 봤다. (She = herself)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I told myself to stay calm."**
> 침착하라고 스스로에게 말했다.
- I = myself (같은 사람)

> **"I pushed myself to keep going."**
> 계속 가라고 나 자신을 밀어붙였다.
- I = myself

> **"I looked at myself in the mirror."**
> 거울에 비친 나를 봤다.
- I (주어) = myself (목적어)

> **"I asked myself why I was doing this."**
> 왜 이러고 있는지 스스로에게 물었다.
- I = myself

---

## C. 강조 용법: "직접, 스스로" (Emphatic Use)

재귀대명사를 사용해 **강조**할 수 있습니다. 이 경우 생략해도 문법적으로 맞습니다.

### From the Original Book:

> **"I'll do it myself."** (= I'll do it without help)
> 내가 직접 할게.

> **"The president himself opened the door."**
> 대통령 본인이 문을 열었다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I did it myself."**
> 내가 직접 했다.
- myself = 강조 (스스로)

> **"You should see it yourself."**
> 네가 직접 봐야 해.
- yourself = 강조

> **"The experience itself was valuable."**
> 경험 자체가 가치 있었다.
- itself = 강조 (그것 자체)

> **"The coach himself came to watch."**
> 코치 본인이 보러 왔다.
- himself = 강조 (직접)

---

## D. by + 재귀대명사 = 혼자서 (by oneself = alone)

**by myself/yourself/etc.** = alone, without help (혼자서, 도움 없이)

### From the Original Book:

> **"I like living by myself."**
> 나는 혼자 사는 게 좋다.

> **"Did you go there by yourself?"**
> 거기 혼자 갔어?

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I walked to school by myself."**
> 혼자 학교까지 걸어갔다.

> **"I run by myself every morning."**
> 매일 아침 혼자 달린다.

> **"I sat by myself on the bench."**
> 벤치에 혼자 앉았다.

> **"I finished the race by myself."**
> 혼자서 경기를 마쳤다.

---

## E. 재귀대명사 vs 목적격 대명사 (Comparison)

| Reflexive (주어=목적어) | Object Pronoun (주어≠목적어) |
|------------------------|----------------------------|
| I hurt **myself**. | He hurt **me**. |
| She taught **herself**. | She taught **him**. |
| I looked at **myself**. | I looked at **him**. |
| They blamed **themselves**. | They blamed **us**. |

### From the Novel:

> **"I pushed myself."** (I = myself)
> 나 자신을 밀어붙였다.

> **"Mom looked at me."** (Mom ≠ me)
> 엄마가 나를 봤다.

> **"I told myself to calm down."** (I = myself)
> 침착하라고 스스로에게 말했다.

> **"Minseo told me to calm down."** (Minseo ≠ me)
> 민서가 나한테 진정하라고 했다.

## F. 재귀대명사가 필요한 동사/표현 (Common Expressions)

일부 동사와 표현은 재귀대명사와 자주 쓰입니다:

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| enjoy oneself | 즐기다 | I enjoyed myself at the party. |
| help oneself | 마음대로 먹다 | Help yourself to the food. |
| hurt oneself | 다치다 | I hurt myself while running. |
| teach oneself | 독학하다 | I taught myself to run. |
| introduce oneself | 자기소개하다 | I introduced myself to the coach. |
| find oneself | 어느새 ~하다 | I found myself thinking about it. |
| ask oneself | 스스로 묻다 | I asked myself why. |
| tell oneself | 스스로 말하다 | I told myself to stay calm. |
| blame oneself | 자책하다 | Don't blame yourself. |
| behave oneself | 얌전히 굴다 | Behave yourself! |

### From the Novel:

> **"I found myself thinking about the race."**
> 어느새 경기 생각을 하고 있었다.

> **"I asked myself why I was doing this."**
> 왜 이러고 있는지 스스로에게 물었다.

## G. 재귀대명사가 필요 없는 동사 (Verbs Without Reflexives)

일부 동사는 한국어와 달리 재귀대명사 **없이** 씁니다:

| English | Korean | Note |
|---------|--------|------|
| wash | 씻다 | NOT: wash myself |
| shave | 면도하다 | NOT: shave myself |
| dress | 옷 입다 | NOT: dress myself |
| feel + 형용사 | ~하게 느끼다 | NOT: feel myself tired |
| concentrate | 집중하다 | NOT: concentrate myself |
| relax | 쉬다 | NOT: relax myself |

### From the Novel:

> **"I washed and got dressed."** (NOT: washed myself)
> 씻고 옷을 입었다.

> **"I felt tired."** (NOT: felt myself tired)
> 피곤했다.

> **"I need to concentrate."** (NOT: concentrate myself)
> 집중해야 해.

### 예외: 문맥에 따라 필요할 때

> **"The baby can dress herself now."** (혼자서 입을 수 있음 강조)
> 아기가 이제 혼자 옷을 입을 수 있다.

## H. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | 설명 |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| I felt **myself** tired. | I felt tired. | feel + 형용사 |
| I wash **myself** every day. | I wash every day. | wash는 재귀 불필요 |
| He and **myself** went. | He and **I** went. | 주어는 I |
| Give it to **myself**. | Give it to **me**. | 주어 ≠ 목적어 |
| **Theirselves** | **Themselves** | 철자 |
| **Hisself** | **Himself** | 철자 |

### 추가 실수 예시:

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) |
|-----------|-------------|
| I concentrate **myself**. | I concentrate. |
| Relax **yourself**. | Relax. |
| Me and **myself** went. | I went by myself. |
| They enjoyed **themself**. | They enjoyed **themselves**. |

### B. 재귀대명사 또는 목적격 대명사를 고르세요.

1. I pushed (myself / me) to run faster.

3. I taught (myself / me) to run.
4. The coach helped (himself / me) with my form.
5. She introduced (herself / her) to the teacher.
6. They blamed (themselves / them) for the loss.
7. He saw (himself / him) on TV. (그 자신을 봄)

**Answers:**
1. myself
2. me
3. myself
4. me
5. herself
6. themselves
7. himself (자신)
8. him (다른 사람)

### C. "by + 재귀대명사"로 바꾸세요.

1. I walked to school alone. → I walked to school _______.
2. He finished the race alone. → He finished the race _______.
3. We trained alone. → We trained _______.
4. Did you do it alone? → Did you do it _______?
5. She lives alone. → She lives _______.

**Answers:**
1. by myself
2. by himself
3. by ourselves
4. by yourself
5. by herself

### D. 틀린 부분을 고치세요.

1. I felt myself tired after running.
2. Me and myself went to the race.
3. He washed hisself.
4. Give the book to myself.
5. They enjoyed themself.

**Answers:**
1. I felt tired after running.
2. I went to the race by myself. / I went alone.
3. He washed himself.
4. Give the book to me.
5. They enjoyed themselves.
6. I need to concentrate.

## Summary: 재귀대명사 용법

| Usage | Example | Meaning |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 재귀 (주어=목적어) | I hurt **myself**. | 내가 나를 |
| 강조 (직접, 스스로) | I did it **myself**. | 직접 |
| by + 재귀 (혼자서) | I walked **by myself**. | 혼자서 |

### 재귀 vs 목적격:
- **주어 = 목적어** → 재귀대명사 (myself, yourself...)
- **주어 ≠ 목적어** → 목적격 대명사 (me, you, him...)

### 재귀 필요 없는 동사:
- wash, shave, dress, feel, concentrate, relax

### 주의할 철자:
- himself (NOT: hisself)
- themselves (NOT: theirselves)
- yourself / yourselves (단수/복수)
