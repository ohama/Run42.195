# Unit 42: Passive (1) - is done / was done

## A. 수동태의 기본 개념 (What is Passive?)

**능동태:** 주어가 동작을 **한다**
**수동태:** 주어가 동작을 **받는다**

### 능동태 vs 수동태:

| 능동태 (Active) | 수동태 (Passive) |
|-----------------|------------------|
| The teacher **praised** me. | I **was praised** by the teacher. |
| 선생님이 나를 칭찬했다. | 나는 선생님에게 칭찬받았다. |
| Someone **pushed** me. | I **was pushed**. |
| 누군가가 나를 밀었다. | 나는 밀렸다. |

### 수동태 기본 공식:
```
be동사 + 과거분사(p.p.)
```

### 시제별 수동태:

| 시제 | 능동태 | 수동태 |
|------|--------|--------|
| 현재 | does | **is done** |
| 과거 | did | **was done** |

---

## B. 현재 수동태 (is/are + p.p.)

현재 **일반적으로** 일어나는 일을 수동태로 표현

### From the Original Book:

> "English **is spoken** all over the world."
> 영어는 전 세계에서 사용된다.

> "The office **is cleaned** every day."
> 사무실은 매일 청소된다.

> "These cars **are made** in Japan."
> 이 차들은 일본에서 만들어진다.

### 수동태 형태표:

| 주어 | Be 동사 | 과거분사 | 예문 |
|------|---------|----------|------|
| I | am | done | I am invited. |
| He/She/It | is | done | It is held. |
| We/You/They | are | done | They are made. |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The race is held every year in March."**
> 대회는 매년 3월에 열린다.

> **"The school is located 7 kilometers from my house."**
> 학교는 우리 집에서 7km 떨어져 있다.

> **"The marathon course is known for its beautiful scenery."**
> 마라톤 코스는 아름다운 경치로 알려져 있다.

> **"The bib number is given to each runner."**
> 배번은 각 주자에게 주어진다.

---

## C. 과거 수동태 (was/were + p.p.)

과거에 **일어난 일**을 수동태로 표현

### From the Original Book:

> "The window **was broken** by a ball."
> 창문이 공에 의해 깨졌다.

> "These houses **were built** 100 years ago."
> 이 집들은 100년 전에 지어졌다.

> "I **was woken** up by a loud noise."
> 나는 큰 소리에 깨어났다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was pushed inside by the crowd."**
> 나는 사람들에 밀려 안으로 들어갔다.

> **"My bag was hit from all sides."**
> 내 가방은 사방에서 맞았다.

> **"I was praised by the teacher."**
> 선생님께 칭찬받았다.

> **"The finisher's medal was given to me."**
> 완주 메달을 받았다.

> **"The race bib was pinned on my chest."**
> 배번이 가슴에 달렸다.

---

## D. 수동태를 사용하는 경우

### 1. 행위자가 중요하지 않을 때

> **"The race is held every year."** (누가 개최하는지 중요하지 않음)
> 대회는 매년 열린다.

> **"This bridge was built in 1920."** (누가 지었는지 중요하지 않음)
> 이 다리는 1920년에 지어졌다.

### 2. 행위자를 모를 때

> **"My bag was hit from all sides."** (누가 쳤는지 모름)
> 내 가방이 사방에서 맞았다.

> **"My bike was stolen."** (누가 훔쳤는지 모름)
> 내 자전거가 도난당했다.

### 3. 행위자가 명확할 때 (일반적인 사람들)

> **"English is spoken all over the world."** (사람들이 말하는 것은 명확)
> 영어는 전 세계에서 사용된다.

> **"Rice is grown in many countries."**
> 쌀은 많은 나라에서 재배된다.

### 4. 결과/상태에 초점을 맞출 때

> **"The work is finished."** (결과 강조)
> 일이 끝났다.

> **"The school is located near the bus stop."**
> 학교는 버스 정류장 근처에 위치해 있다.

---

## E. by + 행위자

행위자를 밝히고 싶을 때 **by**를 사용합니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "The letter **was written by** Shakespeare."
> 그 편지는 셰익스피어에 의해 쓰여졌다.

> "The telephone **was invented by** Alexander Bell."
> 전화는 알렉산더 벨에 의해 발명되었다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was praised by the PE teacher."**
> 체육 선생님께 칭찬받았다.

> **"I was encouraged by my family."**
> 가족에게 격려받았다.

> **"The training plan was made by my coach."**
> 훈련 계획은 코치님이 만드셨다.

### by 사용 시 주의:

| 행위자 표시 필요 | 행위자 생략 가능 |
|------------------|------------------|
| 특정인/중요한 행위자 | 불특정 다수/명확한 행위자 |
| by Shakespeare | (by people) 생략 |
| by my coach | (by someone) 생략 |

## F. 능동태 → 수동태 변환

### 변환 규칙:

| 단계 | 설명 | 예시 |
|------|------|------|
| 1 | 능동태 목적어 → 수동태 주어 | me → I |
| 2 | 동사 → be + p.p. | praised → was praised |
| 3 | 능동태 주어 → by + 목적격 | The teacher → by the teacher |

### 변환 예시:

| 능동태 | 수동태 |
|--------|--------|
| The crowd **pushed** me. | I **was pushed** by the crowd. |
| They **hold** the race every year. | The race **is held** every year. |
| Mom **made** breakfast. | Breakfast **was made** by Mom. |
| The teacher **praised** me. | I **was praised** by the teacher. |

### From the Novel:

**능동태:**
> "People hit my bag from all sides."

**수동태:**
> **"My bag was hit from all sides."**
> 내 가방은 사방에서 맞았다.

## G. 부정문과 의문문

### 부정문: be + not + p.p.

| 시제 | 구조 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| 현재 | is/are + not + p.p. | The race **isn't held** in winter. |
| 과거 | was/were + not + p.p. | The race **wasn't canceled**. |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The race wasn't canceled."**
> 대회는 취소되지 않았다.

> **"The bus isn't crowded in the afternoon."**
> 오후에는 버스가 붐비지 않는다.

### 의문문: Be + 주어 + p.p.?

| 시제 | 구조 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| 현재 | Is/Are + 주어 + p.p.? | **Is** English **spoken** here? |
| 과거 | Was/Were + 주어 + p.p.? | **Was** the race **held** last year? |

### Examples:

> **"Was the race held last year?"**
> 작년에 대회가 열렸나요?

> **"Is English spoken here?"**
> 여기서 영어가 사용되나요?

## H. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| 틀린 문장 (X) | 올바른 문장 (O) | 설명 |
|---------------|-----------------|------|
| The race **held** every year. | The race **is held** every year. | be동사 필요 |
| I **was praise** by the teacher. | I **was praised** by the teacher. | 과거분사 형태 |
| The letter **was wrote** by him. | The letter **was written** by him. | 올바른 p.p. |
| English **is speak** here. | English **is spoken** here. | 과거분사 형태 |
| The bus **was push** inside. | I **was pushed** inside. | 과거분사 형태 |
| My bag **hitted** by people. | My bag **was hit** by people. | be + p.p. |

### 불규칙 과거분사 주의:

| 동사 | 과거형 | 과거분사 |
|------|--------|----------|
| write | wrote | **written** |
| take | took | **taken** |
| give | gave | **given** |
| see | saw | **seen** |
| make | made | **made** |
| hold | held | **held** |

## Exercises

### A. 수동태로 바꾸세요.

1. The teacher praised me.
   → I _______________________ by the teacher.

2. They hold the marathon every year.
   → The marathon _______________________ every year.

3. Someone pushed me into the bus.
   → I _______________________ into the bus.

4. The crowd blocked the door.
   → The door _______________________ by the crowd.

5. My coach gave me a training plan.
   → A training plan _______________________ to me by my coach.

6. People speak English here.
   → English _______________________ here.

7. Shakespeare wrote this play.
   → This play _______________________ by Shakespeare.

8. They built this bridge in 1950.
   → This bridge _______________________ in 1950.

**Answers:**
1. was praised
2. is held
3. was pushed
4. was blocked
5. was given
6. is spoken
7. was written
8. was built

### B. 능동태로 바꾸세요.

1. The race was won by a Korean runner.
   → A Korean runner _______________________.

2. I was encouraged by my family.
   → My family _______________________.

3. The finish line was crossed by hundreds of runners.
   → Hundreds of runners _______________________.

4. The letter was written by my grandmother.
   → My grandmother _______________________.

5. The window was broken by the children.
   → The children _______________________.

**Answers:**
1. won the race
2. encouraged me
3. crossed the finish line
4. wrote the letter
5. broke the window

### C. 빈칸에 알맞은 형태를 쓰세요.

1. The race _______ (hold) every March.
2. I _______ (push) inside by the crowd yesterday.
3. The finisher's medal _______ (give) to me after the race.

6. This bridge _______ (build) 100 years ago.
7. Rice _______ (grow) in many countries.
8. My bike _______ (steal) last week.

**Answers:**
1. is held
2. was pushed
3. was given
4. is known
5. were injured
6. was built
7. is grown
8. was stolen

### D. 수동태 vs 능동태 - 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. The race (holds / is held) every year in March.
2. I (praised / was praised) by my coach.

6. Someone (stole / was stolen) my wallet.
7. The building (destroyed / was destroyed) by fire.

**Answers:**
1. is held
2. was praised
3. pushed
4. speak
5. was written
6. stole
7. was destroyed

## Summary: 수동태 기본 정리

### 수동태 공식:
```
be동사 + 과거분사 (p.p.)

현재 수동태: am/is/are + p.p.
과거 수동태: was/were + p.p.

행위자 표시: by + 행위자
부정: be + not + p.p.
의문: Be + 주어 + p.p.?
```

### 능동태 vs 수동태 비교:

| 능동태 | 수동태 |
|--------|--------|
| 주어가 행동함 | 주어가 행동 받음 |
| The teacher praised me. | I was praised. |
| They hold the race. | The race is held. |

### 수동태 사용 상황:

| 상황 | 예문 |
|------|------|
| 행위자 불명 | My bike was stolen. |
| 행위자 중요하지 않음 | The race is held annually. |
| 결과/상태 강조 | The work is finished. |
| 행위자가 일반적 | English is spoken here. |
