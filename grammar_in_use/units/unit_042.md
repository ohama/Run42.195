# Unit 42: Passive (1) - is done / was done

## A. 수동태의 기본 구조

**능동태:** 주어가 동작을 **한다**
**수동태:** 주어가 동작을 **받는다**

| 시제 | 능동태 | 수동태 |
|------|--------|--------|
| 현재 | does | **is done** |
| 과거 | did | **was done** |

### 공식:
**be동사 + 과거분사(p.p.)**

---

## B. 현재 수동태 (is/are + p.p.)

현재 **일반적으로** 일어나는 일을 수동태로 표현

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The race is held every year in March."**
> 대회는 매년 3월에 열린다.

> **"The school is located 7 kilometers from my house."**
> 학교는 우리 집에서 7km 떨어져 있다.

> **"The marathon course is known for its beautiful scenery."**
> 마라톤 코스는 아름다운 경치로 알려져 있다.

> **"The bib number is given to each runner."**
> 배번은 각 주자에게 주어진다.

---

## C. 과거 수동태 (was/were + p.p.)

과거에 **일어난 일**을 수동태로 표현

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was pushed inside by the crowd."**
> 나는 사람들에 밀려 안으로 들어갔다.

> **"My bag was hit from all sides."**
> 내 가방은 사방에서 맞았다.

> **"I was praised by the teacher."**
> 선생님께 칭찬받았다.

> **"The finisher's medal was given to me."**
> 완주 메달을 받았다.

> **"The race bib was pinned on my chest."**
> 배번이 가슴에 달렸다.

---

## D. 수동태를 사용하는 경우

### 1. 행위자가 중요하지 않을 때

> **"The race is held every year."** (누가 개최하는지 중요하지 않음)
> 대회는 매년 열린다.

### 2. 행위자를 모를 때

> **"My bag was hit from all sides."** (누가 쳤는지 모름)
> 내 가방이 사방에서 맞았다.

### 3. 행위자가 명확할 때 (일반적인 사람들)

> **"English is spoken all over the world."** (사람들이 말하는 것은 명확)
> 영어는 전 세계에서 사용된다.

---

## E. by + 행위자

행위자를 밝히고 싶을 때 **by**를 사용합니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was praised by the PE teacher."**
> 체육 선생님께 칭찬받았다.

> **"I was encouraged by my family."**
> 가족에게 격려받았다.

> **"The training plan was made by my coach."**
> 훈련 계획은 코치님이 만드셨다.

---

## F. 능동태 → 수동태 변환

| 능동태 | 수동태 |
|--------|--------|
| The crowd **pushed** me. | I **was pushed** by the crowd. |
| They **hold** the race every year. | The race **is held** every year. |
| Mom **made** breakfast. | Breakfast **was made** by Mom. |
| The teacher **praised** me. | I **was praised** by the teacher. |

### From the Novel:

**능동태:**
> "People hit my bag from all sides."

**수동태:**
> **"My bag was hit from all sides."**
> 내 가방은 사방에서 맞았다.

---

## G. 부정문과 의문문

### 부정문: be + not + p.p.

> **"The race wasn't canceled."**
> 대회는 취소되지 않았다.

> **"The bus isn't crowded in the afternoon."**
> 오후에는 버스가 붐비지 않는다.

### 의문문: Be + 주어 + p.p.?

> **"Was the race held last year?"**
> 작년에 대회가 열렸나요?

> **"Is English spoken here?"**
> 여기서 영어가 사용되나요?

## Exercises

### A. 수동태로 바꾸세요.

1. The teacher praised me.
   → I _______________________ by the teacher.

2. They hold the marathon every year.
   → The marathon _______________________ every year.

3. Someone pushed me into the bus.
   → I _______________________ into the bus.

4. The crowd blocked the door.
   → The door _______________________ by the crowd.

5. My coach gave me a training plan.
   → A training plan _______________________ to me by my coach.

**Answers:**
1. was praised
2. is held
3. was pushed
4. was blocked
5. was given

### B. 능동태로 바꾸세요.

1. The race was won by a Korean runner.
   → A Korean runner _______________________.

2. I was encouraged by my family.
   → My family _______________________.

3. The finish line was crossed by hundreds of runners.
   → Hundreds of runners _______________________.

**Answers:**
1. won the race
2. encouraged me
3. crossed the finish line

### C. 빈칸에 알맞은 형태를 쓰세요.

1. The race _______ (hold) every March.
2. I _______ (push) inside by the crowd yesterday.
3. The finisher's medal _______ (give) to me after the race.
4. The course _______ (know) for beautiful scenery.
5. Many runners _______ (injure) during the marathon last year.

**Answers:**
1. is held
2. was pushed
3. was given
4. is known
5. were injured

### D. 수동태 vs 능동태 - 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. The race (holds / is held) every year in March.
2. I (praised / was praised) by my coach.
3. The crowd (pushed / was pushed) me inside.
4. Many people (speak / are spoken) English.

**Answers:**
1. is held
2. was praised
3. pushed
4. speak

## Summary

```
수동태 기본 공식:
be동사 + 과거분사 (p.p.)

현재 수동태: am/is/are + p.p.
과거 수동태: was/were + p.p.

행위자 표시: by + 행위자
부정: be + not + p.p.
의문: Be + 주어 + p.p.?
```
