# Unit 103: Enough and Too

## A. enough - 충분한/충분히

**enough**는 "충분한"의 의미로, 형용사/부사 **뒤에**, 명사 **앞에** 옵니다.

| 위치 | 구조 | 예시 |
|------|------|------|
| 형용사/부사 뒤 | 형용사 + enough | strong **enough** |
| 명사 앞 | enough + 명사 | **enough** time |

---

## B. 형용사/부사 + enough

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Am I strong enough to finish?"**
> 내가 완주할 만큼 강한가?

> **"I wasn't fast enough to catch the bus."**
> 버스를 잡을 만큼 빠르지 않았다.

> **"I trained hard enough to complete the race."**
> 대회를 완주할 만큼 충분히 훈련했다.

> **"The weather was warm enough to run in shorts."**
> 반바지 입고 달릴 만큼 따뜻했다.

> **"I woke up early enough to run before school."**
> 학교 가기 전에 달릴 만큼 일찍 일어났다.

### Pattern:
```
형용사/부사 + enough + to부정사
I was strong enough to finish.
```

---

## C. enough + 명사

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I didn't have enough time to rest."**
> 쉴 시간이 충분하지 않았다.

> **"I brought enough water for the run."**
> 달리기에 충분한 물을 가져왔다.

> **"I had enough energy to finish."**
> 완주할 에너지가 충분했다.

> **"There wasn't enough space on the bus."**
> 버스에 공간이 충분하지 않았다.

### Pattern:
```
enough + 명사 + to부정사
I have enough stamina to run 10km.
```

---

## D. too - 너무 (부정적)

**too**는 "너무"라는 의미로, **문제나 어려움**을 나타냅니다. 형용사/부사 **앞에** 옵니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The bus was too crowded."**
> 버스가 너무 붐볐다.

> **"It was too hot to run in the afternoon."**
> 오후에 달리기엔 너무 더웠다.

> **"The distance was too long."**
> 거리가 너무 멀었다.

> **"I was too tired to move."**
> 너무 피곤해서 움직일 수 없었다.

> **"I woke up too late and missed the bus."**
> 너무 늦게 일어나서 버스를 놓쳤다.

### Pattern:
```
too + 형용사/부사 + to부정사
It was too hot to run.
```

---

## E. too vs very

**too**와 **very**는 다릅니다!

| too | very |
|-----|------|
| 부정적/문제 있음 | 단순 강조 |
| 너무 (못할 정도로) | 매우 |

### Comparison:

> **"The race was very hard."** (힘들었다 - 중립적)
> 대회가 매우 힘들었다.

> **"The race was too hard."** (힘들어서 문제가 됨)
> 대회가 너무 힘들었다.

### From the Novel:

> **"It was very hot, but I ran anyway."** (더웠지만 달림)
> 매우 더웠지만 어쨌든 달렸다.

> **"It was too hot to run."** (더워서 달릴 수 없음)
> 너무 더워서 달릴 수 없었다.

---

## F. enough vs too 비교

| enough (충분한) | too (너무) |
|-----------------|-----------|
| 긍정적/가능 | 부정적/불가능 |
| strong enough to finish | too tired to finish |
| warm enough to run | too hot to run |

### Examples:

> **"I was strong enough to finish."** (완주 가능)
> 완주할 만큼 충분히 강했다.

> **"I was too weak to finish."** (완주 불가능)
> 너무 약해서 완주할 수 없었다.

---

## G. too + for + 사람

**too...for**는 "~에게 너무"라는 의미입니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The distance was too long for a beginner."**
> 그 거리는 초보자에게 너무 멀었다.

> **"The race was too hard for me at first."**
> 처음에는 대회가 나에게 너무 힘들었다.

> **"The morning was too cold for running."**
> 아침이 달리기에 너무 추웠다.

## Exercises

### A. enough를 올바른 위치에 넣으세요.

1. I was strong _______ to finish.
   → **enough**

2. I had _______ energy to run.
   → **enough**

3. She ran fast _______ to win.
   → **enough**

4. There wasn't _______ time.
   → **enough**

### B. too 또는 enough 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. It was (too / enough) hot to run in the afternoon.
   → **too**

2. I was strong (too / enough) to finish the marathon.
   → **enough**

3. The bus was (too / enough) crowded to sit.
   → **too**

4. I had (too / enough) water for the run.
   → **enough**

5. I was (too / enough) tired to move.
   → **too**

### C. 문장을 완성하세요.

1. I wasn't fast _______ to catch the bus.
   → **enough**

2. It was _______ hot _______ run.
   → **too** / **to**

3. I had _______ time _______ rest.
   → **enough** / **to**

4. The race was _______ hard _______ me.
   → **too** / **for**

### D. too와 very 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. The race was (very / too) hard, but I finished.
   → **very** (완주했으므로)

2. It was (very / too) hot, so I couldn't run.
   → **too** (달릴 수 없었으므로)

3. The scenery was (very / too) beautiful.
   → **very** (단순 강조)

4. I was (very / too) tired to continue.
   → **too** (계속할 수 없었으므로)

## Summary

| | enough | too |
|---|--------|-----|
| 의미 | 충분한 | 너무 (문제) |
| 위치 | 형용사 뒤, 명사 앞 | 형용사/부사 앞 |
| 결과 | 가능 | 불가능 |
| 예시 | strong enough to | too weak to |
