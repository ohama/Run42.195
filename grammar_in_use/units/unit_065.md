# Unit 65: Adjective + to...

## A. 형용사 + to부정사

많은 형용사 뒤에 **to부정사**가 옵니다.

**주요 패턴:**
- It's + 형용사 + to + 동사
- 주어 + be + 형용사 + to + 동사

---

## B. It's + 형용사 + to + 동사

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"It's hard to run in the heat."**
> 더위 속에서 달리기는 힘들다.

- **It's hard to + 동사**: ~하기 어렵다

> **"It's nice to feel the morning breeze."**
> 아침 바람을 느끼는 건 좋다.

- **It's nice to + 동사**: ~하는 건 좋다

> **"It's important to stretch before running."**
> 달리기 전에 스트레칭하는 게 중요하다.

- **It's important to + 동사**: ~하는 게 중요하다

> **"It's easy to give up."**
> 포기하기는 쉽다.

- **It's easy to + 동사**: ~하기 쉽다

---

## C. It's + 형용사 + (for someone) + to + 동사

누구에게 해당하는지 명시할 때 **for + 사람**을 넣습니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"It's hard for me to wake up early."**
> 나에게 일찍 일어나는 건 힘들다.

- **It's hard for someone to + 동사**: ~에게 ~하기 어렵다

> **"It was impossible for me to run 10km."**
> 나에게 10km 달리기는 불가능했다.

> **"It's important for runners to stay hydrated."**
> 러너들에게 수분 유지가 중요하다.

---

## D. 주어 + be + 형용사 + to + 동사

감정이나 상태를 나타내는 형용사와 함께 쓰입니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was happy to finish the race."**
> 레이스를 끝내서 행복했다.

- **be happy to + 동사**: ~해서 기쁘다

> **"I was surprised to see how far I ran."**
> 얼마나 멀리 뛰었는지 보고 놀랐다.

- **be surprised to + 동사**: ~해서 놀라다

> **"I was relieved to cross the finish line."**
> 결승선을 통과해서 안도했다.

- **be relieved to + 동사**: ~해서 안도하다

> **"I'm ready to run the marathon."**
> 마라톤을 뛸 준비가 됐다.

- **be ready to + 동사**: ~할 준비가 되다

---

## E. 감정 형용사 + to

| Adjective | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| happy | I was happy to finish. |
| glad | I was glad to see you. |
| pleased | I was pleased to hear that. |
| sad | I was sad to leave. |
| sorry | I'm sorry to hear that. |
| surprised | I was surprised to see him. |
| shocked | I was shocked to learn that. |
| relieved | I was relieved to finish. |
| excited | I'm excited to run. |
| afraid | I was afraid to fail. |

## F. 상태/준비 형용사 + to

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I'm ready to start."**
> 시작할 준비가 됐다.

> **"I'm willing to train hard."**
> 열심히 훈련할 의향이 있다.

> **"I'm able to run 10km now."**
> 이제 10km를 달릴 수 있다.

> **"I'm likely to finish under 5 hours."**
> 5시간 안에 끝낼 것 같다.

| Adjective | Meaning | Example |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| ready | 준비된 | I'm ready to run. |
| willing | 기꺼이 하는 | I'm willing to try. |
| able | ~할 수 있는 | I'm able to finish. |
| likely | ~할 것 같은 | I'm likely to win. |
| unlikely | ~할 것 같지 않은 | I'm unlikely to quit. |
| certain | 확실한 | I'm certain to finish. |
| sure | 확신하는 | I'm sure to do well. |

---

## G. 형용사 + to 의미 변화

일부 형용사는 to부정사와 함께 쓰이면 의미가 달라집니다.

| Adjective + to | Meaning |
|----------------|---------|
| nice to meet you | 만나서 반가워요 |
| good to see you | 봐서 좋아요 |
| hard to believe | 믿기 어려운 |
| easy to understand | 이해하기 쉬운 |

### From the Novel:

> **"It was hard to believe I ran 42km."**
> 42km를 뛰었다는 게 믿기 어려웠다.

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. It's important (to stretch / stretching) before running.
2. I was happy (to finish / finishing) the race.
3. It's hard for me (to wake / waking) up early.
4. I'm ready (to start / starting) the race.
5. She was surprised (to see / seeing) me.

**Answers:**
1. to stretch
2. to finish
3. to wake
4. to start
5. to see

### B. 문장을 완성하세요.

1. It's hard _______ (run) in the heat.
2. I was relieved _______ (cross) the finish line.
3. It's important for runners _______ (stay) hydrated.
4. I'm willing _______ (train) hard.
5. She was excited _______ (start) running.

**Answers:**
1. to run
2. to cross
3. to stay
4. to train
5. to start

### C. 형용사를 사용하여 문장을 만드세요.

1. 레이스를 끝내서 기뻤다. (happy)
   → I was _______.

2. 일찍 일어나는 건 힘들다. (hard)
   → It's _______.

3. 마라톤을 뛸 준비가 됐다. (ready)
   → I'm _______.

**Answers:**
1. happy to finish the race
2. hard to wake up early
3. ready to run the marathon
