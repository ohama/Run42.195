# Unit 25: When I do / When I've done / When and if

## A. 핵심 규칙 (Key Rule)

### 시간/조건 부사절에서의 시제

**When, if, after, before, until, as soon as** 등의 접속사 뒤에는 **미래 시제(will)를 쓰지 않습니다.**
대신 현재시제나 현재완료를 사용합니다.

| 주절 (Main clause) | 부사절 (Subordinate clause) |
|-------------------|----------------------------|
| 미래 (will) | 현재 (do) 또는 현재완료 (have done) |

### 기본 형태:

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) |
|-----------|-------------|
| ~~When I will arrive~~ | When I **arrive** |
| ~~If it will rain~~ | If it **rains** |
| ~~After I will finish~~ | After I **finish** |

---

## B. 시간 접속사 (Time Conjunctions)

### From the Original Book:

> "**When** I **get** home, I'll have a shower."
> 집에 도착하면 샤워할 거야.

> "I'll phone you **when** I **arrive**."
> 도착하면 전화할게.

> "**As soon as** they **arrive**, we'll have dinner."
> 그들이 도착하자마자 저녁 먹을 거야.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"When I get to school, I'll rest on the bench."**
> 학교에 도착하면 벤치에서 쉴 거야.

- When + 현재 (get), 주절은 will

> **"As soon as I cross the finish line, I'll call my mom."**
> 결승선을 넘자마자 엄마한테 전화할 거야.

- As soon as + 현재시제

> **"I won't stop until I finish."**
> 끝낼 때까지 멈추지 않을 거야.

- Until + 현재 (finish)

### 시간 접속사 목록:

| Conjunction | Meaning | Example |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| when | ~할 때 | When I arrive, I'll call you. |
| before | ~하기 전에 | Before I run, I'll stretch. |
| after | ~한 후에 | After I finish, I'll rest. |
| until / till | ~할 때까지 | I'll run until I finish. |
| as soon as | ~하자마자 | As soon as I see him, I'll wave. |
| while | ~하는 동안 | While you're here, I'll show you around. |
| by the time | ~할 때쯤이면 | By the time you arrive, I'll be ready. |

## C. 현재 vs 현재완료 (when 절에서)

### 현재시제 (Present Simple): 동시에 일어나는 일

> **"When I see Minseo, I'll tell him."**
> 민서를 보면 말할게.

- see와 tell이 거의 동시에 일어남

### 현재완료 (Present Perfect): 완료된 후에

> **"When I've finished the race, I'll celebrate."**
> 대회를 끝내면 축하할 거야.

- finish가 완료된 **후에** celebrate

### Comparison:

| When + Present | When + Present Perfect |
|----------------|------------------------|
| ~할 때 (동시) | ~하고 나서 (완료 후) |
| When I **see** him, I'll say hi. | When I**'ve finished**, I'll rest. |
| When the race **starts**, I'll be ready. | When I**'ve run** 10km, I'll take a break. |

## D. When vs If

### When: 확실히 일어날 일

> **"When Monday comes, I'll ride the bus again."**
> 월요일이 오면 또 버스를 탈 거야.

- 월요일은 확실히 옴

> **"When the race ends, I'll celebrate."**
> 대회가 끝나면 축하할 거야.

- 대회는 확실히 끝남

### If: 불확실한 일 (조건)

> **"If it rains, I'll run anyway."**
> 비가 오면 그래도 달릴 거야.

- 비가 올지 모름

> **"If my legs hurt, I'll slow down."**
> 다리가 아프면 속도를 줄일 거야.

- 아플지 모름

### Comparison Table:

| When (확실) | If (불확실) |
|-------------|-------------|
| When I finish (끝내면 - 확실) | If I finish (만약 끝내면 - 불확실) |
| When it rains (비 오면 - 확실히 올 것) | If it rains (비가 오면 - 올지 모름) |
| When you arrive (도착하면 - 확실) | If you arrive (도착하면 - 올지 모름) |
| When Monday comes (월요일이 오면) | If we have time (시간이 있으면) |

## E. 부사절 vs 명사절 (Adverb vs Noun Clauses)

### 부사절: will 사용 불가

**시간/조건**을 나타내는 부사절:

> "**When he arrives**, I'll tell him." (그가 도착하면 말할게)
> - When + 현재시제

### 명사절: will 사용 가능

**의문의 대상**이 되는 명사절:

> "I don't know **when he will arrive**." (언제 올지 모르겠어)
> - when + will (명사절)

| 부사절 (Adverb Clause) | 명사절 (Noun Clause) |
|-----------------------|---------------------|
| When he **comes**, I'll tell him. | I don't know when he **will come**. |
| (그가 오면 말할게) | (언제 올지 모르겠어) |
| If it **rains**, we'll stay home. | I wonder if it **will rain**. |
| (비 오면 집에 있을 거야) | (비가 올지 궁금해) |

## F. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~When I will arrive~~ | When I **arrive** | 시간 부사절 = 현재시제 |
| ~~If it will rain~~ | If it **rains** | 조건 부사절 = 현재시제 |
| ~~Before I will go~~ | Before I **go** | 시간 부사절 = 현재시제 |
| ~~I'll call when I'll arrive.~~ | I'll call when I **arrive**. | 부사절에 will 안 씀 |
| ~~Until I will finish~~ | Until I **finish** | 시간 부사절 = 현재시제 |
| ~~As soon as I will see~~ | As soon as I **see** | 시간 부사절 = 현재시제 |

### 주의할 점:

| 문장 유형 | 예문 |
|----------|------|
| 부사절 (O) | I'll tell you **when I arrive**. (도착하면) |
| 명사절 (O) | Tell me **when you will arrive**. (언제 올지) |
| 부사절 (O) | **If it rains**, I'll stay home. (비 오면) |
| 명사절 (O) | I don't know **if it will rain**. (올지 모르겠어) |

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 형태 고르기 (Choose the correct form)

1. When I (arrive / will arrive), I'll call you.
2. If it (rains / will rain) tomorrow, I'll stay home.
3. I'll rest when I (finish / will finish) the race.
4. As soon as I (get / will get) home, I'll take a shower.
5. I won't stop until I (reach / will reach) the finish line.
6. Before the race (starts / will start), I'll stretch.
7. After I (run / will run) 10km, I'll walk home.
8. I'll wait here while you (are / will be) shopping.

**Answers:**
1. arrive
2. rains
3. finish
4. get
5. reach
6. starts
7. run
8. are

### B. 문장 완성하기 (Complete the sentences)

1. When I _______ (see) Minseo, I'll tell him.
2. If you _______ (be) tired, take a rest.
3. After I _______ (run) 10km, I'll walk home.
4. I'll celebrate when I _______ (complete) the marathon.
5. Before the race _______ (start), I'll stretch.
6. As soon as I _______ (finish), I'll call you.
7. I'll keep running until I _______ (reach) the finish line.
8. By the time you _______ (arrive), I'll have finished.

**Answers:**
1. see
2. are
3. have run / run
4. have completed / complete
5. starts
6. finish
7. reach
8. arrive

### C. When vs If 선택 (Choose When or If)

1. _______ Monday comes, I'll go back to school. (확실히 옴)
2. _______ it rains, we'll cancel the run. (불확실)
3. _______ I finish the race, I'll be so happy. (확실히 끝낼 것)
4. _______ I'm too tired, I'll stop. (불확실)
5. _______ the race starts, I'll be ready. (확실히 시작)
6. _______ you need help, call me. (불확실)
7. _______ I see him tomorrow, I'll give him your message. (확실히 볼 것)
8. _______ he comes to the party, I'll introduce you. (불확실)

**Answers:**
1. When
2. If
3. When
4. If
5. When
6. If
7. When
8. If

### D. 부사절 vs 명사절 (Adverb vs Noun Clause)

Choose the correct form.

1. I'll tell you when I (arrive / will arrive). [부사절]
2. I don't know when he (arrives / will arrive). [명사절]
3. If it (rains / will rain), we'll stay inside. [부사절]
4. Do you know if it (rains / will rain) tomorrow? [명사절]
5. Call me when you (get / will get) there. [부사절]
6. I wonder when she (comes / will come). [명사절]

**Answers:**
1. arrive (부사절)
2. will arrive (명사절)
3. rains (부사절)
4. will rain (명사절)
5. get (부사절)
6. will come (명사절)
