# Unit 98: Adjectives Ending in -ing and -ed

## A. -ing vs -ed 형용사의 차이

같은 동사에서 파생된 -ing와 -ed 형용사는 의미가 다릅니다.

| -ing | -ed |
|------|-----|
| ~하게 만드는 (원인) | ~한 감정을 느끼는 (결과) |
| The race was **exciting**. | I was **excited**. |
| 대회가 흥미진진했다. | 나는 흥분했다. |

---

## B. -ing 형용사 (원인/특성)

**-ing 형용사**는 어떤 감정을 **일으키는** 것을 설명합니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The race was exciting."**
> 대회는 흥미진진했다.

- 대회가 흥분을 일으킴

> **"The bus ride was tiring."**
> 버스 타기가 피곤했다.

- 버스가 피로를 일으킴

> **"Walking was more interesting than taking the bus."**
> 걷는 것이 버스 타는 것보다 더 재미있었다.

> **"The crowded bus was suffocating."**
> 만원 버스는 숨 막혔다.

> **"The marathon was exhausting but satisfying."**
> 마라톤은 지치게 했지만 만족스러웠다.

---

## C. -ed 형용사 (감정/상태)

**-ed 형용사**는 감정을 **느끼는** 사람의 상태를 설명합니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was tired after the run."**
> 달리기 후에 피곤했다.

- 내가 피로를 느낌

> **"I was excited about the race."**
> 대회가 기대됐다.

- 내가 흥분을 느낌

> **"I felt exhausted after 30 kilometers."**
> 30킬로미터 후에 지쳤다.

> **"Minseo looked surprised when I told him."**
> 내가 말했을 때 민서는 놀란 것 같았다.

> **"I was embarrassed but also happy."**
> 부끄러웠지만 또한 기뻤다.

---

## D. 같은 상황, 다른 표현

| -ing (원인) | -ed (감정) |
|-------------|-----------|
| The race was **exciting**. | I was **excited** about the race. |
| The news was **surprising**. | I was **surprised** by the news. |
| The bus was **tiring**. | I felt **tired** after the bus ride. |
| The view was **amazing**. | I was **amazed** by the view. |

### From the Novel:

> **"The view of rapeseed flowers was amazing."** (경치가 놀라움을 줌)
> 유채꽃 경치가 놀라웠다.

> **"I was amazed by the rapeseed flowers."** (내가 놀람을 느낌)
> 나는 유채꽃에 놀랐다.

---

## E. 주요 -ing/-ed 형용사 쌍

| -ing (원인) | -ed (결과) | 의미 |
|-------------|-----------|------|
| interesting | interested | 흥미로운 / 흥미를 느끼는 |
| boring | bored | 지루한 / 지루해하는 |
| exciting | excited | 흥미진진한 / 흥분한 |
| tiring | tired | 피곤하게 하는 / 피곤한 |
| surprising | surprised | 놀라운 / 놀란 |
| amazing | amazed | 놀라운 / 감탄한 |
| exhausting | exhausted | 지치게 하는 / 지친 |
| embarrassing | embarrassed | 당황스러운 / 당황한 |
| satisfying | satisfied | 만족스러운 / 만족한 |
| disappointing | disappointed | 실망스러운 / 실망한 |

## F. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| 틀린 표현 | 올바른 표현 |
|-----------|-------------|
| I am ~~interesting~~ in running. | I am **interested** in running. |
| The race was ~~excited~~. | The race was **exciting**. |
| I feel ~~boring~~. | I feel **bored**. |
| The news was ~~surprised~~. | The news was **surprising**. |

### 기억 방법:
- **사람의 감정** → -ed (I am tired, excited, bored)
- **사물/상황의 특성** → -ing (The race is exciting, tiring)

## Exercises

### A. -ing와 -ed 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. The marathon was very (tiring / tired).
   → **tiring**

2. I was (exhausting / exhausted) after 30km.
   → **exhausted**

3. The view was (amazing / amazed).
   → **amazing**

4. I felt (exciting / excited) about the race.
   → **excited**

5. The crowded bus was (suffocating / suffocated).
   → **suffocating**

### B. 문장을 완성하세요.

1. Running for an hour is _______ (tire).
   → **tiring**

2. I was _______ (surprise) by my improvement.
   → **surprised**

3. The news was _______ (excite).
   → **exciting**

4. Minseo looked _______ (amaze) when I finished.
   → **amazed**

5. The race result was _______ (satisfy).
   → **satisfying**

### C. 틀린 부분을 고치세요.

1. I am very interesting in running.
   → I am very **interested** in running.

2. The bus ride was very tired.
   → The bus ride was very **tiring**.

3. He looked very surprising.
   → He looked very **surprised**.

4. The scenery was amazed.
   → The scenery was **amazing**.

5. I felt very boring on the bus.
   → I felt very **bored** on the bus.

### D. 상황에 맞게 문장을 완성하세요.

1. The race was _______. I was _______. (excite)
   → The race was **exciting**. I was **excited**.

2. The long run was _______. I felt _______. (exhaust)
   → The long run was **exhausting**. I felt **exhausted**.

3. The result was _______. I was _______. (disappoint)
   → The result was **disappointing**. I was **disappointed**.

## Quick Rule (빠른 규칙)

| 주어가... | 사용하는 형용사 |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **사람** (감정을 느끼는) | -ed |
| **사물/상황** (감정을 일으키는) | -ing |

```
I was tired. (사람 → -ed)
The race was tiring. (사물/상황 → -ing)
```
