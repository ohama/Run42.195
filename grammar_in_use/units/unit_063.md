# Unit 63: Expressions + -ing

## A. -ing와 함께 쓰이는 표현들

특정 표현들은 **-ing 형태**와 함께 쓰입니다.

**주요 표현:**
- It's no use / It's no good + -ing
- There's no point (in) + -ing
- It's (not) worth + -ing
- have trouble / difficulty + -ing
- spend / waste time + -ing
- be busy + -ing
- go + -ing (운동/활동)

---

## B. It's no use / It's no good + -ing

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"It's no use complaining about the bus."**
> 버스에 대해 불평해봤자 소용없다.

- **It's no use + -ing**: ~해도 소용없다

> **"It's no good worrying about the race."**
> 레이스에 대해 걱정해봤자 안 좋다.

- **It's no good + -ing**: ~해도 좋을 게 없다

---

## C. There's no point (in) + -ing

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"There's no point in taking the bus if I feel sick."**
> 아프면 버스 타봤자 의미가 없다.

- **There's no point (in) + -ing**: ~해도 의미가 없다

> **"There's no point in running if you can't enjoy it."**
> 즐기지 못하면 달려봤자 의미가 없다.

---

## D. It's (not) worth + -ing

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"It's worth getting up early to run."**
> 달리기 위해 일찍 일어나는 건 가치가 있다.

- **It's worth + -ing**: ~할 가치가 있다

> **"The pain is worth enduring."**
> 그 고통은 견딜 가치가 있다.

> **"Walking 7km? It's not worth it."**
> 7km 걷기? 그럴 가치가 없어.

---

## E. have trouble / difficulty + -ing

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I had trouble breathing on the bus."**
> 버스에서 숨쉬기가 힘들었다.

- **have trouble + -ing**: ~하는 데 어려움이 있다

> **"I had difficulty sleeping before the race."**
> 레이스 전날 밤 잠들기가 어려웠다.

- **have difficulty + -ing**: ~하는 데 어려움이 있다

> **"I didn't have any trouble finishing 10km."**
> 10km 완주하는 데 아무 문제 없었다.

---

## F. spend / waste time + -ing

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I spent an hour walking to school."**
> 학교까지 걸어가는 데 한 시간을 썼다.

- **spend time + -ing**: ~하는 데 시간을 쓰다

> **"Don't waste time worrying."**
> 걱정하느라 시간 낭비하지 마.

- **waste time + -ing**: ~하느라 시간을 낭비하다

> **"I spent the morning running 10km."**
> 아침에 10km를 달리는 데 시간을 썼다.

---

## G. be busy + -ing

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was busy preparing for the race."**
> 레이스 준비하느라 바빴다.

- **be busy + -ing**: ~하느라 바쁘다

> **"Mom was busy packing my things."**
> 엄마가 내 짐 싸느라 바빴다.

---

## H. go + -ing (활동/운동)

**go** 뒤에 운동이나 활동을 나타내는 -ing가 옵니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I went running this morning."**
> 오늘 아침에 달리러 갔다.

- **go running**: 달리러 가다

> **"Let's go walking together."**
> 같이 걸으러 가자.

| Activity | Expression |
|----------|------------|
| running | go running |
| walking | go walking |
| jogging | go jogging |
| swimming | go swimming |
| hiking | go hiking |
| shopping | go shopping |

---

## I. 주요 표현 정리

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| It's no use + -ing | ~해봤자 소용없다 | It's no use complaining. |
| It's no good + -ing | ~해봤자 좋을 게 없다 | It's no good worrying. |
| There's no point (in) + -ing | ~해도 의미없다 | There's no point in waiting. |
| It's worth + -ing | ~할 가치가 있다 | It's worth trying. |
| have trouble + -ing | ~하는 데 어려움 | I have trouble sleeping. |
| spend time + -ing | ~하는 데 시간을 쓰다 | I spent time running. |
| waste time + -ing | ~하느라 낭비하다 | Don't waste time worrying. |
| be busy + -ing | ~하느라 바쁘다 | I'm busy training. |
| go + -ing | ~하러 가다 | Let's go running. |

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. It's no use (complain / complaining) about the weather.
2. I have trouble (sleep / sleeping) before races.
3. She spent an hour (run / running).
4. There's no point in (worry / worrying).
5. Let's go (run / running) together.

**Answers:**
1. complaining
2. sleeping
3. running
4. worrying
5. running

### B. 문장을 완성하세요.

1. It's worth _______ (get) up early to run.
2. I had difficulty _______ (breathe) after 10km.
3. Don't waste time _______ (worry) about the race.
4. She was busy _______ (prepare) for the marathon.
5. I spent the morning _______ (train).

**Answers:**
1. getting
2. breathing
3. worrying
4. preparing
5. training

### C. 표현을 사용하여 문장을 완성하세요.

1. 버스에 대해 불평해봤자 소용없다.
   → It's _______ complaining about the bus.

2. 달리기 위해 일찍 일어나는 건 가치가 있다.
   → It's _______ getting up early to run.

3. 레이스 전에 잠들기가 어려웠다.
   → I _______ sleeping before the race.

**Answers:**
1. no use
2. worth
3. had trouble / had difficulty
