# Unit 22: Will/Shall (2)

## A. 형태 복습 (Form Review)

**Will**의 추가 용법을 배웁니다.

### Will의 추가 용법:

1. **확신/확실성 (Certainty)**: 확실하다고 생각하는 것
2. **거부 (Refusal)**: won't = 하지 않겠다
3. **습관적 행동 (Typical behavior)**: 늘 그렇게 하는 행동
4. **요청 (Requests)**: Will you...?

### 축약형 (Contractions)

| Full form | Contraction | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| will not | won't | I won't stop. |
| He will not | He won't | He won't give up. |
| It will not | It won't | It won't work. |

---

## B. 확신/확실성 (Certainty)

확실하다고 **생각하는** 것을 표현합니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "Don't worry. You **won't** be late."
> 걱정 마. 안 늦을 거야.

> "I'm sure they **will** come."
> 분명 올 거야.

> "You **won't** regret this decision."
> 이 결정 후회 안 할 거야.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"You won't regret it."**
> 후회 안 할 거야.

- 확신을 표현

> **"That won't happen."**
> 그런 일은 없을 거야.

- 강한 확신

> **"I'm sure you'll finish."**
> 분명 끝낼 거야.

### Expressions of Certainty:

| Expression | Example |
|------------|---------|
| I'm sure... | I'm sure you'll pass. |
| I know... | I know it won't be easy. |
| Definitely... | You'll definitely finish. |
| Of course... | Of course you'll do well. |

---

## C. 거부/불가능 (Refusal / Inability)

**Won't**은 "하지 않겠다" 또는 "안 되다"를 표현합니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "I've asked him, but he **won't** help."
> 부탁했는데 도와주려고 안 해.

> "The car **won't** start."
> 차가 안 켜져.

> "The door **won't** open."
> 문이 안 열려.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"My legs won't move anymore."**
> 다리가 더 이상 움직이지 않아.

- won't = refuse to / can't (거부/불가능)

> **"I won't give up."**
> 포기하지 않을 거야.

- 강한 의지 표현 (거부)

> **"The pain won't go away."**
> 통증이 사라지지 않아.

- 불가능/거부

### Won't의 두 가지 의미:

| Meaning | Example | Korean |
|---------|---------|--------|
| 미래 부정 | It won't rain tomorrow. | 비가 안 올 거야. |
| 거부 (사람) | He won't listen. | 말을 안 들어. |
| 불가능 (사물) | The door won't open. | 문이 안 열려. |
| 의지 (결심) | I won't give up. | 포기 안 할 거야. |

---

## D. 습관적/전형적 행동 (Typical Behavior)

**늘 그렇게 하는** 행동을 표현합니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "He**'ll** sit for hours doing nothing."
> 그는 아무것도 안 하고 몇 시간이고 앉아 있어.

> "She**'ll** always arrive early."
> 그녀는 항상 일찍 와.

> "He**'ll** often forget things."
> 그는 종종 물건을 잊어버려.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"He'll always push through the pain."**
> 그는 항상 고통을 견디며 나아가.

- 전형적인 행동

> **"She'll always cheer me on."**
> 그녀는 항상 응원해줘.

### With Frequency Words:

| Frequency | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| always | He'll always help. |
| often | She'll often call. |
| sometimes | It'll sometimes rain. |
| usually | They'll usually arrive early. |

---

## E. 요청 (Requests)

**Will you...?** 또는 **Won't you...?**로 요청합니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "**Will you** help me with this?"
> 이거 도와줄래?

> "**Will you** please be quiet?"
> 조용히 해줄래?

> "**Won't you** come in?"
> 들어오지 않을래? (정중한 초대)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Will you run with me?"**
> 나랑 같이 달릴래?

- 정중한 요청

> **"Will you wait for me at the finish line?"**
> 결승선에서 기다려줄래?

### Will vs Would (요청)

| Expression | Politeness | Example |
|------------|------------|---------|
| Will you...? | 일반적 | Will you help me? |
| Would you...? | 더 정중 | Would you help me? |
| Will you please...? | 정중 | Will you please wait? |

---

## F. I'm sure / I expect + Will

확신 표현과 함께 will 사용:

> **"I'm sure you'll finish."**
> 분명 끝낼 거야.

> **"I expect it'll be hard."**
> 힘들 것 같아.

> **"I bet you'll win."**
> 분명 이길 거야.

> **"I doubt he'll come."**
> 그가 올지 모르겠어.

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| I'm sure + will | 확신 | I'm sure you'll pass. |
| I expect + will | 예상 | I expect it'll rain. |
| I bet + will | 장담 | I bet he'll win. |
| I doubt + will | 의심 | I doubt she'll come. |

---

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~He willn't come.~~ | He **won't** come. | 부정은 won't |
| ~~The door will not opens.~~ | The door **won't open**. | will + 동사원형 |
| ~~Do you will help?~~ | **Will you** help? | Will이 맨 앞으로 |
| ~~I sure will finish.~~ | I**'m sure** I**'ll** finish. | I'm sure 필요 |
| ~~He wont come.~~ | He **won't** come. | apostrophe 필요 |
| ~~I will don't go.~~ | I **won't** go. | 부정은 won't |

### Won't의 의미 구분하기:

| 문장 | 의미 |
|------|------|
| I won't give up. | 의지/결심 (포기 안 해) |
| It won't rain. | 미래 부정 (비 안 와) |
| The door won't open. | 불가능/거부 (안 열려) |
| He won't listen. | 거부 (들으려 안 해) |

## Exercises

### A. 빈칸 채우기 (Fill in the blanks)

Use will or won't.

1. Don't worry, you _______ (not regret) it.
2. My legs _______ (not move) anymore.
3. I _______ (not give up). I'll keep going.
4. _______ you run with me tomorrow?
5. I'm sure you _______ (do) great.
6. The car _______ (not start). What's wrong?
7. She _______ always arrive early. That's just how she is.
8. I _______ be there, I promise.

**Answers:**
1. won't regret
2. won't move
3. won't give up
4. Will
5. will do / 'll do
6. won't start
7. will / 'll
8. will / 'll

### B. Won't의 의미 파악 (Identify the meaning)

Choose: (a) 미래 부정 (b) 거부/불가능 (c) 의지/결심

1. "I won't give up." → **(c) 의지/결심**
2. "It won't rain tomorrow." → **(a) 미래 부정**
3. "The door won't open." → **(b) 거부/불가능**
4. "I won't eat that." → **(c) 의지 또는 (b) 거부**
5. "My legs won't move." → **(b) 불가능**
6. "He won't be late." → **(a) 미래 부정**
7. "She won't listen to me." → **(b) 거부**

### C. 문장 완성하기 (Complete the sentences)

1. 포기 안 해! → I _______ give up!
2. 후회 안 할 거야. → You _______ regret it.
3. 같이 달릴래? → _______ you run with me?
4. 분명 잘 할 거야. → I'm sure you _______ great.
5. 문이 안 열려. → The door _______ open.

**Answers:**
1. won't
2. won't
3. Will
4. will do / 'll do
5. won't

### D. 상황에 맞게 작성 (Write appropriate responses)

1. Your friend is worried about a race.
   → Don't worry! You**'ll do great**. / I**'m sure** you**'ll finish**.

2. You want to ask someone to run with you.
   → **Will you** run with me?

3. You're determined not to quit.
   → I **won't give up**! / I **won't** stop!

4. The car doesn't start.
   → The car **won't start**.
