# Unit 107: Comparison (3) - as...as / than

## A. as...as (동등 비교)

**as + 형용사/부사 + as**는 "~만큼 ~한"의 의미로, 두 대상이 **같은 정도**임을 나타냅니다.

### Basic Forms:

| 긍정문 | 부정문 |
|--------|--------|
| as fast as | not as fast as |
| ~만큼 빠른 | ~만큼 빠르지 않은 |

### From the Original Book:

> "I'm not **as** old **as** I look."
> 나는 보이는 것만큼 나이 들지 않았다.

> "The city centre wasn't **as** crowded **as** I expected."
> 시내가 예상한 것만큼 붐비지 않았다.

---

## B. as...as 긍정문 (Affirmative)

### Common Expressions:

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| as fast as I could | 내가 할 수 있는 한 빨리 |
| as hard as possible | 가능한 한 열심히 |
| as soon as possible | 가능한 한 빨리 |
| as much as I can | 내가 할 수 있는 만큼 |

### From the Original Book:

> "I'll call you **as soon as** possible."
> 가능한 한 빨리 전화할게.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I ran as fast as I could."**
> 최대한 빨리 달렸다.

- as fast as I could = 내가 할 수 있는 만큼 빠르게

> **"I want to be as strong as the other runners."**
> 다른 주자들만큼 강해지고 싶다.

> **"The second race was as challenging as the first."**
> 두 번째 대회도 첫 번째만큼 도전적이었다.

> **"I trained as hard as possible."**
> 가능한 한 열심히 훈련했다.

> **"I ate as much as I could before the race."**
> 대회 전에 최대한 많이 먹었다.

---

## C. not as...as (부정 비교)

**not as...as**는 "~만큼 ~하지 않은"의 의미입니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "Jenny is not **as** old **as** she looks."
> 제니는 보이는 것만큼 나이 들지 않았다.

> "It's not **as** cold **as** yesterday."
> 어제만큼 춥지 않다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The afternoon bus is not as crowded as the morning bus."**
> 오후 버스는 아침 버스만큼 붐비지 않는다.

> **"Today was not as hard as yesterday."**
> 오늘은 어제만큼 힘들지 않았다.

> **"I'm not as tired as I expected."**
> 예상만큼 피곤하지 않다.

> **"The 10km was not as difficult as I thought."**
> 10km는 생각만큼 어렵지 않았다.

> **"Running is not as boring as I used to think."**
> 달리기가 예전에 생각했던 것만큼 지루하지 않다.

> **"I'm not as fast as Minseo."**
> 나는 민서만큼 빠르지 않다.

---

## D. not as...as vs 비교급 (Conversion)

**not as...as**는 비교급으로 바꿀 수 있습니다.

### Conversion Rule:

```
A is not as X as B = B is more X than A
```

| not as...as | 비교급 |
|-------------|--------|
| A is **not as** big **as** B | B is **bigger than** A |
| 오후 버스는 아침 버스만큼 붐비지 않는다 | 아침 버스가 오후 버스보다 더 붐빈다 |

### Examples:

> **"The afternoon bus is not as crowded as the morning bus."**
> = **"The morning bus is more crowded than the afternoon bus."**

> **"I'm not as fast as Minseo."**
> = **"Minseo is faster than me."**

> **"This route is not as long as that one."**
> = **"That route is longer than this one."**

---

## E. 비교급 + than (Comparative sentences)

**than**은 비교 대상을 나타냅니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "You're **older than** me." / "You're **older than** I am."
> 너는 나보다 나이가 많아.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Running is faster than walking."**
> 달리기가 걷기보다 빠르다.

> **"The race was harder than I expected."**
> 대회가 예상보다 힘들었다.

> **"I arrived earlier than usual."**
> 평소보다 일찍 도착했다.

> **"My stamina is better than before."**
> 내 체력이 예전보다 좋다.

> **"The full marathon is more than twice as long as the half."**
> 풀마라톤은 하프의 두 배 이상 길다.

---

## F. 배수 비교 (Multiplied comparisons)

### Patterns:

| 표현 | 의미 | 예시 |
|------|------|------|
| twice as...as | 2배 | twice as long |
| three times as...as | 3배 | three times as fast |
| half as...as | 절반 | half as expensive |
| X times as...as | X배 | five times as big |

### From the Original Book:

> "Petrol is **twice as** expensive **as** it was a few years ago."
> 휘발유가 몇 년 전보다 두 배 비싸다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The full marathon is twice as long as the half marathon."**
> 풀마라톤은 하프마라톤의 두 배 길다.

> **"I can run three times as far as before."**
> 예전보다 세 배 멀리 달릴 수 있다.

> **"The bus takes half as long as walking."**
> 버스는 걷는 것의 절반 시간이 걸린다.

> **"I run twice as often as last year."**
> 작년보다 두 배 자주 달린다.

---

## G. the same as (같은)

**the same as**는 "~와 같은"의 의미입니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "Her dress is **the same** colour **as** mine."
> 그녀의 드레스는 내 것과 같은 색이다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"My finishing time was the same as my goal."**
> 완주 시간이 목표와 같았다.

> **"The route was the same as last time."**
> 경로가 저번과 같았다.

> **"I felt the same as yesterday."**
> 어제와 같은 기분이었다.

> **"My pace was the same as his."**
> 내 페이스가 그의 것과 같았다.

---

## H. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | 설명 |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| ~~as fast than~~ | as fast **as** | as...as 패턴 |
| ~~more faster than~~ | **faster than** | 비교급 중복 불가 |
| ~~the same with~~ | the same **as** | same as 패턴 |
| ~~as difficult that~~ | as difficult **as** | as...as 패턴 |
| ~~twice more expensive~~ | **twice as** expensive | 배수 + as...as |

## Exercises

### A. as...as를 사용하여 문장을 완성하세요 (Complete with as...as)

1. I ran _______ fast _______ I could.
   → **as** / **as**

2. The race was _______ challenging _______ expected.
   → **as** / **as**

3. I trained _______ hard _______ possible.
   → **as** / **as**

4. She is _______ tall _______ her brother.
   → **as** / **as**

5. I ate _______ much _______ I could.
   → **as** / **as**

---

### B. not as...as를 사용하여 문장을 바꾸세요 (Rewrite with not as...as)

1. The morning bus is more crowded than the afternoon bus.
   → The afternoon bus is **not as crowded as** the morning bus.

2. Minseo is faster than me.
   → I am **not as fast as** Minseo.

3. Yesterday was harder than today.
   → Today was **not as hard as** yesterday.

4. The marathon was more exhausting than the half.
   → The half was **not as exhausting as** the marathon.

5. The weather was better last week.
   → The weather this week is **not as good as** last week.

---

### C. 비교급 + than을 사용하여 문장을 완성하세요 (Complete with comparative + than)

1. Running is _______ (fast) _______ walking.
   → **faster** / **than**

2. The race was _______ (hard) _______ I expected.
   → **harder** / **than**

3. I feel _______ (good) _______ before.
   → **better** / **than**

4. The 42km is _______ (long) _______ the 21km.
   → **longer** / **than**

5. I arrived _______ (early) _______ usual.
   → **earlier** / **than**

---

### D. 배수 비교를 사용하여 문장을 완성하세요 (Complete with multiplied comparisons)

1. The full marathon is _______ (2배) as long as the half.
   → **twice**

2. I can run _______ (3배) as far as before.
   → **three times**

3. The bus takes _______ (절반) as long as walking.
   → **half**

4. This route is _______ (4배) as difficult as that one.
   → **four times**

5. He runs _______ (2배) as often as me.
   → **twice**

## Summary

### Comparison Patterns:

| 표현 | 의미 | 예시 |
|------|------|------|
| as...as | ~만큼 | as fast as I could |
| not as...as | ~만큼 ~않은 | not as hard as yesterday |
| 비교급 + than | ~보다 더 | faster than walking |
| twice/three times as...as | 2배/3배 | twice as long as |
| the same as | ~와 같은 | the same as before |

### Conversion:

| not as...as | = | 비교급 |
|-------------|---|--------|
| A is not as big as B | = | B is bigger than A |

### Common Expressions:

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| as soon as possible | 가능한 한 빨리 |
| as much as possible | 가능한 한 많이 |
| as hard as I can | 내가 할 수 있는 한 열심히 |
| as fast as I could | 내가 할 수 있는 한 빨리 |
