# Unit 117: Like and as

## A. Like의 형태와 용법 (Preposition)

**Like**는 "~처럼, ~같이"의 의미로 **유사성**을 나타냅니다.
뒤에 **명사, 대명사, 동명사(-ing)**가 옵니다.

### 구조:
> like + 명사/대명사/동명사

### From the Original Book:
> "What a beautiful house! It's **like** a palace."
> 정말 아름다운 집이야! 궁전 같아.

> "What does she do?" "She's a teacher, **like** me."
> 그녀는 뭐 해? - 나처럼 선생님이야.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"My legs felt like lead."**
> 다리가 납처럼 무거웠다.

> **"I ran like the wind."**
> 바람처럼 달렸다.

> **"Nothing like a morning run to wake you up."**
> 아침 달리기만큼 잠 깨는 게 없다.

> **"It was nothing like I expected."**
> 내가 예상했던 것과 전혀 달랐다.

---

## B. As의 형태와 용법 (자격/역할)

**As**는 "~로서"의 의미로 **자격, 역할, 직업**을 나타냅니다.

### 구조:
> as + 명사 (역할/자격)

### From the Original Book:
> "A few years ago I worked **as** a taxi driver."
> 몇 년 전에 나는 택시 운전사로 일했다.

> "We used the garage **as** a storage room."
> 우리는 차고를 창고로 사용했다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"As a beginner, I started slowly."**
> 초보자로서, 천천히 시작했다.

> **"As your coach, I want you to succeed."**
> 너의 코치로서, 네가 성공하길 원한다.

> **"I work as a runner now."**
> 나는 지금 러너로 활동하고 있다.

## C. Like vs As 비교

| Like | As |
|------|-----|
| ~처럼 (유사성/비유) | ~로서 (자격/역할) |
| + 명사/대명사 | + 명사 (역할) |
| like a runner (러너처럼) | as a runner (러너로서) |
| 실제로 러너가 아님 | 실제로 러너임 |

### From the Original Book:
> "Don't use the table **as** a chair." (테이블을 의자로 사용하지 마)
> "Your English is very good. You speak **like** a native speaker." (원어민처럼 - 실제로는 아님)

### Comparison from the Novel:

> **"I ran like a runner."** (러너처럼 달렸다 - 비유)
> **"As a runner, I trained every day."** (러너로서 매일 훈련했다 - 자격)

> **"He treated me like a friend."** (친구처럼 대했다 - 비유)
> **"He worked as a coach."** (코치로 일했다 - 역할)

### 시각적 비교:

```
Like = 비유/유사성:
"She sings like an angel." (천사처럼 - 실제 천사 아님)

As = 자격/역할:
"She works as a singer." (가수로서 - 실제 직업)
```

## D. Like / As + 절

**Like + 절**은 구어체에서 많이 쓰이지만, 격식체에서는 **as**를 사용합니다.

| Informal (구어체) | Formal (격식체) |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| Do it **like** I showed you. | Do it **as** I showed you. |
| It looks **like** it's going to rain. | It looks **as if** it's going to rain. |
| **Like** I said, be careful. | **As** I said, be careful. |

### From the Original Book:
> "**As** I said, I won't be here next week." (격식체)
> "**Like** I said, I won't be here next week." (구어체)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I did it like the coach taught me."** (구어체)
> = **"I did it as the coach taught me."** (격식체)
> 코치님이 가르쳐 준 대로 했다.

## E. As usual / As always / As expected 등 관용 표현

**As**를 사용한 관용 표현들:

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| as usual | 평소처럼 | As usual, I woke up early. |
| as always | 항상 그렇듯이 | As always, I ran in the morning. |
| as expected | 예상대로 | As expected, my legs hurt. |
| as you know | 알다시피 | As you know, I run daily. |
| as I said | 내가 말했듯이 | As I said, running is hard. |
| as planned | 계획대로 | As planned, we met at 6. |
| as before | 전처럼 | As before, I took the same route. |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"As usual, the bus was packed."**
> 평소처럼 버스는 만원이었다.

> **"As expected, it got harder after 30km."**
> 예상대로, 30km 이후에 더 힘들어졌다.

> **"As always, Mom was waiting for me."**
> 항상 그렇듯이, 엄마가 기다리고 계셨다.

## F. Such as vs Like (예시 나열)

**Such as**와 **like**는 예시를 나열할 때 사용됩니다.

| Such as | Like |
|---------|------|
| 격식체 | 구어체 |
| 구체적 예시 | 일반적 예시 |

### From the Original Book:
> "I enjoy sports **such as** tennis and basketball." (격식체)
> "I enjoy sports **like** tennis and basketball." (구어체)

### Examples:

> **"I enjoy outdoor activities such as running and hiking."** (격식체)
> **"I enjoy outdoor activities like running and hiking."** (구어체)
> 달리기나 하이킹 같은 야외 활동을 좋아한다.

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | 설명 |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| ~~I work like a teacher.~~ | I work **as** a teacher. | 직업은 as |
| ~~As me, he's a runner.~~ | **Like** me, he's a runner. | 유사성은 like |
| ~~She looks as her mother.~~ | She looks **like** her mother. | 외모 유사성은 like |
| ~~As you can see, she sings as an angel.~~ | As you can see, she sings **like** an angel. | 비유는 like |
| ~~She used the box like a table.~~ | She used the box **as** a table. | 용도/역할은 as |

### 구분 방법:

```
실제 역할/자격/직업? → As
비유/유사성? → Like

"He works as a teacher." (실제 직업)
"He talks like a teacher." (선생님처럼 - 비유)
```

## Exercises

### A. like 또는 as를 사용해 빈칸을 채우세요.

1. She works _______ a teacher.
2. My legs felt _______ jelly after the run.
3. _______ a runner, I need to stretch every day.
4. Do it _______ I showed you.
5. He runs _______ a professional athlete.
6. _______ I said, running takes practice.
7. We used the room _______ an office.
8. She sings _______ an angel.

**Answers:**
1. as (역할)
2. like (유사성)
3. As (자격)
4. as / like (as가 더 격식체)
5. like (비유)
6. As (관용 표현)
7. as (용도)
8. like (비유)

### B. 다음 문장에서 like와 as 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. (Like / As) usual, I ran this morning.
2. She treated me (like / as) a friend.
3. I work (like / as) a volunteer at the race.
4. It felt (like / as) flying through the air.
5. (Like / As) a coach, I expect dedication.
6. He looks (like / as) his father.
7. She works (like / as) a nurse.
8. (Like / As) I mentioned, we start at 6.

**Answers:**
1. As (관용 표현)
2. like (비유)
3. as (자격)
4. like (비유)
5. As (자격)
6. like (외모 유사성)
7. as (직업)
8. As (관용 표현)

### C. 문장을 완성하세요.

1. My bag felt like _______.
2. As a beginner, _______.
3. As usual, _______.
4. He runs like _______.
5. She works as _______.
6. Like me, he _______.

**Answers (예시):**
1. a ton of bricks / it was full of rocks
2. I started with short distances
3. I woke up at 5 a.m.
4. the wind / a professional
5. a coach / a nurse
6. likes running / is a student

### D. 틀린 부분을 고치세요.

1. I work like a runner.
   → I work **as** a runner.

2. As me, she runs every day.
   → **Like** me, she runs every day.

3. She looks as her mother.
   → She looks **like** her mother.

4. He used the box like a chair.
   → He used the box **as** a chair.

5. As I said before, be careful. (맞음)
   → (No change needed)

## Summary: Like vs As 비교표

| 구분 | Like | As |
|------|------|-----|
| 의미 | ~처럼 (유사성/비유) | ~로서 (자격/역할) |
| 뒤에 오는 것 | 명사/대명사 | 명사 (역할) |
| 예시 | like a runner | as a runner |
| 의미 | 러너처럼 | 러너로서 |
| 실제 여부 | 아님 (비유) | 맞음 (실제) |
| 절과 함께 | 구어체 | 격식체 |
