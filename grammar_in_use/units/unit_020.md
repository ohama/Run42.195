# Unit 20: (I'm) going to (do)

## A. 형태 (Form)

**be going to + 동사원형**은 미래를 표현하는 중요한 구조입니다.

### 긍정문 (Affirmative)

| Subject | Be | going to | Verb | Example |
|---------|-----|----------|------|---------|
| I | am | going to | run | I'm going to run. |
| He/She/It | is | going to | train | He's going to train. |
| We/You/They | are | going to | finish | We're going to finish. |

### 부정문 (Negative)

| Subject | Be + not | going to | Verb | Example |
|---------|----------|----------|------|---------|
| I | am not | going to | give up | I'm not going to give up. |
| He | is not | going to | stop | He isn't going to stop. |
| They | are not | going to | wait | They aren't going to wait. |

### 의문문 (Questions)

| Be | Subject | going to | Verb | Example |
|----|---------|----------|------|---------|
| Am | I | going to | finish? | Am I going to finish? |
| Is | he | going to | run? | Is he going to run? |
| Are | you | going to | train? | Are you going to train? |

---

## B. 이미 결정한 의도/계획 (Intentions - Already decided)

**"I'm going to do something"** = 말하기 전에 **이미 결정한** 의도나 계획

### From the Original Book:

> "**I'm going to** buy some new running shoes tomorrow."
> 내일 새 런닝화를 살 거야. (이미 결정함)

> "**A:** Are you going to eat anything?"
> "**B:** No, **I'm not going to** eat until the race is over."
> 뭐 먹을 거야? - 아니, 대회 끝날 때까지 안 먹을 거야.

> "What **are** you **going to do** this weekend?"
> 이번 주말에 뭐 할 거야?

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I'm going to run to school tomorrow."**
> 내일 학교까지 달릴 거야.

- 이미 결정한 계획 - 말하기 전에 마음먹은 일

> **"I'm going to run 10km."**
> 10km를 달릴 거야.

- 미리 세운 목표/계획

> **"I'm going to finish the race."**
> 나는 대회를 완주할 거야.

- 굳은 결심/의도

### Key Point:
**Be going to**는 말하기 **전에** 이미 마음속으로 결정한 일에 사용합니다.

## C. 현재 증거 기반 예측 (Predictions based on present evidence)

**지금 보이는 상황/증거**를 바탕으로 미래를 예측할 때 사용합니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "Look at those black clouds! It**'s going to** rain."
> 저 검은 구름 봐! 비가 올 것 같아.

> "I feel terrible. I think I**'m going to** be sick."
> 기분이 너무 안 좋아. 토할 것 같아.

> "Look out! We**'re going to** crash!"
> 조심해! 부딪히겠다!

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"My legs are heavy. I'm going to slow down."**
> 다리가 무거워. 속도가 느려질 것 같아.

- 현재 상태(다리가 무거움)를 보고 예측

> **"It's getting dark. It's going to rain."**
> 어두워지고 있어. 비가 올 것 같아.

- 지금 보이는 증거로 예측

> **"He's been training for months. He's going to do well."**
> 그는 몇 달 동안 훈련했어. 잘 할 거야.

- 현재까지의 노력을 보고 예측

### Evidence Words (증거 표현):
- Look! / Look at...!
- I can see... / I feel...
- The sky is... / It's getting...

## D. 발음과 구어체 (Pronunciation and Spoken English)

### Going to의 발음

구어체에서는 "gonna" /ˈɡɒnə/로 발음하기도 합니다:

| Written | Spoken | Example |
|---------|--------|---------|
| going to | gonna | I'm **gonna** run. |
| (글에서) | (말할 때) | (아임 거나 런) |

**주의**: 글을 쓸 때는 항상 "going to"로 씁니다.

### 축약형 (Contractions)

| Full form | Contraction |
|-----------|-------------|
| I am going to | I'm going to |
| He is going to | He's going to |
| We are going to | We're going to |
| It is going to | It's going to |
| I am not going to | I'm not going to |
| He is not going to | He's not going to / He isn't going to |

## E. Be going to vs Will (비교)

### 핵심 차이:

| Be going to | Will |
|-------------|------|
| 이미 결정한 계획 | 즉석에서 결정 |
| 현재 증거 기반 예측 | 일반적인 예측/의견 |

### Comparison Table:

| Situation | Be going to | Will |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| 이미 결정한 계획 | I'm going to run tomorrow. | X |
| 지금 결정 | X | I'll run tomorrow. (지금 결정) |
| 증거 기반 예측 | Look! It's going to rain. | X |
| 일반 예측 | X | I think it will rain. |

### Dialogue Example:

**A:** "Why are you wearing running shoes?"
**B:** "I**'m going to** run. I decided this morning." (이미 결정)

**A:** "It's hot in here."
**B:** "I**'ll** open the window." (지금 결정)

## F. 소설 속 도윤이의 변화 (Character Development)

도윤이의 계획이 점점 구체화되는 모습:

| Month | Expression | Certainty |
|-------|------------|-----------|
| March | "Maybe I should walk." | 불확실 |
| June | "I'm thinking about running." | 생각 중 |
| August | "I'm going to run 10km." | 확실한 계획 |
| October | "I'm going to finish the race." | 굳은 결심 |
| March (next year) | "I'm going to run a full marathon." | 강한 의지 |

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~I will run tomorrow.~~ (이미 계획) | I**'m going to** run tomorrow. | 이미 결정한 계획 = be going to |
| ~~He going to run.~~ | He**'s going to** run. | be 동사 필요 |
| ~~I'm go to run.~~ | I**'m going to** run. | going to (going 필요) |
| ~~I going to finish.~~ | I**'m going to** finish. | be 동사 필요 |
| ~~Are you going run?~~ | Are you **going to** run? | to 필요 |
| ~~It will rain.~~ (구름 보고) | Look! It**'s going to** rain. | 증거 기반 = be going to |

### 흔히 헷갈리는 상황:

| 상황 | 올바른 선택 |
|------|------------|
| 친구에게 주말 계획 말할 때 | I'm going to... (이미 결정) |
| 검은 구름을 보고 | It's going to rain. (증거) |
| 물이 끓는 것을 보고 | The water is going to boil over! (증거) |
| 뭔가 결심했을 때 | I'm going to train harder. (결심) |

## Exercises

### A. 빈칸 채우기 (Fill in the blanks)

Use the correct form of "be going to."

1. I _______ (run) 10km tomorrow.
2. Look at those dark clouds! It _______ (rain).
3. He _______ (finish) the marathon. He's been training hard.
4. We _______ (meet) at the park this weekend.
5. My legs hurt. I _______ (slow down).
6. What _______ you _______ (do) after the race?
7. She _______ (not/give up). She's determined.
8. _______ they _______ (come) to the race?

**Answers:**
1. am going to run
2. is going to rain
3. is going to finish
4. are going to meet
5. am going to slow down
6. are ... going to do
7. is not going to give up / isn't going to give up
8. Are ... going to come

### B. 문장 변환 (Sentence Transformation)

Change using "be going to."

1. I plan to run tomorrow.
   → I **am going to run** tomorrow.

2. He decided to join the track team.
   → He **is going to join** the track team.

3. The sky is getting dark. (rain - 예측)
   → It **is going to rain**.

4. They plan to watch the race.
   → They **are going to watch** the race.

5. I've decided to train every day.
   → I **am going to train** every day.

### C. Will vs Be going to 선택 (Choose the correct form)

1. "I (will / am going to) run a marathon." - 이미 결정한 계획
   → **am going to**

2. "Look! He (will / is going to) fall!" - 현재 상황 기반 예측
   → **is going to**

3. "Oh, I forgot my water!" "I (will / am going to) get you some." - 지금 결정
   → **will**

4. "What (will / are) you (do / going to do) this weekend?" - 계획 묻기
   → **are ... going to do**

5. "I think the race (will / is going to) be difficult." - 일반적 예측
   → **will** (또는 is going to 가능)

### D. 상황에 맞게 문장 완성 (Complete based on context)

1. (계획: 오늘 10km 달리기로 함)
   → I _______ 10km today.

2. (증거: 구름이 많음)
   → Look at those clouds! It _______.

3. (결심: 포기하지 않겠다)
   → I _______ give up!

4. (계획 묻기: 방과 후 뭐 할 거야?)
   → What _______ after school?

**Answers:**
1. am going to run
2. is going to rain
3. am not going to
4. are you going to do

## Be going to vs Will vs Present Continuous

| Form | Use | Example |
|------|-----|---------|
| Be going to | 이미 결정한 계획, 증거 기반 예측 | I'm going to run. |
| Will | 즉석 결정, 일반 예측, 약속 | I'll help you. |
| Present Continuous | 확정된 약속 (시간/장소) | I'm running at 7 tomorrow. |
