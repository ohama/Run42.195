# Unit 127: On/in/at (other uses)

## A. On + 교통수단/미디어

**On**은 대중교통, TV/라디오, 전화 등에 사용합니다.

| Usage | Examples |
|-------|----------|
| 대중교통 | on the bus, on the train |
| TV/라디오 | on TV, on the radio |
| 전화 | on the phone |
| 인터넷 | on the internet |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was on the bus for 40 minutes."**
> 40분 동안 버스에 타고 있었다.

> **"I read about runner's high on the internet."**
> 인터넷에서 러너스 하이에 대해 읽었다.

> **"I heard about the race on the radio."**
> 라디오에서 경기에 대해 들었다.

---

## B. In + 상태/상황

**In**은 특정 상태나 상황을 나타냅니다.

| Usage | Examples |
|-------|----------|
| 상태 | in pain, in danger, in trouble |
| 기분 | in a good mood, in a hurry |
| 복장 | in uniform, in gym clothes |
| 언어 | in English, in Korean |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was in a lot of pain after 30km."**
> 30km 후에 많이 아팠다.

> **"I was in a good mood after the run."**
> 달리기 후에 기분이 좋았다.

> **"I put on my new uniform."**
> 새 교복을 입었다.

> **"Kids in gym clothes were running around."**
> 체육복 입은 아이들이 뛰어다녔다.

---

## C. At + 속도/나이/온도

**At**은 속도, 나이, 온도 등 수치와 함께 사용합니다.

| Usage | Examples |
|-------|----------|
| 속도 | at 6:30 pace, at high speed |
| 나이 | at the age of 14, at 14 |
| 온도 | at 25 degrees |
| 가격 | at 55,000 won |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I ran at a 6:30 pace."**
> 6분 30초 페이스로 달렸다.

> **"At the age of 14, I completed a marathon."**
> 14살에 마라톤을 완주했다.

> **"The temperature was at 25 degrees."**
> 온도가 25도였다.

---

## D. On + 특정 주제/상태

**On**은 특정 주제, 휴가, 다이어트 등에 사용합니다.

| Usage | Examples |
|-------|----------|
| 주제 | on the topic of, a book on running |
| 휴가 | on vacation, on holiday |
| 다이어트 | on a diet |
| 약 | on medication |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I read a book on marathon training."**
> 마라톤 훈련에 대한 책을 읽었다.

> **"During summer break, I was on vacation."**
> 여름 방학 동안 휴가였다.

---

## E. In vs On 비교 (신문/책/사진)

| In | On |
|----|-----|
| in the newspaper | on page 5 |
| in the book | on the cover |
| in the photo | on the screen |

### Examples:

> **"I saw the news in the newspaper."**
> 신문에서 뉴스를 봤다.

> **"The answer is on page 10."**
> 답은 10페이지에 있다.

---

## F. 관용 표현

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| in time | 제시간에 |
| on time | 정시에 |
| at first | 처음에 |
| in the end | 결국 |
| on purpose | 일부러 |
| at once | 즉시 |
| in general | 일반적으로 |

### From the Novel:

> **"At first, I hated running."**
> 처음에는 달리기가 싫었다.

> **"In the end, I finished the marathon."**
> 결국, 마라톤을 완주했다.

> **"I ran today on purpose."**
> 오늘 일부러 달렸다.

## Exercises

### A. on, in, at 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. I was _______ the bus for an hour.
2. I was _______ a good mood after running.
3. I ran _______ a 7-minute pace.
4. I read about it _______ the internet.
5. Kids _______ gym clothes were running.

**Answers:**
1. on
2. in
3. at
4. on
5. in

### B. 빈칸을 채우세요.

1. _______ first, it was hard.
2. I heard it _______ TV.
3. _______ the age of 14, I ran a marathon.
4. She was talking _______ the phone.
5. _______ the end, I succeeded.

**Answers:**
1. At
2. on
3. At
4. on
5. In

### C. 다음 문장이 맞으면 O, 틀리면 X 표시하고 고치세요.

1. I read about it in the internet.
2. She was in a hurry.
3. I ran in high speed.
4. The book is in running techniques.

**Answers:**
1. X → on the internet
2. O
3. X → at high speed
4. X → on running techniques
