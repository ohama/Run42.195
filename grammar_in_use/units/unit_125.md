# Unit 125: In/at/on (place) (3)

## A. In a row / In a line / In a queue

**In**은 줄이나 행렬을 나타낼 때 사용합니다.

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| in a row | 일렬로 |
| in a line | 줄 서서 |
| in a queue | 대기줄에 |
| in order | 순서대로 |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"We stood in a line at the starting area."**
> 출발 지역에서 줄을 서 있었다.

> **"The runners were in a row."**
> 러너들이 일렬로 서 있었다.

---

## B. In the sky / In the world / In the newspaper

**In**은 넓은 공간이나 매체에서 사용합니다.

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| in the sky | 하늘에 |
| in the world | 세상에서 |
| in the newspaper | 신문에 |
| in a book | 책에서 |
| in a photo | 사진에서 |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The sun was rising in the sky."**
> 하늘에서 해가 떠오르고 있었다.

> **"I read about the marathon in the newspaper."**
> 신문에서 마라톤에 대해 읽었다.

> **"I looked at myself in the mirror."**
> 거울에 비친 나를 봤다.

---

## C. At the top / At the bottom / At the end

**At**은 특정 위치의 끝이나 한계를 나타냅니다.

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| at the top | 맨 위에 |
| at the bottom | 맨 아래에 |
| at the end | 끝에 |
| at the beginning | 처음에 |
| at the back | 뒤쪽에 |
| at the front | 앞쪽에 |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was near the bottom in the endurance run."**
> 오래달리기에서 거의 꼴찌였다.

> **"At the end of the race, I was exhausted."**
> 경기가 끝났을 때, 지쳐 있었다.

> **"I sat at the back of the bus."**
> 버스 뒤쪽에 앉았다.

---

## D. On the coast / On the border / On the edge

**On**은 경계선이나 가장자리를 나타냅니다.

| Expression | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| on the coast | 해안에 |
| on the border | 국경에 |
| on the edge | 가장자리에 |
| on the corner | 모퉁이에 |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"We ran on the coast."**
> 해안을 따라 달렸다.

> **"The race was along the coastal road."**
> 경기는 해안 도로를 따라 진행되었다.

---

## E. In the front / At the front 비교

| In the front | At the front |
|--------------|--------------|
| 차량/공간의 앞부분 안 | 건물/줄의 앞쪽 |
| in the front of the car | at the front of the line |

### Examples:

> **"I sat in the front of the bus."** (버스 앞쪽 좌석에)
> 버스 앞쪽에 앉았다.

> **"I was at the front of the line."** (줄 맨 앞에)
> 줄 맨 앞에 있었다.

---

## F. On the way vs In the way

| On the way | In the way |
|------------|------------|
| ~하는 길에 | 방해가 되어 |
| on the way to school | in the way (blocking) |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"On the way to school, I saw rapeseed flowers."**
> 학교 가는 길에 유채꽃을 봤다.

> **"People were in the way on the bus."**
> 버스에서 사람들이 방해가 되었다.

## Exercises

### A. in, at, on 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. I was _______ the bottom of the class in running.
2. The sun was shining _______ the sky.
3. We stood _______ a line.
4. I sat _______ the front of the bus.
5. _______ the way home, I thought about the race.

**Answers:**
1. at
2. in
3. in
4. in
5. On

### B. 빈칸을 채우세요.

1. I read about it _______ a book.
2. _______ the end of the day, I was tired.
3. The bus stop is _______ the corner.
4. I looked at myself _______ the mirror.
5. The runners stood _______ a row.

**Answers:**
1. in
2. At
3. on
4. in
5. in

### C. in the front / at the front 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. I sat _______ of the car.
2. She was _______ of the queue.
3. The driver sits _______ of the bus.

**Answers:**
1. in the front
2. at the front
3. in the front
