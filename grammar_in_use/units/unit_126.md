# Unit 126: To, at, in, and into

## A. To (방향/목적지)

**To**는 "~로, ~에게"의 의미로, **이동의 방향이나 목적지**를 나타냅니다.

| Usage | Example |
|-------|---------|
| 이동 | go to school, walk to the bus stop |
| 전달 | give to, send to, talk to |
| 방향 | turn to the left |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I walked to the bus stop."**
> 버스 정류장까지 걸어갔다.

> **"I ran to school."**
> 학교까지 달렸다.

> **"Mom gave the medal to me."**
> 엄마가 메달을 나에게 주셨다.

---

## B. At (목표 지점에)

**At**은 동작이 **향하는 대상이나 목표**를 나타냅니다.

| Usage | Example |
|-------|---------|
| 바라보기 | look at, stare at |
| 던지기 | throw at, aim at |
| 웃기/소리치기 | laugh at, shout at |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The teacher looked at me."**
> 선생님이 나를 쳐다보셨다.

> **"I looked at the finish line."**
> 결승선을 바라봤다.

> **"Minseo laughed at my joke."**
> 민서가 내 농담에 웃었다.

---

## C. In vs Into

| In | Into |
|----|------|
| 위치 (안에 있음) | 이동 (안으로 들어감) |
| I'm **in** the room. | I walked **into** the room. |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I got into the bus."**
> 버스에 탔다. (안으로 들어감)

> **"I was in the bus for 40 minutes."**
> 40분 동안 버스 안에 있었다. (안에 있는 상태)

> **"I walked into the classroom."**
> 교실 안으로 들어갔다.

> **"I was in the classroom."**
> 교실 안에 있었다.

---

## D. To vs At 비교

**Throw to** (전달) vs **Throw at** (공격)

| Throw to | Throw at |
|----------|----------|
| ~에게 던지다 (받으라고) | ~을 향해 던지다 (맞추려고) |
| He threw the ball **to** me. | He threw the ball **at** me. |

### Other comparisons:

> **"She talked to me."** (대화함)
> 그녀가 나에게 말했다.

> **"She shouted at me."** (화내며 소리침)
> 그녀가 나에게 소리 질렀다.

---

## E. Home, here, there에는 to를 쓰지 않음

**Home, here, there** 앞에는 **to**를 쓰지 않습니다.

| Correct | Incorrect |
|---------|-----------|
| go **home** | go to home |
| come **here** | come to here |
| go **there** | go to there |

### From the Novel:

> **"I went home after the race."** (not: went to home)
> 경기 후에 집에 갔다.

> **"Come here."** (not: come to here)
> 이리 와.

---

## F. 동사 + 전치사 패턴

| Verb + to | Verb + at | Verb + into |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| go to | look at | walk into |
| run to | stare at | get into |
| talk to | shout at | dive into |
| listen to | laugh at | turn into |

## Exercises

### A. to, at, in, into 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. I walked _______ the classroom.
2. I was _______ the classroom all morning.
3. She looked _______ me and smiled.
4. I ran _______ school every day.
5. He threw the ball _______ me (so I could catch it).

**Answers:**
1. into
2. in
3. at
4. to
5. to

### B. 빈칸을 채우세요.

1. I went _______ after the race.
2. He walked _______ the room and sat down.
3. The teacher stared _______ me.
4. I talked _______ Minseo about running.
5. She shouted _______ me because she was angry.

**Answers:**
1. home (to 없음)
2. into
3. at
4. to
5. at

### C. 틀린 문장을 고치세요.

1. I went to home after school.
2. Come to here, please.
3. I looked to the finish line.
4. She was into the room.

**Answers:**
1. I went home after school.
2. Come here, please.
3. I looked at the finish line.
4. She was in the room.

## Key Sentences (암기 문장)

| English | Korean |
|---------|--------|
| I walked to the bus stop. | 버스 정류장까지 걸어갔다. |
| I walked into the classroom. | 교실 안으로 들어갔다. |
| The teacher looked at me. | 선생님이 나를 쳐다보셨다. |
| I went home after the race. | 경기 후에 집에 갔다. |
| He threw the ball to me. | 그가 나에게 공을 던졌다. |

## To vs At vs Into 비교표

| Preposition | Meaning | Example |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| to | 방향/목적지로 | run to school |
| at | 대상을 향해 | look at the finish line |
| into | 안으로 (이동) | walk into the room |
| in | 안에 (위치) | be in the room |
