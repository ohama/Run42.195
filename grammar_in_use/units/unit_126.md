# Unit 126: To, at, in, and into

## A. To - 방향/목적지 (Direction/Destination)

**To**는 "~로, ~에게"의 의미로, **이동의 방향이나 목적지**를 나타냅니다.

### 구조:
> 동사 + to + 장소/사람

### From the Original Book:
> "I'm going **to** work."
> 직장에 가고 있어.

> "Give the book **to** me."
> 그 책을 나에게 줘.

### To 사용 상황:

| Usage | Example | Korean |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 이동 | go to school, walk to the bus stop | ~로 가다 |
| 전달 | give to, send to, talk to | ~에게 |
| 방향 | turn to the left | ~쪽으로 |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I walked to the bus stop."**
> 버스 정류장까지 걸어갔다.

> **"I ran to school."**
> 학교까지 달렸다.

> **"Mom gave the medal to me."**
> 엄마가 메달을 나에게 주셨다.

---

## B. At - 목표 지점에 (Target/Direction of Action)

**At**은 동작이 **향하는 대상이나 목표**를 나타냅니다.

### 구조:
> 동사 + at + 대상

### From the Original Book:
> "Don't shout **at** me!"
> 나한테 소리 지르지 마!

> "He threw a stone **at** the window."
> 그가 창문에 돌을 던졌다. (맞추려고)

### At 사용 상황:

| Usage | Example | Korean |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 바라보기 | look at, stare at, glance at | ~을 보다 |
| 던지기/쏘기 | throw at, aim at, shoot at | ~을 향해 |
| 웃기/소리치기 | laugh at, shout at | ~에게 |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The teacher looked at me."**
> 선생님이 나를 쳐다보셨다.

> **"I looked at the finish line."**
> 결승선을 바라봤다.

> **"Minseo laughed at my joke."**
> 민서가 내 농담에 웃었다.

---

## C. In vs Into (위치 vs 이동)

| In | Into |
|----|------|
| 위치 (안에 있음) | 이동 (안으로 들어감) |
| I'm **in** the room. | I walked **into** the room. |
| 상태 | 동작 |

### From the Original Book:
> "I went **into** the shop." (안으로 들어감)
> "I was **in** the shop." (안에 있음)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I got into the bus."**
> 버스에 탔다. (안으로 들어감)

> **"I was in the bus for 40 minutes."**
> 40분 동안 버스 안에 있었다. (안에 있는 상태)

> **"I walked into the classroom."**
> 교실 안으로 들어갔다.

> **"I was in the classroom."**
> 교실 안에 있었다.

### 동사에 따른 선택:

| In (상태) | Into (이동) |
|-----------|-------------|
| be in | go into |
| stay in | walk into |
| sit in | run into |
| live in | get into |

---

## D. Throw to vs Throw at (전달 vs 공격)

**Throw to** (전달) vs **Throw at** (공격/맞추기)

| Throw to | Throw at |
|----------|----------|
| ~에게 던지다 (받으라고) | ~을 향해 던지다 (맞추려고) |
| He threw the ball **to** me. | He threw the ball **at** me. |
| 긍정적 | 부정적/공격적 |

### From the Original Book:
> "Throw the ball **to** me." (나한테 던져 - 받으라고)
> "Someone threw an egg **at** the car." (차에 던짐 - 맞추려고)

### Other comparisons:

> **"She talked to me."** (대화함 - 중립적)
> 그녀가 나에게 말했다.

> **"She shouted at me."** (화내며 소리침 - 부정적)
> 그녀가 나에게 소리 질렀다.

## E. Home, here, there에는 to를 쓰지 않음

**Home, here, there** 앞에는 **to**를 쓰지 않습니다.

| Correct (O) | Incorrect (X) |
|-------------|---------------|
| go **home** | ~~go to home~~ |
| come **here** | ~~come to here~~ |
| go **there** | ~~go to there~~ |

### From the Original Book:
> "I'm going **home**." (not: to home)
> "Come **here**." (not: to here)

### From the Novel:

> **"I went home after the race."** (not: went to home)
> 경기 후에 집에 갔다.

> **"Come here."** (not: come to here)
> 이리 와.

---

## F. 동사 + 전치사 패턴 (Verb + Preposition Patterns)

| Verb + to | Verb + at | Verb + into |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| go to | look at | walk into |
| run to | stare at | get into |
| talk to | shout at | dive into |
| listen to | laugh at | turn into |
| give to | aim at | run into |
| send to | throw at | crash into |

### From the Original Book:
> "He **walked into** the room and **sat** down."
> 그가 방에 들어가 앉았다.

---

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | 설명 |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| ~~go to home~~ | go **home** | home 앞에 to 없음 |
| ~~come to here~~ | come **here** | here 앞에 to 없음 |
| ~~I looked to him~~ | I looked **at** him | look at |
| ~~She was into the room~~ | She was **in** the room | 상태는 in |
| ~~I walked in the room~~ | I walked **into** the room | 이동은 into |
| ~~throw the ball at me~~ (전달) | throw the ball **to** me | 전달은 to |

## Exercises

### A. to, at, in, into 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. I walked _______ the classroom.
2. I was _______ the classroom all morning.
3. She looked _______ me and smiled.
4. I ran _______ school every day.
5. He threw the ball _______ me (so I could catch it).
6. I got _______ the car.
7. She lives _______ a small house.
8. He dived _______ the water.

**Answers:**
1. into
2. in
3. at
4. to
5. to
6. into / in
7. in
8. into

### B. 빈칸을 채우세요.

1. I went _______ after the race.
2. He walked _______ the room and sat down.
3. The teacher stared _______ me.
4. I talked _______ Minseo about running.
5. She shouted _______ me because she was angry.
6. Please listen _______ me carefully.
7. The car crashed _______ a tree.
8. I sent the message _______ my friend.

**Answers:**
1. home (to 없음)
2. into
3. at
4. to
5. at
6. to
7. into
8. to

### C. 틀린 문장을 고치세요.

1. I went to home after school.
   → I went **home** after school.

2. Come to here, please.
   → Come **here**, please.

3. I looked to the finish line.
   → I looked **at** the finish line.

4. She was into the room.
   → She was **in** the room.

5. He threw a rock at me. (전달하려고)
   → He threw a rock **to** me.

### D. throw to / throw at 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. He threw the ball _______ me and I caught it.
2. Someone threw eggs _______ the car.
3. Can you throw the towel _______ me?
4. The angry man threw a bottle _______ the wall.
5. She threw the keys _______ her husband.

**Answers:**
1. to (전달)
2. at (공격)
3. to (전달)
4. at (공격)
5. to (전달)

## Summary: To vs At vs Into 비교표

| Preposition | Meaning | Example |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| to | 방향/목적지로 | run to school |
| at | 대상을 향해 | look at the finish line |
| into | 안으로 (이동) | walk into the room |
| in | 안에 (위치) | be in the room |

---

## 선택 가이드

```
목적지/방향으로 이동? → To
"I walked to school."

대상을 향해 동작? → At
"I looked at him."

안으로 이동? → Into
"I walked into the room."

안에 있는 상태? → In
"I was in the room."

home/here/there로 이동? → 전치사 없음
"I went home."
```

## Talk to vs Talk at

| Talk to | Talk at |
|---------|---------|
| 대화하다 (양방향) | 일방적으로 말하다 |
| I talked **to** her. | He talks **at** you, not to you. |
| 정상적 대화 | 상대방 무시 |
