# Unit 106: Comparison (2) - much better / any better / better and better

## A. 비교급 강조하기 (Intensifying comparatives)

비교급을 강조할 때 **much, a lot, far, even, a little, slightly** 등을 사용합니다.

### Intensifiers Table:

| 강조어 | 의미 | 강도 | 예시 |
|--------|------|------|------|
| much | 훨씬 | ★★★★★ | much better |
| a lot | 훨씬 | ★★★★★ | a lot faster |
| far | 훨씬 | ★★★★★ | far more difficult |
| even | 더욱 | ★★★★☆ | even harder |
| a bit | 조금 | ★★☆☆☆ | a bit easier |
| a little | 조금 | ★★☆☆☆ | a little better |
| slightly | 약간 | ★☆☆☆☆ | slightly taller |

### From the Original Book:

> "The exam was **much** more difficult than I expected."
> 시험이 예상보다 훨씬 더 어려웠다.

> "You're driving **a bit** too fast."
> 너 좀 너무 빨리 운전하고 있어.

---

## B. much/a lot/far + 비교급 (Strong intensifiers)

### From the Original Book:

> "This bag is **much** heavier than the other one."
> 이 가방이 다른 것보다 훨씬 무겁다.

> "Don't go by train. It's **a lot** more expensive."
> 기차로 가지 마. 훨씬 비싸.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Running feels much better than sitting on the bus."**
> 달리기가 버스에 앉아 있는 것보다 훨씬 기분이 좋다.

> **"The second semester was far harder than the first."**
> 2학기가 1학기보다 훨씬 힘들었다.

> **"I feel a lot stronger now."**
> 지금 훨씬 더 강해진 기분이다.

> **"The 42km was much longer than I imagined."**
> 42km는 상상보다 훨씬 길었다.

> **"Walking is far more enjoyable than I expected."**
> 걷기가 예상보다 훨씬 즐거웠다.

### 주의: very는 비교급과 함께 쓰지 않습니다!

| 올바름 | 틀림 |
|--------|------|
| much better | ~~very better~~ |
| far easier | ~~very easier~~ |
| a lot faster | ~~very faster~~ |

---

## C. a little/slightly + 비교급 (Weak intensifiers)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I felt a little better after resting."**
> 쉬고 나서 조금 나아졌다.

> **"Today was slightly easier than yesterday."**
> 오늘이 어제보다 약간 쉬웠다.

> **"I ran a little faster this time."**
> 이번에 조금 더 빨리 달렸다.

> **"The morning was a bit cooler than expected."**
> 아침이 예상보다 약간 서늘했다.

---

## D. even + 비교급 (예상보다 더)

**even**은 "예상보다 더"의 뉘앙스를 줍니다. 이미 ~한데 더욱 더.

### From the Original Book:

> "It was cold yesterday, but today it's **even** colder."
> 어제 추웠는데, 오늘은 더 춥다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The full marathon was even harder than the half."**
> 풀마라톤이 하프보다 더욱 힘들었다.

> **"I woke up even earlier today."**
> 오늘 더 일찍 일어났다.

> **"My legs felt even heavier after 35km."**
> 35km 후에 다리가 더욱 무겁게 느껴졌다.

> **"The second try was even better than the first."**
> 두 번째 시도가 첫 번째보다 더 좋았다.

---

## E. any + 비교급 (의문문/부정문)

**any**는 의문문이나 부정문에서 비교급과 함께 사용됩니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "Do you feel **any** better today?"
> 오늘 조금이라도 나아졌어?

> "I can't stay **any** longer."
> 더 이상 있을 수 없어.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Is running any easier now?"**
> 지금 달리기가 조금이라도 쉬워졌어?

> **"I can't run any faster."**
> 더 이상 빨리 달릴 수 없다.

> **"Do you feel any better?"**
> 기분이 조금이라도 나아졌어?

> **"I couldn't go any farther."**
> 더 이상 멀리 갈 수 없었다.

> **"I don't want to wait any longer."**
> 더 이상 기다리고 싶지 않다.

---

## F. 비교급 + and + 비교급 (점점 더)

"점점 더"를 표현할 때 **비교급 + and + 비교급**을 사용합니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "Your English is getting **better and better**."
> 네 영어가 점점 좋아지고 있어.

> "It's becoming **more and more** difficult to find a job."
> 일자리를 찾기가 점점 더 어려워지고 있다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I got stronger and stronger."**
> 점점 더 강해졌다.

> **"Running became easier and easier."**
> 달리기가 점점 더 쉬워졌다.

> **"The weather got hotter and hotter."**
> 날씨가 점점 더 더워졌다.

> **"My pace got faster and faster."**
> 내 페이스가 점점 더 빨라졌다.

> **"The race became more and more challenging."**
> 대회가 점점 더 도전적이 됐다.

---

## G. the + 비교급, the + 비교급 (더 ~할수록)

"더 ~할수록 더 ~하다"를 표현합니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "**The warmer** the weather, **the better** I feel."
> 날씨가 따뜻할수록 기분이 더 좋다.

> "**The more** you practice, **the better** you'll play."
> 더 많이 연습할수록 더 잘할 거야.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The more I ran, the better I felt."**
> 더 달릴수록 기분이 더 좋아졌다.

> **"The harder I trained, the stronger I got."**
> 더 열심히 훈련할수록 더 강해졌다.

> **"The longer I walked, the more I enjoyed it."**
> 더 오래 걸을수록 더 즐거웠다.

> **"The closer I got to the finish, the more excited I became."**
> 결승선에 가까워질수록 더 흥분됐다.

> **"The sooner you start, the sooner you finish."**
> 일찍 시작할수록 일찍 끝난다.

## H. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | 설명 |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| ~~very better~~ | **much** better | very + 비교급 불가 |
| ~~more better~~ | **much** better | more + 불규칙비교급 불가 |
| ~~I feel more and more good~~ | I feel **better and better** | 불규칙도 반복 |
| ~~The more hard I try~~ | **The harder** I try | 비교급 사용 |
| ~~bigger and more bigger~~ | **bigger and bigger** | 같은 형태 반복 |

## Exercises

### A. 알맞은 강조어를 고르세요 (Choose the correct intensifier)

1. I feel (much / very) better now.
   → **much**

2. Today was (slightly / very) easier than yesterday.
   → **slightly**

3. The race was (far / very) harder than expected.
   → **far**

4. I ran (a little / very) faster this time.
   → **a little**

5. The marathon was (even / very) more challenging.
   → **even**

6. Do you feel (any / very) better?
   → **any**

---

### B. 문장을 완성하세요 (Complete the sentences)

1. Running is _______ (much/good) than taking the bus.
   → **much better**

2. I got stronger _______ stronger.
   → **and**

3. The _______ I trained, the better I got.
   → **more**

4. Do you feel _______ better?
   → **any**

5. I can't run _______ faster.
   → **any**

6. The weather got _______ and hotter.
   → **hotter**

7. The _______ you practice, the better you become.
   → **more**

8. I felt _______ (even/good) after the second day.
   → **even better**

---

### C. "점점 더"를 사용하여 문장을 바꾸세요 (Rewrite using "...and...")

1. The weather got hot.
   → The weather got **hotter and hotter**.

2. Running became easy.
   → Running became **easier and easier**.

3. The race became challenging. (more)
   → The race became **more and more challenging**.

4. I got strong.
   → I got **stronger and stronger**.

5. My pace got fast.
   → My pace got **faster and faster**.

---

### D. "더 ~할수록"을 사용하여 문장을 완성하세요 (Complete with "the...the...")

1. _______ I practiced, _______ I improved.
   → **The more** I practiced, **the more** I improved.

2. _______ you run, _______ it becomes.
   → **The more** you run, **the easier** it becomes.

3. _______ I trained, _______ I got.
   → **The harder** I trained, **the stronger** I got.

4. _______ we get to the finish, _______ excited I become.
   → **The closer** we get to the finish, **the more** excited I become.

5. _______ you start, _______ you finish.
   → **The earlier** you start, **the earlier** you finish.

## Summary

### Intensifiers with Comparatives:

| 표현 | 의미 | 예시 |
|------|------|------|
| much/far + 비교급 | 훨씬 | much better |
| a little + 비교급 | 조금 | a little easier |
| even + 비교급 | 더욱 (이미 ~한데) | even harder |
| any + 비교급 | 조금이라도 (의문/부정) | any better? |
| 비교급 + and + 비교급 | 점점 더 | stronger and stronger |
| the + 비교급, the + 비교급 | 더 ~할수록 | the more, the better |

### Pattern Summary:

```
강조: much/far/a lot + 비교급
     I feel much better.

약화: a little/slightly + 비교급
     I feel a little better.

점점: 비교급 + and + 비교급
     stronger and stronger

~할수록: the + 비교급, the + 비교급
     The more you practice, the better you become.
```

### Key Rule:

```
very + 비교급 = WRONG ✗
much + 비교급 = CORRECT ✓
```
