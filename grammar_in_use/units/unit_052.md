# Unit 52: Question Tags (do you? isn't it?, etc.)

## A. 부가 의문문이란?

문장 끝에 붙여서 **확인, 동의, 강조**를 구하는 짧은 질문

| 긍정문 | + 부정 태그 |
|--------|-------------|
| You **are** running, | **aren't** you? |
| He **can** run, | **can't** he? |

| 부정문 | + 긍정 태그 |
|--------|-------------|
| You **aren't** tired, | **are** you? |
| He **can't** run, | **can** he? |

---

## B. 부가 의문문의 기본 규칙

1. **긍정문 → 부정 태그**
2. **부정문 → 긍정 태그**
3. **같은 조동사/be동사 사용**
4. **주어는 대명사로**

### 긍정문 + 부정 태그:

> **"You are tired, aren't you?"**
> 피곤하지, 그렇지?

> **"He can run fast, can't he?"**
> 그는 빨리 달릴 수 있지, 그렇지?

> **"You finished the race, didn't you?"**
> 대회 끝냈지, 그렇지?

### 부정문 + 긍정 태그:

> **"You aren't tired, are you?"**
> 안 피곤하지, 그렇지?

> **"He can't run fast, can he?"**
> 그는 빨리 못 달리지, 그렇지?

> **"You didn't finish, did you?"**
> 못 끝냈지, 그렇지?

---

## C. 다양한 시제의 부가 의문문

| 시제 | 긍정문 + 부정 태그 |
|------|-------------------|
| 현재 be | You **are** running, **aren't** you? |
| 현재 일반 | You **run** every day, **don't** you? |
| 과거 be | It **was** hard, **wasn't** it? |
| 과거 일반 | You **finished**, **didn't** you? |
| 현재완료 | You **have** trained, **haven't** you? |
| 미래 | You **will** come, **won't** you? |
| 조동사 | You **can** run, **can't** you? |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The bus comes at 7:30, doesn't it?"**
> 버스 7시 30분에 오지, 그렇지?

> **"You ran this morning, didn't you?"**
> 오늘 아침에 달렸지, 그렇지?

> **"The race was hard, wasn't it?"**
> 대회 힘들었지, 그렇지?

> **"You've been training, haven't you?"**
> 훈련하고 있었지, 그렇지?

> **"You'll run tomorrow, won't you?"**
> 내일 달릴 거지, 그렇지?

---

## D. 특수한 경우

### 1. I am → aren't I?

> **"I'm running well, aren't I?"**
> 나 잘 달리고 있지, 그렇지?

*주의: I amn't (X) → aren't I (O)*

### 2. Let's → shall we?

> **"Let's run together, shall we?"**
> 같이 달리자, 그래?

### 3. 명령문 → will you? / won't you?

> **"Run faster, will you?"**
> 더 빨리 달려, 알았지?

> **"Don't stop, will you?"**
> 멈추지 마, 알았지?

### 4. There is/are → is/are there?

> **"There's a finish line ahead, isn't there?"**
> 앞에 결승선이 있지, 그렇지?

### 5. Nobody/Nothing → 긍정 태그

> **"Nobody came, did they?"**
> 아무도 안 왔지, 그렇지?

> **"Nothing happened, did it?"**
> 아무 일도 없었지, 그렇지?

## E. 억양 (Intonation)

| 억양 | 의미 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| 내림↓ | 확인 (답을 안다) | You're tired, aren't you?↓ |
| 올림↑ | 진짜 질문 (모른다) | You're coming, aren't you?↑ |

### 확인 (내림 억양):
> "You finished the race, didn't you?↓"
> (완주한 거 아는데 확인)

### 진짜 질문 (올림 억양):
> "You finished the race, didn't you?↑"
> (정말 완주했는지 모름)

## F. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

## Exercises

### A. 부가 의문문을 완성하세요.

1. You are tired, _______?
2. He can run fast, _______?
3. She finished the race, _______?
4. They haven't left, _______?
5. It was hard, _______?

**Answers:**
1. aren't you
2. can't he
3. didn't she
4. have they
5. wasn't it

### B. 부정문에 부가 의문문을 붙이세요.

1. You aren't coming, _______?
2. He can't run, _______?
3. She didn't finish, _______?
4. They haven't trained, _______?
5. It wasn't easy, _______?

**Answers:**
1. are you
2. can he
3. did she
4. have they
5. was it

### C. 특수한 경우의 부가 의문문을 쓰세요.

1. I'm doing well, _______?
2. Let's go running, _______?
3. Run faster, _______?
4. There's a bus coming, _______?
5. Nobody finished, _______?

**Answers:**
1. aren't I
2. shall we
3. will you
4. isn't there
5. did they

### D. 올바른 부가 의문문을 고르세요.

1. You run every day, (don't you / aren't you)?
2. He has finished, (hasn't he / didn't he)?
3. She will come, (won't she / doesn't she)?
4. They were tired, (weren't they / didn't they)?
5. I am running well, (amn't I / aren't I)?

**Answers:**
1. don't you
2. hasn't he
3. won't she
4. weren't they
5. aren't I

## Summary

```
부가 의문문 규칙:

1. 긍정문 → 부정 태그
   You are running, aren't you?

2. 부정문 → 긍정 태그
   You aren't running, are you?

3. 같은 조동사/be동사 사용
4. 주어는 대명사로

특수 경우:
- I am → aren't I?
- Let's → shall we?
- 명령문 → will you?
- There is → isn't there?
- Nobody/Nothing → 긍정 태그

억양:
- 내림↓ = 확인 (답을 안다)
- 올림↑ = 진짜 질문 (모른다)
```
