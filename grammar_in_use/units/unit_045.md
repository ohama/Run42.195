# Unit 45: It is said that... / He is said to...

## A. 두 가지 구조 (Two Structures)

**사람들이 ~라고 한다**를 표현하는 두 가지 방법

### From the Original Book:

> "People say that he is 110 years old."
> → "**It is said that** he is 110 years old."
> → "**He is said to be** 110 years old."
> 그가 110살이라고 한다.

### 두 구조 비교:

| 구조 | 형태 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| **구조 1** | It is said that + 주어 + 동사 | It is said that **he runs** every day. |
| **구조 2** | 주어 + is said to + 동사원형 | **He is said to run** every day. |

### 의미는 동일:
두 구조 모두 "사람들이 ~라고 한다"는 의미를 전달합니다.

---

## B. It is said that... 구조

**It is + 과거분사 + that절** 형태

### From the Original Book:

> "**It is said that** the weather will be good tomorrow."
> 내일 날씨가 좋을 거라고 한다.

> "**It is believed that** the company is losing money."
> 그 회사가 돈을 잃고 있다고 믿어진다.

> "**It is expected that** the meeting will last two hours."
> 회의가 2시간 동안 지속될 것으로 예상된다.

### 자주 쓰이는 동사:

| 동사 | 의미 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| **said** | ~라고 한다 | It is said that... |
| **believed** | ~라고 믿어진다 | It is believed that... |
| **thought** | ~라고 생각된다 | It is thought that... |
| **known** | ~라고 알려져 있다 | It is known that... |
| **reported** | ~라고 보도된다 | It is reported that... |
| **expected** | ~라고 예상된다 | It is expected that... |
| **considered** | ~라고 여겨진다 | It is considered that... |
| **understood** | ~라고 이해된다 | It is understood that... |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"It is said that the Jeju Marathon course is beautiful."**
> 제주 마라톤 코스가 아름답다고 한다.

> **"It is known that running helps reduce stress."**
> 달리기가 스트레스를 줄이는 데 도움이 된다고 알려져 있다.

> **"It is believed that walking before running builds stamina."**
> 달리기 전에 걷는 것이 체력을 기른다고 믿어진다.

> **"It is expected that the race will be held next March."**
> 대회가 내년 3월에 열릴 것으로 예상된다.

## C. 주어 + is said to... 구조

**주어를 앞으로 이동**시킨 형태

### From the Original Book:

> "**He is said to be** 110 years old."
> 그가 110살이라고 한다.

> "**She is believed to have** left the country."
> 그녀가 나라를 떠났다고 믿어진다.

> "**The company is reported to be** losing money."
> 그 회사가 돈을 잃고 있다고 보도된다.

### 구조:
```
주어 + is/are + said/believed/known... + to + 동사원형
```

### 변환 방법:

| It is said that... | 주어 + is said to... |
|--------------------|-----------------------|
| It is said that **he runs**. | **He is said to run**. |
| It is said that **she is** smart. | **She is said to be** smart. |
| It is known that **they work** hard. | **They are known to work** hard. |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The Jeju Marathon course is said to be beautiful."**
> 제주 마라톤 코스가 아름답다고 한다.

> **"Running is known to help reduce stress."**
> 달리기가 스트레스를 줄이는 데 도움이 된다고 알려져 있다.

> **"Walking is believed to build stamina."**
> 걷기가 체력을 기른다고 믿어진다.

> **"The race is expected to be held next March."**
> 대회가 내년 3월에 열릴 것으로 예상된다.

## D. 과거 사실에 대한 표현

과거 사실을 말할 때는 **to have + p.p.** 사용

### From the Original Book:

> "**He is said to have been** a good player."
> 그가 좋은 선수였다고 한다.

> "**She is believed to have left** the country."
> 그녀가 나라를 떠났다고 믿어진다.

### 현재 vs 과거 비교:

| 시점 | 구조 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| **현재** | is said **to + 동사** | He is said **to run** every day. |
| **과거** | is said **to have + p.p.** | He is said **to have run** a marathon. |

### 더 많은 비교:

| 현재 | 과거 |
|------|------|
| She is known **to be** a good runner. | She is known **to have been** a good runner. |
| 그녀는 좋은 주자라고 알려져 있다. | 그녀가 좋은 주자였다고 알려져 있다. |
| He is believed **to live** in Jeju. | He is believed **to have lived** in Jeju. |
| 그가 제주에 산다고 믿어진다. | 그가 제주에 살았다고 믿어진다. |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"He is said to have completed over 10 marathons."**
> 그가 10개 이상의 마라톤을 완주했다고 한다.

> **"The course is known to have been changed last year."**
> 코스가 작년에 바뀌었다고 알려져 있다.

> **"The runner is reported to have broken the record."**
> 그 주자가 기록을 깼다고 보도되었다.

## E. There is said to be...

**There is/are** 구문의 수동태

### From the Original Book:

> "**There is said to be** a lot of oil under the sea."
> 바다 아래에 많은 석유가 있다고 한다.

> "**There are thought to be** millions of insects in the world."
> 세계에 수백만 종의 곤충이 있다고 생각된다.

### 구조:
```
There + is/are + said/thought/believed + to be + 명사
```

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"There is said to be a beautiful view at the 30km mark."**
> 30km 지점에 아름다운 경치가 있다고 한다.

> **"There are thought to be thousands of runners."**
> 수천 명의 주자가 있을 것으로 생각된다.

> **"There is known to be a water station every 5km."**
> 5km마다 급수대가 있다고 알려져 있다.

## F. 능동태와 수동태 비교

| 능동태 | It is said that... | 주어 + is said to... |
|--------|--------------------|-----------------------|
| People say that he runs. | It is said that he runs. | He is said to run. |
| People believe that she won. | It is believed that she won. | She is believed to have won. |
| People think he is fast. | It is thought that he is fast. | He is thought to be fast. |
| People know she trains. | It is known that she trains. | She is known to train. |
| People report he left. | It is reported that he left. | He is reported to have left. |

### 언제 어떤 구조를 사용?

| 구조 | 사용 상황 |
|------|-----------|
| It is said that... | 격식적, 문어체 |
| 주어 + is said to... | 주어를 강조하고 싶을 때 |

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| 틀린 문장 (X) | 올바른 문장 (O) | 설명 |
|---------------|-----------------|------|
| **Said that** he is rich. | **It is said that** he is rich. | It 필요 |
| He is said **be** fast. | He is said **to be** fast. | to 필요 |
| It said that he runs. | It **is said** that he runs. | be동사 필요 |
| He is said **run** fast. | He is said **to run** fast. | to 필요 |
| She is said **to ran**. | She is said **to run**. | to + 동사원형 |
| He is said to **has gone**. | He is said to **have gone**. | to have p.p. |

### 과거 표현 오류:

```
(X) He is said to completed the race.
(O) He is said to have completed the race.

과거 사실 = to have + p.p.
```

### to be 누락:

```
(X) He is said intelligent.
(O) He is said to be intelligent.

형용사 앞에는 to be 필요
```

## Exercises

### A. It is said that... 구조로 바꾸세요.

1. People say that running is good for health.
   → It is said that _______________________.

2. People believe that the marathon is challenging.
   → It is believed that _______________________.

3. People think that Doyun runs every day.
   → It is thought that _______________________.

4. People know that the course is beautiful.
   → It is known that _______________________.

5. People expect that the race will be difficult.
   → It is expected that _______________________.

**Answers:**
1. running is good for health
2. the marathon is challenging
3. Doyun runs every day
4. the course is beautiful
5. the race will be difficult

### B. 주어 + is said to... 구조로 바꾸세요.

1. It is said that running is good for health.
   → Running is said _______________________.

2. It is believed that the marathon is challenging.
   → The marathon is believed _______________________.

3. It is thought that Doyun runs every day.
   → Doyun is thought _______________________.

4. It is known that the course is beautiful.
   → The course is known _______________________.

5. It is expected that he will win.
   → He is expected _______________________.

**Answers:**
1. to be good for health
2. to be challenging
3. to run every day
4. to be beautiful
5. to win

### C. 과거 사실에 대해 표현하세요. (to have + p.p.)

1. People say that he completed the marathon.
   → He is said _______________________.

2. People believe that she broke the record.
   → She is believed _______________________.

3. People think that the race started late.
   → The race is thought _______________________.

4. People know that he won the race.
   → He is known _______________________.

5. People report that she finished first.
   → She is reported _______________________.

**Answers:**
1. to have completed the marathon
2. to have broken the record
3. to have started late
4. to have won the race
5. to have finished first

### D. 알맞은 형태를 고르세요.

1. Running (is said / said) to be good for health.
2. It is known that the marathon (is / to be) challenging.
3. He is believed (to finish / to have finished) the race yesterday.
4. There (is said / are said) to be many runners at the event.
5. She is thought (be / to be) the fastest runner.
6. It is reported that he (left / to leave) the country.
7. The course is known (to change / to have changed) last year.
8. People (are said / is said) to live longer if they exercise.

**Answers:**
1. is said
2. is
3. to have finished
4. are said
5. to be
6. left
7. to have changed
8. are said

## Summary: 전언 수동태 정리

### 두 구조 비교:

```
It is said/known/believed/thought that + 주어 + 동사
= 주어 + is said/known/believed/thought to + 동사원형

과거 사실: to have + p.p.
예: He is said to have completed the marathon.

There 구문: There is said to be...
```

### 자주 쓰이는 동사 정리:

| 동사 | 의미 | It is... | ...is ... to |
|------|------|----------|--------------|
| said | ~라고 한다 | It is said that... | He is said to... |
| believed | ~라고 믿어진다 | It is believed that... | He is believed to... |
| thought | ~라고 생각된다 | It is thought that... | He is thought to... |
| known | ~라고 알려져 있다 | It is known that... | He is known to... |
| reported | ~라고 보도된다 | It is reported that... | He is reported to... |
| expected | ~라고 예상된다 | It is expected that... | He is expected to... |
| considered | ~라고 여겨진다 | It is considered that... | He is considered to... |

### 시제 표현:

| 시점 | 구조 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| 현재 | to + 동사 | He is said to run. |
| 과거 | to have + p.p. | He is said to have run. |
| 상태 | to be + 형용사 | He is said to be fast. |
