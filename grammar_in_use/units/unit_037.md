# Unit 37: Can/Could/Would you...? (Requests, offers, permission)

## A. 문법 설명 (Grammar Explanation)

### 요청, 제안, 허락 표현

영어에서 요청/제안/허락을 할 때 **Can, Could, Would, May**를 사용합니다.

| 표현 | 용도 | 정중함 |
|------|------|--------|
| Can I...? | 허락 요청 | 일상적 |
| Could I...? | 허락 요청 | 더 정중 |
| Can you...? | 요청 | 일상적 |
| Could you...? | 요청 | 더 정중 |
| Would you...? | 요청 | 정중 |
| May I...? | 허락 요청 | 격식체 |

---

## B. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: Can I...? (허락)

> **"Can I sit here?"**
> 여기 앉아도 돼?

- 일상적인 허락 요청

### Example 2: Could you...? (정중한 요청)

> **"Could you help me with my stretching?"**
> 스트레칭 좀 도와주시겠어요?

- 정중한 요청

### Example 3: Would you...? (정중한 요청)

> **"Would you run with me tomorrow?"**
> 내일 나랑 같이 달릴래요?

- 정중한 부탁

### Example 4: Can you...? (일상 요청)

> **"Can you pass me the water?"**
> 물 좀 건네줄래?

- 친구/가족 간의 요청

### Example 5: May I...? (격식체)

> **"May I join the track team?"**
> 육상부에 들어가도 될까요?

- 선생님께 정중하게 허락 요청

---

## C. 추가 설명 (Additional Points)

### 정중함의 정도

| 표현 | 정중함 | 상황 |
|------|--------|------|
| Can you...? | ★☆☆☆ | 친구, 가족 |
| Could you...? | ★★☆☆ | 일반적 상황 |
| Would you...? | ★★★☆ | 정중한 상황 |
| Would you mind...? | ★★★★ | 매우 정중 |

### 제안/제의 표현

내가 무언가를 해주겠다고 할 때:

| 표현 | 예문 | 한국어 |
|------|------|--------|
| I'll... | I'll help you. | 도와줄게. |
| Shall I...? | Shall I get you some water? | 물 가져다 줄까? |
| Would you like me to...? | Would you like me to help? | 도와드릴까요? |

### 응답하기

| 긍정 응답 | 부정 응답 |
|-----------|-----------|
| Sure. / Of course. | Sorry, I can't. |
| Yes, please. | I'm afraid not. |
| That would be great. | I'd rather not. |

## D. 비교 (Comparison)

### 요청 (Requests)

| 표현 | 정중함 | 예문 |
|------|--------|------|
| Can you...? | 낮음 | **Can you** help me? |
| Could you...? | 중간 | **Could you** help me? |
| Would you...? | 높음 | **Would you** help me? |
| Would you mind...? | 매우 높음 | **Would you mind** helping me? |

### 허락 (Permission)

| 표현 | 정중함 | 예문 |
|------|--------|------|
| Can I...? | 낮음 | **Can I** sit here? |
| Could I...? | 중간 | **Could I** sit here? |
| May I...? | 높음 | **May I** sit here? |

### 제안 (Offers)

| 표현 | 예문 |
|------|------|
| I'll... | I'll get it for you. |
| Shall I...? | Shall I help? |
| Would you like...? | Would you like some water? |

## Exercises

### A. 적절한 표현 선택

1. (친구에게) 물 좀 건네줄래?
   → _______ you pass me the water?

2. (선생님께) 여기 앉아도 될까요?
   → _______ I sit here?

3. (정중하게) 스트레칭 좀 도와주시겠어요?
   → _______ you help me with my stretching?

4. (제안) 물 가져다 줄까?
   → _______ I get you some water?

**Answers:**
1. Can
2. May / Could
3. Could / Would
4. Shall

### B. 상황에 맞게 작성

1. 친구에게 같이 달리자고 요청 (일상적)
   → _______ run with me?

2. 코치에게 질문해도 되냐고 허락 요청 (정중)
   → _______ ask you a question?

3. 엄마에게 물 건네달라고 요청 (일상적)
   → _______ pass me the water?

**Answers:**
1. Can you / Would you
2. Could I / May I
3. Can you

### C. 응답 연습

1. "Could you help me?"
   긍정: _______ / 부정: _______

2. "Would you like some water?"
   긍정: _______ / 부정: _______

**Answers:**
1. Sure. / Of course. / Sorry, I can't.
2. Yes, please. / No, thank you. / I'm fine, thanks.
