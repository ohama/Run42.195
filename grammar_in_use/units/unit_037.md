# Unit 37: Can/Could/Would you...? (Requests, offers, permission)

## A. 형태 (Form)

### 요청, 제안, 허락 표현

영어에서 요청/제안/허락을 할 때 **Can, Could, Would, May**를 사용합니다.

| Expression | Use | Politeness |
|------------|-----|------------|
| Can I...? | 허락 요청 | 일상적 |
| Could I...? | 허락 요청 | 더 정중 |
| Can you...? | 요청 | 일상적 |
| Could you...? | 요청 | 더 정중 |
| Would you...? | 요청 | 정중 |
| May I...? | 허락 요청 | 매우 격식 |
| Would you mind...? | 요청 | 매우 정중 |

---

## B. 요청 (Requests)

### Can you...? (일상적)

친구, 가족과 사용:

> **"Can you pass me the water?"**
> 물 좀 건네줄래?

> "Can you help me?"
> 도와줄 수 있어?

### Could you...? (더 정중)

일반적인 정중한 상황:

> **"Could you help me with my stretching?"**
> 스트레칭 좀 도와주시겠어요?

> "Could you wait a moment?"
> 잠깐 기다려주실 수 있어요?

### Would you...? (정중)

정중한 부탁:

> **"Would you run with me tomorrow?"**
> 내일 나랑 같이 달릴래요?

> "Would you please close the door?"
> 문 좀 닫아주시겠어요?

### Would you mind...? (매우 정중)

가장 정중한 표현:

> "Would you mind opening the window?"
> 창문 좀 열어주시겠어요?

> "Would you mind helping me?"
> 도와주실 수 있을까요?

**응답 주의**: "Would you mind...?"에 대한 긍정 응답은 "No, not at all." (아니요, 괜찮아요)

### 정중함의 정도:

| Expression | Politeness | Context |
|------------|------------|---------|
| Can you...? | ★☆☆☆ | 친구, 가족 |
| Could you...? | ★★☆☆ | 일반적 상황 |
| Would you...? | ★★★☆ | 정중한 상황 |
| Would you mind...? | ★★★★ | 매우 정중 |

---

## C. 허락 (Permission)

### Can I...? (일상적)

> **"Can I sit here?"**
> 여기 앉아도 돼?

> "Can I use your phone?"
> 전화기 써도 돼?

### Could I...? (더 정중)

> "Could I borrow your pen?"
> 펜 좀 빌려도 될까요?

> **"Could I ask you a question?"**
> 질문 하나 드려도 될까요?

### May I...? (격식체)

가장 격식 있는 표현:

> **"May I join the track team?"**
> 육상부에 들어가도 될까요?

> "May I come in?"
> 들어가도 될까요?

### 허락의 정중함:

| Expression | Politeness | Context |
|------------|------------|---------|
| Can I...? | ★☆☆☆ | 친구, 가족 |
| Could I...? | ★★☆☆ | 일반적 상황 |
| May I...? | ★★★☆ | 격식 상황 (선생님, 어른) |

---

## D. 제안/제의 (Offers)

### I'll... (즉석 제안)

> **"I'll help you."**
> 도와줄게.

> "I'll get the door."
> 내가 문 열게.

### Shall I...? (제안/도움)

> **"Shall I get you some water?"**
> 물 가져다 줄까?

> "Shall I carry that for you?"
> 들어드릴까요?

### Would you like...? (정중한 제안)

> "Would you like some coffee?"
> 커피 드실래요?

> **"Would you like me to help?"**
> 도와드릴까요?

### 제안 표현 비교:

| Expression | Example | Korean |
|------------|---------|--------|
| I'll... | I'll help you. | 도와줄게. |
| Shall I...? | Shall I get you water? | 물 가져다 줄까? |
| Would you like me to...? | Would you like me to help? | 도와드릴까요? |
| Do you want me to...? | Do you want me to help? | 도와줄까? |

---

## E. 응답하기 (Responding)

### 긍정 응답 (Positive):

| Request | Response |
|---------|----------|
| Can you help? | Sure. / Of course. / No problem. |
| Could you wait? | Certainly. / Of course. |
| Would you help? | Yes, of course. / I'd be happy to. |
| Would you like coffee? | Yes, please. / That would be great. |

### 부정 응답 (Negative):

| Request | Response |
|---------|----------|
| Can you help? | Sorry, I can't. / I'm afraid I can't. |
| Could you wait? | I'm sorry, but I have to go. |
| Would you like coffee? | No, thank you. / I'm fine, thanks. |

### "Would you mind...?" 응답:

| Response | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| No, not at all. | 괜찮아요. (해드릴게요) |
| Of course not. | 물론이죠. (해드릴게요) |
| Yes, I would. (드묾) | 싫어요. (안 해드릴게요) |

## F. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~Can you to help me?~~ | **Can you help** me? | to 없음 |
| ~~May you help me?~~ | **Can/Could you help** me? | 요청은 Can/Could |
| ~~Would you mind to help?~~ | Would you mind **helping**? | mind + -ing |
| ~~I'll helping you.~~ | **I'll help** you. | will + 동사원형 |
| ~~Shall I to get water?~~ | **Shall I get** water? | to 없음 |
| ~~Could I to sit here?~~ | **Could I sit** here? | to 없음 |

### May vs Can 구분:

| May (허락 요청) | Can (허락/능력) |
|----------------|----------------|
| May I sit here? | Can I sit here? |
| (앉아도 될까요? - 격식) | (앉아도 돼? - 일상) |

### 상황별 선택 가이드:

| 상대 | 요청 | 허락 |
|------|------|------|
| 친구 | Can you...? | Can I...? |
| 일반인 | Could you...? | Could I...? |
| 선생님, 어른 | Would you...? / Could you...? | May I...? |
| 매우 격식 | Would you mind...? | May I...? |

## Exercises

### A. 적절한 표현 선택 (Choose the appropriate expression)

1. (친구에게) 물 좀 건네줄래?
   → _______ you pass me the water?

2. (선생님께) 여기 앉아도 될까요?
   → _______ I sit here?

3. (정중하게) 스트레칭 좀 도와주시겠어요?
   → _______ you help me with my stretching?

4. (제안) 물 가져다 줄까?
   → _______ I get you some water?

5. (매우 정중) 창문 좀 열어주시겠어요?
   → _______ opening the window?

**Answers:**
1. Can
2. May / Could
3. Could / Would
4. Shall
5. Would you mind

### B. 상황에 맞게 작성 (Write based on context)

1. 친구에게 같이 달리자고 요청 (일상적)
   → _______ run with me?

2. 코치에게 질문해도 되냐고 허락 요청 (정중)
   → _______ ask you a question?

3. 엄마에게 물 건네달라고 요청 (일상적)
   → _______ pass me the water?

4. 가게에서 직원에게 도움 요청 (정중)
   → _______ help me find running shoes?

5. 선생님께 들어가도 되냐고 (격식)
   → _______ come in?

**Answers:**
1. Can you / Would you
2. Could I / May I
3. Can you
4. Could you
5. May I

### C. 응답 연습 (Practice responding)

1. "Could you help me?"
   긍정: _______ / 부정: _______

2. "Would you like some water?"
   긍정: _______ / 부정: _______

3. "Would you mind closing the door?"
   긍정: _______ / 부정: _______

4. "Can I use your phone?"
   긍정: _______ / 부정: _______

**Answers:**
1. Sure. / Of course. / Sorry, I can't.
2. Yes, please. / No, thank you. / I'm fine, thanks.
3. No, not at all. / Of course not. / I'm sorry, but...
4. Sure, go ahead. / Of course. / Sorry, I need it right now.

### D. 틀린 부분 고치기 (Error Correction)

1. Can you to help me?
   → **Can you help** me?

2. Would you mind to wait?
   → Would you mind **waiting**?

3. May you help me?
   → **Can/Could you help** me?

4. Shall I to get you some water?
   → **Shall I get** you some water?

5. I'll helping you.
   → **I'll help** you.
