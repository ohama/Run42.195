# Unit 54: Verb + to... (decide to... / forget to..., etc.)

## A. 동사 + to부정사 (Verbs followed by to + infinitive)

특정 동사 뒤에는 **to부정사**가 옵니다. 이런 동사들은 -ing가 아닌 to + 동사원형을 목적어로 취합니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "I **decided to go** out."
> 나가기로 결정했다.

> "They **agreed to help** us."
> 그들이 도와주기로 했다.

> "I **hope to see** you again."
> 다시 만나기를 바란다.

### 주요 동사 + to:

| 동사 | 의미 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| **decide** | 결정하다 | I **decided to run**. |
| **choose** | 선택하다 | He **chose to walk**. |
| **plan** | 계획하다 | I **planned to leave**. |
| **hope** | 바라다 | I **hope to finish**. |
| **want** | 원하다 | I **want to run**. |
| **need** | 필요하다 | I **need to rest**. |
| **expect** | 기대하다 | I **expect to win**. |
| **promise** | 약속하다 | I **promised to come**. |
| **agree** | 동의하다 | She **agreed to help**. |
| **refuse** | 거부하다 | He **refused to stop**. |
| **learn** | 배우다 | I **learned to run**. |
| **offer** | 제안하다 | He **offered to help**. |
| **manage** | 해내다 | I **managed to finish**. |
| **fail** | 실패하다 | I **failed to wake up**. |
| **afford** | 여유가 되다 | I **can't afford to buy**. |
| **seem** | ~인 것 같다 | He **seems to know**. |
| **appear** | ~인 것 같다 | She **appears to be** happy. |
| **pretend** | ~인 척하다 | He **pretended to sleep**. |

## B. decide / choose / plan + to

### From the Original Book:

> "Have you **decided to go** to the party?"
> 파티에 가기로 했어?

> "They **chose to stay** at home."
> 그들은 집에 있기로 했다.

> "I'm **planning to travel** next year."
> 내년에 여행할 계획이야.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I decided to run to school."**
> 학교까지 달려가기로 결심했다.

- **decide + to**: ~하기로 결정하다

> **"I planned to run the whole way."**
> 전 구간을 달릴 계획이었다.

- **plan + to**: ~할 계획이다

> **"Today, I planned to run."**
> 오늘은 달리기로 했다.

---

## C. want / need / hope + to

### From the Original Book:

> "I **want to go** home."
> 집에 가고 싶어.

> "You **need to work** harder."
> 더 열심히 일해야 해.

> "I **hope to see** you soon."
> 곧 만나기를 바란다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I wanted to run."**
> 달리고 싶었다.

- **want + to**: ~하고 싶다

> **"I want to hear the birds again."**
> 새소리를 다시 듣고 싶다.

> **"I need to sleep early."**
> 일찍 자야 해.

- **need + to**: ~해야 하다

> **"I hope to finish the race."**
> 레이스를 완주하고 싶다.

- **hope + to**: ~하기를 바라다

---

## D. learn / manage / fail + to

### From the Original Book:

> "I'm **learning to drive**."
> 운전을 배우고 있어.

> "We **managed to find** a hotel."
> 어떻게든 호텔을 찾았다.

> "He **failed to pass** the exam."
> 그는 시험에 떨어졌다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I learned to pace myself."**
> 페이스 조절하는 법을 배웠다.

- **learn + to**: ~하는 법을 배우다

> **"I managed to finish 10km."**
> 어떻게든 10km를 완주했다.

- **manage + to**: 어렵지만 ~해내다

> **"I failed to wake up on time."**
> 제때 일어나지 못했다.

- **fail + to**: ~하지 못하다

---

## E. promise / agree / refuse + to

### From the Original Book:

> "She **promised to help** me."
> 그녀가 도와주겠다고 약속했다.

> "He **agreed to wait** for me."
> 그가 나를 기다리기로 했다.

> "She **refused to answer** my questions."
> 그녀가 내 질문에 답하기를 거부했다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I promised to run with Minseo on Saturday."**
> 토요일에 민서랑 달리기로 약속했다.

- **promise + to**: ~하기로 약속하다

> **"Minseo agreed to run with me."**
> 민서가 같이 달리기로 했다.

- **agree + to**: ~하기로 동의하다

> **"I refused to give up."**
> 포기하지 않았다.

- **refuse + to**: ~하기를 거부하다

## G. would like to vs want to

| Expression | Politeness | Example |
|------------|------------|---------|
| **want to** | 직접적 | I **want to** run tomorrow. |
| **would like to** | 공손함 | I **would like to** join the race. |

### From the Original Book:

> "I'**d like to** go now." (공손함)
> "I **want to** go now." (직접적)

### From the Novel:

> **"What do you want for dinner?"**
> 저녁에 뭐 먹고 싶어?

- 가족 간의 대화에서는 want to 사용

### would like to의 축약형:

| Full | Contracted |
|------|------------|
| I would like to | I'**d like to** |
| Would you like to...? | 'd you like to...? |

## H. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| 틀린 문장 (X) | 올바른 문장 (O) | 설명 |
|---------------|-----------------|------|
| I decided **go**. | I decided **to go**. | to 필요 |
| I want **going**. | I want **to go**. | want + to |
| I hope **seeing** you. | I hope **to see** you. | hope + to |
| I need **rest**. | I need **to rest**. | to 필요 |
| I managed **finish**. | I managed **to finish**. | to 필요 |
| I failed **to arrived**. | I failed **to arrive**. | to + 동사원형 |

### to 누락:

```
(X) I decided go home.
(O) I decided to go home.

to가 반드시 필요!
```

### to + 동사원형:

```
(X) I hope to seeing you.
(O) I hope to see you.

to 다음에는 동사원형!
```

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. I decided (run / to run) every morning.
2. She hopes (finish / to finish) the race.
3. He wants (join / to join) the track team.
4. I need (rest / to rest) before the race.
5. They agreed (meet / to meet) at the park.
6. She refused (give up / to give up).
7. I learned (pace / to pace) myself.
8. He failed (arrive / to arrive) on time.

**Answers:**
1. to run
2. to finish
3. to join
4. to rest
5. to meet
6. to give up
7. to pace
8. to arrive

### B. 문장을 완성하세요.

1. I learned _______ (pace) myself during long runs.
2. She promised _______ (come) to the race.
3. He managed _______ (finish) despite the pain.
4. I failed _______ (hear) my alarm this morning.
5. They planned _______ (leave) at 6 AM.
6. I decided _______ (train) every day.
7. She agreed _______ (help) me.
8. He offered _______ (drive) us to the race.

**Answers:**
1. to pace
2. to come
3. to finish
4. to hear
5. to leave
6. to train
7. to help
8. to drive

### C. want to 또는 would like to를 사용해 문장을 완성하세요.

1. I _______ run a marathon someday. (원해)
2. _______ you _______ come to my race? (공손히 초대)
3. She _______ join the team. (원해)
4. I _______ thank you for your help. (공손히 감사)
5. He _______ rest now. (원해)

**Answers:**
1. want to
2. Would / like to
3. wants to
4. 'd like to / would like to
5. wants to

### D. 틀린 부분을 고치세요.

1. I hope seeing you again.
   → _______________________

2. She decided leave early.
   → _______________________

3. I need resting.
   → _______________________

4. He promised to helped me.
   → _______________________

5. I want going home.
   → _______________________

**Answers:**
1. I hope **to see** you again.
2. She decided **to leave** early.
3. I need **to rest**.
4. He promised **to help** me.
5. I want **to go** home.

## Summary: 동사 + to부정사 정리

### to부정사를 취하는 주요 동사:
```
decide, choose, plan, hope, want, need,
expect, promise, agree, refuse, learn,
offer, manage, fail, afford, seem, pretend
```

### 구조:
```
동사 + to + 동사원형

I decided to go.
I want to run.
I hope to finish.
```

### want to vs would like to:
```
want to: 직접적, 비격식적
would like to: 공손함, 격식적
```

### -ing vs to 비교:
```
-ing 동사: enjoy, finish, stop, avoid, mind...
to 동사: decide, want, hope, need, learn...
```

### 암기 팁:
```
결정/계획: decide, choose, plan
원함/바람: want, need, hope, expect
약속/동의: promise, agree, refuse
성취/실패: manage, fail, learn
```
