# Unit 32: Must, mustn't, needn't

## A. 문법 설명 (Grammar Explanation)

### Must: 의무/필요
"~해야 한다" - 강한 의무

### Mustn't: 금지
"~하면 안 된다" - 하지 말아야 함

### Needn't: 불필요
"~할 필요 없다" - 해도 되고 안 해도 됨

| 표현 | 의미 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| must | ~해야 한다 | You must rest. |
| mustn't | ~하면 안 된다 | You mustn't run. |
| needn't | ~할 필요 없다 | You needn't worry. |

---

## B. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: Must (강한 의무)

> **"You must rest before the race."**
> 대회 전에 꼭 쉬어야 해.

- 필수적인 것

### Example 2: Mustn't (금지)

> **"You mustn't push too hard before the race."**
> 대회 전에 너무 무리하면 안 돼.

- 하지 말아야 할 것

### Example 3: Needn't (불필요)

> **"You needn't run every day. Rest is important too."**
> 매일 달릴 필요는 없어. 휴식도 중요해.

- 필요하지 않음

### Example 4: Needn't have done (과거)

> **"I needn't have worried. The race went well."**
> 걱정할 필요 없었어. 대회 잘 됐으니까.

- 과거에 불필요했던 걱정

### Example 5: Mustn't vs Needn't

> **"You mustn't skip warm-ups. But you needn't do them for 30 minutes."**
> 준비운동은 꼭 해야 해. 하지만 30분씩 할 필요는 없어.

- mustn't = 금지 / needn't = 선택

---

## C. 추가 설명 (Additional Points)

### Mustn't vs Don't have to vs Needn't

| 표현 | 의미 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| mustn't | 금지 | You **mustn't** run. (달리면 안 돼) |
| don't have to | 불필요 | You **don't have to** run. (안 달려도 돼) |
| needn't | 불필요 | You **needn't** run. (달릴 필요 없어) |

**don't have to** = **needn't** (같은 의미)

### Needn't have done: 했지만 필요 없었음

> "I needn't have brought my umbrella. It didn't rain."
> 우산 안 가져와도 됐었어. 비 안 왔거든.

실제로 했지만, 나중에 보니 필요 없었던 일

### Didn't need to vs Needn't have

| Didn't need to | Needn't have |
|----------------|--------------|
| 필요 없어서 안 함 | 했는데 필요 없었음 |
| I didn't need to run. (안 달림) | I needn't have run. (달렸는데 필요 없었음) |

---

## D. 비교 (Comparison)

| 표현 | 의미 | 행동 |
|------|------|------|
| must | 의무 | 해야 함 |
| mustn't | 금지 | 하면 안 됨 |
| needn't | 불필요 | 안 해도 됨 |
| don't have to | 불필요 | 안 해도 됨 |

### 확인 질문과 응답

Q: "Must I run today?"
A: "No, you **needn't**." (필요 없어)
A: "No, you **don't have to**." (안 해도 돼)

**주의**: "No, you mustn't."는 "하면 안 돼"라는 금지의 의미!

## Exercises

### A. Must, Mustn't, Needn't 선택

1. You _______ rest before the marathon. It's important. (의무)
2. You _______ run if your legs hurt. (금지)
3. You _______ buy expensive shoes. Cheap ones work too. (불필요)
4. You _______ skip breakfast on race day. (금지)
5. You _______ wake up at 4 a.m. 5 a.m. is fine. (불필요)

**Answers:**
1. must
2. mustn't
3. needn't / don't have to
4. mustn't
5. needn't / don't have to

### B. 응답 완성

1. "Must I run today?"
   "No, you _______." (필요 없어)

2. "Can I skip the warm-up?"
   "No, you _______." (하면 안 돼)

3. "Should I bring water?"
   "Yes, you _______." (가져와야 해)

**Answers:**
1. needn't / don't have to
2. mustn't
3. must

### C. Needn't have 문장 만들기

1. 우산 가져올 필요 없었어. (비 안 옴)
   → I _______ my umbrella.

2. 걱정할 필요 없었어. (잘 됨)
   → I _______ .

**Answers:**
1. needn't have brought
2. needn't have worried
