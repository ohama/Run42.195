# Unit 4: Present Continuous and Present Simple (2)

## A. 문법 설명 (Grammar Explanation)

### English
Some verbs are **not normally used in the continuous form** (non-action verbs / stative verbs).
These include verbs of **thinking**, **feeling**, **possession**, and **senses**.

### Korean
일부 동사는 **보통 진행형으로 쓰지 않습니다** (상태 동사).
**생각**, **감정**, **소유**, **감각**을 나타내는 동사들이 여기에 해당합니다.

---

## B. 진행형을 쓰지 않는 동사 (Verbs Not Used in Continuous)

### 1. 감정/선호 (Feelings/Preferences)
| Verb | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| like | 좋아하다 | I **like** running. (NOT: I'm liking) |
| love | 사랑하다 | I **love** the morning air. |
| hate | 싫어하다 | I **hate** the bus. |
| want | 원하다 | I **want** to walk. |
| need | 필요하다 | I **need** to rest. |
| prefer | 선호하다 | I **prefer** walking. |

### 2. 인지/사고 (Mental States)
| Verb | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| know | 알다 | I **know** that. |
| understand | 이해하다 | I **understand** now. |
| believe | 믿다 | I **believe** you. |
| remember | 기억하다 | I **remember** the smell. |
| think (의견) | 생각하다 | I **think** it's hard. |
| realize | 깨닫다 | I **realize** that now. |

### 3. 소유 (Possession)
| Verb | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| have (소유) | 가지다 | I **have** a bag. |
| belong | 속하다 | This **belongs** to me. |
| own | 소유하다 | He **owns** a tangerine orchard. |

### 4. 감각 (Senses)
| Verb | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| see | 보다 | I **see** the school. |
| hear | 듣다 | I **hear** birds. |
| smell | 냄새 맡다 | It **smells** like spring. |
| taste | 맛이 나다 | This **tastes** good. |

## C. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: like (감정)

> **"I don't really like running."**
> 나는 달리기를 별로 좋아하지 않는다.

- **like** = 감정 동사 → 진행형 사용 안 함
- NOT: "I'm not really liking running."

### Example 2: know (인지)

> **"I know that."**
> 나도 알아.

- **know** = 인지 동사 → 진행형 사용 안 함
- NOT: "I'm knowing that."

### Example 3: have (소유)

> **"I don't have much stamina."**
> 나는 체력이 별로 없다.

- **have** (소유의 의미) = 진행형 사용 안 함
- NOT: "I'm not having much stamina."

### Example 4: hear (감각)

> **"I heard birds singing."**
> 새가 노래하는 소리가 들렸다.

- **hear** = 감각 동사 → 진행형 사용 안 함
- "I heard" (과거) - 자연스러움
- NOT: "I was hearing birds."

### Example 5: understand (인지)

> **"I understood why I run."**
> 왜 달리는지 이해했다.

- **understand** = 인지 동사
- 진행형 "I was understanding" 사용 안 함

## D. 주의: think의 두 가지 용법

### think = 의견 (believe) → 진행형 안 씀

> **"I think it's hard."**
> 힘들다고 생각해.

- "I think" = "I believe" → Present Simple

### think = 생각하는 행위 → 진행형 가능

> **"I'm thinking about it."**
> 그것에 대해 생각하는 중이야.

- "I'm thinking" = 지금 생각하는 중 → Present Continuous OK

---

## E. 예외: have의 두 가지 용법

### have = 소유 → 진행형 안 씀

> **"I have a bag."**
> 나는 가방이 있다.

### have = 행위 (식사, 경험 등) → 진행형 가능

| Expression | Example |
|------------|---------|
| have breakfast | I'm **having** breakfast now. |
| have fun | We're **having** fun. |
| have a good time | They're **having** a good time. |
| have a shower | I'm **having** a shower. |

## F. 비교표 (Comparison)

| Correct | Incorrect |
|---------|-----------|
| I **like** walking. | ~~I'm liking walking.~~ |
| I **know** the way. | ~~I'm knowing the way.~~ |
| I **want** to run. | ~~I'm wanting to run.~~ |
| I **have** a medal. | ~~I'm having a medal.~~ |
| I **understand** now. | ~~I'm understanding now.~~ |
| I **remember** that day. | ~~I'm remembering that day.~~ |

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. I (don't like / am not liking) the morning bus.
2. He (knows / is knowing) the way to school.
3. I (think / am thinking) running is good. (의견)
4. What (do you think / are you thinking) about? (생각하는 중)
5. She (has / is having) a medal from the race.

**Answers:**
1. don't like
2. knows
3. think
4. are you thinking
5. has

### B. 맞으면 O, 틀리면 X

1. I'm wanting to walk to school. ( )
2. Do you understand the problem? ( )
3. She is knowing the answer. ( )
4. I'm having breakfast now. ( )
5. This food is tasting good. ( )

**Answers:**
1. X (I want to walk)
2. O
3. X (She knows the answer)
4. O (have = 먹다)
5. X (This food tastes good)

### C. 문장 완성하기

1. I _______ (remember) the first day of school.
2. She _______ (not understand) why I walk.
3. We _______ (have) dinner right now.
4. I _______ (not believe) it's already over.
5. He _______ (think) about joining the track team. (생각하는 중)

**Answers:**
1. remember
2. doesn't understand
3. are having
4. don't believe
5. is thinking

### D. 오류 수정

Find and correct the errors:
1. "I'm not believing you."
2. "He is having a car."
3. "Are you understanding me?"
4. "I'm thinking this is wrong." (의견의 의미로)

**Answers:**
1. I don't believe you.
2. He has a car.
3. Do you understand me?
4. I think this is wrong.

## 상태 동사 정리 (Summary of Stative Verbs)

| Category | Verbs |
|----------|-------|
| 감정 | like, love, hate, want, need, prefer |
| 인지 | know, understand, believe, remember, realize |
| 소유 | have (소유), belong, own, possess |
| 감각 | see, hear, smell, taste, feel |
| 기타 | seem, appear, mean, contain |
