# Unit 4: Present Continuous and Present Simple (2)

## A. 상태 동사 개념 (Stative Verbs Concept)

### English
Some verbs are **not normally used in the continuous form** (non-action verbs / stative verbs).
These include verbs of **thinking**, **feeling**, **possession**, and **senses**.

### Korean
일부 동사는 **보통 진행형으로 쓰지 않습니다** (상태 동사).
**생각**, **감정**, **소유**, **감각**을 나타내는 동사들이 여기에 해당합니다.

### From the Original Book:
> "I **know** what you mean." (NOT: ~~I'm knowing~~)
> "Do you **like** London?" (NOT: ~~Are you liking~~)
> "This food **tastes** good." (NOT: ~~is tasting~~)

## B. 진행형을 쓰지 않는 동사 분류 (Categories of Stative Verbs)

### 1. 감정/선호 (Feelings/Preferences)

| Verb | Meaning | Correct Example | Wrong Example |
|------|---------|-----------------|---------------|
| like | 좋아하다 | I **like** running. | ~~I'm liking running.~~ |
| love | 사랑하다 | I **love** the morning air. | ~~I'm loving~~ |
| hate | 싫어하다 | I **hate** the bus. | ~~I'm hating~~ |
| want | 원하다 | I **want** to walk. | ~~I'm wanting~~ |
| need | 필요하다 | I **need** to rest. | ~~I'm needing~~ |
| prefer | 선호하다 | I **prefer** walking. | ~~I'm preferring~~ |

### 2. 인지/사고 (Mental States)

| Verb | Meaning | Correct Example | Wrong Example |
|------|---------|-----------------|---------------|
| know | 알다 | I **know** that. | ~~I'm knowing~~ |
| understand | 이해하다 | I **understand** now. | ~~I'm understanding~~ |
| believe | 믿다 | I **believe** you. | ~~I'm believing~~ |
| remember | 기억하다 | I **remember** the smell. | ~~I'm remembering~~ |
| think (의견) | 생각하다 | I **think** it's hard. | ~~I'm thinking~~ (의견일 때) |
| realize | 깨닫다 | I **realize** that now. | ~~I'm realizing~~ |
| suppose | 추측하다 | I **suppose** so. | ~~I'm supposing~~ |
| mean | 의미하다 | What **does** this **mean**? | ~~is meaning~~ |

### 3. 소유 (Possession)

| Verb | Meaning | Correct Example | Wrong Example |
|------|---------|-----------------|---------------|
| have (소유) | 가지다 | I **have** a bag. | ~~I'm having a bag.~~ |
| belong | 속하다 | This **belongs** to me. | ~~is belonging~~ |
| own | 소유하다 | He **owns** an orchard. | ~~is owning~~ |
| possess | 보유하다 | She **possesses** skill. | ~~is possessing~~ |

### 4. 감각 (Senses)

| Verb | Meaning | Correct Example | Wrong Example |
|------|---------|-----------------|---------------|
| see | 보다 | I **see** the school. | ~~I'm seeing~~ |
| hear | 듣다 | I **hear** birds. | ~~I'm hearing~~ |
| smell | 냄새나다 | It **smells** like spring. | ~~is smelling~~ |
| taste | 맛이 나다 | This **tastes** good. | ~~is tasting~~ |
| feel (상태) | 느끼다 | It **feels** soft. | ~~is feeling~~ |

### 5. 기타 (Others)

| Verb | Meaning | Correct Example |
|------|---------|-----------------|
| seem | ~인 것 같다 | It **seems** difficult. |
| appear | ~처럼 보이다 | He **appears** tired. |
| contain | 포함하다 | This **contains** sugar. |
| consist of | 구성되다 | The team **consists of** 5 people. |
| depend on | 달려 있다 | It **depends on** the weather. |

## C. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: like (감정)

> **"I don't really like running."**
> 나는 달리기를 별로 좋아하지 않는다.

- **like** = 감정 동사 → 진행형 사용 안 함
- NOT: "I'm not really liking running."

### Example 2: know (인지)

> **"I know that."**
> 나도 알아.

- **know** = 인지 동사 → 진행형 사용 안 함
- NOT: "I'm knowing that."

### Example 3: have (소유)

> **"I don't have much stamina."**
> 나는 체력이 별로 없다.

- **have** (소유의 의미) = 진행형 사용 안 함
- NOT: "I'm not having much stamina."

### Example 4: hear (감각)

> **"I heard birds singing."**
> 새가 노래하는 소리가 들렸다.

- **hear** = 감각 동사 → 진행형 사용 안 함
- NOT: "I was hearing birds."

### Example 5: understand (인지)

> **"I understood why I run."**
> 왜 달리는지 이해했다.

- **understand** = 인지 동사
- 진행형 "I was understanding" 사용 안 함

## D. 예외: 두 가지 의미를 가진 동사 (Verbs with Two Meanings)

일부 동사는 **상태**와 **동작** 두 가지 의미가 있습니다.

### Think

| Stative (현재시제) | Action (진행형 가능) |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| I **think** it's good. (의견) | I'**m thinking** about it. (고민 중) |
| "think" = believe (생각하다) | "think" = consider (고민하다) |

### Have

| Stative (현재시제) | Action (진행형 가능) |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| I **have** a car. (소유) | I'**m having** lunch. (먹는 중) |
| I **have** three medals. | I'**m having** a good time. (즐기는 중) |

### Common "have" expressions (진행형 가능):
- have breakfast/lunch/dinner (식사하다)
- have a shower/bath (씻다)
- have fun / a good time (즐기다)
- have a meeting (회의하다)
- have a break (쉬다)

### Taste / Smell

| Stative (현재시제) | Action (진행형 가능) |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| This soup **tastes** good. (맛) | I'**m tasting** the soup. (맛보는 중) |
| It **smells** nice. (냄새) | I'**m smelling** the flower. (냄새 맡는 중) |

### See

| Stative (현재시제) | Action (진행형 가능) |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| I **see** you. (시각) | I'**m seeing** the doctor tomorrow. (만남) |
| I **see** what you mean. (이해) | I'**m seeing** someone. (사귀는 중) |

### Feel

| Stative (현재시제) | Action (진행형 가능) |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| This fabric **feels** soft. (촉감) | I'**m feeling** tired today. (상태 - 둘 다 가능) |
| I **feel** happy. (감정) | I'**m feeling** better now. (변화 - 둘 다 가능) |

## E. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~I'm knowing the answer.~~ | I **know** the answer. | know = 상태동사 |
| ~~She's liking this song.~~ | She **likes** this song. | like = 상태동사 |
| ~~I'm wanting to go.~~ | I **want** to go. | want = 상태동사 |
| ~~I'm having a car.~~ | I **have** a car. | have (소유) = 상태동사 |
| ~~This is tasting good.~~ | This **tastes** good. | taste (맛) = 상태동사 |
| ~~I'm agreeing with you.~~ | I **agree** with you. | agree = 상태동사 |
| ~~Are you understanding?~~ | **Do** you **understand**? | understand = 상태동사 |
| ~~I'm believing you.~~ | I **believe** you. | believe = 상태동사 |

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. I (don't like / am not liking) the morning bus.
2. He (knows / is knowing) the way to school.
3. I (think / am thinking) running is good. (의견)
4. What (do you think / are you thinking) about? (생각하는 중)
5. She (has / is having) a medal from the race.
6. We (have / are having) lunch at the moment.
7. This food (tastes / is tasting) delicious.
8. The chef (tastes / is tasting) the soup right now.

**Answers:**
1. don't like (상태동사)
2. knows (상태동사)
3. think (의견)
4. are you thinking (동작 - 고민 중)
5. has (소유)
6. are having (동작 - 식사 중)
7. tastes (상태 - 맛)
8. is tasting (동작 - 맛보는 중)

### B. 맞으면 O, 틀리면 X

1. I'm wanting to walk to school. ( )
2. Do you understand the problem? ( )
3. She is knowing the answer. ( )
4. I'm having breakfast now. ( )
5. This food is tasting good. ( )
6. I believe you. ( )
7. He's seeing the doctor tomorrow. ( )

**Answers:**
1. X (I want to walk)

3. X (She knows the answer)

**Answers:**

2. doesn't understand

8. are having

### D. 오류 수정 (Error Correction)

Find and correct the errors:

1. "I'm not believing you."
   → I **don't believe** you.

2. "He is having a car."
   → He **has** a car.

3. "Are you understanding me?"
   → **Do** you **understand** me?

4. "I'm thinking this is wrong." (의견의 의미로)
   → I **think** this is wrong.

5. "She is seeming tired today."
   → She **seems** tired today.

6. "This perfume is smelling nice."
   → This perfume **smells** nice.

## Summary: Stative Verbs Categories

| Category | Verbs | Korean |
|----------|-------|--------|
| 감정/선호 | like, love, hate, want, need, prefer | 좋아하다, 사랑하다, 싫어하다, 원하다, 필요하다, 선호하다 |
| 인지/사고 | know, understand, believe, remember, realize, think(의견), mean | 알다, 이해하다, 믿다, 기억하다, 깨닫다, 생각하다, 의미하다 |
| 소유 | have(소유), belong, own, possess | 가지다, 속하다, 소유하다, 보유하다 |
| 감각 | see, hear, smell, taste, feel | 보다, 듣다, 냄새나다, 맛나다, 느끼다 |
| 기타 | seem, appear, contain, consist of, depend on, agree | ~처럼 보이다, 포함하다, 구성되다, 달려있다, 동의하다 |

| Verb | State (Simple) | Action (Continuous) |
|------|---------------|---------------------|
| think | I think so. (의견) | I'm thinking about it. (고민) |
| have | I have a car. (소유) | I'm having dinner. (식사) |
| taste | It tastes good. (맛) | I'm tasting the soup. (맛보기) |
| smell | It smells nice. (냄새) | I'm smelling the flowers. (냄새 맡기) |
| see | I see you. (시각) | I'm seeing the doctor. (만남) |
| feel | It feels soft. (촉감) | I'm feeling better. (변화) |
