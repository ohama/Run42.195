# Unit 129: Noun + Preposition (reason for, cause of, etc.)

## A. 형태 (Form/Structure)

특정 명사들은 특정 전치사와 함께 사용됩니다. 이러한 조합은 암기가 필요합니다.

### Structure:
```
Noun + preposition + noun/-ing
```

| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
| reason **for** | the reason **for** my success |
| cause **of** | the cause **of** the problem |
| increase **in** | an increase **in** stamina |
| solution **to** | the solution **to** the problem |
| relationship **with** | my relationship **with** Minseo |

---

## B. Noun + for

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| reason for | ~의 이유 | the reason **for** my success |
| need for | ~의 필요성 | the need **for** training |
| demand for | ~에 대한 수요 | demand **for** running shoes |
| application for | ~에 대한 지원 | application **for** the race |
| responsibility for | ~에 대한 책임 | responsibility **for** my training |
| check for | ~을 위한 점검 | check **for** injuries |
| cure for | ~의 치료법 | a cure **for** the disease |
| recipe for | ~의 레시피 | a recipe **for** success |
| request for | ~에 대한 요청 | request **for** help |

### From the Original Book:
> "What was the **reason for** your decision?"
> 결정의 이유가 뭐였어?

> "There is an urgent **need for** change."
> 변화가 절실히 필요하다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The reason for my improvement was daily practice."**
> 내 발전의 이유는 매일의 연습이었다.

> **"I felt the need for better running shoes."**
> 더 좋은 러닝화가 필요하다고 느꼈다.

> **"I took responsibility for my own training."**
> 내 훈련에 대한 책임을 졌다.

## C. Noun + of

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| cause of | ~의 원인 | the cause **of** my pain |
| advantage of | ~의 장점 | the advantage **of** running |
| disadvantage of | ~의 단점 | the disadvantage **of** buses |
| picture/photo of | ~의 사진 | a picture **of** the finish line |
| example of | ~의 예 | an example **of** hard work |
| risk of | ~의 위험 | the risk **of** injury |
| possibility of | ~의 가능성 | the possibility **of** winning |
| fear of | ~에 대한 두려움 | fear **of** failure |
| lack of | ~의 부족 | lack **of** sleep |

### From the Original Book:
> "What was the **cause of** the accident?"
> 사고의 원인이 뭐였어?

> "One of the **advantages of** living alone is..."
> 혼자 사는 것의 장점 중 하나는...

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The cause of my motion sickness was the crowded bus."**
> 내 멀미의 원인은 붐비는 버스였다.

> **"The advantage of walking is that you see more."**
> 걷기의 장점은 더 많이 볼 수 있다는 것이다.

> **"Doyun is an example of determination."**
> 도윤이는 결단력의 예시다.

## D. Noun + in

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| increase in | ~의 증가 | an increase **in** stamina |
| decrease in | ~의 감소 | a decrease **in** pain |
| rise in | ~의 상승 | a rise **in** temperature |
| fall in | ~의 하락 | a fall **in** prices |
| interest in | ~에 대한 관심 | interest **in** running |
| change in | ~의 변화 | a change **in** routine |
| success in | ~의 성공 | success **in** the race |
| belief in | ~에 대한 믿음 | belief **in** myself |
| difference in | ~의 차이 | a difference **in** speed |

### From the Original Book:
> "There has been an **increase in** road accidents."
> 교통사고가 증가했다.

> "I've lost **interest in** football."
> 축구에 대한 관심을 잃었다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"There was an increase in my stamina."**
> 체력이 증가했다.

> **"I noticed a change in my body."**
> 몸의 변화를 느꼈다.

> **"Dad showed interest in my running."**
> 아빠가 내 달리기에 관심을 보이셨다.

## E. Noun + to

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| answer to | ~에 대한 답 | the answer **to** the question |
| solution to | ~에 대한 해결책 | a solution **to** the problem |
| key to | ~의 열쇠/비결 | the key **to** success |
| reaction to | ~에 대한 반응 | my reaction **to** the news |
| damage to | ~에 대한 피해 | damage **to** my legs |
| invitation to | ~에 대한 초대 | invitation **to** the party |
| attitude to/toward | ~에 대한 태도 | attitude **to** running |
| reply to | ~에 대한 답장 | reply **to** the email |
| threat to | ~에 대한 위협 | a threat **to** health |

### From the Original Book:
> "Do you know the **answer to** this question?"
> 이 질문의 답을 아세요?

> "The **key to** success is hard work."
> 성공의 비결은 노력이다.

### 주의: "to" vs "of"
- solution **to** (O) / solution of (X)
- answer **to** (O) / answer of (X)
- key **to** (O) / key of (X)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Walking was the solution to my bus sickness."**
> 걷기가 내 버스 멀미의 해결책이었다.

> **"The key to finishing was not giving up."**
> 완주의 비결은 포기하지 않는 것이었다.

> **"I was worried about damage to my legs."**
> 다리 손상이 걱정되었다.

## F. Noun + with

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| relationship with | ~와의 관계 | my relationship **with** Minseo |
| connection with | ~와의 연결 | connection **with** nature |
| problem with | ~의 문제 | a problem **with** my legs |
| contact with | ~와의 접촉 | contact **with** the ground |
| trouble with | ~의 문제 | trouble **with** the bus |
| difficulty with | ~의 어려움 | difficulty **with** pacing |

### From the Original Book:
> "Do you have a good **relationship with** your parents?"
> 부모님과 관계가 좋으세요?

> "I have a **problem with** my phone."
> 폰에 문제가 있어.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"My relationship with Minseo grew stronger."**
> 민서와의 관계가 더 가까워졌다.

> **"I felt a connection with nature while running."**
> 달리면서 자연과의 연결을 느꼈다.

## G. Noun + between

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| difference between | ~의 차이 | the difference **between** A and B |
| connection between | ~간의 연결 | connection **between** effort and success |
| relationship between | ~간의 관계 | relationship **between** two things |

### From the Original Book:
> "What's the **difference between** 'speak' and 'talk'?"
> 'speak'와 'talk'의 차이가 뭐야?

### From the Novel:

> **"I learned the difference between being forced and wanting to."**
> 강제와 원하는 것의 차이를 배웠다.

## H. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~the reason of~~ | **the reason for** | reason은 for와 함께 |
| ~~the answer of~~ | **the answer to** | answer는 to와 함께 |
| ~~the solution of~~ | **the solution to** | solution은 to와 함께 |
| ~~the key of success~~ | **the key to success** | key는 to와 함께 |
| ~~increase of~~ | **increase in** | increase는 in과 함께 |
| ~~interest for~~ | **interest in** | interest는 in과 함께 |
| ~~problem of~~ | **problem with** | problem은 with과 함께 |
| ~~difference of~~ | **difference between** | difference는 between과 함께 |

### More Common Errors:

> ~~What's the reason of your absence?~~
> ✓ What's the **reason for** your absence?

> ~~There was an increase of sales.~~
> ✓ There was an **increase in** sales.

> ~~He has no interest for running.~~
> ✓ He has no **interest in** running.

### A. 알맞은 전치사 고르기 (Choose the correct preposition)

for, of, in, to, with, between 중에서 선택하세요.

1. The reason _______ my success was hard work.
2. The cause _______ the problem was clear.
3. There was an increase _______ my speed.
4. Walking was the solution _______ my problem.
5. I have a good relationship _______ my coach.
6. The key _______ finishing is perseverance.
7. What's the difference _______ running and jogging?
8. I noticed a change _______ my body.

**Answers:**
1. for
2. of
3. in
4. to
5. with
6. to
7. between
8. in

---

### B. 틀린 부분 고치기 (Error Correction)

1. What's the reason of your decision?
2. There was an increase of traffic.
3. He has no interest for sports.
4. The solution of the problem is simple.
5. What's the difference of A and B?

**Answers:**
1. What's the reason **for** your decision?
2. There was an increase **in** traffic.
3. He has no interest **in** sports.
4. The solution **to** the problem is simple.
5. What's the difference **between** A and B?

## Noun + Preposition 요약표 (Summary Table)

| Preposition | Nouns |
|-------------|-------|
| **for** | reason, need, demand, responsibility, application, cure, request |
| **of** | cause, advantage, disadvantage, example, picture, risk, fear, lack |
| **in** | increase, decrease, rise, fall, change, interest, success, belief |
| **to** | answer, solution, key, reaction, damage, invitation, attitude, reply |
| **with** | relationship, connection, problem, contact, trouble, difficulty |
| **between** | difference, connection, relationship |

---

## 전치사 선택 팁 (Tips for Choosing Prepositions)

| Pattern | Remember |
|---------|----------|
| 이유/원인 | reason **for**, cause **of** |
| 변화/증감 | increase/decrease **in** |
| 해결책/답 | solution/answer/key **to** |
| 관계/문제 | relationship/problem **with** |
| 비교/차이 | difference **between** |
