# Unit 16: Past Perfect Continuous (I had been doing)

## A. 과거완료진행형의 형태 (Form)

**had been + -ing**

| Subject | Had been | -ing | Full Example |
|---------|----------|------|--------------|
| I/You/He/She/It/We/They | had been | running | I had been running. |

### Negative and Questions:

| Type | Structure | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Positive | had been + -ing | I had been running. |
| Negative | hadn't been + -ing | I hadn't been running. |
| Question | Had + subject + been + -ing? | Had you been running? |

### Contractions:
- I had been → I'd been
- had not been → hadn't been

---

## B. 기본 개념 (Basic Concept)

**"I had been doing something" = An activity was in progress BEFORE another past event.**

과거완료진행형은:
1. **과거 사건 이전에 진행 중이던 활동**
2. **과거의 어떤 일이 일어나기 전까지 계속되던 활동**

과거 시점까지의 **기간**과 **활동**을 강조합니다.

### From the Original Book:
> "I was very tired when I arrived home. I **had been working** hard all day."
> 집에 도착했을 때 매우 피곤했다. 하루 종일 열심히 일했기 때문이다.

---

## C. 시간 순서 이해하기 (Timeline)

```
더 이전 ──────────── 과거 ─────────── 현재
   |__________________|                |
   (진행 중이던 활동)    (결과/중단점)
   had been doing       Past moment
```

### Example:
```
Running for 2 hours ───────> Got tired (Past)     Now
|__________________________|                        |
    had been running         was tired
```

> "I was tired. I **had been running** for two hours."

---

## D. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: 과거 시점까지 계속된 활동

> **"I was tired. I had been running for over an hour."**
> 피곤했다. 한 시간 넘게 달려왔기 때문이다.

- **was tired** = 과거의 상태 (결과)
- **had been running** = 그 전까지 계속 달리고 있었음 (원인)

### Example 2: 대회 전까지의 준비

> **"I had been training for a whole year before the marathon."**
> 마라톤 전에 1년 동안 훈련해 왔다.

- 마라톤 (과거 시점)
- 1년간의 훈련 (그 전까지 계속)

### Example 3: 결과의 원인

> **"I was sweating because I had been running in the sun."**
> 햇볕 아래서 달려와서 땀이 났다.

- **was sweating** = 과거의 결과
- **had been running** = 그 결과의 원인 (진행 중이던 활동)

### Example 4: 기간 강조

> **"By the time I finished, I had been running for over 4 hours."**
> 끝났을 때, 4시간 넘게 달리고 있었다.

- **finished** = 완주 시점 (과거)
- **had been running** = 그 시점까지 계속된 활동

### Example 5: 행동의 지속과 변화

> **"I had been walking to school, but that day I decided to run."**
> 걸어서 학교에 다니고 있었는데, 그날 뛰기로 결심했다.

- **had been walking** = 그때까지 계속 걸어다님
- **decided** = 변화의 시점

## E. Past Perfect Continuous vs Past Perfect Simple

| Aspect | Past Perfect Continuous | Past Perfect Simple |
|--------|------------------------|-------------------|
| Focus | 활동/과정 강조 | 완료/결과 강조 |
| Form | had been + -ing | had + p.p. |
| Question | How long...? | How many/much...? |
| Emphasis | 기간/지속 | 완료된 양/횟수 |

### 비교 예문:

> **"I was tired. I had been running for two hours."** (과정)
> 피곤했다. 두 시간 동안 달렸기 때문이다. (기간 강조)

> **"I was proud. I had run 42 kilometers."** (결과)
> 자랑스러웠다. 42km를 달렸기 때문이다. (완료된 양)

---

## F. 과거완료진행형을 쓰는 상황 (When to Use)

### 1. 과거 시점에 피곤함/증거의 원인:

> **"My legs were sore. I had been walking all day."**
> 다리가 아팠다. 하루 종일 걸었기 때문이다.

> **"His eyes were red. He had been crying."**
> 그의 눈이 빨갰다. 울고 있었기 때문이다.

### 2. How long + before/when:

> **"How long had you been waiting when the bus arrived?"**
> 버스가 왔을 때 얼마나 오래 기다렸어?

### 3. 과거까지의 계속된 활동:

> **"I had been thinking about the race for weeks."**
> 몇 주 동안 대회에 대해 생각해 왔었다.

### 4. For/Since + 과거완료진행형:

> **"I had been training for six months before I ran my first 10km."**
> 첫 10km를 뛰기 전에 6개월 동안 훈련했었다.

---

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~I had been run for hours.~~ | I **had been running** for hours. | -ing 형태 필요 |
| ~~She had being waiting.~~ | She **had been waiting**. | had been 사용 |
| ~~I had been knowing him.~~ | I **had known** him. | know = 상태동사 |
| ~~I was tired. I had run.~~ | I was tired. I **had been running**. | 과정 강조 시 진행형 |
| ~~He had been finished.~~ | He **had finished**. | finish = 완료 동사 |

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. I was tired because I (had run / had been running) all morning.
2. She (had trained / had been training) for months before the race.
3. By noon, I (had walked / had been walking) for three hours.
4. I was sweating. I (had run / had been running) in the heat.
5. When I stopped, I (had run / had been running) 7km.
6. His eyes were red. He (had cried / had been crying).
7. I (had waited / had been waiting) for an hour when the bus came.
8. She was exhausted. She (had worked / had been working) all day.

**Answers:**
1. had been running (피곤함의 원인 = 과정)
2. had been training (기간)
3. had been walking (기간)
4. had been running (땀의 원인 = 과정)
5. had run (완료된 거리) / had been running (과정) - 둘 다 가능
6. had been crying (증거 = 과정)
7. had been waiting (기간)
8. had been working (피곤함의 원인)

### B. 문장 완성하기

1. My legs hurt. I _______ (walk) for over an hour.
2. I was out of breath. I _______ (run) fast.
3. By the time I finished, I _______ (train) for a year.
4. How long _______ you _______ (wait) when the bus came?
5. She was exhausted. She _______ (run) since dawn.
6. His clothes were dirty. He _______ (work) in the garden.
7. I _______ (think) about the race for weeks before it happened.
8. They were hungry. They _______ (walk) all day without eating.

**Answers:**
1. had been walking
2. had been running
3. had been training
4. had ... been waiting
5. had been running
6. had been working
7. had been thinking
8. had been walking

### C. 원인과 결과 연결하기

Match the result with the activity:

| Result (Past) | Cause (Past Perfect Continuous) |
|---------------|--------------------------------|
| 1. I was exhausted. | a. I had been training for months. |
| 2. My shoes were worn out. | b. I had been running in the rain. |
| 3. I was soaking wet. | c. I had been running every day. |
| 4. I felt confident. | d. I had been running for 4 hours. |
| 5. The ground was wet. | e. It had been raining. |

**Answers:**
1-d, 2-c, 3-b, 4-a, 5-e

### D. 문장 변환

Change to Past Perfect Continuous:

1. I ran for two hours. I was tired.
   → I was tired because I **had been running** for two hours.

2. She trained for months. Then the race started.
   → She **had been training** for months before the race started.

3. I waited for an hour. Then the bus came.
   → I **had been waiting** for an hour when the bus came.

4. It rained all morning. The streets were wet.
   → The streets were wet because it **had been raining** all morning.

5. He worked all day. He was exhausted.
   → He was exhausted because he **had been working** all day.

## Summary: 과거 시제 비교

| Tense | Example | Focus |
|-------|---------|-------|
| Past Simple | I ran for 2 hours. | 단순 과거 사실 |
| Past Continuous | I was running at 7 AM. | 특정 시점의 진행 |
| Past Perfect | I had run 10km. | 과거 이전의 완료 |
| Past Perfect Continuous | I had been running for 2 hours. | 과거 이전까지의 진행/기간 |

### When to Use Each:

| Situation | Tense | Example |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| 과거 완료된 동작 | Past Simple | I finished the race. |
| 과거 특정 시점 진행 | Past Continuous | I was running at 8 AM. |
| 과거 이전 완료 | Past Perfect | I had finished before he arrived. |
| 과거 이전 진행/기간 | Past Perfect Continuous | I had been running for 2 hours. |
