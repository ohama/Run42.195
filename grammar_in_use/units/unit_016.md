# Unit 16: Past Perfect Continuous (I had been doing)

## A. 문법 설명 (Grammar Explanation)

### English
**Past Perfect Continuous** (had been + -ing) describes an activity that:
1. **Was in progress before a past event**
2. **Had been continuing for some time before something happened**

It emphasizes the **duration** and **activity** leading up to a past moment.

### Korean
**과거완료진행형** (had been + -ing)은:
1. **과거 사건 이전에 진행 중이던 활동**
2. **과거의 어떤 일이 일어나기 전까지 계속되던 활동**

과거 시점까지의 **기간**과 **활동**을 강조합니다.

---

## B. 과거완료진행형의 형태 (Form)

| Subject | Had been | -ing |
|---------|----------|------|
| I/You/He/She/It/We/They | had been | running, walking, training |

**축약형:** I had been → I'd been
**부정문:** hadn't been + -ing
**의문문:** Had + 주어 + been + -ing?

---

## C. 시간 순서 이해하기 (Timeline)

```
더 이전 ──────────── 과거 ─────────── 현재
   |__________________|                |
   (진행 중이던 활동)    (결과/중단점)
   had been doing       과거 시점
```

---

## D. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: 과거 시점까지 계속된 활동

> **"I was tired. I had been running for over an hour."**
> 피곤했다. 한 시간 넘게 달려왔기 때문이다.

- **was tired** = 과거의 상태
- **had been running** = 그 전까지 계속 달리고 있었음

### Example 2: 대회 전까지의 준비

> **"I had been training for a whole year before the marathon."**
> 마라톤 전에 1년 동안 훈련해 왔다.

- 마라톤 (과거 시점)
- 1년간의 훈련 (그 전까지 계속)

### Example 3: 결과의 원인

> **"I was sweating because I had been running in the sun."**
> 햇볕 아래서 달려와서 땀이 났다.

- **was sweating** = 과거의 결과
- **had been running** = 그 결과의 원인 (진행 중이던 활동)

### Example 4: 기간 강조

> **"By the time I finished, I had been running for over 4 hours."**
> 끝났을 때, 4시간 넘게 달리고 있었다.

- **finished** = 완주 시점 (과거)
- **had been running** = 그 시점까지 계속된 활동

### Example 5: 행동의 지속과 변화

> **"I had been walking to school, but that day I decided to run."**
> 걸어서 학교에 다니고 있었는데, 그날 뛰기로 결심했다.

- **had been walking** = 그때까지 계속 걸어다님
- **decided** = 변화의 시점

## E. Past Perfect Continuous vs Past Perfect Simple

| Past Perfect Continuous | Past Perfect Simple |
|------------------------|-------------------|
| 활동/과정 강조 | 완료/결과 강조 |
| I had been running. | I had run 10km. |
| 기간 (how long) | 양/횟수 |
| 최근까지 진행 중 | 완료된 상태 |

### 비교 예문:

> **"I was tired. I had been running for two hours."** (과정)
> 피곤했다. 두 시간 동안 달렸기 때문이다.

> **"I was proud. I had run 42 kilometers."** (결과)
> 자랑스러웠다. 42km를 달렸기 때문이다.

---

## F. 과거완료진행형을 쓰는 상황

### 1. 과거 시점에 피곤함/증거의 원인:

> **"My legs were sore. I had been walking all day."**
> 다리가 아팠다. 하루 종일 걸었기 때문이다.

### 2. How long + before/when:

> **"How long had you been waiting when the bus arrived?"**
> 버스가 왔을 때 얼마나 오래 기다렸어?

### 3. 과거까지의 계속된 활동:

> **"I had been thinking about the race for weeks."**
> 몇 주 동안 대회에 대해 생각해 왔었다.

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. I was tired because I (had run / had been running) all morning.
2. She (had trained / had been training) for months before the race.
3. By noon, I (had walked / had been walking) for three hours.
4. I was sweating. I (had run / had been running) in the heat.
5. When I stopped, I (had run / had been running) 7km.

**Answers:**
1. had been running (피곤함의 원인 = 과정)
2. had been training (기간)
3. had been walking (기간)
4. had been running (땀의 원인 = 과정)
5. had run (완료된 거리) / had been running (과정) - 둘 다 가능

### B. 문장 완성하기

1. My legs hurt. I _______ (walk) for over an hour.
2. I was out of breath. I _______ (run) fast.
3. By the time I finished, I _______ (train) for a year.
4. How long _______ you _______ (wait) when the bus came?
5. She was exhausted. She _______ (run) since dawn.

**Answers:**
1. had been walking
2. had been running
3. had been training
4. had ... been waiting
5. had been running

### C. 원인과 결과 연결하기

Match the result with the activity:

| Result (Past Simple) | Cause (Past Perfect Continuous) |
|---------------------|--------------------------------|
| 1. I was exhausted. | a. I had been training for months. |
| 2. My shoes were worn out. | b. I had been running in the rain. |
| 3. I was soaking wet. | c. I had been running every day. |
| 4. I felt confident. | d. I had been running for 4 hours. |

**Answers:**
1-d, 2-c, 3-b, 4-a

### D. 문장 변환

Change to Past Perfect Continuous:

1. I ran for two hours. I was tired.
   → I was tired because I _______ for two hours.

2. She trained for months. Then the race started.
   → She _______ for months before the race started.

3. I waited for an hour. Then the bus came.
   → I _______ for an hour when the bus came.

**Answers:**
1. had been running
2. had been training
3. had been waiting

## 정리: 과거완료진행형 vs 다른 시제

| Tense | Example | Focus |
|-------|---------|-------|
| Past Simple | I ran for 2 hours. | 단순 과거 사실 |
| Past Continuous | I was running at 7 AM. | 특정 시점의 진행 |
| Past Perfect | I had run 10km. | 과거 이전의 완료 |
| Past Perfect Continuous | I had been running for 2 hours. | 과거 이전까지의 진행 |
