# Unit 83: A friend of mine / my own house / on my own

## A. a friend of mine (이중 소유격)

**a/the + 명사 + of + 소유대명사** 형태를 "이중 소유격"이라고 합니다.

| 소유대명사 | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| mine | a friend of **mine** |
| yours | a friend of **yours** |
| his | a friend of **his** |
| hers | a friend of **hers** |
| ours | a friend of **ours** |
| theirs | a friend of **theirs** |

### 비교:

| a friend of mine | my friend |
|------------------|-----------|
| 내 친구들 중 한 명 | 내 친구 (특정) |
| one of my friends | the specific friend |

---

## B. 소설 속 예문

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Minseo is a friend of mine."**
> 민서는 내 친구야. (여러 친구 중 한 명)

> **"A classmate of mine asked about running."**
> 우리 반 애 중 하나가 달리기에 대해 물었다.

> **"A neighbor of ours has tangerines."**
> 우리 이웃 한 분이 감귤을 키운다.

---

## C. my own: 직접, 나만의

**own**은 소유격(my, your, his, etc.) 뒤에서 **"직접의, 나만의"**를 강조합니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"This was my own choice."**
> 이건 내 스스로의 선택이었다.

- my own = 내 자신의

> **"I did it at my own pace."**
> 내 페이스대로 했다.

- my own pace = 나만의 페이스

> **"I made my own decision."**
> 내 스스로 결정했다.

> **"I ran at my own speed."**
> 내 속도로 달렸다.

---

## D. on my own: 혼자서, 스스로

**on my own** = by myself = 혼자서, 독립적으로

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I walked to school on my own."**
> 혼자 학교까지 걸어갔다.

> **"I trained on my own before joining the team."**
> 팀에 들어가기 전에 혼자 훈련했다.

> **"I learned to run on my own."**
> 혼자서 달리기를 배웠다.

---

## E. 비교: on my own vs by myself vs alone

| Expression | Meaning | Nuance |
|------------|---------|--------|
| on my own | 혼자서, 독립적으로 | 자립/선택 강조 |
| by myself | 혼자서 | 도움 없이 |
| alone | 혼자서 | 사실적 진술 |

### From the Novel:

> **"I walked on my own."** (내 선택으로 혼자)
> **"I walked by myself."** (도움 없이 혼자)
> **"I was alone."** (혼자라는 사실)

---

## F. own의 다른 용법

### 동사로서 own (소유하다):

> **"I own a pair of running shoes."**
> 러닝화 한 켤레가 있다.

### 형용사 + own:

> **"I have my own reasons."**
> 나만의 이유가 있다.

> **"Every runner has their own style."**
> 모든 러너는 자기만의 스타일이 있다.

---

## G. 비교표

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| a friend of mine | 내 친구들 중 한 명 | Minseo is a friend of mine. |
| my friend | 내 친구 (특정) | My friend Minseo is here. |
| my own choice | 내 스스로의 선택 | This was my own choice. |
| on my own | 혼자서, 독립적으로 | I walked on my own. |
| by myself | 혼자서 | I walked by myself. |

## Exercises

### A. 빈칸을 채우세요.

1. Minseo is a friend of _______ (I).
2. I walked at my _______ pace.
3. I did it on my _______.
4. A classmate of _______ (we) asked about the race.
5. This was my _______ decision.

**Answers:**
1. mine
2. own
3. own
4. ours
5. own

### B. 같은 의미끼리 연결하세요.

1. on my own - a) one of my friends
2. a friend of mine - b) by myself
3. my own choice - c) the choice I made myself

**Answers:**
1. b
2. a
3. c

### C. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. (A friend of me / A friend of mine) told me about running.
2. I did it at (my own / mine own) pace.
3. I trained (on my own / on myself).
4. This is (my / mine) own decision.

**Answers:**
1. A friend of mine
2. my own
3. on my own
4. my
