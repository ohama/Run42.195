# Unit 48: Reported Speech (2) - Questions

## A. 의문문의 간접화법 (Reported Questions)

의문문을 간접화법으로 바꿀 때 **어순이 평서문처럼** 바뀝니다.

### From the Original Book:

| 직접화법 | 간접화법 |
|----------|----------|
| "**Where do you** live?" | He asked **where I lived**. |
| "**Are you** nervous?" | He asked **if I was** nervous. |
| "**What time does** the bus come?" | He asked **what time the bus came**. |

### 핵심 변화:
1. 의문문 어순 → 평서문 어순
2. 시제 변화 (한 단계 뒤로)
3. 물음표 없음

---

## B. Yes/No 의문문: if / whether

Yes/No 질문은 **if** 또는 **whether**를 사용합니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "**Are you** married?"
> → He asked **if/whether I was** married.

> "**Did you** go to the party?"
> → She asked **if/whether I had gone** to the party.

> "**Can you** help me?"
> → He asked **if/whether I could** help him.

### 구조:
```
ask + if/whether + 주어 + 동사 (평서문 어순)
```

### Examples from the Novel:

**직접화법:**
> Mom asked, **"Nervous?"**
> 엄마가 "긴장돼?" 라고 물었다.

**간접화법:**
> Mom asked **if/whether** I was nervous.
> 엄마가 긴장되냐고 물었다.

**직접화법:**
> Minseo asked, **"Did I see you this morning?"**
> 민서가 "오늘 아침에 너 봤나?"라고 물었다.

**간접화법:**
> Minseo asked **if/whether** he had seen me that morning.
> 민서가 그날 아침에 나를 봤냐고 물었다.

**직접화법:**
> Dad asked, **"Are you ready?"**
> 아빠가 "준비됐어?"라고 물었다.

**간접화법:**
> Dad asked **if/whether** I was ready.
> 아빠가 준비됐냐고 물었다.

## C. Wh- 의문문: 의문사 유지

Wh- 질문은 **의문사를 그대로** 사용하고 어순만 바꿉니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "**Where** do you live?"
> → He asked **where** I lived.

> "**What** are you doing?"
> → She asked **what** I was doing.

> "**Why** did you leave?"
> → He asked **why** I had left.

### 구조:
```
ask + 의문사 + 주어 + 동사 (평서문 어순)
```

### Examples from the Novel:

**직접화법:**
> Minseo asked, **"Where do you catch the bus?"**
> 민서가 "어디서 버스 타?"라고 물었다.

**간접화법:**
> Minseo asked **where** I caught the bus.
> 민서가 어디서 버스 타냐고 물었다.

**직접화법:**
> Mom asked, **"What do you want for dinner?"**
> 엄마가 "저녁 뭐 먹을래?"라고 물었다.

**간접화법:**
> Mom asked **what** I wanted for dinner.
> 엄마가 저녁에 뭐 먹고 싶냐고 물었다.

**직접화법:**
> The teacher asked, **"How far is your commute?"**
> 선생님이 "통학 거리가 얼마나 되니?"라고 물었다.

**간접화법:**
> The teacher asked **how far** my commute was.
> 선생님이 통학 거리가 얼마나 되냐고 물었다.

## D. 어순 변화 (Word Order)

**직접화법 (의문문 어순):** 조동사/be동사 + 주어
**간접화법 (평서문 어순):** 주어 + 동사

### From the Original Book:

| 직접화법 | 간접화법 |
|----------|----------|
| "Where **does he** live?" | He asked where **he lived**. |
| "What **are you** doing?" | He asked what **I was** doing. |
| "**Is she** coming?" | He asked if **she was** coming. |

### 더 많은 비교:

| 직접화법 (의문문 어순) | 간접화법 (평서문 어순) |
|------------------------|------------------------|
| "Where **is** the bus?" | He asked where the bus **was**. |
| "What **did** you eat?" | He asked what I **had eaten**. |
| "When **will** you arrive?" | He asked when I **would arrive**. |
| "Why **are** you running?" | He asked why I **was running**. |
| "How **does** it work?" | He asked how it **worked**. |

### 주의: 어순 오류가 가장 흔한 실수!

```
(X) He asked where is the bus.
(O) He asked where the bus was.

(X) She asked what am I doing.
(O) She asked what I was doing.
```

## E. Ask vs Wonder vs Want to know

세 가지 모두 간접의문문을 이끌 수 있습니다.

| 표현 | 의미 | 사용 상황 |
|------|------|-----------|
| **ask** | 물었다 | 직접 질문했을 때 |
| **wonder** | 궁금해하다 | 혼자 궁금할 때 |
| **want to know** | 알고 싶다 | 알고 싶을 때 |

### From the Original Book:

> He **asked** where I lived.
> 그가 어디 사냐고 물었다.

> I **wondered** where she lived.
> 나는 그녀가 어디 사는지 궁금했다.

> I **wanted to know** what had happened.
> 나는 무슨 일이 있었는지 알고 싶었다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **I wondered** if I would ever get used to the bus.
> 버스에 익숙해질 수 있을지 궁금했다.

> **I wanted to know** how far the race course was.
> 대회 코스가 얼마나 긴지 알고 싶었다.

> The teacher **asked** if I had been running.
> 선생님이 달리기를 했냐고 물었다.

## F. 명령/요청의 간접화법

명령이나 요청은 **to + 동사원형**으로 바꿉니다.

### From the Original Book:

| 직접화법 | 간접화법 |
|----------|----------|
| "**Run** faster!" | He told me **to run** faster. |
| "**Don't stop**!" | He told me **not to stop**. |
| "**Please help** me." | She asked me **to help** her. |
| "**Don't be** late." | He told me **not to be** late. |

### 구조:
```
긍정 명령: tell/ask + 목적어 + to + 동사원형
부정 명령: tell/ask + 목적어 + not to + 동사원형
```

### Examples from the Novel:

**직접화법:**
> The coach said, **"Don't push too hard."**

**간접화법:**
> The coach told me **not to push** too hard.
> 코치가 너무 무리하지 말라고 했다.

**직접화법:**
> Mom said, **"Come earlier next time."**

**간접화법:**
> Mom told me **to come** earlier next time.
> 엄마가 다음에는 일찍 오라고 했다.

**직접화법:**
> She said, **"Please be careful."**

**간접화법:**
> She asked me **to be** careful.
> 그녀가 조심하라고 부탁했다.

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| 틀린 문장 (X) | 올바른 문장 (O) | 설명 |
|---------------|-----------------|------|
| He asked where **is** the bus. | He asked where the bus **was**. | 평서문 어순 |
| She asked **did I** finish. | She asked **if I had** finished. | if + 평서문 어순 |
| He asked me **where do I** live. | He asked me **where I lived**. | 평서문 어순 |
| She asked **that** I was tired. | She asked **if** I was tired. | that 아닌 if |
| He told me **don't** stop. | He told me **not to** stop. | not to + 동사 |
| She asked to help her. | She asked me **to help** her. | 목적어 필요 |

### 어순 오류 (가장 흔함):

```
직접화법: "Where does he live?"
            (의문문 어순: does + 주어)

(X) He asked where does he live.
(O) He asked where he lived.
            (평서문 어순: 주어 + 동사)
```

### if/whether 누락:

```
(X) He asked I was tired.
(O) He asked if I was tired.

Yes/No 질문 → if/whether 필요
```

## Summary: 간접 의문문 정리

### Yes/No 의문문:
```
ask/wonder/want to know + if/whether + 주어 + 동사 (평서문 어순)

"Are you coming?" → He asked if I was coming.
```

### Wh- 의문문:
```
ask/wonder/want to know + 의문사 + 주어 + 동사 (평서문 어순)

"Where do you live?" → He asked where I lived.
```

### 명령/요청:
```
tell/ask + 목적어 + to + 동사원형 (긍정)
tell/ask + 목적어 + not to + 동사원형 (부정)

"Run faster!" → He told me to run faster.
"Don't stop!" → He told me not to stop.
```

### 핵심 규칙:
```
1. 의문문 어순 → 평서문 어순
2. Yes/No → if/whether 사용
3. 시제 한 단계 뒤로
4. 물음표 없음
```
