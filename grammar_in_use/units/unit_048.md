# Unit 48: Reported Speech (2) - Questions

## A. 의문문의 간접화법

의문문을 간접화법으로 바꿀 때 **어순이 평서문처럼** 바뀝니다.

| 직접화법 | 간접화법 |
|----------|----------|
| "**Are** you nervous?" | He asked if I **was** nervous. |
| "**Do** you run every day?" | He asked if I **ran** every day. |
| "**Where do** you live?" | He asked where I **lived**. |

---

## B. Yes/No 의문문: if / whether

Yes/No 질문은 **if** 또는 **whether**를 사용합니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

**직접화법:**
> Mom asked, **"Nervous?"**
> 엄마가 "긴장돼?" 라고 물었다.

**간접화법:**
> Mom asked **if/whether** I was nervous.
> 엄마가 긴장되냐고 물었다.

**직접화법:**
> Minseo asked, **"Did I see you this morning?"**
> 민서가 "오늘 아침에 너 봤나?"라고 물었다.

**간접화법:**
> Minseo asked **if/whether** he had seen me that morning.
> 민서가 그날 아침에 나를 봤냐고 물었다.

**직접화법:**
> Dad asked, **"Are you ready?"**
> 아빠가 "준비됐어?"라고 물었다.

**간접화법:**
> Dad asked **if/whether** I was ready.
> 아빠가 준비됐냐고 물었다.

---

## C. Wh- 의문문: 의문사 유지

Wh- 질문은 **의문사를 그대로** 사용하고 어순만 바꿉니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

**직접화법:**
> Minseo asked, **"Where do you catch the bus?"**
> 민서가 "어디서 버스 타?"라고 물었다.

**간접화법:**
> Minseo asked **where** I caught the bus.
> 민서가 어디서 버스 타냐고 물었다.

**직접화법:**
> Mom asked, **"What do you want for dinner?"**
> 엄마가 "저녁 뭐 먹을래?"라고 물었다.

**간접화법:**
> Mom asked **what** I wanted for dinner.
> 엄마가 저녁에 뭐 먹고 싶냐고 물었다.

**직접화법:**
> The teacher asked, **"How far is your commute?"**
> 선생님이 "통학 거리가 얼마나 되니?"라고 물었다.

**간접화법:**
> The teacher asked **how far** my commute was.
> 선생님이 통학 거리가 얼마나 되냐고 물었다.

---

## D. 어순 변화 (Word Order)

**직접화법 (의문문 어순):**
> "Where **does** he live?"
> "What **are** you doing?"

**간접화법 (평서문 어순):**
> He asked where **he lived**.
> He asked what **I was** doing.

### 비교:

| 직접화법 | 간접화법 |
|----------|----------|
| "Where **is** the bus?" | He asked where the bus **was**. |
| "What **did** you eat?" | He asked what I **had eaten**. |
| "When **will** you arrive?" | He asked when I **would arrive**. |
| "Why **are** you running?" | He asked why I **was running**. |

---

## E. Ask vs Wonder vs Want to know

세 가지 모두 간접의문문을 이끌 수 있습니다.

| 표현 | 의미 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| ask | 물었다 | He **asked** where I lived. |
| wonder | 궁금해하다 | I **wondered** where he lived. |
| want to know | 알고 싶다 | I **wanted to know** where he lived. |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **I wondered** if I would ever get used to the bus.
> 버스에 익숙해질 수 있을지 궁금했다.

> **I wanted to know** how far the race course was.
> 대회 코스가 얼마나 긴지 알고 싶었다.

> The teacher **asked** if I had been running.
> 선생님이 달리기를 했냐고 물었다.

---

## F. 명령/요청의 간접화법

명령이나 요청은 **to + 동사원형**으로 바꿉니다.

| 직접화법 | 간접화법 |
|----------|----------|
| "Run faster!" | He told me **to run** faster. |
| "Don't stop!" | He told me **not to stop**. |
| "Please help me." | She asked me **to help** her. |

### Examples from the Novel:

**직접화법:**
> The coach said, **"Don't push too hard."**

**간접화법:**
> The coach told me **not to push** too hard.
> 코치가 너무 무리하지 말라고 했다.

**직접화법:**
> Mom said, **"Come earlier next time."**

**간접화법:**
> Mom told me **to come** earlier next time.
> 엄마가 다음에는 일찍 오라고 했다.

## Exercises

### A. Yes/No 의문문을 간접화법으로 바꾸세요.

1. "Are you nervous?" → He asked _______________________.
2. "Did you miss the bus?" → She asked _______________________.
3. "Will you run tomorrow?" → He asked _______________________.
4. "Can you help me?" → She asked _______________________.

**Answers:**
1. if/whether I was nervous
2. if/whether I had missed the bus
3. if/whether I would run the next day
4. if/whether I could help her

### B. Wh- 의문문을 간접화법으로 바꾸세요.

1. "Where do you live?" → He asked _______________________.
2. "What are you doing?" → She asked _______________________.
3. "When did you start running?" → He asked _______________________.
4. "Why are you tired?" → She asked _______________________.
5. "How far is the school?" → He asked _______________________.

**Answers:**
1. where I lived
2. what I was doing
3. when I had started running
4. why I was tired
5. how far the school was

### C. 명령문을 간접화법으로 바꾸세요.

1. "Run faster!" → He told me _______________________.
2. "Don't stop!" → She told me _______________________.
3. "Please help me." → He asked me _______________________.
4. "Be careful." → She told me _______________________.
5. "Don't give up!" → He told me _______________________.

**Answers:**
1. to run faster
2. not to stop
3. to help him
4. to be careful
5. not to give up

### D. 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. He asked (where I lived / where did I live).
2. She asked (if was I tired / if I was tired).
3. I wondered (what he was doing / what was he doing).
4. The teacher asked (why I was late / why was I late).

**Answers:**
1. where I lived
2. if I was tired
3. what he was doing
4. why I was late

| "Where do you catch the bus?" | He asked where I caught the bus. |
| "What do you want for dinner?" | She asked what I wanted for dinner. |

## Summary

```
Yes/No 의문문:
ask/wonder/want to know + if/whether + 주어 + 동사

Wh- 의문문:
ask/wonder/want to know + 의문사 + 주어 + 동사
(평서문 어순)

명령/요청:
tell/ask + 목적어 + to + 동사원형
tell/ask + 목적어 + not to + 동사원형
```
