# Unit 93: Relative Clauses (2) - Clauses with and without who/that/which

## A. 관계대명사 생략 (Omitting relative pronouns)

관계대명사가 **목적어**일 때는 생략할 수 있습니다. 하지만 **주어**일 때는 생략할 수 없습니다.

When the relative pronoun is the **object** of the relative clause, we can leave it out. When it's the **subject**, we cannot omit it.

### Summary Table:

| 역할 | 생략 가능? | 예시 |
|------|-----------|------|
| 주어 (Subject) | 불가능 | The bus **that comes** at 7:30... |
| 목적어 (Object) | 가능 | The medal (that) **I received**... |

### From the Original Book:

> "The woman **who** lives next door is a doctor." (subject - cannot omit)
> "The woman **(that/who)** I wanted to see was away." (object - can omit)

---

## B. 목적어일 때 - 생략 가능 (Object - can be omitted)

관계대명사 뒤에 **주어 + 동사**가 오면, 관계대명사는 목적어이므로 생략 가능합니다.

### Pattern Recognition:

```
관계대명사 + 주어 + 동사 → 목적어 역할 → 생략 가능!

The medal (that) I received
             ↑    ↑    ↑
       [생략가능] [주어] [동사]
```

### From the Original Book:

> "Did you find the keys (that) you lost?"
> 잃어버린 열쇠 찾았어?

> "The film (which) we saw was very good."
> 우리가 본 영화는 아주 좋았다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The medal (that) I received was heavy."**
> 내가 받은 메달은 무거웠다.

- "I received" = 주어 + 동사 → that은 목적어 → 생략 가능

> **"The running shoes (which) I bought this weekend are comfortable."**
> 이번 주말에 내가 산 런닝화는 편하다.

> **"The route (that) I run every day passes through tangerine orchards."**
> 내가 매일 달리는 길은 귤밭을 지나간다.

> **"The friend (who) I met on the first day was Minseo."**
> 첫날 내가 만난 친구는 민서였다.

> **"The candy (that) Minseo gave me helped with nausea."**
> 민서가 준 사탕이 멀미에 도움이 됐다.

> **"The coach (whom) the school hired helped me a lot."**
> 학교에서 고용한 코치가 나를 많이 도와주었다.

## C. 주어일 때 - 생략 불가 (Subject - cannot be omitted)

관계대명사 뒤에 바로 **동사**가 오면, 관계대명사는 주어이므로 생략할 수 없습니다.

### Pattern Recognition:

```
관계대명사 + 동사 → 주어 역할 → 생략 불가!

The bus that comes at 7:30
         ↑    ↑
    [생략불가] [동사]
```

### From the Original Book:

> "I met a woman **who** works in television." (who = subject, cannot omit)
> TV에서 일하는 여자를 만났다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The bus that comes at 7:30 is always packed."**
> 7시 30분에 오는 버스는 항상 만원이다.

- "comes" = 바로 동사 → that은 주어 → 생략 불가
- (WRONG: ~~The bus comes at 7:30 is always packed.~~)

> **"The kid who hated exercise became a runner."**
> 운동을 싫어하던 그 아이가 달리기 선수가 되었다.

- (WRONG: ~~The kid hated exercise became a runner.~~)

> **"The grandfather who works at the tangerine orchard always encourages me."**
> 귤밭에서 일하시는 할아버지가 항상 나를 격려해 주신다.

> **"The teacher who noticed my improvement was impressed."**
> 내 발전을 눈여겨본 선생님이 감동하셨다.

## D. 비교: 생략 가능 vs 불가능

### Comparison Table:

| 문장 | 관계대명사 역할 | 생략 | 판별 방법 |
|------|-----------------|------|-----------|
| The medal **that** I received | 목적어 | 가능 | that 뒤에 주어(I) |
| The medal **that** was heavy | 주어 | 불가능 | that 뒤에 동사(was) |
| The friend **who** I met | 목적어 | 가능 | who 뒤에 주어(I) |
| The friend **who** helped me | 주어 | 불가능 | who 뒤에 동사(helped) |
| The bus **that** he takes | 목적어 | 가능 | that 뒤에 주어(he) |
| The bus **that** arrives early | 주어 | 불가능 | that 뒤에 동사(arrives) |

### Side-by-side Examples:

| With relative pronoun | Without (if possible) |
|-----------------------|-----------------------|
| The shoes **that** I bought | The shoes I bought ✓ |
| The bus **that** comes at 7:30 | ~~The bus comes at 7:30~~ ✗ |
| The teacher **who** I met | The teacher I met ✓ |
| The teacher **who** helped me | ~~The teacher helped me~~ ✗ |
| The candy **that** she gave me | The candy she gave me ✓ |
| The candy **that** tastes good | ~~The candy tastes good~~ ✗ |

## E. 구어체와 문어체 (Spoken vs Written)

### Usage Patterns:

| 상황 | 경향 | 예시 |
|------|------|------|
| 구어체 (Spoken) | 목적어일 때 주로 생략 | The shoes I bought |
| 문어체 (Written) | 생략하거나 유지 모두 가능 | The shoes (which) I bought |
| 격식체 (Formal) | 유지하는 경향 | The items which we discussed |

### From the Novel:

**Casual/Spoken style (생략):**
> "The shoes I bought are great."
> 내가 산 신발 좋아.

**More formal style (유지):**
> "The shoes which I bought are of excellent quality."
> 제가 구매한 신발은 품질이 아주 좋습니다.

### From the Original Book:

> "In spoken English, we often leave out **who/that/which** when it is the object."

## F. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | 설명 |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| ~~The bus comes at 7:30 is packed~~ | The bus **that** comes at 7:30 is packed | 주어 역할 관계대명사 생략 불가 |
| ~~The man helped me was kind~~ | The man **who** helped me was kind | 주어 역할 관계대명사 필수 |
| ~~I met the girl works here~~ | I met the girl **who** works here | 주어 역할 관계대명사 필수 |
| ~~The book I reading is good~~ | The book I **am** reading is good | 동사 형태 오류 |
| ~~The shoes that I bought them~~ | The shoes that I bought | 목적어 반복 금지 |

### 특히 주의할 점:

**1. 주어 역할일 때 반드시 관계대명사 필요:**
```
✗ The woman lives next door is a doctor.
✓ The woman WHO lives next door is a doctor.
```

**2. 목적어 역할 + 관계대명사 생략 시, 목적어 대명사도 없어야 함:**
```
✗ The book I read it was good.
✓ The book I read was good.
```

### B. 관계대명사 없이 문장을 다시 쓰세요 (가능한 경우만) (Rewrite without relative pronoun)

1. The candy that Minseo gave me helped.
   → **The candy Minseo gave me helped.**

2. The bus that comes in the morning is packed.
   → **(Cannot omit - that is the subject)**

3. The race that I completed was 10km.
   → **The race I completed was 10km.**

4. The kid who used to hate running became a marathoner.
   → **(Cannot omit - who is the subject)**

5. The shoes which I bought yesterday are blue.
   → **The shoes I bought yesterday are blue.**

6. The coach who trained me was excellent.
   → **(Cannot omit - who is the subject)**

7. The route that I run every day is scenic.
   → **The route I run every day is scenic.**

### D. 틀린 부분 고치기 (Error correction)

1. The man lives next door is friendly.
   → The man **who** lives next door is friendly.

2. The book I read it was excellent.
   → The book I read was excellent. (it 삭제)

3. The woman helped me was very kind.
   → The woman **who** helped me was very kind.

4. The movie we watched it last night was boring.
   → The movie we watched last night was boring. (it 삭제)

5. The teacher teaches math is strict.
   → The teacher **who** teaches math is strict.

## Quick Rule (빠른 규칙)

### The One-Second Test:

관계대명사 바로 뒤를 보세요:

| 뒤에 오는 것 | 관계대명사 역할 | 생략 |
|--------------|-----------------|------|
| **주어 + 동사** | 목적어 | 가능 |
| **동사** | 주어 | 불가능 |

```
The book (that) I read → 주어(I) + 동사(read) → 목적어 → 생략 가능 ✓
The book that was... → 동사(was) → 주어 → 생략 불가 ✗
```

### Visual Pattern:

```
[관계대명사] + [주어] + [동사] = 목적어 → 생략 OK
    that         I      read

[관계대명사] + [동사] = 주어 → 생략 NO
    that      comes
```
