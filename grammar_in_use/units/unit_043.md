# Unit 43: Passive (2) - be done / been done / being done

## A. 다양한 시제의 수동태 (Passive in Different Tenses)

수동태는 모든 시제에서 사용할 수 있습니다.

| 시제 | 능동태 | 수동태 | 공식 |
|------|--------|--------|------|
| 현재진행 | is doing | **is being done** | am/is/are being + p.p. |
| 현재완료 | has done | **has been done** | has/have been + p.p. |
| 과거완료 | had done | **had been done** | had been + p.p. |
| 미래 | will do | **will be done** | will be + p.p. |
| 조동사 | can/must do | **can/must be done** | 조동사 + be + p.p. |

---

## B. 현재진행 수동태 (is/are being + p.p.)

**지금 진행 중인** 수동 동작

### From the Original Book:

> "The room **is being cleaned** at the moment."
> 방이 지금 청소되고 있다.

> "A new bridge **is being built** near the station."
> 역 근처에 새 다리가 건설되고 있다.

> "I think I'**m being watched**."
> 누군가 나를 지켜보고 있는 것 같다.

### 구조:
```
am/is/are + being + p.p.
```

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The race course is being prepared right now."**
> 대회 코스가 지금 준비되고 있다.

> **"My training plan is being updated by the coach."**
> 내 훈련 계획이 코치님에 의해 수정되고 있다.

> **"The stadium is being filled with runners."**
> 경기장이 주자들로 채워지고 있다.

### 현재 수동태 vs 현재진행 수동태:

| 현재 수동태 | 현재진행 수동태 |
|-------------|-----------------|
| is done (일반적) | is being done (지금 진행 중) |
| The room **is cleaned** every day. | The room **is being cleaned** now. |
| 방은 매일 청소된다. | 방이 지금 청소되고 있다. |

---

## C. 현재완료 수동태 (has/have been + p.p.)

**완료된** 수동 동작 (결과가 현재까지 영향)

### From the Original Book:

> "The meeting **has been canceled**."
> 회의가 취소되었다.

> "Have you ever **been bitten** by a dog?"
> 개에게 물린 적 있어?

> "The car **has been repaired**. You can pick it up."
> 차가 수리되었어요. 가져가실 수 있습니다.

### 구조:
```
has/have + been + p.p.
```

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The race has been canceled due to rain."**
> 비 때문에 대회가 취소되었다.

> **"I've been told to rest for a week."**
> 일주일 쉬라는 말을 들었다.

> **"The finish line has been crossed by thousands of runners."**
> 수천 명의 주자들이 결승선을 통과했다.

> **"My running has been noticed by the PE teacher."**
> 내 달리기가 체육 선생님에게 주목받았다.

### 현재완료 수동태의 의미:

| 예문 | 의미 |
|------|------|
| The door **has been locked**. | 문이 잠겨 있다. (잠긴 상태) |
| The work **has been finished**. | 일이 끝났다. (완료 상태) |
| I'**ve been invited** to the party. | 파티에 초대받았다. (결과) |

---

## D. 미래 수동태 (will be + p.p.)

**미래에 일어날** 수동 동작

### From the Original Book:

> "You **will be met** at the airport."
> 공항에서 마중 나갈 거예요.

> "The new school **will be opened** next month."
> 새 학교가 다음 달에 개교할 것이다.

> "Dinner **will be served** at 7 o'clock."
> 저녁 식사가 7시에 제공될 것입니다.

### 구조:
```
will + be + p.p.
```

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The race will be held next Sunday."**
> 대회는 다음 주 일요일에 열릴 것이다.

> **"The finisher's medal will be given after the race."**
> 완주 메달은 대회 후에 주어질 것이다.

> **"I will be cheered on by my family."**
> 가족들이 나를 응원해줄 것이다.

---

## E. 조동사 + 수동태 (can/must/should be + p.p.)

조동사와 함께 쓰이는 수동태

### From the Original Book:

> "This problem **can be solved**."
> 이 문제는 해결될 수 있다.

> "The work **must be finished** by tomorrow."
> 일은 내일까지 끝나야 한다.

> "Children **should be taught** to be polite."
> 아이들은 예의를 배워야 한다.

### 조동사별 수동태:

| 조동사 | 수동태 | 의미 | 예문 |
|--------|--------|------|------|
| can | can be done | ~될 수 있다 | It **can be fixed**. |
| must | must be done | ~되어야 한다 | It **must be finished**. |
| should | should be done | ~되어야 한다 | It **should be checked**. |
| might | might be done | ~될지도 모른다 | It **might be canceled**. |
| have to | has to be done | ~되어야 한다 | It **has to be done**. |

### 구조:
```
조동사 + be + p.p.
```

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The race can be completed in under 5 hours."**
> 대회는 5시간 안에 완주될 수 있다.

> **"This distance must be run at a slower pace."**
> 이 거리는 더 느린 페이스로 달려야 한다.

> **"Injuries should be avoided during training."**
> 훈련 중 부상은 피해야 한다.

> **"The race might be postponed if it rains."**
> 비가 오면 대회가 연기될 수도 있다.

---

## F. 과거완료 수동태 (had been + p.p.)

**과거의 특정 시점 이전에 완료된** 수동 동작

### From the Original Book:

> "When I arrived, the work **had been finished**."
> 내가 도착했을 때, 일은 이미 끝나 있었다.

> "She said the car **had been stolen**."
> 그녀는 차가 도난당했다고 말했다.

### 구조:
```
had + been + p.p.
```

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"By the time I arrived, the race had already been started."**
> 내가 도착했을 때, 대회는 이미 시작되어 있었다.

> **"I realized I had been noticed by the teacher."**
> 선생님께서 나를 눈여겨보고 계셨다는 걸 알았다.

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| 틀린 문장 (X) | 올바른 문장 (O) | 설명 |
|---------------|-----------------|------|
| The room **is cleaning** now. | The room **is being cleaned** now. | being 누락 |
| The race **has canceled**. | The race **has been canceled**. | been 누락 |
| It **will done** tomorrow. | It **will be done** tomorrow. | be 누락 |
| It **can fixed**. | It **can be fixed**. | be 누락 |
| It **has been build**. | It **has been built**. | 과거분사 형태 |
| The car **is being repair**. | The car **is being repaired**. | 과거분사 형태 |

### 구조 오류 정리:

```
(X) is cleaning (현재진행 능동)
(O) is being cleaned (현재진행 수동)

(X) has canceled (현재완료 능동)
(O) has been canceled (현재완료 수동)

(X) will done (구조 오류)
(O) will be done (미래 수동)
```

| 시제 | 예문 | 한국어 |
|------|------|--------|
| 현재 | The race **is held** every year. | 매년 열린다 |
| 과거 | The race **was held** last year. | 작년에 열렸다 |
| 현재진행 | The course **is being prepared**. | 준비되고 있다 |
| 현재완료 | The race **has been canceled**. | 취소되었다 |

| 조동사 | The race **can be completed** in 5 hours. | 5시간에 완주될 수 있다 |

## Exercises

### A. 알맞은 수동태 형태를 쓰세요.

1. The marathon _______ (hold) next month. (미래)
2. The course _______ (prepare) right now. (현재진행)
3. The race _______ (cancel) due to bad weather. (현재완료)
4. The training plan _______ (follow) every day. (현재)
5. Injuries _______ (avoid) during training. (조동사: should)
6. The building _______ (build) when I arrived. (과거진행)
7. The letter _______ (send) yesterday. (과거)
8. The work _______ (finish) by 5 o'clock. (미래)

**Answers:**
1. will be held
2. is being prepared
3. has been canceled
4. is followed
5. should be avoided
6. was being built
7. was sent
8. will be finished

### B. 능동태를 수동태로 바꾸세요.

1. They have canceled the race.
   → The race _______________________.

2. Someone is preparing the course.
   → The course _______________________.

3. They will give the medal to the winner.
   → The medal _______________________ to the winner.

4. You should complete the training by next week.
   → The training _______________________ by next week.

5. People can see the finish line from here.
   → The finish line _______________________ from here.

6. They had finished the work before I arrived.
   → The work _______________________ before I arrived.

7. Someone is cleaning the stadium.
   → The stadium _______________________.

8. They must check the equipment.
   → The equipment _______________________.

**Answers:**
1. has been canceled
2. is being prepared
3. will be given
4. should be completed
5. can be seen
6. had been finished
7. is being cleaned
8. must be checked

### C. 빈칸에 알맞은 형태를 고르세요.

1. The race (is being held / has been held / will be held) next Sunday.
2. My bag (was hit / is being hit / has been hit) from all sides yesterday.
3. The course (is prepared / is being prepared / was prepared) right now.
4. I (have been told / am being told / was told) to rest last week.
5. The finish line (can be seen / is seen / was seen) from here.
6. The letter (has written / has been written / is being written) by Shakespeare.
7. When I arrived, the meeting (has been started / had been started / was started).
8. The new hospital (will built / will be built / is built) next year.

**Answers:**
1. will be held
2. was hit
3. is being prepared
4. was told
5. can be seen
6. has been written (또는 was written)
7. had been started
8. will be built

## Summary: 수동태 시제 공식표

### 시제별 수동태 공식:

```
현재:         am/is/are + p.p.
과거:         was/were + p.p.
현재진행:     am/is/are + being + p.p.
현재완료:     has/have + been + p.p.
과거완료:     had + been + p.p.
미래:         will + be + p.p.
조동사:       can/must/should + be + p.p.
```

### 핵심 구성요소:

| 시제 | 핵심 요소 |
|------|-----------|
| 진행형 수동 | being |
| 완료형 수동 | been |
| 미래 수동 | will be |
| 조동사 수동 | 조동사 + be |

### 능동태 → 수동태 변환 패턴:

| 능동태 | 수동태 |
|--------|--------|
| is doing | is being done |
| has done | has been done |
| will do | will be done |
| can do | can be done |
