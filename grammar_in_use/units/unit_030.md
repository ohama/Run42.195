# Unit 30: May and might (2)

## A. 문법 설명 (Grammar Explanation)

### May/Might have done: 과거에 대한 추측

과거에 일어났을 수도 있는 일을 추측할 때 사용합니다.

> "He **might have missed** the bus."
> 그가 버스를 놓쳤을 수도 있어.

### Might (but didn't): 할 수 있었는데 안 함

과거에 일어날 수 있었지만 일어나지 않은 일:

> "I **might have given up**, but I didn't."
> 포기할 수도 있었는데, 안 했어.

---

## B. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: May/Might have done (과거 추측)

> **"He might have forgotten about the race."**
> 그가 대회를 잊었을 수도 있어.

- 과거에 대한 불확실한 추측

### Example 2: Might have done (일어났을 수 있던 일)

> **"I might have given up at 30km."**
> 30km에서 포기할 수도 있었어.

- 일어날 뻔했지만 안 일어난 일

### Example 3: May not have done

> **"They may not have heard me."**
> 그들이 내 말을 못 들었을 수도 있어.

- 과거에 대한 부정적 추측

### Example 4: Might have been (과거 상태 추측)

> **"He might have been tired after the run."**
> 달리기 후에 피곤했을 수도 있어.

- 과거 상태에 대한 추측

### Example 5: Could have vs Might have

> **"I could have walked, but I might have been late."**
> 걸을 수도 있었는데, 늦었을 수도 있어.

- could have: 가능한 선택
- might have: 결과의 가능성

---

## C. 추가 설명 (Additional Points)

### May/Might have vs Could have

| May/Might have done | Could have done |
|--------------------|-----------------|
| ~했을 수도 있다 (추측) | ~할 수 있었다 (능력/선택) |
| He might have missed the bus. | He could have taken the bus. |
| (버스를 놓쳤을 수도) | (버스를 탈 수 있었는데) |

### Might vs May (과거 추측에서)

과거 추측에서 might가 더 자주 사용됨:
- "He **might have** been sick." (더 일반적)
- "He **may have** been sick." (가능)

### 반사실적 상황 (Counterfactual)

실제로 일어나지 않은 일:
> "You might have been hurt!" (but you weren't)
> 다쳤을 수도 있었잖아! (하지만 안 다침)

---

## D. 비교 (Comparison)

### 과거 추측 비교

| 표현 | 확신도 | 예문 |
|------|--------|------|
| must have | 90% | He **must have** trained hard. |
| may/might have | 50% | He **may/might have** trained hard. |
| can't have | 0% | He **can't have** trained hard. |

### 현재 vs 과거

| 현재 | 과거 |
|------|------|
| He may be tired. | He may have been tired. |
| (지금 피곤할 수도) | (그때 피곤했을 수도) |

## Exercises

### A. May/Might have done 완성

1. 그가 버스를 놓쳤을 수도 있어.
   → He _______ the bus.

2. 그녀가 피곤했을 수도 있어.
   → She _______ tired.

3. 그들이 못 들었을 수도 있어.
   → They _______ me.

**Answers:**
1. might have missed
2. might have been
3. may not have heard

### B. Must have vs Might have 선택

1. He finished 42km. He _______ trained for months. (확실히)
2. I don't know why he's late. He _______ missed the bus. (불확실)
3. She looks exhausted. She _______ run a long distance. (확실히)
4. Maybe he forgot. He _______ not remembered the date. (불확실)

**Answers:**
1. must have
2. might have
3. must have
4. might have

### C. 문장 변환

1. Perhaps he was tired. → He _______ tired.
2. Maybe they didn't see me. → They _______ me.
3. It's possible that I made a mistake. → I _______ a mistake.

**Answers:**
1. might have been
2. might not have seen
3. might have made

### D. 상황에 맞게 작성

1. (일어날 뻔한 일) 포기할 수도 있었어.
   → I _______, but I didn't.

2. (과거 추측) 그가 늦었을 수도 있어.
   → He _______ late.

**Answers:**
1. might have given up
2. might have been
