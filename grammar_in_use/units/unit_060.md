# Unit 60: Preposition (in/for/about, etc.) + -ing

## A. 전치사 + -ing

전치사(in, for, about, of, without, before, after 등) 뒤에는 **-ing 형태**가 옵니다.
to부정사가 아닌 -ing만 가능합니다!

**주의:** to가 전치사일 때도 -ing가 옵니다.
- look forward **to** + -ing
- be used **to** + -ing

---

## B. before / after / without + -ing

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Before running, I always stretch."**
> 달리기 전에 항상 스트레칭을 한다.

- **before + -ing**: ~하기 전에

> **"After running 10km, I felt great."**
> 10km를 뛴 후에 기분이 좋았다.

- **after + -ing**: ~한 후에

> **"I ran without stopping."**
> 멈추지 않고 달렸다.

- **without + -ing**: ~하지 않고

> **"I finished the race without walking."**
> 걷지 않고 레이스를 끝냈다.

---

## C. by / for / about + -ing

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"By running every day, I built my stamina."**
> 매일 달리면서 체력을 키웠다.

- **by + -ing**: ~함으로써

> **"Thank you for coming to the race."**
> 레이스에 와줘서 고마워.

- **for + -ing**: ~해줘서 (감사/사과의 이유)

> **"I'm thinking about running a marathon."**
> 마라톤을 뛰는 것에 대해 생각하고 있어.

- **about + -ing**: ~에 대해

> **"What about walking to school?"**
> 걸어서 학교 가는 건 어때?

---

## D. of / instead of / in spite of + -ing

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was tired of sitting on the bus."**
> 버스에 앉아 있는 게 지쳤다.

- **be tired of + -ing**: ~하는 게 지치다

> **"Instead of taking the bus, I walked."**
> 버스를 타는 대신 걸었다.

- **instead of + -ing**: ~하는 대신에

> **"In spite of feeling tired, I kept running."**
> 피곤함에도 불구하고 계속 달렸다.

- **in spite of + -ing**: ~에도 불구하고

---

## E. look forward to + -ing

**주의:** 여기서 to는 전치사입니다!

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I'm looking forward to running the marathon."**
> 마라톤을 뛰는 것이 기대된다.

- **look forward to + -ing**: ~을 기대하다

> **"I'm looking forward to seeing you at the finish line."**
> 결승선에서 만나는 게 기대돼.

**오류 주의:**
- I'm looking forward to **see** you. (X)
- I'm looking forward to **seeing** you. (O)

---

## F. 주요 전치사 + -ing 표현

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| before + -ing | ~하기 전에 | Before running, I stretch. |
| after + -ing | ~한 후에 | After running, I feel great. |
| without + -ing | ~하지 않고 | I finished without stopping. |
| by + -ing | ~함으로써 | By running, I got stronger. |
| for + -ing | ~해줘서 | Thanks for coming. |
| about + -ing | ~에 대해 | I'm thinking about running. |
| instead of + -ing | ~대신에 | Instead of running, I walked. |
| in spite of + -ing | ~에도 불구하고 | In spite of being tired, I ran. |
| look forward to + -ing | ~을 기대하다 | I'm looking forward to racing. |

---

## G. 전치사 to vs to부정사

| to + -ing (전치사) | to + 동사원형 (부정사) |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| look forward to running | want to run |
| be used to walking | used to walk |
| object to working | plan to work |

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. Before (run / running), I always warm up.
2. I finished the race without (stop / stopping).
3. Thank you for (come / coming) to the race.
4. I'm looking forward to (see / seeing) you.
5. Instead of (take / taking) the bus, I walked.

**Answers:**
1. running
2. stopping
3. coming
4. seeing
5. taking

### B. 문장을 완성하세요.

1. After _______ (finish) the race, I cried.
2. By _______ (practice) every day, I improved.
3. I'm tired of _______ (sit) on the bus.
4. What about _______ (run) together?
5. In spite of _______ (be) tired, I kept going.

**Answers:**
1. finishing
2. practicing
3. sitting
4. running
5. being

### C. 문장을 전치사 + -ing로 바꾸세요.

1. I ran and then I felt great.
   → After _______, I felt great.

2. I didn't stop. I finished the race.
   → I finished the race without _______.

3. I exercised every day. That's how I got stronger.
   → By _______ every day, I got stronger.

**Answers:**
1. running
2. stopping
3. exercising
