# Unit 60: Preposition (in/for/about, etc.) + -ing

## A. 전치사 + -ing (Form)

전치사(in, for, about, of, without, before, after 등) 뒤에는 **-ing 형태**가 옵니다.
to부정사가 아닌 -ing만 가능합니다!

| Preposition | Example |
|-------------|---------|
| before | before **running** |
| after | after **finishing** |
| without | without **stopping** |
| by | by **practicing** |
| for | for **coming** |
| about | about **running** |

### From the Original Book:

> "Are you **interested in working** for us?"
> 우리와 일하는 데 관심 있으세요?

**주의:** to가 전치사일 때도 -ing가 옵니다.
- look forward **to** + -ing
- be used **to** + -ing

---

## B. before / after / without + -ing (Primary Usage)

### From the Original Book:

> "**Before leaving**, I checked my bag."
> 떠나기 전에, 가방을 확인했어.

> "**After finishing** work, I went home."
> 일을 마친 후에, 집에 갔어.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Before running, I always stretch."**
> 달리기 전에 항상 스트레칭을 한다.

- **before + -ing**: ~하기 전에

> **"After running 10km, I felt great."**
> 10km를 뛴 후에 기분이 좋았다.

- **after + -ing**: ~한 후에

> **"I ran without stopping."**
> 멈추지 않고 달렸다.

- **without + -ing**: ~하지 않고

> **"I finished the race without walking."**
> 걷지 않고 레이스를 끝냈다.

### 시각적 이해:

```
before + -ing:    [PREP] ──→ [ACTION]
                  stretch → run

after + -ing:     [ACTION] ──→ [RESULT]
                  run → feel great

without + -ing:   [ACTION] ── X ── [NOT THIS]
                  run ── X ── stop
```

## C. by / for / about + -ing (Additional Uses)

### From the Original Book:

> "You can improve your English **by reading** more."
> 더 많이 읽음으로써 영어를 향상시킬 수 있어.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"By running every day, I built my stamina."**
> 매일 달리면서 체력을 키웠다.

- **by + -ing**: ~함으로써 (방법)

> **"Thank you for coming to the race."**
> 레이스에 와줘서 고마워.

- **for + -ing**: ~해줘서 (감사/사과의 이유)

> **"I'm thinking about running a marathon."**
> 마라톤을 뛰는 것에 대해 생각하고 있어.

- **about + -ing**: ~에 대해

> **"What about walking to school?"**
> 걸어서 학교 가는 건 어때?

### by + -ing 용법:

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| How did you improve? | **By practicing** every day. |
| How do you get there? | **By walking** to school. |
| How do you stay fit? | **By running** regularly. |

---

## D. of / instead of / in spite of + -ing (Special Cases)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I was tired of sitting on the bus."**
> 버스에 앉아 있는 게 지쳤다.

- **be tired of + -ing**: ~하는 게 지치다

> **"Instead of taking the bus, I walked."**
> 버스를 타는 대신 걸었다.

- **instead of + -ing**: ~하는 대신에

> **"In spite of feeling tired, I kept running."**
> 피곤함에도 불구하고 계속 달렸다.

- **in spite of + -ing**: ~에도 불구하고

### Expressions with 'of':

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| tired of | ~에 지친 | tired of waiting |
| fond of | ~을 좋아하는 | fond of running |
| afraid of | ~을 두려워하는 | afraid of failing |
| capable of | ~할 수 있는 | capable of finishing |
| accused of | ~로 비난받는 | accused of cheating |

---

## E. look forward to + -ing (Important Exception)

**주의:** 여기서 to는 전치사입니다! (to부정사 아님)

### From the Original Book:

> "I'm looking forward **to seeing** you soon."
> 곧 만나뵙기를 기대하고 있어요.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I'm looking forward to running the marathon."**
> 마라톤을 뛰는 것이 기대된다.

- **look forward to + -ing**: ~을 기대하다

> **"I'm looking forward to seeing you at the finish line."**
> 결승선에서 만나는 게 기대돼.

**오류 주의:**
| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) |
|-----------|-------------|
| ~~I'm looking forward to **see** you.~~ | I'm looking forward to **seeing** you. |
| ~~I look forward to **meet** you.~~ | I look forward to **meeting** you. |

### 'to'가 전치사인 표현들:

| Expression | Example |
|------------|---------|
| look forward to | look forward to **seeing** you |
| be used to | be used to **running** |
| object to | object to **waiting** |
| get around to | get around to **finishing** |
| be committed to | be committed to **training** |

## F. 주요 전치사 + -ing 표현 정리

| Expression | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| before + -ing | ~하기 전에 | Before running, I stretch. |
| after + -ing | ~한 후에 | After running, I feel great. |
| without + -ing | ~하지 않고 | I finished without stopping. |
| by + -ing | ~함으로써 | By running, I got stronger. |
| for + -ing | ~해줘서 | Thanks for coming. |
| about + -ing | ~에 대해 | I'm thinking about running. |
| instead of + -ing | ~대신에 | Instead of running, I walked. |
| in spite of + -ing | ~에도 불구하고 | In spite of being tired, I ran. |
| look forward to + -ing | ~을 기대하다 | I'm looking forward to racing. |
| be used to + -ing | ~에 익숙하다 | I'm used to running early. |

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~Before to run, I stretch.~~ | Before **running**, I stretch. | 전치사 + -ing |
| ~~I'm looking forward to see you.~~ | I'm looking forward to **seeing** you. | to = 전치사 |
| ~~Thank you for to come.~~ | Thank you for **coming**. | for + -ing |
| ~~Instead of to take the bus...~~ | Instead of **taking** the bus... | instead of + -ing |
| ~~I'm interested in to learn.~~ | I'm interested in **learning**. | in + -ing |
| ~~Without to stop, I ran.~~ | Without **stopping**, I ran. | without + -ing |
| ~~By to practice daily...~~ | By **practicing** daily... | by + -ing |

## Exercises

### A. 올바른 형태를 고르세요.

1. Before (run / running), I always warm up.
2. I finished the race without (stop / stopping).
3. Thank you for (come / coming) to the race.
4. I'm looking forward to (see / seeing) you.
5. Instead of (take / taking) the bus, I walked.
6. By (practice / practicing) daily, I improved.
7. I'm tired of (wait / waiting) for the bus.
8. In spite of (be / being) tired, I kept going.

**Answers:**
1. running
2. stopping
3. coming
4. seeing
5. taking
6. practicing
7. waiting
8. being

6. I'm looking forward to _______ (meet) you.
7. Instead of _______ (walk), I ran.

**Answers:**
1. finishing
2. practicing
3. sitting
4. running
5. being
6. meeting
7. walking
8. helping

### C. 문장을 전치사 + -ing로 바꾸세요.

1. I ran and then I felt great.
   → After _______, I felt great.

2. I didn't stop. I finished the race.
   → I finished the race without _______.

3. I exercised every day. That's how I got stronger.
   → By _______ every day, I got stronger.

4. I took the bus. But I could have walked.
   → Instead of _______, I took the bus.

5. I was tired. But I kept running.
   → In spite of _______ tired, I kept running.

**Answers:**
1. running
2. stopping
3. exercising
4. walking
5. being

### D. 빈칸에 알맞은 전치사를 넣으세요.

1. I'm thinking _______ running a marathon.
2. Thank you _______ coming to the race.
3. I improved _______ practicing every day.
4. _______ running, I always stretch.
5. I'm looking forward _______ seeing you.
6. I finished _______ stopping.
7. I'm tired _______ waiting.
8. _______ spite of being tired, I continued.

**Answers:**
1. about
2. for
3. by
4. Before
5. to
6. without

8. In

## Summary: 전치사 + -ing vs to + 동사원형

| 전치사 + -ing | to + 동사원형 |
|---------------|---------------|
| **after** running | want **to** run |
| **before** running | need **to** run |
| **without** stopping | have **to** stop |
| **by** practicing | learn **to** practice |
| look forward **to** running | hope **to** run |
| be used **to** running | used **to** run (과거 습관) |

### Key Distinction:
- **to가 전치사일 때** → + **-ing**
  - look forward **to** seeing
  - be used **to** running

- **to가 to부정사일 때** → + **동사원형**
  - want **to** run
  - need **to** practice
