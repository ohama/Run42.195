# Unit 35: Had better / It's time...

## A. 형태 (Form)

### Had better: 강한 조언/경고

"~하는 게 좋겠다" - should보다 강한 표현, **안 하면 문제가 생길 수 있음**을 암시

| Form | Structure | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| 긍정 | had better + 동사원형 | You'd better rest. |
| 부정 | had better not + 동사원형 | You'd better not run. |

### It's time: ~할 시간이다

| Form | Structure | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| 일반적 | It's time + to + 동사원형 | It's time to go. |
| 가정법 (늦었음) | It's time + 주어 + 과거동사 | It's time we went. |

---

## B. Had better: 강한 조언/경고 (Strong Advice/Warning)

### From the Original Book:

> "It's cold. You**'d better** wear a coat."
> 추워. 코트 입는 게 좋겠어.

> "I**'d better** phone my parents. They'll be worried."
> 부모님께 전화하는 게 좋겠어. 걱정하실 거야.

> "You**'d better not** be late. The boss is in a bad mood."
> 늦지 않는 게 좋겠어. 사장님 기분이 안 좋으시거든.

### Key Points:

1. **"had"가 있지만 과거형이 아님** - 현재/미래를 말함
2. **축약형**: I'd better, You'd better (대화에서 자주 사용)
3. **부정형**: had better **not** (hadn't better는 잘 안 씀)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"You'd better go to bed early."**
> 일찍 자는 게 좋겠어.

- 안 자면 내일 힘들 것이라는 암시

> **"You'd better not skip breakfast before the race."**
> 대회 전에 아침 거르지 않는 게 좋겠어.

- 거르면 문제가 생길 수 있음

> **"I'd better start training now."**
> 지금 훈련 시작하는 게 좋겠어.

---

## C. Had better vs Should

| Had better | Should |
|------------|--------|
| 강한 조언/경고 | 일반적인 조언 |
| 안 하면 문제 발생 가능 | 단순 권유 |
| You'd better rest. | You should rest. |
| (안 쉬면 안 좋을 거야) | (쉬는 게 좋아) |

### Comparison:

| Situation | Had better | Should |
|-----------|------------|--------|
| 경고 포함 | You'd better leave now. | You should leave now. |
| (지금 안 가면 늦어) | (가는 게 좋아) |

### From the Original Book:

> "**Should** I take an umbrella?" "Yes, you **should**. It might rain."
> 우산 가져갈까? - 응, 가져가. 비 올 수도 있어. (일반 조언)

> "You**'d better** take an umbrella. It's definitely going to rain."
> 우산 가져가는 게 좋겠어. 비가 분명 올 거야. (경고)

---

## D. It's time + to + 동사원형 (일반적)

**적절한 시간**을 나타냄:

> **"It's time to start running."**
> 달리기 시작할 시간이야.

> "It's time to go home."
> 집에 갈 시간이야.

> "It's time for lunch."
> 점심 시간이야.

---

## E. It's time + 주어 + 과거동사 (가정법)

**늦었다는 뉘앙스** - 이미 해야 했는데 아직 안 함

### From the Original Book:

> "It's time **we went**. We've been here too long."
> 이제 가야 해. 너무 오래 있었어.

> "It's late. It's time the children **were** in bed."
> 늦었어. 애들이 자야 할 시간이야.

### Note: 과거동사이지만 **현재**를 말함 (가정법)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"It's time I started training seriously."**
> 이제 진지하게 훈련을 시작해야 해.

- 이미 시작했어야 했다는 뉘앙스

> **"It's about time you stopped making excuses."**
> 이제 변명 좀 그만해야지.

- 더 강한 표현, 이미 늦었다는 느낌

---

## F. It's time의 강도 (Intensity)

| Expression | Intensity | Example |
|------------|-----------|---------|
| It's time to go. | 중립 | 갈 시간이야. |
| It's time we went. | 약간 늦음 | 이제 가야 해. |
| It's about time we went. | 꽤 늦음 | 이제 진짜 가야 해. |
| It's high time we went. | 매우 늦음 | 벌써 갔어야 했어. |

### From the Original Book:

> "You've been watching TV all day. It's **about time** you did something useful."
> 하루 종일 TV 봤잖아. 이제 뭔가 유용한 걸 해야지.

> "It's **high time** the government did something about this."
> 정부가 이것에 대해 뭔가 해야 할 때가 한참 지났어.

---

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~You better go.~~ | You**'d better** go. | had 필요 |
| ~~You'd better to go.~~ | You**'d better go**. | to 없음 |
| ~~You hadn't better run.~~ | You**'d better not** run. | 부정은 had better not |
| ~~It's time to we go.~~ | It's time **we went**. | 가정법 구조 |
| ~~It's time going.~~ | It's time **to go**. | to + 동사원형 |
| ~~You had better will rest.~~ | You**'d better rest**. | 동사원형 |

### Had better vs Would better:

| Wrong | Correct |
|-------|---------|
| ~~I would better go.~~ | I**'d better** go. (had better) |

**'d better**의 'd는 **had**입니다 (would 아님).

## Exercises

### A. Had better / Had better not 완성 (Complete the sentences)

1. 일찍 자는 게 좋겠어.
   → You _______ to bed early.

2. 대회 전에 무리하지 않는 게 좋겠어.
   → You _______ too hard before the race.

3. 물 많이 마시는 게 좋겠어.
   → You _______ plenty of water.

4. 지금 출발하는 게 좋겠어.
   → We _______ now.

5. 그 말 하지 않는 게 좋겠어.
   → You _______ that.

**Answers:**
1. had better go / 'd better go
2. had better not push / 'd better not push
3. had better drink / 'd better drink
4. had better leave / 'd better leave
5. had better not say / 'd better not say

### B. It's time 문장 완성 (Complete the sentences)

1. 달리기 시작할 시간이야. (일반)
   → It's time _______ running.

2. 이제 진지하게 훈련해야 해. (늦었음)
   → It's time I _______ seriously.

3. 이제 좀 쉬어야지. (늦었음)
   → It's about time you _______.

4. 집에 갈 시간이야. (일반)
   → It's time _______ home.

5. 이제 잘 시간이야. (늦었음)
   → It's time we _______ to bed.

**Answers:**
1. to start
2. started training / trained
3. rested / took a rest
4. to go
5. went

### C. Should vs Had better 선택 (Choose the correct form)

1. You _______ try running earlier. (일반 조언)
2. You _______ leave now or you'll miss the bus. (경고)
3. I think you _______ drink more water. (의견)
4. You _______ not run on that injured leg. (강한 경고)
5. Maybe you _______ take a break. (부드러운 제안)
6. You _______ hurry. The race starts in 10 minutes. (경고)
7. You _______ try this restaurant. (권유)
8. You _______ not be late. The boss is angry. (경고)

**Answers:**
1. should
2. had better / 'd better
3. should
4. had better / 'd better
5. should
6. had better / 'd better
7. should
8. had better / 'd better

### D. 상황에 맞게 작성 (Write based on context)

1. You need to leave or you'll be late.
   → You**'d better leave now**.

2. A friend should try a new restaurant.
   → You **should try** this restaurant.

3. It's past bedtime for children.
   → It's time the children **went to bed**.

4. Someone hasn't started studying yet.
   → It's about time you **started studying**.

## Summary Table (요약표)

| 표현 | 의미 | 강도 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|------|
| should | 조언 | 약함 | You should go. |
| had better | 강한 조언/경고 | 강함 | You'd better go. |
| It's time to... | 시간이다 | 중립 | It's time to go. |
| It's time + 과거동사 | 해야 할 때다 | 늦음 | It's time we went. |
| It's about time + 과거동사 | 진짜 해야 할 때다 | 더 늦음 | It's about time we went. |
| It's high time + 과거동사 | 한참 전에 했어야 했다 | 매우 늦음 | It's high time we went. |
