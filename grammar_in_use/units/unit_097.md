# Unit 97: -ing and -ed Clauses (the girl playing / the man injured)

## A. -ing 절이란? (What are -ing clauses?)

**-ing 절**은 관계절을 짧게 줄인 형태입니다. **진행 중인 동작**이나 **능동적인 상태**를 나타냅니다.

### Form (형태):

| 관계절 (Full) | -ing 절 (Reduced) |
|---------------|-------------------|
| the kids **who are running** | the kids **running** |
| the bus **that was approaching** | the bus **approaching** |
| people **who live** in Jeju | people **living** in Jeju |

### From the Original Book:

> "Do you know the woman **talking** to Tom?"
> = Do you know the woman **who is talking** to Tom?
> 톰과 이야기하고 있는 여자 알아?

> "Who is the man **wearing** the funny hat?"
> = Who is the man **who is wearing** the funny hat?
> 우스운 모자를 쓴 남자가 누구야?

---

## B. -ing 절 예문 (Examples with -ing clauses)

### Describing ongoing actions:

> **"Kids in gym clothes were running around."**
> 체육복 입은 아이들이 뛰어다니고 있었다.

- = Kids **who were wearing** gym clothes

> **"I could see the bus approaching in the distance."**
> 버스가 멀리서 다가오는 게 보였다.

- = the bus **that was approaching**

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I heard birds singing."**
> 새들이 노래하는 소리가 들렸다.

- = birds **that were singing**

> **"The runners passing me looked exhausted."**
> 나를 지나가는 주자들이 지쳐 보였다.

- = The runners **who were passing** me

> **"I saw people waiting at the bus stop."**
> 버스 정류장에서 기다리는 사람들이 보였다.

> **"The coach standing at the finish line cheered for me."**
> 결승선에 서 계신 코치님이 나를 응원해 주셨다.

> **"I noticed the grandfather working in the orchard."**
> 귤밭에서 일하시는 할아버지를 보았다.

---

## C. -ed 절이란? (What are -ed clauses?)

**-ed 절**은 **수동의 의미**를 가진 관계절을 줄인 형태입니다.

### Form (형태):

| 관계절 (Full) | -ed 절 (Reduced) |
|---------------|------------------|
| the medal **that was received** | the medal **received** |
| the food **that is served** | the food **served** |
| the route **that was recommended** | the route **recommended** |

### From the Original Book:

> "The injured man was taken to hospital."
> = The man **who was injured** was taken to hospital.
> 다친 남자가 병원에 실려 갔다.

> "Most of the goods **made** in this factory are exported."
> = Most of the goods **that are made** in this factory...
> 이 공장에서 만들어진 상품 대부분은 수출된다.

---

## D. -ed 절 예문 (Examples with -ed clauses)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The route recommended by my coach was scenic."**
> 코치님이 추천해 준 경로는 경치가 좋았다.

- = The route **that was recommended** by my coach

> **"The training plan given to me was perfect."**
> 나에게 주어진 훈련 계획은 완벽했다.

- = The training plan **that was given** to me

> **"The medal received at the finish line was heavy."**
> 결승선에서 받은 메달은 무거웠다.

- = The medal **that was received** at the finish line

> **"The candy given by Minseo helped with my nausea."**
> 민서가 준 사탕이 멀미에 도움이 됐다.

> **"The distance covered that day was 10 kilometers."**
> 그날 달린 거리는 10킬로미터였다.

> **"The advice offered by my coach changed my approach."**
> 코치님이 해주신 조언이 내 접근법을 바꿨다.

---

## E. -ing vs -ed 비교 (Comparison)

### Key Difference:

| -ing (능동/진행) | -ed (수동/완료) |
|------------------|-----------------|
| 동작을 **하는** | 동작을 **받는** |
| the kids **running** | the distance **covered** |
| (뛰고 있는 아이들) | (달린 거리) |
| the bird **singing** | the medal **received** |
| (노래하는 새) | (받은 메달) |

### Meaning Comparison:

> **"The boy running in the park is Doyun."** (능동 - 달리고 있는)
> 공원에서 달리고 있는 소년은 도윤이다.

> **"The boy injured in the race was taken to hospital."** (수동 - 다친)
> 대회에서 다친 소년이 병원에 실려 갔다.

### From the Original Book:

| -ing (Active) | -ed (Passive) |
|---------------|---------------|
| the man **making** the speech | the speech **made** by the man |
| the woman **wearing** the hat | the hat **worn** by the woman |

---

## F. 위치: 명사 앞 vs 명사 뒤 (Position)

### 짧은 분사: 명사 앞 (Short participles: before noun)

> **"a running boy"** (달리는 소년)
> **"a tired runner"** (지친 주자)
> **"the exhausted athletes"** (지친 선수들)
> **"broken shoes"** (망가진 신발)

### 긴 분사구: 명사 뒤 (Long participle phrases: after noun)

> **"the boy running in the park"** (공원에서 달리는 소년)
> **"the medal received at the race"** (대회에서 받은 메달)
> **"people waiting for the bus"** (버스를 기다리는 사람들)
> **"the route recommended by my coach"** (코치님이 추천한 경로)

### From the Original Book:

| Before noun | After noun |
|-------------|------------|
| a **boring** movie | a movie **boring** everyone |
| a **broken** window | the window **broken** by the ball |

## G. 감각 동사 + 목적어 + -ing (Sense verbs)

**see, hear, watch, feel, notice** 등 감각 동사 뒤에 목적어 + -ing 형태가 옵니다.

### Pattern:

```
감각동사 + 목적어 + -ing
I heard    birds    singing
```

### From the Original Book:

> "I looked out the window and saw a man **running** towards the house."
> 창밖을 보니 남자가 집을 향해 달려오고 있었다.

> "Did you notice anyone **waiting** outside?"
> 밖에서 누가 기다리는 거 봤어?

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I heard birds singing in the morning."**
> 아침에 새들이 노래하는 걸 들었다.

> **"I saw kids running on the field."**
> 운동장에서 아이들이 뛰는 걸 봤다.

> **"I felt my heart pounding."**
> 심장이 뛰는 게 느껴졌다.

> **"I watched the scenery passing by."**
> 풍경이 지나가는 걸 바라봤다.

> **"I noticed the grandfather waving at me."**
> 할아버지가 손 흔드는 걸 보았다.

## H. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | 설명 |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| ~~The medal receiving was heavy~~ | The medal **received** was heavy | 수동 의미 → -ed |
| ~~The man injured he was taken~~ | The man **injured** was taken | 주어 반복 불필요 |
| ~~I saw him ran~~ | I saw him **running** | 감각동사 + 목적어 + -ing |
| ~~Kids run in the park are healthy~~ | Kids **running** in the park | 분사형 필요 |
| ~~The route recommending by coach~~ | The route **recommended** by coach | 수동 의미 → -ed |
| ~~I heard singing birds~~ | I heard birds **singing** | 감각동사 구문 |

### Active vs Passive Choice:

```
스스로 하는 동작 → -ing (능동)
The boy running = 달리고 있는 소년

당하는 동작 → -ed (수동)
The medal received = 받은 메달
```

## Summary

### -ing vs -ed Comparison:

| 형태 | 의미 | Voice | 예시 |
|------|------|-------|------|
| -ing | 능동/진행 | Active | kids **running** (뛰고 있는 아이들) |
| -ed | 수동/완료 | Passive | medal **received** (받은 메달) |

### Position Rules:

| 위치 | 조건 | 예시 |
|------|------|------|
| 명사 앞 | 짧은 분사 | a **running** boy |
| 명사 뒤 | 긴 분사구 | the boy **running in the park** |

### Sense Verb Pattern:

```
see/hear/watch/feel/notice + 목적어 + -ing

I heard birds singing.
I saw kids running.
I felt my heart pounding.
```

### Quick Decision Guide:

```
동작을 하는 쪽인가? → -ing (능동)
동작을 받는 쪽인가? → -ed (수동)

The boy running → 소년이 달린다 (능동)
The medal received → 메달을 받다 (수동)
```
