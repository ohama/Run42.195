# Unit 97: -ing and -ed Clauses (the girl playing / the man injured)

## A. -ing 절 (현재분사 절)

**-ing 절**은 관계절을 짧게 줄인 형태입니다. 진행 중인 동작이나 상태를 나타냅니다.

| 관계절 | -ing 절 |
|--------|---------|
| the kids **who are running** | the kids **running** |
| the bus **that was approaching** | the bus **approaching** |

---

## B. -ing 절 예문 (Examples with -ing clauses)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"Kids in gym clothes were running around."**
> 체육복 입은 아이들이 뛰어다니고 있었다.

- = Kids **who were wearing** gym clothes

> **"I could see the bus approaching in the distance."**
> 버스가 멀리서 다가오는 게 보였다.

- = the bus **that was approaching**

> **"I heard birds singing."**
> 새들이 노래하는 소리가 들렸다.

- = birds **that were singing**

> **"The runners passing me looked exhausted."**
> 나를 지나가는 주자들이 지쳐 보였다.

- = The runners **who were passing** me

> **"I saw people waiting at the bus stop."**
> 버스 정류장에서 기다리는 사람들이 보였다.

---

## C. -ed 절 (과거분사 절)

**-ed 절**은 수동의 의미를 가진 관계절을 줄인 형태입니다.

| 관계절 | -ed 절 |
|--------|--------|
| the medal **that was received** | the medal **received** |
| the food **that is served** | the food **served** |

---

## D. -ed 절 예문 (Examples with -ed clauses)

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"The route recommended by my coach was scenic."**
> 코치님이 추천해 준 경로는 경치가 좋았다.

- = The route **that was recommended** by my coach

> **"The training plan given to me was perfect."**
> 나에게 주어진 훈련 계획은 완벽했다.

- = The training plan **that was given** to me

> **"The medal received at the finish line was heavy."**
> 결승선에서 받은 메달은 무거웠다.

- = The medal **that was received** at the finish line

> **"The candy given by Minseo helped with my nausea."**
> 민서가 준 사탕이 멀미에 도움이 됐다.

> **"The distance covered that day was 10 kilometers."**
> 그날 달린 거리는 10킬로미터였다.

---

## E. -ing vs -ed 비교

| -ing (능동/진행) | -ed (수동/완료) |
|------------------|-----------------|
| the kids **running** | the distance **covered** |
| (뛰고 있는 아이들) | (달린 거리) |
| the bird **singing** | the medal **received** |
| (노래하는 새) | (받은 메달) |

### 의미 차이:

> **"The boy running in the park is Doyun."** (능동 - 달리고 있는)
> 공원에서 달리고 있는 소년은 도윤이다.

> **"The boy injured in the race was taken to hospital."** (수동 - 다친)
> 대회에서 다친 소년이 병원에 실려 갔다.

---

## F. 위치: 명사 앞 vs 명사 뒤

### 짧은 분사: 명사 앞

> **"a running boy"** (달리는 소년)
> **"a tired runner"** (지친 주자)

### 긴 분사구: 명사 뒤

> **"the boy running in the park"** (공원에서 달리는 소년)
> **"the medal received at the race"** (대회에서 받은 메달)

---

## G. 감각 동사 + 목적어 + -ing

**see, hear, watch, feel** 등 감각 동사 뒤에 목적어 + -ing 형태가 옵니다.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I heard birds singing in the morning."**
> 아침에 새들이 노래하는 걸 들었다.

> **"I saw kids running on the field."**
> 운동장에서 아이들이 뛰는 걸 봤다.

> **"I felt my heart pounding."**
> 심장이 뛰는 게 느껴졌다.

> **"I watched the scenery passing by."**
> 풍경이 지나가는 걸 바라봤다.

## Exercises

### A. 관계절을 -ing 또는 -ed 절로 바꾸세요.

1. The bus that was approaching in the distance
   → The bus **approaching** in the distance

2. The medal that was received at the race
   → The medal **received** at the race

3. The kids who are running in the park
   → The kids **running** in the park

4. The route that was recommended by the coach
   → The route **recommended** by the coach

5. The birds that were singing in the morning
   → The birds **singing** in the morning

### B. -ing와 -ed 중 알맞은 것을 고르세요.

1. I saw a boy (running / run) on the track.
   → **running**

2. The food (serving / served) at the event was good.
   → **served**

3. The runners (passing / passed) me looked tired.
   → **passing**

4. The medal (receiving / received) was heavy.
   → **received**

5. I heard someone (calling / called) my name.
   → **calling**

### C. 문장을 완성하세요.

1. I could see the finish line _______ (approach) in the distance.
   → **approaching**

2. The training plan _______ (give) by my coach was helpful.
   → **given**

3. I heard my mom _______ (cheer) from the sidelines.
   → **cheering**

4. The distance _______ (cover) that day was 42km.
   → **covered**

## Summary

| 형태 | 의미 | 예시 |
|------|------|------|
| -ing | 능동/진행 | kids **running** (뛰고 있는 아이들) |
| -ed | 수동/완료 | medal **received** (받은 메달) |

### 감각동사 패턴:
```
see/hear/watch/feel + 목적어 + -ing
I heard birds singing.
```
