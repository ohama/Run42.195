# Unit 31: Have to and must

## A. 형태 (Form)

### Have to: 외부적 의무/필요

| Tense | Form | Example |
|-------|------|---------|
| 현재 | have/has to + 동사원형 | I have to go. |
| 과거 | had to + 동사원형 | I had to go. |
| 미래 | will have to + 동사원형 | I will have to go. |
| 현재완료 | have/has had to + 동사원형 | I have had to go. |

### Must: 내부적 의무/화자의 의견

| Form | Example |
|------|---------|
| 현재만 | I must go. |
| (과거/미래 없음) | (had to / will have to 사용) |

### 의문문과 부정문:

| Type | Have to | Must |
|------|---------|------|
| 의문문 | Do I have to go? | Must I go? (격식체) |
| 부정문 | I don't have to go. | I mustn't go. |

---

## B. Have to vs Must: 핵심 차이

| Have to | Must |
|---------|------|
| 외부적 의무/규칙 | 내부적 의무/화자의 의견 |
| "They say I have to..." | "I feel I must..." |
| 상황이나 규칙 때문에 | 내 생각에 해야 하는 것 |

### From the Original Book:

> "I **have to** wear glasses for reading."
> 읽을 때 안경을 써야 해. (필요해서)

> "I **must** phone my parents. I promised I'd call."
> 부모님께 전화해야 해. 전화하겠다고 약속했거든. (내 결심)

> "You **must** come and see us."
> 우리한테 꼭 놀러와야 해. (내 의견/권유)

---

## C. Have to: 외부적 의무 (External Obligation)

상황, 규칙, 또는 다른 사람이 정한 것:

### From the Original Book:

> "I **have to** get up early tomorrow. My train leaves at 7:30."
> 내일 일찍 일어나야 해. 기차가 7시 30분에 출발하거든.

> "You **have to** pass a test to get a driving license."
> 운전면허를 따려면 시험에 붙어야 해.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I have to take the bus to school."**
> 학교에 버스를 타고 가야 해.

- 상황적으로 필요한 것 (학교가 멀어서)

> **"School starts at 8:30, so I have to catch the 7:30 bus."**
> 학교가 8시 30분에 시작해서 7시 30분 버스를 타야 해.

- 외부적 규칙/일정

---

## D. Must: 내부적 의무 (Internal Obligation)

화자 자신의 결심이나 강한 권유:

### From the Original Book:

> "I haven't called my parents for ages. I **must** call them tonight."
> 오래 부모님께 전화 못 했어. 오늘 밤 전화해야지.

> "You **must** read this book. It's wonderful."
> 이 책 꼭 읽어봐. 정말 좋아.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I must finish this race."**
> 이 대회를 완주해야만 해.

- 화자 자신의 강한 결심

> **"I must train harder."**
> 더 열심히 훈련해야 해.

- 내 결심

---

## E. 시제별 표현 (Tenses)

Must는 현재만 있으므로, 과거와 미래에는 have to 사용:

| Tense | Have to | Must |
|-------|---------|------|
| 현재 | I have to run. | I must run. |
| 과거 | I had to run. | (X) |
| 미래 | I will have to run. | (X) |
| 현재완료 | I have had to run. | (X) |

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"I had to squeeze my way through the crowded bus."**
> 붐비는 버스에서 비집고 들어가야 했다.

- 과거에 해야 했던 일 = had to

> **"I'll have to train harder for the full marathon."**
> 풀 마라톤을 위해 더 열심히 훈련해야 할 거야.

- 미래에 해야 할 일 = will have to

---

## F. Don't have to vs Mustn't: 중요한 차이!

| Don't have to | Mustn't |
|---------------|---------|
| ~할 필요 없다 | ~하면 안 된다 |
| 선택 가능 (해도 되고 안 해도 됨) | 금지 (절대 하면 안 됨) |
| You don't have to run. | You mustn't run. |
| (안 달려도 돼) | (달리면 안 돼) |

### From the Original Book:

> "I **don't have to** wear a suit to work, but I usually do."
> 일할 때 정장 안 입어도 되는데, 보통 입어.

> "You **mustn't** tell anyone what I said."
> 내가 말한 거 아무에게도 말하면 안 돼.

### Examples from the Novel:

> **"You don't have to run every day. Rest is important too."**
> 매일 달릴 필요는 없어. 휴식도 중요해.

> **"You mustn't give up."**
> 포기하면 안 돼.

## G. 흔한 실수 (Common Mistakes)

| Wrong (X) | Correct (O) | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| ~~I must to go.~~ | I **must go**. | to 없음 |
| ~~He musts go.~~ | He **must go**. | must는 변하지 않음 |
| ~~I musted go yesterday.~~ | I **had to go** yesterday. | 과거 = had to |
| ~~I must not to run.~~ | I **mustn't run**. | to 없음 |
| ~~Do I must go?~~ | **Must** I go? / Do I **have to** go? | 의문문 형태 |
| ~~I don't must go.~~ | I **don't have to** go. / I **mustn't** go. | 의미 구분 필요 |

### Don't have to vs Mustn't 구분:

| 상황 | 선택 |
|------|------|
| 안 해도 되는 일 | don't have to |
| 하면 안 되는 일 | mustn't |
| 의무 없음 | don't have to |
| 금지 | mustn't |

## Exercises

### A. Have to vs Must 선택 (Choose the correct form)

1. School starts at 8:30. I _______ catch the 7:30 bus. (규칙)
2. I _______ finish this race. I've trained too hard to give up. (결심)
3. Yesterday I _______ wake up at 5 a.m. (과거)
4. You _______ try running. It's great! (강한 권유)
5. Next week, I _______ run 20km for training. (미래 필요)
6. We _______ wear uniforms at school. (규칙)
7. I _______ call my mom. I promised. (결심)
8. In Korea, you _______ drive on the right. (법)

**Answers:**
1. have to
2. must
3. had to
4. must
5. will have to
6. have to
7. must
8. have to

### B. Don't have to vs Mustn't 선택 (Choose the correct form)

1. You _______ run if you're tired. (필요 없음)
2. You _______ run on an injured leg. (금지)
3. I _______ take the bus today; I can walk. (필요 없음)
4. You _______ give up! Keep going! (금지)
5. You _______ pay. It's free. (필요 없음)
6. You _______ be late for the race. (금지)
7. We _______ hurry. We have plenty of time. (필요 없음)
8. You _______ tell anyone. It's a secret. (금지)

**Answers:**
1. don't have to
2. mustn't
3. don't have to
4. mustn't
5. don't have to
6. mustn't
7. don't have to
8. mustn't

### C. 문장 완성 (Complete the sentences)

1. 7시 30분 버스를 타야 해. (외부적)
   → I _______ the 7:30 bus.

2. 이 대회를 끝내야만 해. (결심)
   → I _______ this race.

3. 어제 일찍 일어나야 했어. (과거)
   → Yesterday I _______ early.

4. 안 달려도 돼. (필요 없음)
   → You _______ run.

5. 포기하면 안 돼. (금지)
   → You _______ give up.

**Answers:**
1. have to catch
2. must finish
3. had to wake up
4. don't have to
5. mustn't

### D. 틀린 부분 고치기 (Error Correction)

1. I must to go now.
   → I **must go** now.

2. He musted study hard yesterday.
   → He **had to study** hard yesterday.

3. You don't must run every day.
   → You **don't have to** run every day.

4. Do I must finish this today?
   → **Must** I finish this today? / Do I **have to** finish this today?

5. I will must train harder.
   → I **will have to** train harder.
