# Unit 31: Have to and must

## A. 문법 설명 (Grammar Explanation)

### Have to vs Must: 의무/필요

둘 다 "~해야 한다"는 의미이지만 뉘앙스가 다릅니다.

| Have to | Must |
|---------|------|
| 외부적 의무/규칙 | 내부적 의무/화자의 의견 |
| "They say I have to..." | "I feel I must..." |

### 형태:

**Have to:**
- 현재: have/has to + 동사원형
- 과거: had to + 동사원형
- 미래: will have to + 동사원형

**Must:**
- 현재만 사용 (과거/미래는 have to 사용)

---

## B. 소설 속 예문 (Examples from the Novel)

### Example 1: Have to (외부적 의무)

> **"I have to take the bus to school."**
> 학교에 버스를 타고 가야 해.

- 상황적으로 필요한 것 (학교가 멀어서)

### Example 2: Have to (규칙)

> **"School starts at 8:30, so I have to catch the 7:30 bus."**
> 학교가 8시 30분에 시작해서 7시 30분 버스를 타야 해.

- 외부적 규칙/일정

### Example 3: Must (개인적 결심)

> **"I must finish this race."**
> 이 대회를 완주해야만 해.

- 화자 자신의 강한 결심

### Example 4: Had to (과거 의무)

> **"I had to squeeze my way through the crowded bus."**
> 붐비는 버스에서 비집고 들어가야 했다.

- 과거에 해야 했던 일

### Example 5: Will have to (미래 의무)

> **"I'll have to train harder for the full marathon."**
> 풀 마라톤을 위해 더 열심히 훈련해야 할 거야.

- 미래에 해야 할 일

## C. 추가 설명 (Additional Points)

### Have to의 시제 변화

| 시제 | Have to | Must |
|------|---------|------|
| 현재 | I have to run. | I must run. |
| 과거 | I had to run. | (X) |
| 미래 | I will have to run. | (X) |
| 현재완료 | I have had to run. | (X) |

### Don't have to vs Mustn't

**중요한 차이!**

| Don't have to | Mustn't |
|---------------|---------|
| ~할 필요 없다 | ~하면 안 된다 |
| 선택 가능 | 금지 |
| You don't have to run. | You mustn't run. |
| (안 달려도 돼) | (달리면 안 돼) |

### 의문문

| Have to | Must |
|---------|------|
| Do I have to go? | Must I go? (격식체) |
| Does he have to run? | - |

## D. 비교 (Comparison)

| 상황 | Have to | Must |
|------|---------|------|
| 외부 규칙 | I **have to** be at school by 8:30. | - |
| 개인 결심 | - | I **must** finish the race. |
| 조언 (강함) | - | You **must** try this. |
| 과거 | I **had to** wake up early. | - |

### 소설 속 상황

도윤이의 의무:
- **Have to**: 버스 타야 함, 학교 가야 함 (외부적)
- **Must**: 대회 완주해야 함, 포기하면 안 됨 (내부적 결심)

## Exercises

### A. Have to vs Must 선택

1. School starts at 8:30. I _______ catch the 7:30 bus. (규칙)
2. I _______ finish this race. I've trained too hard to give up. (결심)
3. Yesterday I _______ wake up at 5 a.m. (과거)
4. You _______ try running. It's great! (강한 권유)
5. Next week, I _______ run 20km for training. (미래 필요)

**Answers:**
1. have to
2. must
3. had to
4. must
5. will have to

### B. Don't have to vs Mustn't 선택

1. You _______ run if you're tired. (필요 없음)
2. You _______ run on an injured leg. (금지)
3. I _______ take the bus today; I can walk. (필요 없음)
4. You _______ give up! Keep going! (금지)

**Answers:**
1. don't have to
2. mustn't
3. don't have to
4. mustn't

### C. 문장 완성

1. 7시 30분 버스를 타야 해. (외부적)
   → I _______ the 7:30 bus.

2. 이 대회를 끝내야만 해. (결심)
   → I _______ this race.

3. 어제 일찍 일어나야 했어. (과거)
   → Yesterday I _______ early.

**Answers:**
1. have to catch
2. must finish
3. had to wake up
